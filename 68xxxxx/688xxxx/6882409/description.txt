The Dewarists is a musical television series on Star World India. The series is part music documentary and part travelogue.The show casts musicians from various parts of the world, collaborating to create original music while travelling across India. So far the first five episodes has been showcased. And it features musicians Imogen Heap, Vishal-Shekhar, Zeb and Haniya, Shantanu Moitra, Swanand Kirkire, Indian Ocean, Mohit Chauhan, Parikrama, Agnee, Shilpa Rao, Shri, Monica Dogra and Rajasthan Roots. And the show is hosted by actor-singer Monica Dogra.
