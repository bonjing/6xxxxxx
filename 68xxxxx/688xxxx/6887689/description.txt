Synopsis & Plot :
Geet Gaata Chal is Produced by Tarachand Barjatya for Rajshri Productions the film is directed by Hiren Nag. The songs written and composed by Ravindra Jain played a large part in its unexpected success.
Rajshri Productions as well as Barjatyas are known for movies which revolve around social and family situations.
Geet Gaata Chal was a big success for its lead actors as well as the music director Ravindra Jain.

Durga Babu, and his wife, Ganga, come across Shyam, an orphan, who is a vagabond heart and sings and dances on special occasions, and they decide to take him home. Shyam meets with their daughter, Radha, and she slowly falls in love with him, but he thinks that she just wants to be friends. In his eyes, Shyam wants to be a free person, not tied down to anyone or anything, and would like to spend the rest of his life wondering, singing, and dancing. When he finds out that the family intend to get him married, he likens his plight to that of a caged bird, and flees, breaking Radha's heart. Will Shyam ever return? What impact will this have on the family?
