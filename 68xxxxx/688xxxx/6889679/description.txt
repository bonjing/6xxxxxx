After restoring the Atlantis World Museum, you have been accepted into the Grand Pelasgian University to become an apprentice in Chronology! Solve pictographic puzzles while learning about the origins of Mardi Gras, Oktoberfest, Chinese New Year, and other world holidays as you progress throughout your classes. Graduate to become a Chronologist in this fun and exciting Puzzle game!
