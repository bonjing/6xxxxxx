Russ Money and Cryaotic gave a public call to those with bravery, cunning, intelligence and free time to create a Amnesia map within the time span of twenty hour hours.

Those who were amazing enough to answer the call brought forth their awesome modding skills to produce this...

The 24 Hour Amnesia Compilation!

The story?  You take control of Daniel who wakes up with a terrible hangover, very little memory of the prior night and find out that your best-friend, a puppy named, Ralphie is missing!  Search for him and hopefully he's just fine!

Do you have what it takes to find your beloved Ralphie?
