Mac Wiseman 'Tis Sweet To Be Remembered - Complete Recordings [1951-1964] 6CD

Bear Family Records. Release: 2003. 



Bluegrass mainstay Mac Wiseman is given the royal treatment on this fine compilation, which cover his complete recordings from 1951 to 1964. The set comprises six CDs, and numbers "Little White Church," "I'll Still Write Your Name in the Sand," and "You're Sweeter Than Honey" among its highlights.
