Dire Straits were a British rock band active from 1977 to 1995, composed of Mark
Knopfler (lead vocals and lead guitar), his younger brother David Knopfler
(rhythm guitar and backing vocals), John Illsley (bass guitar and backing
vocals), and Pick Withers (drums and percussion).
