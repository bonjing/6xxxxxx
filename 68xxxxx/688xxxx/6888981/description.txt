Plot

As the film begins New Year\'s Eve  is on its way and television\'s most famous punk rock lady icon, Diane Sullivan or Blaze as her fans call her, is holding a late night countdown celebration of music and partying. 

All is going well until Diane receives a phone call from an odd sounding stranger claiming his name is Evil, who announces on live television that when the clock strikes twelve in each time zone, a \'Naughty Girl\' will be punished (murdered), then the killer signs off with a threat claiming that Diane will be the last one to die
