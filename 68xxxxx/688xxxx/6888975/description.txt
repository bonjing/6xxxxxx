Show Info:

X-Play Best of 2011 Awards

An entire year of gaming all come to this. At 6PM ET tonight, it's a special one-hour X-Play Best of 2011 Award Show. Over twenty categories, including the biggest one of all, Game of the Year.


Game of the Year

The nominees are - Batman: Arkham City, The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim, Gears of War 3, L.A. Noire, Portal 2, Saints Row: The Third, and Super Mario 3D Land.


Best Multiplayer Game

The nominees are - Assassin's Creed: Revelations, Battlefield 3, Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3, Dark Souls, and Gears of War 3.


Best Sports Game

The nominees are: FIFA 12, MLB 11: The Show, NBA 2K12, NHL 12, and WWE '12.


Best Shooter

The nominees are - Battlefield 3, Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3, Gears of War 3, RAGE, and Resistance 3.

  
Best Action/Adventure Game

The nominees are - L.A. Noire, Rayman Origins, Super Mario 3D Land, and Uncharted 3: Drake's Deception.


Best Motion Control Game

The nominees are - Dance Central 2, Killzone 3 with Sharpshooter, The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword, and Sesame Street: Once Upon a Monster.


...And So Many More!

Awards for Most Original Game, Best Gameplay Innovation, Best Multiplayer, Best Downloadable Game, Best RPG, Best Fighting Game, Best Writing, and still so many more. Don't miss it, tonight at a special time 6PM ET!
