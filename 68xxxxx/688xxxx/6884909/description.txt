Skeets McDonald - Dont Let The Stars Get In You Eyes [1949-1967] 

Bear Family Records. Release: 1998.



5 CD boxset includes a book that includes a newly-researched biography by Colin Escott. Photos & ephemera from family, friends & fans complete this long-overdue retrospective of a Post-War Country music pioneer & one of the best singers in country music history!

Recorded between 1949 and 1967.
