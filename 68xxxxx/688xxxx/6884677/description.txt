From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Anne Bronte; Full-cast dramatisation starring Poppy Miller, Robert Whelan and Alison Darling; Dramatised by Judith Adams.

When her family becomes impoverished after a disastrous financial speculation, Agnes Grey determines to find work as a governess in order to contribute to their meagre income and assert her independence. But Agnes' enthusiasm is swiftly extinguished...
