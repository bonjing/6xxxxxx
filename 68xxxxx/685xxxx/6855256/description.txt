Burandt's Backcountry Adventure is back with an action packed DVD featuring nothing but extreme backcountry riding and crazy technical tree riding. This years film offers two seasons worth of mind blowing footage featuring Chris Burandt and friends pushing the limits of backcountry riding as you know it. With footage from the 2010 and 2011 winter, this year's movie promises to offer insane footage and a first hand look at a true adventure Chris Burandt style!
Featuring Chris Burandt, Sahen Skinner, with special guest appearances from Brett Rasmussen, Burandt's mechanic and George Marsh, Burandt's Backcountry Adventures follows the Burandt team, offering a behind the scenes look into the true adventures of deep backcountry riding
