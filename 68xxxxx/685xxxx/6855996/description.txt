We Will Take You With Us is the title of the first DVD released by the Dutch symphonic metal band Epica.
The DVD has only material that was already released on Epica's debut album, The Phantom Agony, with the exception of "Memory", which is a cover of a song from the musical Cats.
Feint and Run for a Fall were both acoustic versions recorded for 2 Meter Sessies, a Dutch live music television show.
The title of this release is taken from the lyrics of "FaÃ§ade of Reality".

The nine tracks from the session were also released on audio CD.
