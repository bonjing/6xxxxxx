This a a bit of a mixture - there should be something here for most people's tastes and moods.

It will play like a random selection or a car radio being tuned but it's not; the selections are carefully chosen to surprise the listener on long car journeys etc. with some well-known numbers and also some rarer cuts.


The quality is mostly 320 kb/sec but there are a few tracks in variable bit rate and maybe the odd 128 kb/sec.It doesn't sound too bad in general considering it covers recordings from the early sixties up to the present.
