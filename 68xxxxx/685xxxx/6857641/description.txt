when Geordie actor Sammy Johnston died suddenlyin 1998,his friends Tim Haely and Jimmy Nail were determined he would be remembered.the results of their lobours were the formation of the Sammy Johnson Memorial Fund and a series of bi-annual concerts. On the 1st of February 2004,an all star cast of Tynesiders performed two sell-out concerts at Newcastle City Hall in support of the fund.Those lucky enough to get tickets laughed and sang their way through two hours of fantastic geordie entertainment
