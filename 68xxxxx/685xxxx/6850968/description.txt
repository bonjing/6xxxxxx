Now part 2 was REALLY nice for a cam .....
Just so you now part 1 last for roughly 32-33 mins then it switches over to the mint cam.
Work done .....hell fecking loads,firstly it was best to sync both parts before any cutting so this way i had the full video
to sync to and makes it alot easier....well sort of lol.
The first part had some movements that seem to make the audio go oos,so needed to cut into eight parts and synced it that way,will
say some parts are really hard to see so i've got it as close as i could.
2nd part ....well that was really easy,cut audio too match,shrinked to match video runtime and then it just needed one cut :D

so had to cut the file into 8 parts and cut and clean each part on their own.
Now to get these to append together i needed to stick to one resize (AR) and went with 720 x 400 as it seemed the best option.

Finally once all done muxed all the cuts together and then removed some video on the join as there was an overlap and also cut some intro out
