PLOT

Seven months have passed since the dead took over. Many cities and states 
have been abandoned and left to die. Several self sufficient hunting 
groups have been established to take on the threat in a war aptly 
referred to as the 'War on Dead'. Jillian Hurst, a former news writer and 
amateur documentarian, has set out to make a pro-war propaganda film to 
support the W.O.D. She joins up with a pack of renegade hunters known 
infamously to North America as 'the deadheads'. The moral dynamics and 
hardships of fighting in a war against the undead are told from several 
different perspectives.
