Ernest Tubb & The Texas Troubadours - Another Story [1966-1975]  

Bear Family Records 6 disc set. Release: 1999. With Covers & Discography


The fifth and last volume of Bear Family's complete Ernest Tubb retrospective! This one takes us from April 1966 to June 1975, featuring his last hits on Decca like I'm Gonna Make Like a Snake; Saturday Satan Sunday Saint , and Another Story . Includes 179 songs (six unreleased) and a 40-page book.
