1965 Melodiya recordings remastered and issued by Mobile Fidelity Sound Labs as MFCD 899 in their Cultural Exchange Release program. Violinist David Oistrakh performs the Khatachaturian concerto with composer Aram Khatachaturian conducting the Moscow Radio Great Symphony Orchestra. Oistrakh performs the Sibelius concerto with the Moscow Radio Symphony Orchestra conducted by Genneady Rozhestvensky.
