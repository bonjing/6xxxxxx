Whatever is the second studio album by Nashville pop rock band Hot Chelle Rae.It is the band's first album on RCA Records considering they were dropped from Jive Records at the time of it's closing. Two singles have been released from the album prior to its release: Tonight Tonight and I Like It Like That, featuring New Boyz. The album's name is taken from a lyric in Tonight Tonight and the title track.
