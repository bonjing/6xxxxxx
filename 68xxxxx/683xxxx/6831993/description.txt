Info
The Help is a 2011 comedy-drama film adaptation of Kathryn Stockett's novel of the same name. 
The film is about a young white woman, Eugenia "Skeeter" Phelan, and her relationship with two 
black maids during Civil Rights era America in the early 1960s. Skeeter is a journalist who 
decides to write a controversial book from the point of view of the maids (known as the Help), 
exposing the racism they are faced with as they work for white families.

The film takes place in Jackson, Mississippi, and stars Emma Stone, Viola Davis, Octavia 
Spencer, Bryce Dallas Howard, Jessica Chastain, Sissy Spacek, Mike Vogel, Mary Steenburgen 
and Allison Janney. The Help opened to positive reviews and became a box office success 
with gross of $189.7 million against its budget of $25 million.
