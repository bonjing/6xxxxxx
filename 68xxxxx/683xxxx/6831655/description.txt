The Blade of the Poisoner

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Douglas Hill; Full-cast dramatisation starring Eric Allen, James Holland, Hugh Dixon, Susan Sheridan, Ben Onwukwe and Elizabeth Bell; Dramatised by Wally K. Daly.

Episode 1: Three allies with psychic powers help 12-year-old Jarral to fight an evil prince.

Episode 2: Cut by a tainted blade, young Jarral faces death unless his three friends can save him.

Episode 3: Jarral and his friends head to Prince Mephtik's garden, where a battle awaits them.

Episode 4: Jarral must destroy Prince Mephtik and his knife before the full moon, or he'll die.
