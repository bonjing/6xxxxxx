Scrivener is a powerful content-generation tool for writers that allows you to concentrate on composing and structuring long and difficult documents. While it gives you complete control of the formatting, its focus is on helping you get to the end of that awkward first draft.


How to install:

1. Disconnect Internet.

2. Install Scrivener with the installer.

3. Execute the Patch, look for the Scrivener executable (it should be in 'C:\Program Files\Scrivener' or 'C:\Program Files (x86)\Scrivener' and patch it).

4. Execute Scrivener and use SCRWIN (in caps letters) as serial.
