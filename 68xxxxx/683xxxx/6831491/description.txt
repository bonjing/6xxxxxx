                                 Release Notes
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Founded in 1998 with the help of their mother Nyima, the brothers\' only caretaker, 
manager, and first fan, the JJI Exile Brothers maintain that they would not have 
been able to succeed without the support of their mother. 

The band, comprised of three brothers, takes its title from the initials of the 
three musicians, Jamyang, Jigme and Ingsel. Born and raised in exile, the brothers 
use their music to spread awareness of the Tibetan freedom struggle and to convey 
messages of love and peace on a global scale. In their own words, the brothers 
humbly serve to \"carry the spirit of the freedom struggle from the past, re-live 
it in the present, and preserve it for the future\".
