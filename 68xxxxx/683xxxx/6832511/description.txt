Vampire Adventures: Blood Wars v1.0.0 
Requirements: Android 2.1
Overview: The Drakul clan have started a Vampire war over the town of Hunger Hill. 
 

The Drakul clan has taken over the town of Hunger Hill, by capturing vampires,draining their blood, and are about to start a Vampire war.

Find and free the missing Vampires, and destroy the army of creatures that they have created to restore peace to Hunger Hill.
