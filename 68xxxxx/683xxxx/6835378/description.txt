The album contains live versions of many of the songs from his 1980s and 1990s solo albums, consciously avoiding his 1970s hits. It was accompanied by a video release on VHS, recorded at The Fillmore in San Francisco on June 15, 1995.

Frampton Comes Alive! II was released October 10, 1995 on IRS Records. The album reached No. 121 on the UK Albums Chart.
