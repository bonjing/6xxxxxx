an isolated East London industrial estate and setup
temporary accommodation in a disused clothing factory.
Armed only with antiquated studio-hardware, a polybag full

One vendor, he set about creating the conditions for a
series of musical events on his own terms.

want*.

Now, in the autumn of 2011, the sounds of this musical
journey are ready to make their way into the World.
