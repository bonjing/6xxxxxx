Dikanda - Polish band playing folk music.
Musicians of his work called the Four Party Music East, because it is inspired by the folk music of many ethnic groups, including Polish, Belarusian, Bulgarian, Macedonian, Jewish, Gypsy and Kurdish.
A characteristic feature Dikanda style is to create new words and meanings in composed songs.
