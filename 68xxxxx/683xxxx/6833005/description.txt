    Condemned has returned and the street date is set for the release of their
    2nd album entitled Realms of the Ungodly due out on Unique Leader Records.

    devastating brutality fused with dark visions of ancient evil, bestial
    figures and barren worlds of tormented spirits. Condemned ravages through
    scores of enfeebled souls to bring to you the heaviest death metal release
    of the year. The release was recorded by enfineer Samur Khouja of Seahorse
    Sound Studios and mastered by Scott Hull of Visceral Sound Mastering giving
    the final product a massive & high quality sound. Phenomenal art concept
    created by the famous Jon Zig. Realms of the Ungodly will be available
    worldwide November 8th.
