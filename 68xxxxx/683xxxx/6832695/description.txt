Synopsis:

40 19 "Lions and Lambs"   November 4, 2011  

Breach has her portal-creating abilities greatly strengthened and magnified by Van Kleiss, 
to the point where she can now create bright golden-colored swirling portals through time itself. 
To stop this dangerous threat, Rex must try and convince Breach to come over to the good side willingly. 
White Knight is being pressured by an unknown superior of Providence. A cat and mouse game ensues 
as Van Kleiss tracks Breach around the world. After an attempt to force Breach to come to Providence by Knight, 
Rex gives Breach control of her full power which sends Van Kleiss and apprently herself somewhere in time. 
Rex, in the meantime, wakes to find himself in the middle of a empty desert landscape and calls for 
assistance on her communicator. Upon returning to Providence, Rex's brother tells him that 6 months have 
passed and apprently Agent 6 and Dr. Holiday are no longer working at Providence. 
There, Rex meets a woman calling herself Black Kinght- White's replacement.
