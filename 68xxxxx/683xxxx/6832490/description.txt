A crossover between "Twin Peaks" and "Northern Exposure".
"Timrot Ashan" is an Israeli Thriller/Drama TV series, about the events surrounding the mysterious disappearance of the settlers of "Timrot Ashan".

Filmed in the beautiful and pastoral Golan heights. In Hebrew.
