Happy Feet Two - The Videogame is a 3D musical adventure game for the
entire family inspired by the whimsical Antarctic world of the upcoming
animated film Happy Feet Two. The upbeat game is scored by the three
time Grammy Award winning band Ozomatli to get players into the penguin groove.
