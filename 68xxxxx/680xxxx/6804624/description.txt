The Dewarists is a musical television series on Star World India.[1][2][3] The series is part music documentary and part travelogue.[4] The show casts musicians from various parts of the world, collaborating to create original music while travelling across India. So far the first five episodes has been showcased.[5] And it features musicians Imogen Heap, Vishal-Shekhar[6], Zeb and Haniya, Shantanu Moitra, Swanand Kirkire, Indian Ocean, Mohit Chauhan, Parikrama, Agnee, Shilpa Rao, Shri, Monica Dogra and Rajasthan Roots. And the show is hosted by actor-singer Monica Dogra.
