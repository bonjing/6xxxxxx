Super Granny is back and better than ever! As Super Granny, Kamila and Margaret reminisce about their past adventures, help the ladies navigate exotic locales. Everything will lead up to a final showdown with the nefarious, and dastardly named, Colonel Hans Foote, the leader of a mysterious shadow organization. With all-new enemies and a hilarious storyline, help the ladies run, dig and climb their way through exciting levels in this puzzle-solving, item-tossing, addictive adventure.
