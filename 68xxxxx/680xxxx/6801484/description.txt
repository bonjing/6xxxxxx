Jahan Pyar Mile is directed by Lekh Tandon. The film stars Shashi Kapoor, Hema Malini, Nadira and Helen. The films music is by Shankar Jaikishan.
This is Hema Malini's second film and she has still done a commendable job. Shashi kapoor has however stolen the show with his remarkable act.
