Mr Standfast

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by John Buchan; Full-cast dramatisation starring David Robb, Clive Merrison, Jasmine Hyde and Jon Glover; Dramatised by Bert Coules.

Richard Hannay is recalled from the trenches of the Somme and sent to hunt down the spy ring he first encountered in The Thirty-Nine Steps.
