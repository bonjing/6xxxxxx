Granny loves her kitties, but she needs your help! Navigate through an exotic park and help rescue her feline friends in this fun and hilarious adventure. You can upgrade items to make this task a bit easier, or make your own version using the level editor. Either way, Super Granny 3 will keep you on the kitty hunt!

200+ levels over 5 scenes.
8 item upgrades.
Discover secret areas.
