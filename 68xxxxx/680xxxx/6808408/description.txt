The adventures of a dapper former Confederate officer who returns home to New Orleans several years after the end of the Civil War and becomes secretly employed as a crime fighter by the Yankee administrator of the city.  Yancy has a family plantation, Waverly, and owns a riverboat, the Sultana.  He carries a four barrel Derringer gun in his hat and a cane with a hidden sword.  He has a silent Indian friend and bodyguard, Pahoo-Ka-Ta-Wah, who communicates only by hand gestures but carries a shotgun and throwing knife.
