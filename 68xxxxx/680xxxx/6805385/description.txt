

             Drake- Take Care: Album Trailer. November 15




       Trailer for Drake's upcoming Sophomore album, "Take Care", which'll be 
         getting relesed on 15th November.
      

Drake mentioned after OVO Fest 2011 that Take Care could have up to 18 songs on it, and added that Stevie Wonder contributed to the creative direction of the album and will be featured on the album as well.Drake also revealed that the album was recorded mainly in Toronto.
