For those who enjoy 'adult' takes on fairy tale motifs (and given the fact that we have 'Wicked' on Broadway and 'Once Upon a Time' & 'Grimm' on network TV, there's a lot of you), this is one of the essential authors of the genre. Angela Carter's collected works are brilliant stories, alternately terrifying and sweet.
