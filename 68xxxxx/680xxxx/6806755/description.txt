 Helicopters:  Military,  Civilian and Rescue Rotorcraft



Each of over 120 types is afforded a double-page entry which typically includes a colour profile, a photo file of around ten colour or b/w images, a brief history, assorted facts and figures, a specification table and colour graphics illustrating aspects of performance in comparison with its contemporaries.
