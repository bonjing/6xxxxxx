An exciting and gutsy album of Christmas and Midwinter music from a band at the forefront of the English traditional folk scene. 
Formed in 2002, Kerfuffle have become one of the most popular bands on the English folk scene. Their new offering, Lighten The Dark: A Midwinter Album, is an exciting mixture of traditional Christmas and Winter themed material and will be released in time for their December tour 2009. The album features band members Sam Sweeney, Hannah James, Jamie Roberts, Tom Sweeney and two guest musicians, Andy Letcher and Lily Neill.
