A student of kung fu learns that he possesses incredible powers in KUNG FU FIGHTER. When a group of Shanghai gangsters cross his path the fists start to fly and incredible action sequences ensue.

Director : Yip Wing-Kin
Cast : Vanness Wu, Lam Chi-Chung, Chan Kwok-Kwan, Tin Kai-Man, Emme Wong Yi-Man, Bruce Leung Siu-Lung, Louis Fan Siu-Wong, Lee Wai, Max Zhang, Cheung Man-Kit
