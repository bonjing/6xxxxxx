Title: Koala Lumpur: Journey to the Edge
Genre: adventure / puzzle-solving / 3rd-person
Released: 3rd February 1997
Developer: Broderbund Software
Publisher: Colossal Pictures
Description:
You play the part of Fly, a yellow and black buzzing insect who leads the marsupial Koala Lumpur on a quest to find the pieces of the all-important Lost Scroll of Cartoon Prophecies. According to Ella Mental, the Supreme Being of the Cartoon Universe, failure to complete the quest will result in the release of the sinister Macho de Nada who will unleash a Comedy Apocalypse, destroying all.

Bonus content: manual.

How to play:
1) Download the game;
2) Mount or burn disc image;
3) Run SETUP.EXE to install (it will start automatically if autorun is enabled);
4) Play the game with the disc inserted.

Warning!
The installer doesn't work on Vista/Windows 7. I played the game on Windows XP.
