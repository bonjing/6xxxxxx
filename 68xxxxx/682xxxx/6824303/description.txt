Release Description:

AD Sound Recorder is a sound record program. It can record all audio
from your sound card into MP3, WMA or WAV format. Real-time sound
visualization enables you to adjust your audio source and make high
quality recordings.
AD Sound Recorder features include hot switching of the working audio
source, waveform and spectrum monitoring, a peak indicator with sound
clipping detectors, recording control with the hotkeys, a built-in
player, a file list and an ID3 Tag editor.

Just unpack&install. Register with the serial in serial.txt.
