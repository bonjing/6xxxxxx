Way of the Warrior is a TV documentary series of programs about Martial Arts from around the world.

The quality of the movies are not the best, but very watchable. If you want HD, go to the store and buy your very own copy.

Aikido, Kendo, Kalapayit, Karate, KungFu, T'aiChi, Samurai and Kempo are investigated in a dry but interesting way.
