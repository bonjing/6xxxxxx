A side scrolling where the player takes the role of a young assassin/thief type girl character. The game isn't finished yet, but it's one of the better hentai games out there. The graphics are smooth, and the hitboxes are fair. It also has game over rape cinematics.

You will probably need to set your locale to Japanese, or use applocale. If the game fails to launch, that's probably your issue!

The controls are arrow keys to move, Z does the normal attack, X does the projectile attack (you will run out of ammo unless you pick up more daggers), and C is the jump key. There's a variety of moves you can do, from aerial attacks to ground moves and whatnot.

If you end up dying, the top option is the continue (you got unlimited continues, and they go back to your last checkpoint you picked up), and the bottom option is the game over, which will take you to a rape scene or a defeated scene depending on what killed you. If you choose to view the HCG, you can click the second main-menu option and be taken back to your last checkpoint.

System requirements: 1 GB RAM and 1 GHz CPU suggested.

Creator's Blog: http://irisaction.blog.fc2.com/

Full game expected to be released in early 2012.
