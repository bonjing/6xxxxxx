Take Care is the second studio album by Canadian recording artist Drake, scheduled for release through Young Money Entertainment, Cash Money Records and Universal Republic Records on November 15, 2011.The album will be released in three versions: the Standard edition, the Birthday edition and the OG Ron C edition.
Singles \"Headlines\" and \"Make Me Proud\" preceded the album\'s release, each attaining chart success on the Billboard Hot 100.
