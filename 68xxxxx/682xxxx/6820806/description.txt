Learn to play Pink Floyd - Volume 1 - Disc 2

Product Description

Learn five Pink Floyd tracks note for note with Jamie Humphries

This excellent 2 DVD set will guide you through the bluesy rock licks of Dave Gilmour, one of the greatest Strat players of all time! Tracks include; Shine On You Crazy Diamond, Money, Wish You Were Here, Comfortably Numb, Another Brick In The Wall

Jamie Humphries is the author of the acclaimed Giants Of Rock and Giants Of Metal guitar courses. He is a tutor at one of the UKs leading music colleges, and a regular contributor to Guitar Techniques magazine.
