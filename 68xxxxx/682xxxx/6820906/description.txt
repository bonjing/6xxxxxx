A martial arts student (Sammo Hung) is beaten in a fight and insists on becoming the servant and student of the other fighter, a young man on the run from his brother. The insistent pupil gets involved in the feud between the two brothers, but the main focus in this film is the non-stop martial arts action, featuring terrific stunts and a superior plot. 

Starring:    John Li, Hung Kam-Bo, Sammo Hung, Leung Ka Yan
Directed by: Sammo Hung
Genre:       Action, Drama, Martial Art
