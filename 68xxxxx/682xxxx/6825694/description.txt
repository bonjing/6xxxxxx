Don 2: The Chase Continues is an upcoming Indian action thriller film directed by Farhan Akhtar. It is the sequel to the 2006 film, Don: The Chase Begins Again, which itself was a remake of the 1978 film Don. The film stars Shahrukh Khan, Priyanka Chopra in the lead roles and Boman Irani, Om Puri, Lara Dutta and Kunal Kapoor in supporting roles. The film is set to release on 23 December 2011 in both 2D and 3D formats and will also be available in Tamil and Telugu languages.
