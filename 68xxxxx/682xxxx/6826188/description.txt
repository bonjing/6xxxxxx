This text is your comprehensive resource for a progression from the rudiments of AutoCAD to the advanced concepts of 3D modeling and customization, Problems in each chapter challenge students and professionals to think beyond step-by-step approaches and teach the "whys" and "hows" behind using AutoCAD to solve drafting and design problems. Catering to the basic needs of beginners as well as to the advanced requirements of industry professionals, detailed explanations of AutoCAD 2010 commands enable users to take maximum advantage of Autodesk's newest and most essential software features.
