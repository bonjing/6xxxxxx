

WWIII is a hard edged metal band with sexual lyrics, the brainchild of eccentric vocalist Mandy Lion. The group has gone through several lineup changes throughout its history and hasn't reached much more then cult status.

Mandy Lion - vocals
Tracy G. - guitar
Jimmy Bain - bass
Vinny Appice - drums
