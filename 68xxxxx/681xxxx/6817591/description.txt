Title: Disney's Villains' Revenge
Genre: adventure / puzzle-solving / 1st-person
Released: 28th September 1999
Developer: Disney Interactive
Publisher: Disney Interactive
Description:
Jiminy Cricket is the guardian of a book in the player's room which features several stories with happy endings. However, the book's happy ending pages are ripped out by a very bored Jiminey Cricket, who has read them so often that they put him to sleep. As a game, he asks the player to put them back where they belong. Suddenly, the book is possessed by the spirits of several Disney Villains, who alter the stories to their advantage. The Blue Fairy appears and explains that because stories live on in the hearts of readers, removing the happy endigs left the stories at their climax, with the heroes in peril and the villains in control. Jiminy and the player venture into the worlds of the stories to correct the happy endings.

How to play:
1) Download the game;
2) Mount or burn disc images;
3) Run Burst.exe to install;
