WIA compressed image, must be converted to ISO or WBFS with tools like wit, see: 
http://wit.wiimm.de/info/wia.html

to convert to iso:
wit copy "The Legend of Zelda Skyward Sword [SOUP01].wia" -d . -I

to wbfs:
wit copy "The Legend of Zelda Skyward Sword [SOUP01].wia" -d . -B
