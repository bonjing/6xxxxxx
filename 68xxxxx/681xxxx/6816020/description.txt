This is a small collection of special recipes from around the world.  Also included are instructions for processing raw pumpkin (including the old Jack-o-Lantern from Halloween, if desired) to make pumpkin pies from scratch, and a instructions for creating a Thanksgiving table decoration - a Bread Cornucopia - from Pillsbury Breadstick dough.

For those who celebrate this US Holiday, or simply love to cook, Happy Thanksgiving!
