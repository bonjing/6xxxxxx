Birdsong

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Sebastian Faulks; Full-cast dramatisation starring Toby Stephens, Sophie Ward, John Rowe and Alison Pettitt; Dramatised by Nick Stafford.

Episode 1: In 1910, Stephen Wraysford falls in love with the wife of a wealthy baron.

Episode 2: After Isabelle leaves, Stephen becomes immersed in one of the 20th century's bloodiest wars.

Episode 3: Stephen Wraysford returns to his old haunts in France, ravaged by the First World War.
