Co:Writer is the pencil, your applications are the paper. Co:Writer works in conjunction with any application you write in like MS Word, Online, Blogs, Email, etc. As you type, Co:Writer interprets spelling and grammar mistakes and offers word suggestions in real time. 

Students with:

Illegible handwriting
Very poor phonetic or inventive spelling 
A physical disability makes typing difficult
Difficulty translating thoughts into writing 

Co:Writer's Linguistic Word Prediction technology is the only option for students with significant spelling and grammar challenges.
