 The little known hilarious sequel to the Christmas classic "A Christmas Story". Author Jean Shepherd gives us another fantastic tale with Ralphie Parker and his funny family.

For 10 year old Ralphie Parker, it would be a summer filled with adventure. Whether conducting a quest for the ultimate in spinning tops, watching his mother lead the gravy boat rebellion at the Orpheum Theatre, or aiding the Old man in his one man war against their hillbilly neighbors, this was the summer Ralphie would always remember as the greatest of his life.

If you liked A Christmas Story, you have to see this sequel.
