The Turn of the Screw

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Henry James; Full-cast dramatisation starring Cathy Sara, Joseph Tremain, Lulu Popplewell, Tina Gray and Robert Lister; Dramatised by Neville Teller.

A governess suspects her two charges are harbouring a secret - but the truth is more terrifying than she could ever have imagined.
