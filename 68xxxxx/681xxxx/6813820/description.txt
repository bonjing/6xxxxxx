I have to say that this CD set could do with a remaster. I compared
the joint stereo 5CD mp3 version already being offered around (its
bloated with several huge png files that are simply images of what each of the
cd covers themselves look like etc) - and found the sound wasnt good enough
- so we tried the flac version next - and found that converting these to the
mp3 versions we have here as as good as it gets - until we have a remastered
version that is as there are times when the background seems to need a filtered
fine-tune and the other mp3 versions we tried didnt succeed in getting that 
accomplished in any event.

I dont know of anyone that thinks theres more than 320k mp3 at 44khz
frequency highs (into the harmonics at that rate certainly) can be had
from a CD even if you make it a flac fle first - however feel free to
comment if you dont think so - its a free world anyhows - thats how we 
all learn and improve for the benefit of the community wich we serve.

Michael Rizzo
