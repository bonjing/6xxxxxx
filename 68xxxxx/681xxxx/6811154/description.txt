"Robin: Year One" is the title of a DC Comics story arc written by Chuck Dixon and Scott Beatty and illustrated by Javier Pulido and Marcos Martin. It was originally published in single magazine form as Robin: Year One #1-#4. The story is narrated by Alfred Pennyworth and tells of Dick Grayson's first year as The Batman's sidekick, Robin.
