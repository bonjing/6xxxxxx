**********************************************************************************

M.A.M.E. Artwork Special Edition is a collection of the Artwork, Artwork 
Preview, Bosses, Cabinets, Control Panels, Flyers, Game Over, How To, Icons, 
Logo, Manuals, Marquees, PCBs, Samples, Scores, Select, Snaps, Titles & Versus.

get the best experience possible out of MameUI. I welcome any suggestions, 
comments or complaints that will help me create a better release.
