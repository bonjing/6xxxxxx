Lego Ninjago: Masters of Spinjitzu is a 2011 action comedy family animated film that centers around a teenager named Kai who transforms his life from Samurai to Ninja. The film was based on the Lego toy series of the same name. The movie is split in to two seasons with 2 episodes in each.
