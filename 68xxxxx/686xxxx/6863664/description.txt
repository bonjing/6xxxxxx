Title: Falcon Gold
Genre: simulation / flight / military
Released: 1991
Developer: Spectrum Holobyte
Publisher: Spectrum Holobyte
Description:
Falcon Gold includes updated versions of Falcon 3.0 with its three expansions (MiG-29, Hornet: Naval Strike Fighter and Operation: Fighting Tiger), and the video documentary on dogfighting, Art of the Kill.

Bonus content: manual.

How to play:
1) Download the game;
2) Mount or burn disc image;
3) Run INSTALL.EXE to install;
4) Play the games using their main executables.

! Warning
This is an old game, use an old OS or DOSBox (included) if you want to play it. 

! Notice
If you're having problems with the Art of the Kill video player, extract NEWAOTK.zip archive to the folder where you installed Art of the Kill and use NEWAOTK.EXE to watch the videos.
