
Omschrijving:
Two hours of classic Kylie, capturing the energy, grandeur and extravagance of the massively acclaimed Aphrodite: Les Folies tour. Filmed at Kylie's triumphant O2 Arena show in London, this concert film was produced using a lavishly hi-tech multi-camera shoot affording the viewer a spectacular experience from start to finish. The 2-hour show proved to be one of the most elaborate and extravagant productions ever staged by a pop performer, a multi-million dollar visual and musical feast, that delighted critics and fans alike wherever it played. Now Kylie's fans can re-live the experience with this amazing 2-hour document, which includes all of her best-known modern hits- "Can t Get You Out Of MyHead", "Spinning Around"- as well as her four Billboard Club smashes from 2010's acclaimed "Aphrodite" album including "All The Lovers" and "Get Outta My Way".
