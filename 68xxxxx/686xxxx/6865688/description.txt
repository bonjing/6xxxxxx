Health-Medical Documentary hosted by Piers Gibbon, published by National Geographic broadcasted as part of NG The Witch Doctor Will See You Now series in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

NG The Witch Doctor Will See You Now: Goat Blood Bath 
Jenna has suffered from panic attacks and claustrophobia throughout her life, while Dwight has endured chronic back pain, arthritis and sleep apnoea. They're travelling with Piers Gibbon to Africa to see if ancient medical practices can succeed where Western medicine has failed. First, they meet a renowned traditional healer, who decides that the best way for Jenna to overcome her anxiety is to swallow the still-beating heart of a freshly killed chicken.
