Beyond the Moon

Science Documentary hosted by James Burke, published by BBC broadcasted as part of BBC Horizon series in 1984 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

In this special episode, Horizon brings you a report on space exploration and exploitation. The first half of this documentary looks back at the Apollo 11 moon landing (using material of the time), and the second half looks at the future plans of the space program. Originally aired on 21st July, 1984. Presented by James Burke.
