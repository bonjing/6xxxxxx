Revolver (stylized as rEVOLVEr) is the fourth studio album by American R&B recording artist T-Pain, set to be released through his own label Nappy Boy Entertainment, Konvict Muzik, and RCA Records. Recording sessions for the album have taken place from 2009 to 2011.
