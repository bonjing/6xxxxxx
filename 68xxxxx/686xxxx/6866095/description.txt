Galaxy Bowling 3D v1.3
Requirements: Android 2.0.1+
Overview: Galaxy Bowl - the best bowling since the Big Bang!



Galaxy Bowl is a 3D 10-pin bowling game of universal proportions! It sports realistic physics, pin point control, 17 different balls, over 20 achievements, and planet-loads of fun!
Unlock new galaxy-themed balls, conquer challenging achievements, and complete your voyage of the solar system. Can you reach Pluto?
Also looks great on large screen tablets!
Achievements and online leader boards powered by OpenFeint.
