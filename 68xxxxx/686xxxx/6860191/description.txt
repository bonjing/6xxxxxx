The Invasion has begun..
It's an ordinary summer's day and the sky is clear. But without warning, something very extraordinary started to happen. Flying saucers darkened the skies, strange alien critters landed, and before long the whole world was in great danger. With the fate of our planet at stake, it's up to you to take control of the alien chickens before they eradicate our land forever.
