You will play the game as a doctor who has been called to the hotel to attend to a patient. Shortly after your arrival, a terrible storm hits and washes out all the roads leading in and out of the hotel. You and the other guests of the hotel are trapped. This is, of course, when things begin to wrong. One by one, guests start dying. Its up to you to find the killer and stop them before its too late.

System Requirements:

OS: Windows XP/Vista/Win7
CPU: 1.2 GHz
RAM: 512 MB
DirectX: 9.0
Hard Drive: 0.98 GB
