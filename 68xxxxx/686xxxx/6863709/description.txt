FIFA 11 (Jimbus edition)
-------



Features of this edition:


  - It can be burnt into a single DVD-5.

  - The game is updated to 1.01 version.

  - Audio and texts are in several languages, English and Spanish among them.

  - L.A.N. play works.

  - Nothing has been deleted: the game is complete.

  - It includes a trainer.

  - Custom installer with everything included: just install and play.
