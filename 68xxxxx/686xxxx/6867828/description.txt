The Alterer

From BBC Radio 4. Written by Final Welsh; Full-cast dramatisation starring Cal MacAninch, Liam Brennan, Pauline Knowles and Finn den Hertog.

Scotland, 1791: A watchmaker pours all of his skill and knowledge into making a machine that will alter time and create a different universe; one in which he hopes his desperately ill daughter will be returned to him, fully recovered.
