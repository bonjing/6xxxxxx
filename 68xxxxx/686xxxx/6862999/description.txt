

The Story : Melvilasam is the malayalam movie directed by Madhav Ramdas, Suresh Gopi play the lead rol in this film along with Parthiban. The movie is a remeake of yester years hit drama 'Melvilasam' written and directed by Soory Krishnamurthy. Movie produced by Muhammed Salim under the banner of Mark movies
