The World As Hologram

Science Documentary hosted by Leonard Susskind, published by TVO broadcasted as part of TVO Big Ideas series in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Leonard Susskind of the Stanford Institute for Theoretical Physics discusses the indestructability of information and the nature of black holes in a lecture entitled The World As Hologram. Presented on June 28, 2011

Originally broadcast on November 5, 2011
