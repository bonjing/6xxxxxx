In shock and denial over his Marine fathers death in battle, star Lacrosse player Conor Sullivan, always a maverick and a hothead, starts acting out in self-destructive ways that have his mom, Claire, at her wits end. But arduous training in a wilderness Lacrosse camp under the tutelage of his dads old combat buddy, Sgt. Major Duke Wayne, opens Conors eyes to the true meaning of maturity, sportsmanship and manhood
