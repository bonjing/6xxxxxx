Plot:
Nastik is essentially a movie about faith (or the lack thereof: nastik = atheist), and unusually for a Hindi movie, contains a fairly strong condemnation of the hypocrisy in organized religion. Comedian IS Johar both wrote and directed the film, and has a supporting role. A strikingly young and handsome Ajit stars alongside the beautiful Nalini Jaywantâ€”who looks a lot like Nargis, and is absolutely wonderful as Rama, the moral center of the story. The real star of the show, though, is the sublime music by Chitalkar Ramchandra (a severely underrated music director) with lyrics by the great lyricist and poet Kavi Pradeep.
