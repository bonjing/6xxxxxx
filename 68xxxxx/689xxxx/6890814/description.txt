Jon Lord Blues Project Live
[320k MP3]

The recording was released in July 2011 by MFP Concerts Music Productions. The show was recorded live at Rottweil Jazzfestival on May 14, 2011 and features classic blues songs written by Willie Dixon and Robert Johnson as well as more contemporary compositions by Deep Purple, Free and Tom Waits.

    "Back At The Chicken Shack" (James O. Smith)
    "Hoochie Coochie Man" (Willie Dixon)
    "Wishing Well" (John Bundrick/Simon Kirke/Kossoff/Rodgers)
    "It Never Rains But It Pours" (George Money/Colin Allen)
    "Fog On The Highway" (Miller Anderson)
    "Lazy" (Jon Lord/Gillan/Blackmore/Roger Glover/Ian Paice)
    "Walkin' Blues" (Robert Johnson)
    "Way Down In The Hole" (Tom Waits)
    "Houston (Scotland)" (Miller Anderson)
    "Respect Yourself" (Luther Ingram/Mack Rice)
    "When A Blind Man Cries" (Lord/Gillan/Blackmore/Glover/Ian Paice)
    "I'm A Man" (James D. Miller/Stephen L. Winwood)

Personnel

    Jon Lord: Hammond organ
    Miller Anderson: guitar and vocals (2, 5, 9, 11, 12)
    Maggie Bell: vocals (3, 6, 8, 10, 12)
    Colin Hodgkinson: bass and vocals (7)
    Zoot Money: keyboards and vocals (4, 6, 10)
    Pete York: Drums
