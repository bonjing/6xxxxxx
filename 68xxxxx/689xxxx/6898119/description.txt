From the Oscar-winning team behind MAN ON WIRE comes the story of 
Nim, the chimpanzee who in the 1970s became the focus of a 
landmark experiment which aimed to show that an ape could learn 
to communicate with language if raised and nurtured like a human 
child. Following Nim's extraordinary journey through human 
society, and the enduring impact he makes on the people he meets 
along the way, the film is an unflinching and unsentimental 
biography of an animal we tried to make human. What we learn 
about his true nature - and indeed our own - is comic, revealing 
and profoundly unsettling.
