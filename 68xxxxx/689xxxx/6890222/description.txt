Description: 
The Victorian farm team tackle their biggest project yet: 
restoring the village blacksmith's forge. First they must 
make bricks to restore the chimney - a full five day and 
night process in which the farmer gets no sleep.
As winter marches on and Christmas nears, they must 
source a yule log: firewood to burn for the Twelve Days 
of Christmas. At the cottage, Ruth winter-proofs the house, making a paper blanket and remedies for chilblains, rheumatism, coughs and colds.
It is also a chance to begin preparations for the Christmas 
banquet in earnest - in particular, a very Victorian 
invention, Christmas crackers. Ruth enlists the help of 
Christmas cracker historian Peter Kimpton.
