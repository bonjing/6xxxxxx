Voxit Budgie Pro is a program developed in Sweden which will convert text to speech. Apart from the Nordic languages, many other languages are supported such as British English, American English, German, French and Spanish.

The program has an English UI.
Supported operative systems are Windows XP, Vista and 7, 32 and 64 bits.
