Changing Video Settings
Default is Low Settings with 1280 x 800 Resolution
To change it, simply drag a new SkyrimPrefs.ini from \"The Elder Scrolls V Skyrim/SkyrimPrefs.ini Files/Low, Medium, High or Ultra\" to \"/The Elder Scrolls V Skyrim/The Elder Scrolls V Skyrim.app/Contents/Resources/transgaming/c_drive/Program Files/The Elder Scrolls V Skyrim/%USERPROFILE%/My Documents/My Games/Skyrim\"
Changing the resolution can be done by altering the iSize W and H values in the SkyrimPrefs.ini in the game itself

Installation
For Cider Wrapper!
1. Drag your game files to /Contents/Resources/transgaming/c_drive/Program Files/The Elder Scrolls V Skyrim
2. Select your correct Video Settings by dragging either Low, Medium, High or Ultra to /Contents/Resources/transgaming/c_drive/Program Files/The Elder Scrolls V Skyrim/%USERPROFILE%/My Documents/My Games/Skyrim
3. Play!
