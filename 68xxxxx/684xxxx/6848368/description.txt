Two CD set released on November 8th, 2011 that was recorded on April 15th, 2001 in Cleveland, OH as a tribute to the city that first played their music on the radio. The Time Machine tour was notable for featuring the album Moving Pictures played in its entirety for the first time live, as well as material from the band's upcoming studio album Clockwork Angels. This tour is one of three where the setlist has been consistent throughout the entire tour, the others being the 1987 Hold Your Fire tour and the 2004 R30: 30th Anniversary Tour.
