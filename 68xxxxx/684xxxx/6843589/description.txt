Synopsis:
Baazi is directed by Guru Dutt. Dev Anand\'s Navketan production house produced the movie, and as per a commitment given by Dev Anand to Guru Dutt in their days of struggle, the movie was directed by Guru Dutt.

The movie stars Dev Anand with Geeta Bali and Kalpana Kartik. It is a crime thriller and has superhit music composed by S.D. Burman.

The film is a tribute to the Forties\' Film Noir Hollywood with the morally ambiguous hero, the transgressing siren, and shadow lighting. It was very successful at the box-office.
