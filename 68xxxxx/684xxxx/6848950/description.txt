Ahinsa is a story of how a man due to his non-violence action changes a dacoits heart and makes him surrender to the law.

This film depicts the efforts of non-violent follower and Mahatma Gandhi's disciple Jai Prakash Narayan, when a large number of dacoits of Chambal ravines in Uttar Pradesh,India surrendered to Jaiprakash Narayan in 1973.
