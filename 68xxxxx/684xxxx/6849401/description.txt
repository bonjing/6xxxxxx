Rock'n'Roll Party (Honoring Les Paul) is a live album by Jeff Beck, recorded as a tribute album to the late guitarist Les Paul.
The album is recorded at the Iridium Jazz Club in New York City where Les Paul played almost every week until his death in August 2009.
