Emergency 3: mission - life, is the third game in the series, developed by Sixteen Tons Entertainment and published by Strategy First in 2006. If you like tough situations this is the game for you. It is a nice blend of simulation and strategy game. You take control of fire and rescue teams, police and specialist personnel and you must act accordingly to save lives and properties from fires, accidents and even terrorist acts. The campaign includes 20 missions and in freeplay mode random events occur with the player trying to act as fast as possible. Just run the setup and play the game. Enjoy and seed!
