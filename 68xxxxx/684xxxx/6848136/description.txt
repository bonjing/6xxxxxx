Early 1960s recordings of works by Francis Poulenc - Gloria for Soprano, Choir and Orchestra, featuring soprano Rosanna Carteri, and Concerto for Organ, Strings and Timpani, featuring organist Maurice Durufle. French National Radio and Television Orchestra, Georges Pretre, conductor. The Gloria was premiered January 20, 1961, in Boston and recorded the following day under the supervision of Poulenc. The recordings were originall released on the Angel label. This comes from a fine commercial four-track open reel tape produced in the 1960s.
