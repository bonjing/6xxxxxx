Barbra Joan Streisand (born April 24, 1942) is an American singer, actress,
film producer and director. She has won two Academy Awards, eight Grammy
Awards, four Emmy Awards, a Special Tony Award, an American Film Institute
award, a Peabody Award, and is one of the few entertainers who have won
an Oscar, Emmy, Grammy, and Tony Award.
