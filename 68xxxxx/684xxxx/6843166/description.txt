The Ambassadors

From BBC Radio 4. Written by Henry James; Full-cast dramatisation starring Henry Goodman, Orlando Seale and Joanna Bergin; Dramatised by Graham White.

Paris, 1903: American Lambert Strether arrives in the French capital intent on bringing his fiancee's wayward son back home - but his plans change when he becomes infatuated with both the city and a charismatic countess.
