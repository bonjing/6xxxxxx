 Oreo (Favorites, 79 Easy Recipes & Cookie Crafts)


Everybody knows that Oreo tastes great dunked in milk,but that's not the only way to enjoy it. Now discover all the scrumptious ice cream desserts,cakes,snack mixes and more you can make with this creme-filled chocolate gem. Sandwiched between these covers,you'll find: 79 delectable treats for all ocassions;Oreo fun facts and activities; 40 color food and craft photographs; Holiday desserts featuring Halloween,Holiday and Spring Oreo Cookies; Recipes kids can make.
