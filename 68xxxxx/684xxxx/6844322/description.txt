Plot :
Chaitali is a 1975 film directed by Hrishikesh Mukherjee. The film was produced by Mrs. Monabai Roy wife of lengendary film director, Bimal Roy. The music of the film was composed by the duo Laxmikant-Pyarelal. The film has actors Dharmendra, Pradeep Kumar and Saira Banu in lead.

This is the story about a poor girl Chaitali, who enters a loving/caring family with an intension of stealing, but with a good reason/cause to cure her father. But every one in this family is so kind to her that she completely turns with her intensions ....
