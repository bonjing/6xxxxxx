Society Island Sunrise

Nature Documentary published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC Sunrise Earth Viewers Choice series in 2008 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Halfway between California and Australia lie long curling strings of island chains, the five archipelagos of French Polynesia. As day brightens, terns dip the waves, chasing fish to feed their young. On a white crushed-coral beach, hermit crabs emerge.
