Dolly Rebecca Parton (born January 19, 1946) is an American singer-songwriter,
author, multi-instrumentalist, actress and philanthropist, best known for her
work in country music. Dolly Parton has appeared in movies like 9 to 5, The
Best Little Whorehouse in Texas, Steel Magnolias and Straight Talk. She is one
of the most successful female country artists of all time, with more #1 hits
than any other and is known as "The Queen of Country Music."
