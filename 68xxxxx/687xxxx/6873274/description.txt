Armed with pots, pans and his spiffing caravan, Ade Edmondson hits the road to explore the best of British food.

In Ade in Britain, Adrian Edmondson hits the road in his Mini to sample the best food Britain has to offer. In the first series, he travels from North Wales, to the Lancashire coast and to the Peak District, where he samples and cooks up some of the best delicacies the country has to offer.
