The Pretenders are an English rock band formed in Hereford, England in March
1978. The original band consisted of initiator and main songwriter Chrissie
Hynde (lead vocals, rhythm guitar), James Honeyman-Scott (lead guitar, backing
vocals, keyboards), Pete Farndon (bass guitar, backing vocals) and Martin
Chambers (drums, backing vocals, percussion).
