Pee Wee King - Pee Wee King And His Golden West Cowboys [1946-1958] 6CD

Bear Family Records. Release: 1994. 128Kbps 



Includes the original versions of some of the best known songs of the 40's & 50's in country music, his 1946 recordings for Bullet reissued for the first time, all of his RCA Victor recordings made between 1946-58, a rare 1952 Record Preview disc in which Pee Wee talks about his music, 151 songs total, 6 CD set plus 68 pg. booklet
