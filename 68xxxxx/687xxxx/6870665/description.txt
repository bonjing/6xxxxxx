BFM are the swedish unsigned boogierockers from the swedish westcoast. At the moment BFM are in the process of recording a brand new ep. So be sure to look out for that one. But for now you can get your hands on this special edition of their debut album plus the christmas boogie "Rock around the xmas tree"

For all you fans of classic boogierock and 70s rock like Status Quo, Kiss, Slade, Queen... etc. This is for YOU. Download and just sit back and enjoy the ride....

Visit BFM at facebook or bfmtheband.se.
