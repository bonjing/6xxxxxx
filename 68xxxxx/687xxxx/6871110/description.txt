New and Old Gospel is an album by American saxophonist Jackie McLean recorded in 1967 and released on the Blue Note label. The Allmusic review by Thom Jurek awarded the album 5 stars and stated "This is one legendary Blue Note date that isn't mentioned often enough in that label's great history".

    Jackie McLean - alto saxophone
    Ornette Coleman - trumpet
    LaMont Johnson - piano
    Scott Holt - bass
    Billy Higgins - drums
