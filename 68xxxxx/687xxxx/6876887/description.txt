World's Fastest Train
A look at the mind-blowing engineering breakthroughs that made the TGV possible, including the Japanese bullet train and Stephenson's record-breaking Rocket.
Trains are one of the most popular land transportation means. With the development of technology, people can make High Speed Rail that used for fastest train in the world. Several countries in the world are trying to make the fastest train in the world. Some of the trains are still in service but some of them become a failure for the company that invested on the development of the railways.
