Title: Blitzkrieg II
Genre: RTT / historical / isometric

Description:
Blitzkrieg II is a real-time tactics game set during World War 2 featuring three campaigns: USA's war in the Pacific, Germany fighting in Europe and North Africa and the Soviet Union defending its homeland. The player starts a mission with some core units, with which he should accomplish the given goals. During the missions, additional backup troops can be called in. Blitzkrieg II focuses on the battles of World War 2 rather than real-time strategy aspects like base building and resource extraction.
