A fair-sized custom story for Amnesia: The Dark Descent, in witch you (Whoever you may be) take an experimental sleeping potion and are plunged in to an endless sea of nightmares. Can you escape it?

Readme:
Thanks for downloading my mod, I hope you enjoy playing it as much as I did making it!"I Dream"V 1.0 (FINAL)By Rockclaw / RAIN BO DAH / Tyler
