
A personal journey of the director and eight friends back to the days of their youth at boardings school.


The memories of the first generation of children grown up in a kibbutz in Israel.
They meet more than 40 years after finished school to discuses their life during and after this period. It is a first hand documentary of one of the most unique experiments of the 20th century. The creation of a new society, new educations form and new community.

The film was directed by Yigal Tibon,.
Language: Hebrew
Subtitles: English
