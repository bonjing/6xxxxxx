I collected (probably) all the bhop maps, there are some really rare ones from private servers and websites.

The folder contains 620file with .bsp extension

How to play them:

1. Download the torrent

2.Copy the files to C:/Program Files/Steam/steamapps/*account name*/counter-strike source/cstrike/maps

3. Upload it to your server or
create a server(LAN) and set the basic configs:
-100tick
sv_airaccelerate 100
sv_accelerate 5
sv_enablebunnyhopping 1
sv_maxspeed 320
sv_gravity 800

your config:
cl_cmdrate 101
cl_updaterate 101
rate 25000
