The complete 2011 Ruse miniseries from Marvel. One of two miniseries that Marvel is using to reboot Crossgen, this one has the original writer, Mark Waid, and is based upon the old series.
Ruse was Crossgen's detective comic, based upon Sherlock Holmes to a large extent.  More info here: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ruse_(comics)
