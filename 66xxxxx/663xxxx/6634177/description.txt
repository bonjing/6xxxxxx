From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Anne Holm; Full-cast dramatisation starring Rory Copus, Andrew Wincott and Sarah Badel; Dramatised by John Peacock.

Episode 1: A young refugee's brave escape from a labour camp sparks a courageous lone journey.

Episode 2: The weary young refugee is in despair - until he meets Signora Bang, who changes everything.
