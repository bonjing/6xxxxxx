Summary: Nothing But The Beat is the fifth studio album by French disc jockey and record producer David Guetta. The album was released on August 26, 2011 through Virgin Records. Also making appearances are will.i.am and Akon, both of whom previously collaborated with Guetta on his fourth album, One Love. In comparison, the second disc features purely instrumental tracks.
