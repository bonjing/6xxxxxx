Garmin City Navigator South America NT 2012.20


Travel to South America

Navigate the streets of six South American countries and Florida with confidence. This product provides detailed road maps and points of interest for your compatible device, so you can navigate with exact, turn-by-turn directions to any address or intersection. Route to restaurants, gas stations, lodging, attractions and more.

* Includes more than 1,950,000 km (1,200,000 mi) of roads throughout the six countries and Florida.
* Displays more than 1,450,000 points of interest throughout the country, including restaurants, lodging, attractions, shopping and more.
* Gives turn-by-turn directions on your compatible device.
* Speaks street names (example: "Turn right on Main Street")

Coverage:
Brazil, Colombia, Argentina, Venezuela, Chile, French Guyane, Florida and Antilles islands.
