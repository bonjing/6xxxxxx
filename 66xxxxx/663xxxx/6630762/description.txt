The movie depicts the corrupt political system that has landed the people of Indian at a coma stage...

He is a big patriot, who loves India despite being in London.His love for India makes him to fall for to a girl called Indra(Nayantara). who acts like a traditional Indian girl.But her revenge mission makes him realise the dirty political sustem of India.
