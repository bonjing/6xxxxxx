Brain of Britain 2008 (Series 56)

Broadcast on BBC Radio 4, 29th September 2008 to 19th January 2009



Chairman: Robert Robinson
Adjudicator: Kevin Ashman [nicknamed "Jorkins"]
Producer: Paul Bejoria

The complete series from 2008-2009, the final appearance of the late Robert Robinson as chairman before ill health forced his retirement. As always, he intersperses the questions with aposite quotations and short anecdotes.

Also including two specials:  

"Brain of Brains 2008" [15th September 2008] The triennial contest between the champions from the previous three years.

"Top Brain 2008" [22nd September 2008] The 9-yearly programme, contested by the 3 previous "Brain of Brains".

Sound quality: these were encoded at 48kbps, so expect a slight amount of distortion.

Many thanks to Warren who kindly provided these classic recordings.
