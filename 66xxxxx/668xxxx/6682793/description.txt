
Lakis Papadopoulos is a Greek singer and composer.
He was born December 7, 1948 in Athens. He is considered one of the most talented songwriters of his generation.

During the 1960s he played music with his friends in a band called Dragon. Some of his friends at the time where Yiannis Sardis, Stratos Aggelakis and Giorgos Karatzaferis.

He entered the public scene in 1981, but became well known to the public with in 1982 with the song Koursaros performed by Vassilis Papakonstantinou.

He is also know as "Lakis me ta Psila Rever".
