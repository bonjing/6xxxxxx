IDW Publishing, who recently released the entire Rocketeer original saga in several volumes, culminating in last years preeminent The Rocketeer: The Artist's Edition, will launch an all-original anthology miniseries this May titled Rocketeer Adventures.

A whos who of the most creative talent in the comics world will be handling the writing and drawing duties for Stevens, who passed away from Leukemia in 2008.

The miniseries will feature contributions from legendary artists and writers including Alex Ross, Kurt Busiek, Mike Mignola, Mike Allred, John Cassaday, Michael Kaluta, and Jim Silke. And those are just the names attached to the first issue alone!
