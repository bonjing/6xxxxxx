Thor - Spotlight 2011
#Book
Marvel comics
cbr

Written by JOHN RHETT THOMAS. THOR SPOTLIGHT  showcases all thats great and good about Marvels hammer-wielding God of Thunder! With behind-the-scenes looks at the latest and greatest four-color runs by J. Michael Straczynski, Kieron Gillen, and the titanic tandem of Matt Fraction and Pasqual Ferry taking the lead, well also cast our gaze back in time at the greatest storylines and characters of tales past. And with insights into the movie-directed by Kenneth Branagh.
