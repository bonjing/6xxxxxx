Battle of the Blades S03E00: Game On

Battle of the Blades is a Canadian figure skating reality show and competition that airs on CBC. The series resembles Dancing with the Stars, but in this case, professional figure skaters are teamed with well-known hockey players.

If you are a fan of figure skating, ice hockey or Dancing With The Stars, this show is worth a try.

In this episode, we get some background on each of the 16 competitors and see them going through figure skating boot camp.

Russ Courtnall replaces Wade Belak who passed away after the boot camp started but before the competition.

In Season 3, the figure skaters are Violetta Afanasieva, Tanith Belbin, Elena Berezhnaya, David Pelletier, Marie-France Dubreuil, Marcy Hinzmann-Harris, Anabelle Langlois and Kim Navarro

The hockey players this season are Cale Hulse, Boyd Devereaux, Curtis Leschyshyn, Tessa Bonhomme, Bryan Berard, Todd Simpson, Brad May, Russ Courtnall.  

This episode aired on CBC on Sep 18, 2011. 

CBC has a big website for this show at http://www.cbc.ca/battle/
