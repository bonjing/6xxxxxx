Hollywood Stuntmakers - History of Special Effects/Futuristic Films (1992)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
Take a look of the art of the Special FX and how the artists evolve them for the films: A Trip to the Moon, T2, Star Wars, The Lawnmower Man and more. Interviews of the FX artists about their progress and achievements on miniatures, matte painting and CGI, also included.
A hard to find episode of these rare vintage TV series, hosted by Chris Reeve.
Ripped from a Greek TV network that broadcasted these series in the mid 90s.
