THE INFINITE begins the story of Bowen, a freedom fighter who has lost everything to war. His only hope of saving the world is to travel back in time to prevent world domination before it starts. Bowen knows he cant do this alone, so he decides he must turn to the only ally he knows he can trust: himself! Now Bowen, a man in his 40s, damaged by the horrors of war, and his younger, reckless, and optimistic self will attempt to save the world!
