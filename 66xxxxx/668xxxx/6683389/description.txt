Sweeter is the fourth studio album by American singer-songwriter Gavin DeGraw. The album will be released on September 20, 2011 and will feature tracks co-written with other artists. The first single "Not Over You" was released on iTunes in June. The track was co-written and produced by Ryan Tedder, with lyrical arrangements by J. Barrera.
