Elite Keylogger 4.9x is software that allows you to log and view keystrokes from your computer... With the added feature of taking a screenshot of your computer at set intervals (changable). Want to see what your kids are doing/typing when you are away? Or do you just plain want to catch someones every keystroke? Then this is exactly what you need.

Comes with 5 serial codes (1 tested, 4 untested) and everything you need to start logging. Its completely safe and invisible and is almost impossible to access for someone who doesnt know it is there.

To unhide the keylogger open run (windows button + r) and type your unhide keyword which is changeable in the settings (default = unhide)
