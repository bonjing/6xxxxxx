Fling from snow-covered peaks and platforms in this zany jumping game.

Take the role of Herman, a crazy old hermit that lives high up in the Himalayan Mountains. With years and years of meditation and training, Herman has developed Ninja-like skills and the mindset of a mad man. Take control over this lunatic and jump from platform to platform in this endless, extremely addictive jumper game!

Brag about your highscore to your friends using Facebook and Openfeint!
