Complete Boyce Avenue Discography Until September 2011

Boyce Avenue is an American acoustic rock band. It was formed in Sarasota, Florida by brothers Alejandro, Daniel and Fabian Manzano. The band is named after a combination of two streets the brothers lived on as children. As of 9 August, 2011 they are no longer signed to Universal Republic, and have started their own independent record label called 3 Peace Records.
