How Facebook Changed the World: The Arab Spring

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Mishal Husain, published by BBC in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

The story of how the Arab world erupted in revolution, as a new generation used the internet and social media to try to overthrow their hated leaders. In the first of this two-part series, Mishal Husain charts events in Tunisia and Egypt by meeting those who led the revolts and showing the unique footage they shot.


1)  Tunisia and Egypt 
Mishal Husain charts events in Tunisia and Egypt by meeting those who led the revolts and showing the unique footage they shot.

2)  Libya, Bahrain and Syria 
Mishal Husain meets those who spread the revolt to Libya and Bahrain, and those who are still fighting the Syrian regime.
