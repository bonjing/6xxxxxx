Developer deank is back with another update to his famous multifunctional tool for the PS3,
releasing multiMAN v2.07.01. Version 2.07.00 was released with a bug, which was quickly
fixed with this current release, and what a release it is! He has added Lv1 and Lv2 patches,
as well as the ability to backup blu ray and dvd media, and even psx and ps2 discs thanks
to the works of graf_chokolo and the SACD-Ripper people.
