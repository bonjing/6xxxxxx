From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Compton Mackenzie; Full-cast dramatisation starring Bernard Holley, Delina Maclennan, Dinalda Samuel, Michael Elder, Bill Riddick, John Sheddon and Paul Young; Dramatised by Hector MacMillan.

The inhabitants of Great and Little Toddy think they can cope with World War II - but then the whisky runs out. Life becomes intolerable, until fate delivers them a shipwreck with a cargo of whisky.
