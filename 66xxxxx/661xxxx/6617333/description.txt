Welcome Reality is the first album by UK dubstep act Nero. It is due to be released by Chase & Status' MTA Records, the label which has released all of their singles to date. On 24 May 2011 Nero posted on Facebook that a remix of The Jets' 1985 single "Crush on You" will be featured on the album. "Guilt" was the third single released taking 8th position on the UK Singles Chart.
