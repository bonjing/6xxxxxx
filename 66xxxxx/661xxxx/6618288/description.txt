Information
------------------------------

Extreme Lives: Hoarders Out Of Control
Modern consumers want it all but some people just can't get enough. From London junk addict Mark who battles with a 15 year obsession of accumulation and a retired charity shopaholic, Sylvia forced out of her home due to her inability to throw items away, and check out an American record collector with over 2million and getting bigger, which he stores in his home. Enter the world of compulsive hoarding.
