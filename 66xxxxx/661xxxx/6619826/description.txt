Rebecca

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Daphne du Maurier; Full-cast dramatisation starring Janet Maw, Christopher Cazenove, Nicholas Grace, Rosalie Crutchley and Frederick Treves; Dramatised by Brian Miller.

"Last Night I Dreamt I Went To Manderley Again."

So the second Mrs. Maxim de Winter remembered the chilling events that led her down the turning drive past ther beeches, to the isolated gray stone manse on the windswept Cornish coast. With a husband she barely knew, the young bride arrived at this immense estate, only to be inexorably drawn into the life of the first Mrs. de Winter, the beautiful Rebecca, dead but never forgotten...
