PLOT SUMMARY

"Which Way Home" is a feature documentary film that follows unaccompanied child 
migrants, on their journey through Mexico, as they try to reach the United States. We
follow children like Olga and Freddy, nine-year old Hondurans, who are desperately
trying to reach their parents in the US.; children like Jose, a ten-year old El 
Salvadoran, who has been abandoned by smugglers and ends up alone in a Mexican 
detention center; and Kevin, a canny, streetwise fourteen-year old Honduran, whose 
mother hopes that he will reach the U.S. and send money back to her. These are stories
of hope and courage, disappointment and sorrow. They are the children you never hear 
about; the invisible ones.
