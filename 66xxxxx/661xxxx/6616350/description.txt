The Rise and Rise of Ashlyn, Superstar
Ashlyn Gere is a woman reduced to a mere shell, with no self confidence or self image. A friend persuades her to seek psychiatric therapy. There's only two problems... The friend's in love with her. And so is the psychotherapist.
Acclaimed director Layne Parker delves deep into the psyche of a superstar and pulls out what is sure to be an award-winning performance. Both threatrically and sexually.
