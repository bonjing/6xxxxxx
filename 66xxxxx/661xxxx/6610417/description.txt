Ritsuka, once a popular studant in school, becomes a sullen and withdrawn after his brother Seimei passes away.The arrival of the enigmatic Soubi, his brother's partner in a hidden paranormal war, only serves to further unsettle his delicate emotional state. Not only must the introverted young man deal with this unexpected newcomer's open intimacy, he is also bound to him as a physical sacrifice,literally the defense to his companion's offense in conflicts that follow them continuously. Will Ritsuka, now also known by his true name, Loveless, find happiness chained to Soubi?
