\\\"Promises\\\" is a song by English dubstep duo Nero from their upcoming debut album, Welcome Reality. It was released as the fourth single from the album on August 5, 2011. The song debuted at number one on the UK Singles Chart on August 14, becoming the group\\\'s first number-one single.[1] It sold 46,700 copies in its first week, the lowest first-week sales for a number one in 97 weeks
