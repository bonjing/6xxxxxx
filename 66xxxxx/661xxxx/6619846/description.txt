Commercial Photography Handbook: Business Techniques for Professional Digital Photographers
128 pages | Aug 31 2010 |ISBN: 1584282606 | PDF | 5.5 Mb

Targeting new and experienced commercial photographers alike, this invaluable guide explores the different aspects and challenges of succeeding in the industry. Approaching the subject systematically, the topics begin with determining what kind of commercial photography to pursue and how to get the training needed to carve out a niche in the market. Continuing to delve further, the topics expand to marketing techniques, negotiation skills, estimating and charging for work, maximizing profits while minimizing expenses, and ethical business behavior.
