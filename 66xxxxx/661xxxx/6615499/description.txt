Notes:

On Saturday the 20st of August b2s will organize the 2011 edition 
of Decibel outdoor festival. The Decibel festival is the biggest 
harder styles festival of the world, with a total of 50.000 
dedicated party people that can choose between different harder 
styles on 12 stages represented by the best global DJs. 

The 3 CD's of Decibel 2011 are mixed by the headlining artists of
the festival; the hardstyle super team: Psyko Punkz, hardcore 
legends Endymion and freestyle hero Ruthless. In their own typical 
style they delivered each a marvelous mix with the phattest tracks 
of this moment and some phuture classics. 

A great souvenir for whom visited the festival and a must have for 
everybody who's into hardstyle and/or hardcore!
