Why the combination? 2007 Streets & Trips was the last version that did not require activation and the 2011 Map Data is the most recent. Enjoy, and keep on truckin'!

Interesting Streets & Trips version information:

    * S&T 2006 was the last version for Windows 98/Me
    * S&T 2007 is the last version for Windows 2000
    * S&T 2008 Microsoft started requiring Product Activation
    * S&T 2009 Map of Mexico now includes street level data
    * S&T 2011 is mainly a database update adding 88,000 miles of new mapping
