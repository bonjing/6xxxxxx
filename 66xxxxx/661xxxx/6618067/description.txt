19. Compilation album  1975 - We Sold Our Soul For Rock 'N' Roll (WBR 2923-2 USA 1976)
We Sold Our Soul for Rock 'n' Roll is a compilation album by Black Sabbath, originally released on December 1, 1975 in the UK and then on February 3, 1976 in the U.S.

20. Live album 1982 - Live Evil (Vertigo 826 881-2 Germany 1983)
Live Evil, released in December 1982 (see 1982 in music) and in January 1983 in the UK, is the first "official" live album by British heavy metal band Black Sabbath, the previously released Live at Last (1980) not having been sanctioned by the band. It was recorded with Ronnie James Dio singing and is a document of the Mob Rules World Tour, that ran from 15 November 1981 to 13 August 1982.
