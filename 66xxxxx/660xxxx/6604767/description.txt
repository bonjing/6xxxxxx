Dragon Age 2: (Direct-Play)

Information:

Dragon Age 2 is the sequel to Dragon Age: Origins. You play as Hawke, the champion of Kirkwall. The game is influenced and shaped by decisions you have made. Varric, Hawkes dwarven friend tells the story of your quest. This game as stunning graphics, awesome real time battle clashes and action that will enhance your playing experience. With great differences between races such as elves or dwarves, they have been revisions since the last game. Dialog plays a huge role on the game, that it effects your experience and different game-play is guaranteed every time!

Requirements: (Minimum) 

Operating System: Microsoft Windows XP.
Computer/Processor: 2.4 (GHz) processor.
DirectX: 9.0 or later.
Memory: 1 GB of RAM (1.5 GB Vista and Windows 7).
Hard Disk: 7 gigabytes (GB) of free hard disk space.
Video card: 256 MB(Radeon HD 2600 or NVIDIA GeForce 7900).
