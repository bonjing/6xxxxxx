The Cry of the Owl

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Patricia Highsmith; Full-cast dramatisation starring John Sharian, Adrian Lester, Briony Glassco, Laurel Lefkow, Joanne McQuinn, Matt Rippy; Dramatised by Shaun McKenna; Directed by Marion Nancarrow.

Robert Forester has moved to Pennsylvania, escaping his oppresive wife and no longer needing to see a psychiatrist. So why is he outside a young woman's house, watching her every move?
