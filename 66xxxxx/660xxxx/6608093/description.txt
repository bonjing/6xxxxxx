In Driving Simulator 2011, you can put on your lights and siren and scream through the city at the wheel of a high performance police car in a thrilling high speed pursuit. Alternatively, try to keep to schedule as you choose to pick up your fares from a fleet of buses. If nostalgia is your nirvana, take to the road at the wheel of a beautifully recreated vintage roadster. You can even add your own vehicles or share amongst the community with a specially designed editor and modding tool that lets you create finely crafted and realistic vehicles of any shape or size! 

 From trucks to ambulances, to high performance race cars and buses, Driving Simulator 2011 puts you behind the wheel of the most diverse collection of road vehicles ever assembled in one package.
