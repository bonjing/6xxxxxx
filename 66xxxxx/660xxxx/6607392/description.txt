Workers Unite!: The American Labor Movement (Defining Moments)
Author : Kevin Hillstrom
Publisher: Omnigraphics Inc
Published: 2010
ISBN: 0780811305 
Format: PDF 
236 pages 


Explains the history of the American labor movement from its earliest origins through 19th-century industrialization, the growth of the labor movement, the current declining influence of labor, and efforts to revitalize American unions. Features include a narrative overview, biographies, primary sources, chronology, glossary, bibliography, and index
