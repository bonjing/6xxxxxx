This is a collection of the comic strip Pearls Before Swine by Stephan Pastis up to the date 15th August 2011. Much thanks goes to dc7dc at Demonoid as this collection is built from his contribution. It has been updated to include the strips added since and the older strips have been renamed to a more consistent style of yyyy-mm-dd.
