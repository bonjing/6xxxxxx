This 2 disc set boasts a plethora of TV performances, such as \'Oh! You Pretty Things\', \'Five Years\' and \'Queen Bitch\' (all taken from the \'Old Grey Whistle Test\'), \'Young Americans\' (from The \'Dick Cavett Show\') and the now legendary \'Starman\' performance from \'Top Of The Pops\'.

The DVD will also include the only contemporary performance of \'Rebel Rebel\' on film, which was taken from the Dutch TV programme \'TopPop\'.
