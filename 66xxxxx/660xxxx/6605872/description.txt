	The Darkest Faerie has escaped from her watery prison and
	all of Neopia is in peril! Only the courage of two young
	heroes can stand against her awesome armies of evil minions
	and free the world Neopia from her dark influence. You will
	embark on an adventure in the fantastic land of Meridell,
	defending its castles and delving into its dungeons. For the
	first time, the world of Neopets comes to life to create a
	beautiful and rich 3D experience that includes many of your
	favorite Neopets characters.
