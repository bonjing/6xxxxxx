From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written and Dramatised by Alexander McCall Smith; Full-cast dramatisations starring Claire Benedict as Precious Ramotswe.

Episode 1: How to Handle Men through the Application of Psychology

Precious Ramotswe sets out to release her fiance from an obligation.

Episode 2: House of Hope

Precious and her assistant visit some bad girls in their love quest.

Episode 3: The Return of Note

When Precious Ramotswe's ex-husband turns up to cause trouble, she finds an unlikely ally.

Episode 4: The Ceremony

Precious Ramotswe works her way through her list of problems.
