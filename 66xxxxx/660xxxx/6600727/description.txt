Lewis is a murderer and Clark is a brainy nerd who has done time for computer crime. They find a map to a gold mine and are forced together because Lewis can't read. Along comes George, a cigar smoking femme fatale who steals cars and has a poisonous pet snake that she stole from a thug who is close behind.

Genres: Action/Adventure and Drama

Running Time: 1 hr. 24 min.

MPAA Rating: R

Starring: Rose McGowan, James Brolin, Salvator Xuereb, Dan Gunther, Dwier Brown
Directed by: Rod McCall
