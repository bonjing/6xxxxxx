A documentary about the Syrian Revolution that started in March 15, 2011. Some scenes are graphic but it is important to show the world what happened and is happening is Syria.
The totalitarian regime in Syria has been ruling the country for over 48 years and when people protested for reform and democratic government and an end to Baath ruling, Bashar Assad regime started a vicious campaign against unarmed civilians using the most sophisticated and heavy arms to silence the people demands.
