Robert Youngson's compilation of classic scenes from silent comedies dating from about 1915 up to 1929, featuring Charlie Chaplin, Buster Keaton, Harry Langdon, Fatty Arbuckle, Mabel Norman, Gloria Swanson, Ben Turpin and, in one of their finest shorts, Laurel and Hardy as Christmas tree salesmen in "Big Business." With narration and sound effects, this is a grand introduction to the greats of the early days of film.
