Joomla Extensions Bundle

This bundle includes both commercial and free extensions. 

Extensions are mostly components, but also plugins, modules, templates, and tools.

Extensions vary in compatibility including Joomla 1.5, 1.6, and 1.7.

Some ZIP archives may include additional extensions: components, plugins, modules, or documentation.

Most of these extensions were downloaded through various torrents and file-sharing sites, with some paid for by me and others downloaded from the provided URLS for free.

Some extensions may be out-dated or contain bugs. Many commercial extensions require a "license code" to update, none of which are provided. 

Most, but not all, have been tested.
