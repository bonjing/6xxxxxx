From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Agatha Christie; Full cast dramatisation starring June Whitfield as Miss Marple.

The villagers of Chipping Cleghorn, including Miss Marple, are agog with curiosity over an advertisement in the local gazette which reads: A murder is announced and will take place on Friday October 29th, at Little Paddocks at 6.30pm.

Is it a hoax? As the time approaches, the crowds gather at Little Paddocks; and then the lights go out...
