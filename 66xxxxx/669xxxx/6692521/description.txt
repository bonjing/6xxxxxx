Poop Office #2

Ever had a crappy job? Then you must've worked at Poop Office, an office staffed entirely by poop! Join Poopert and his co-workers for more mundane adventures in the Poop Office! Featuring office Chrustmas parties, a broken photocrapier machine, and Mr. Poopson's first name!

Also included are 8-Panel Epics, movie-length stories told in a single page! Featuring "For a Good Time Call," the story of a man who calls one of those phone numbers scribbled on the men's room wall!
