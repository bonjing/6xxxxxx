Deep Sea Highway

Technology Documentary hosted by Tony Hirst, published by National Geographic broadcasted as part of NG Megastructures series in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Megastructures 
Deep Sea Highway A look at the most deeply submerged road on the planet, which runs through a tunnel that is both earthquake and typhoon-proof.The deepest immersed tunnel to be built in open waters along with two cable-stayed bridges have opened to traffic in South Korea. The tunnel represents a major leap forward in immersed tunnel technology.
