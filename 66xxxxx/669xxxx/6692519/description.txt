Poop Office #1

Ever had a crappy job? Then you must've worked at Poop Office, an office staffed entirely by poop! In this issue, see Poopert's first day at Poop Office. Flash back to his job interview. Marvel at the Poop Office Fumetti (photo comic) featuring photos of real poop!

Also included are 8-Panel Epics, movie-length stories told in a single page! Featuring "Framed!," the story of a man framed for a crime he DID commit!
