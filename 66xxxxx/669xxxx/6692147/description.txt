Complete podcast collection of the radio show named 'Get This', hosted by Tony Martin and Ed Kavalee, with the late Richard Marsland.

This show was broadcast on Triple M in Australia over 2 years, 2006 and 2007.

Contains chat and comedy sketches revolving around TV and Movies, new and old.


May contain references to Rex Hunt making girls happy :)
