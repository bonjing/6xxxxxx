Collection of songs by the California-based music collective Cricket Engine. This free release of songs is just a few of many made, including 2 self-released albums. Often written in cabins between hikes or time away from classes.
 
The songs will appeal most to fans of Glo-fi, Ratatat, Sigur Ros, WARP Records, & Caribou and is FREE for public distribuition. Creative Commons rights apply!
