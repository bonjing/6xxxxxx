
 
   Jennifer Lopez - Papi (official music video) HD



   .Jennifer Lopez is chased down by hordes of men in the music video for her latest single 'Papi'. The clip sees Lopez attracting the attention of the men after eating a magic cookie she hopes will bring her army lover back to her. The five-and-a-half-minute promo, which was directed by Paul Hunter (Michael Jackson, Britney Spears, Eminem), was shot in partnership with car giant Fiat.
