The album contains all the Top 20 hits up to that point released in the U.K. and internationally, and one new single, "Laid So Low (Tears Roll Down)", which also made the Top 20 in the U.K. (peaking at #17), and in several other European countries (France, Italy, etc.). The album has been certified double platinum in the U.K., and platinum in the U.S. The album originally peaked at #1 in Italy and #2 in the U.K. and France upon its release, but returned to the U.K. Top 10 (#6) in 2004 for several weeks following the success of Gary Jules' cover version of "Mad World".
