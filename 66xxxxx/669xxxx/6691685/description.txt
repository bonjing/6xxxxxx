Stepniak

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Martyn Wade; Full-cast dramatisation starring Justine Waddell, David Horovitch, Jennie Stoller, Charles Simpson, Jasmine Hyde and Elizabeth Bell; Directed by Cherry Cookson.

Fact-based drama. Olive, the sister of Russian translator Constance Garnett, falls for a charming expatriate - unaware he has a sinister past...
