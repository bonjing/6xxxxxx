In Crash Time 4 you have to chase the criminal syndicate, whose members are trying to stop police and gain control over the entire Cologne. You're playing for the main characters of the famous television series in Germany, Alarm for Cobra 11, Shemer and Ben. Mission in Crash Time 4 will be based on the events of the series. You will need to patrol Cologne, look for members of the syndicate, which are scattered through the streets of the city, and do away with their criminal acts.
