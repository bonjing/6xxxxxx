Story :

Nat (Pimradapha Wright) is a tomboy who is disappointed with guys. She is one close friend with Doh (Charlie Trairat). Both are students in the Faculty of Fine Arts. One day Nat starts to pursue Jane (Phulada Luechatham) and ignores Doh. What is the outcome of their friendship in the end?
