Driver: San Francisco is an upcoming sandbox-style action driving video game developed by Ubisoft Reflections, and it will be published by Ubisoft. The game formally was unveiled at E3 2010 and is scheduled for release in North America on September 6, 2011[1] and in Europe on September 2, 2011[2] on PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360[6] and the OnLive[7] system[7][8], while the Microsoft Windows version will be released on September 27, 2011.[3] The game was leaked on August 31st, 2011.
