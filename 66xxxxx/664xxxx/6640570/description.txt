Murdoch: The Mogul Who Screwed the News

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Jacques Peretti, published by Channel 4 in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

The incredible story of how Rupert Murdoch used celebrity scandal to cashflow his expanding media empire, before scandal ultimately engulfed the News of the World itself. Jacques Peretti talks to everyone from Hugh Grant to Murdoch insiders to find out how the world of celebrities, cops and politicians first cosied up with, and then turned against, the world's most powerful media mogul.
