Jewel - Spirit (1998)

FLAC



Spirit is the second album by singer/songwriter Jewel, released in 1998. 
Singles include \"Hands\", \"Down So Long,\" and a newly recorded version of \"Jupiter,\" followed by a remix of \"What\'s Simple Is True\" to promote Jewel\'s debut film Ride with the Devil. In addition, a one track CD containing a live version of \"Life Uncommon\" was released to music stores in hopes to raise money and awareness for Habitat for Humanity.

Spirit debuted at number 3 on the Billboard 200 with 368,000 copies sold.
