"Raindrops Keep Fallin' on My Head" is B. J. Thomas's #1 song, written by Hal David and Burt Bacharach for the 1969 film Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid. It won an Academy Award for Best Original Song. David and Bacharach also won Best Original Score.
It was recorded in seven takes, after Bacharach expressed dissatisfaction with the first six. The film version featured a separate instrumental break when Paul Newman undertook stunts on a bicycle.
