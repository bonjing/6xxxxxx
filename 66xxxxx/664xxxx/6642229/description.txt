Australian Legal Drama series revolves a group of solicitors (Lawyers) fresh from law school, working as Crown Prosecutors or 'Crownies' who are the public prosecutors in the legal system of Australia, working for the Office of the Department of Public Prosecution (Sometimes called DPP for short, and previously known as Crown Law Department).
