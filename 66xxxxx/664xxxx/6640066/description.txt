This the most important of Jack's specials, because it features 
all of his primary radio & TV cast members....except for 
radio-only Phil Harris, who was in his next TV special a year later.

Guest Stars --- Frank Sinatra, Dean Martin, Dinah Shore, Bob Hope, 
and cameo appearances by Red Skelton and Lucille Ball.

With former Jack Benny Program regulars:
Don Wilson, Frank Nelson, Eddie "Rochester" Anderson,
Jack's wife Mary Livingstone (pictured above, who come out of a long 
retirement briefly for this), Mel Blanc, and Dennis Day.
