Sun Valley Serenade [1941]

A publicity stunt for the Dartmouth Troubadours band gets complicated when
the Norwegian refugee their pianist Ted Scott has announced he will be taking
in turns out to be fully grown Karen Benson. When his girlfriend realises that
Karen and Ted are spending an awful lot of time together on the ski slopes at
the band's venue, things start to turn very frosty.

Starring: Glenn Miller and his Orchestra, Sonja Henie, John Payne,
          Milton Berle, Lynn Bari, Nicholas Brothers and The Modernaires
Director: H.Bruce Humberstone

Trivia: Sonja Henie won 3 consecutive Olympic Gold medals in figure skating
        1928, 1932 and 1936. She also became World Champion in 10 (!)
        consecutive championships 1927-1936.
