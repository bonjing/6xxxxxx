Michelin Green Guide Wine Regions of France, 3e (Green Guide/Michelin)
Michelin (Author)

Paperback: 432 pages
Publisher: Michelin Travel & Lifestyle; 3 edition (April 16, 2010)
Language: English
ISBN-10: 1906261814
ISBN-13: 978-1906261818

Respected cultural travel guides featuring Michelin stars and tours. This brand new, completely revised edition features the best of the wine regions of France. New regional introductions give an overview of the history, geography and climate, and the characteristics and quality of the wines of the local area. The cities, towns and villages are explored, as well as the vineyards and other sights of interest from archaeological sites in Corsica to water sports in Jura and open air museums in Alsace. Official wine routes are followed, along with suggested Michelin Driving Tours. Maps, full-color photos and addresses for wine buying, hotels and restaurants help you discover the regions and choose where to stay and eat. Michelin's celebrated star-rating system makes sure you see the best that the Wine Regions of France have to offer.
