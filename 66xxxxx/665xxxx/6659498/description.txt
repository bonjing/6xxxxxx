Cheever and DeLillo

Someone requested these two authors not long ago, so here they are. The torrent includes everything I have by and about both authors. Various formats are included. Wherever possible I've included an .epub version, but there are usually other formats as well. Most of the fiction does not have a retail source, so quality varies, but mostly it's pretty good. For a couple of the books, if there are significantly different versions, I've included both.

The folders are all copied directly from my calibre directory and include covers and metadata. So these folders can be easily imported into your version of calibre. If you don't have calibre, now's the time to get it.

My thanks to the original uploaders.
