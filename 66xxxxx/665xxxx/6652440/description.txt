Lucas Secon, also known by the stage name Lucas, is a Danish-American Grammy, MTV Award and Emmy Award-nominated record producer, songwriter, DJ, rapper,singer and artist/conceptualist. He lives in London, United Kingdom.

Secon has contributed to seven number one records and many other singles and albums. Secon is also known for his own single, "Lucas with the Lid Off", from his 1994 album Lucacentric.

He has contributed to over 40 million album sales and has garnered many multi-platinum, platinum and gold plaques for singles and albums sold.
