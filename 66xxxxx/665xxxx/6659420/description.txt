Ninjas, pirates, mutants and disco collide in this bizarre comedy, which follows intrepid hero and ladies man Kung Fu Joe (Zak Van Winkle) as he reluctantly teams with his former nemesis (Jeremy Parrish) to derail a sneaky mad scientist (David Neevel). Written and directed by Glen Berry, this fun-filled flick co-stars Victoria Maurette as a mysterious femme fatale and Alan Shore as Joe's sage master.

2009, US. 86 minutes.
