From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Jane Austen; Full-cast dramatisation starring Amanda Root, Emily Wachter, David Harewood, Jenny Agutter, Claire Skinner, Saskia Reeves and Julia McKenzie; Dramatised by Dominic Power.

Episode 1: Young, naive Catherine Morland is launched into the intriguing world of Bath society.

Episode 2: At the Tilneys' isolated gothic home, Catherine's visit proves to be a terrifying affair.

Episode 3: Suspicious of Gen Tilney, Catherine is determined to discover Northanger Abbey's secrets.
