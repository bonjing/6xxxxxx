America's premiere news network, CNN, was the channel that millions of Americans turned to upon being shocked and devastated by the September 11th terrorist attacks. Here, the cable channel presents a heartfelt, commemorative tribute to those affected by the horrific attacks on the nation. Divided into two parts, the first footage of the terror unfolding and the second a record of the aftermath and recovery, AMERICA REMEMBERS is a documentary meant to pay homage to the resilience and courage of the American people.
