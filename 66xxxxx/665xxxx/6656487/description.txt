Westlife - Our Story is the autobiographical book by the Irish boyband Westlife, released by HarperCollins (UK) on 16 June 2008. The book tells the story of their lives for the past ten years, from how it started in Sligo to today's multi-platinum record charters, along with private pictures of the members, exclusively to the book.
