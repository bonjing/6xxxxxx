The Playboy Club is about a time when it was all about social mores and where a entrepreneur saw the potential into something and created an empire that has spanned the world and continues to grow. The show focuses in the 1960s at the legendary Playboy Club in Chicago. This was the most sought-after status symbol anyone could get and the epitome of beauty and service. Nick Dalton (Eddie Cibrian), a top attorney tries his luck at the Playboy Club and with mysterious ties to the mob, Nick puts himself out there and helps Maureen (Amber Heard), a new bunny to the club who kills the leader of the Bianchi crime family accidentally.

Running time is 6:08.
