This is "After the Fire" Albums recorded at CBS 1979 - 83. I have added a  192k version of the extended single "Der Kommissar" which was a hit for them. 


After the Fire (or ATF) are a British rock band that progressed from playing progressive rock to New Wave over their initial twelve-year career, while having only one hit in the United States, ("Der Kommissar") and one hit in the United Kingdom ("One Rule For You").(Wikipedia)
