 The Message (in Arabic:  Ar Risalah; U.S. title: The Message) is a 1976 film directed by Moustapha Akkad, chronicling the life and times of the prophet of Islam, Muhammad. Released in both Arabic and English, Mohammad, Messenger of God serves as an introduction to early Islamic history.


    The film follows Muhammad first years as a prophet starting with Islam\'s beginnings in Mecca in which the Muslims are persecuted, the exodus to Medina, and ending with the Muslims\' triumphant return to Mecca.
