Title: The Cougar Club

Studio: Sweet Sinner

Stars: Randi James, Melissa Monet, Wendy Breeze, Dane Cross, Dick James

Description: Dane is in love with Wendy, but Wendy has other things on her mind -- and sex ain't one of them. Intrigued, Dane visits the club and gets picked up by blonde Randi James, while Dick brings home horny brunette Melissa Monet. But how long will the fun last before Wendy finds out? An improbable love tale of hot sex and true love starring Dane Cross, Dick James, Randi James, Melissa Monet, and Wendy Breeze. Written and directed by Nica Noelle.
