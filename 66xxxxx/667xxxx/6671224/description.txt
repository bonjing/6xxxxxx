This collection of Scrubs TV series songs is smaller than all the previous season collections.  The TV series episodes were halted before airing 1/2 way through that season because of the NBC artist strikes.  As a result, only half as many songs were aired in the episodes that made it to TV.

While this torrent contains only 18 songs, it is complete for the season.

Scrubs Season 7 has not previously been assembled and uploaded, at least to my knowledge, so for Scrubs fans, this is new material.
