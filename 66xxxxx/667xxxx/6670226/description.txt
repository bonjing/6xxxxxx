HOMESTUCK SOUND TEST is a compilation of music from Homestuck.

It contains unreleased tracks (songs that have been seen in Homestuck, but haven't been released, including a lot of extras), removed tracks (the Bill Bolin and SolusLunes songs, with the original song picture and track number), and remixed tracks (songs that have been officially released and then altered for use in a Homestuck flash). There is also an in-depth credits text doc. Enjoy!

You can always contact me over Pesterchum (I'm incisivePlayer) if you want to suggest anything that can be added, or correct an error, or whatever.

Remember to seed!
