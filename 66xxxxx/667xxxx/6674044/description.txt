The dark ones are coming. Who can defeat them?

20 hours of gameplay!

Kyle and Casper are brothers who grew up on a farm on the outskirts of a small village. Everything changes one day when the boys return home from the village market to find their parents and all of the field workers gone.

The brothers set off to find their parents and friends, only to learn that three powerful entities who call themselves the Pure ones were behind the attack. Why did they target Kyle and Casper's family? They boys will soon find out and an epic adventure will begin.
