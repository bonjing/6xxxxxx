401 recordings of The House of the Rising Sun, with historical information and sheet music. Many international versions, especially Spanish and Russian language.

This is version 4 of the collection.

Version 1 in approximately 2007 contained over 100 recordings.
Version 2 in 2009 contained 333 recordings.
Version 3 uploaded by komart in 2010 also contained about 333 recordings.
Version 4, this version, September 2011, contains 401 recordings.


File List:


TheHouseOfTheRisingSun.html - Some information about the song.
TheHouseOfTheRisingSun-GuthrieLyrics.txt - Lyrics as sung by Woody Guthrie.
TheHouseOfTheRisingSun-LomaxAndGuthrie.pdf - Lyrics, tune, and information from the 1941 book \"Our Singing Country\" by ethnomusicologists Lomax & Lomax, plus Guthrie lyrics.
TheHouseOfTheRisingSun-LyricsGuitarChords.txt - Lyrics with guitar chords.
