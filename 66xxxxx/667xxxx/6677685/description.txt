This is a spin off of the main Felarya series, largely focused on the fetish aspects. A group of adventurers is trapped inside an old temple, while the giantess who chased after them is sleeping outside. They are now facing some difficult choices in order to escape and avoid ending up in her belly! Contains mature content (nudity) and soft vore (creatures/people being devoured whole and alive). The manga is 44 pages long.
