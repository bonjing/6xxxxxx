Published in 1972 by American Greetings Corp., this 22-page brightly-coloured booklet contains several stories related to Christmas. Selections were by Gail Golladay and Jean Kyler McManus.

Contents:
When the Animals Spoke
Christmas Candles
A Legend of the Christmas Tree
The Legend of the Poinsettia
The Little Drummer Boy
The Christmas Holly
The Three Wise Men
The Legend of the Evergreen Wreath
The Christmas Rose
