Developer behind the popular multifunctional tool for your PS3, deank has some more
updates for our jailbroken consoles, going from ver 02.05.03 to multiMAN ver 02.05.04. In
the last release, he introduced us to the new experimental Motion Backgrounds, as well as
support for more controllers and remotes. With this update he brings us two different
versions of the Motion Background for us to try out.
