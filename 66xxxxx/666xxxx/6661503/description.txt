Photographers are employed in every branch of American industry - from communications to science to law enforcement to construction. New technology continues to revolutionize photographic techniques and make photography increasingly accessible to individuals, but in professional settings, the role of the experienced photographer who has mastered new and old techniques alike has become more important. The perfect guide to photography careers for high school students and career changers, Career Opportunities in Photography profiles approximately 60 jobs in photography and photography-related fields.
