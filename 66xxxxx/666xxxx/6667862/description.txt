Thor Movie Theme for Windows 7


This is a Thor Movie Theme for Windows 7. This ThemePack contains 15 High Quality Thor Movie Wallpapers and a color change scheme.

 There is no change in the icons, sounds or mouse pointers. The pack consists of Marvel Comics' official Thor movie wallpapers and some fan made wallpapers. You can also find a comic artwork with the movie title in it. Each wallpaper depicts a character from the movie, such as Thor, Loki, Jane and Odin, or depicts an important event in the movie. Its a good theme for anyone who just saw the movie and liked it and would like to theme out with The God of Thunder!
