GOMTV presents GSL August.

The new and improved GSL meets its second season.

The competition is getting tougher and tougher each season and only the strongest survives.

Skillful newcomers have joined the league by dominating the new and massive preliminaries. Will the new players take down the veterans who boast their battle wounds?

GSL August will run from August 8th to September 10th, 2011.
