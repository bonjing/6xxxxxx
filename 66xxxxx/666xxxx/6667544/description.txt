The Mystery of a Butcher's Shop (a Gladys Mitchell Mystery)

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Gladys Mitchell; Full-cast dramatisation starring Mary Wimbush as Gladys Mitchell; Dramatised by Elizabeth Proud.

Episode 1: An Offal Discovery

When Rupert Sethleigh disappears, the amateur sleuth investigates some alarming village events.

Episode 2: The Bones of the Matter

With Rupert Sethleigh still missing, can amateur sleuth Mrs Bradley solve the mystery of a headless corpse?
