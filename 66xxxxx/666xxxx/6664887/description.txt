Chimera

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written and Dramatised by Stephen Gallagher; Full-cast dramatisatrion starring Sarah Badel, Christian Rodska, Brian Glover and John Webb; Directed by Martin Jenkins.

There has been a tragedy at the remote Jenner Clinic. It has always been a place of secrets and now, something terrible has happened. Within hours Government officials hush up the investigation - why?
