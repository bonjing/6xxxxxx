Ninjas, pirates, mutants and disco collide in this bizarre comedy, which follows intrepid hero and ladies man Kung Fu Joe (Zak Van Winkle) as he reluctantly teams with his former nemesis (Jeremy Parrish) to derail a mad scientist (David Neevel) bent on ruling the world through the use of his mind control drug, Soylent Green. 

Written and directed by Glen Berry, this fun-filled flick co-stars Victoria Maurette as a mysterious Femme Fatale.
