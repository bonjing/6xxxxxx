Ruse v1 from Crossgen follows the detective Archard and his partner Emma Bishop in their adventures. Set in a Victorian-esque world, the first half of the series was written by Mark Waid and the second half by Scott Beatty.
If you enjoy this series also consider the spinoff miniseries Archard's Agents, and Marvel's new revival of the series both on TPB.
