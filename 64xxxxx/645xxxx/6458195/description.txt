The Terminator is a soundtrack album by Brad Fiedel, featuring the score to the film, The Terminator. It was released in 1984 through Enigma Records (vinyl) and reissued in 1991 through DCC Compact Classics (CD); a remastered edition was reissued as The Definite Edition in 1994 through Edel Music.
