About:

Odin Screen Capture is a full-featured screen capture print tool that allows you to easily capture and annotate anything on the screen including windows, objects, menus, full screen, rectangular/freehand regions and even scrolling windows/web pages. You can save images in jpg,bmp,png all kinds of formats and the hotkey makes more convenient for you to use it.The Hightlight is it can let you to print the images out.
