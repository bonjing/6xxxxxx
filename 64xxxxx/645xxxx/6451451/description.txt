The sixth series of British science fiction television series Doctor Who is being shown in two parts, the first seven episodes began in April and the final six episodes in September. Matt Smith, Karen Gillan and Arthur Darvill are continuing their roles as The Doctor, Amy Pond and Rory Williams. The series will continue story threads from Series 5, investigating the mysterious Silence that caused the TARDIS to explode in "The Pandorica Opens" / "The Big Bang" and the identity of River Song
