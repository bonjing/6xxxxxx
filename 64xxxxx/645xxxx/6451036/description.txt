Round Triangle are back on the scene with another release out. This time they brought a newbie to the label. Hailing from Colombia, James Aville arrived with full force. An upbeat progressive track with great pace and elements, also involved are his fellow countryman Nicolas Agudelo (Particles/Quadraphonic/Liquid Grooves) and Russian Andrew Benson (Black Hole/Nueva/Olaris) on remix duty. Supported by Aaron Static, No Sonic Limits, Flash Brothers, tyDi, Gai Barone, Darin Epsilon and Hyline among others. Check out the beast!
