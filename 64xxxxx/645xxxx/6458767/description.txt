"Condor" was the name given to the sinister connection between South American military governments, with support from the CIA, which culminated in the death of about 30,000 people in 70 years. Other 400 thousand were arrested and four million exiles. The director Roberto Mader tells this story through exciting and amazing testimonies of generals and political activists, torturers, victims and relatives of the disappeared. "Condor" was filmed in four countries and provides an archival material, accompanied by beautiful compositions by Victor Biglione.
