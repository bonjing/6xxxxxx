Anna Leddra Chapman is a British singer-songwriter from Brentwood in
Essex. Her debut single Story will be released on 2 November 2009. Her
debut album Telling Tales has been produced by Peter-John Vettese and
will be released in late December 2009. She is a student at London
College of Music and she is also an ambassador for clothing company
Quiksilver. In this respect she highlights a growing trend amongst
emerging music artists to find other sources of revenue as alternatives
to Record Labels. She has been interviewed by industry intelligence
magazine, Five Eight, and mentioned by [Music Ally]. On 29 October 2009
her song Story was played on BBC Radio 2 by Terry Wogan, who described
it as beautiful.
