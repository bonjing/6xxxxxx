Based on the Grappler Baki Anime, Fighting Fury is one of the only venues where the World Champions in Karate, amateur wrestling, Judo, professional wrestling, and other unarmed combat techniques can meet and trade blows. Features include 13 different characters to master, an Endorphin Gauge that unlocks super powerful moves, Tournament and Sparring modes, and a customizable fighter tool. Available only in Japan and Europe.
