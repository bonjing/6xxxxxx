ARTIST/ALBUM INFO

More Monsters and Sprites is the first remix and third EP released by electro house/dubstep artist Skrillex. The EP consists of 3 new tracks and of 4 additional remixes of the title track from his previous EP Scary Monsters and Nice Sprites. It was released on June 7, 2011, on Beatport and will be released on June 21, 2011, on iTunes.
