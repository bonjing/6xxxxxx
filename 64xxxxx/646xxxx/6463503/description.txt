The Serpent and the Rainbow (1988):
Director: Wes Craven
Writers: Wade Davis (inspired by the book), Richard Maxwell (screenplay),  
Stars: Bill Pullman, Cathy Tyson and Zakes Mokae

Storyline:
A Harvard Scientist's Astonishing Journey into 
the Secret Societies of Haitian Voodoo, Zombies, and Magic 
An anthropologist goes to Haiti after hearing rumors about a drug used by black magic practitioners to turn people in zombies.
