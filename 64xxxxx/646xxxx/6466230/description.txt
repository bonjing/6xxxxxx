Story:
A violent tale, the story begins with Shanker Prasad (vijay) who is seen in various get ups and he goes about killing people. Well, these people are all anti-social elements and criminals. Due to this, the police department and the politicians begin to get disturbed. Finally, he comes out of his den and also brings three girls who are victims of acid attacks. One among them is the Miss India contestant Shilpa (catherine). A flashback is revealed about Shanker Prasad and what happens from there forms the rest of the story.
