Site Journal v2011.5-DOA


The Site Journal for Windows is a software for the daily contruction report (building journal). It is intended to help site-supervising architects, engineers, and construction companies document site inspections. The construction diary is one of the most important tool, to head off a quarrel, and furthermore it probatives in court. All important data, events and associated site photos are conveniently processed and managed with this slim and fast construction diary software.
