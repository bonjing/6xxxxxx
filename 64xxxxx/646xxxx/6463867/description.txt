The Sherlock Holmes Novels by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle.
 
Dramatised for radio by Bert Coules. Starring Clive Merrison as Sherlock Holmes & Michael Williams as Doctor John Watson.

All four stories are made up of two 60 minute episodes. Total running time approximately 8 hours.

A Study in Scarlet (published 1887)
Our heroes meet for the first time. Very soon they're investigating a murder.

The Sign of The Four (published 1890)
A stolen treasure and a secret pact between four ex-convicts brings tragedy into Miss Mary Morstan's life.

The Hound of the Baskervilles (published 1901-1902)
Can our heroes save Sir Henry Baskerville, the last of the Baskervilles, from the legendary beast?

The Valley of Fear (published 1914-1915)
Revenge and treachery link a Sussex country house with the sinister secret societies of 1890s industrial America.
