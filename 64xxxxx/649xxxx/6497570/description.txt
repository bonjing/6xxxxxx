Least of These is a full length book developed in the Hunger Games universe. It follows Book 1 of the Hunger Games series from District 11 and District 12, offering unique perspectives on the 74th Hunger Games, as well as providing several brand new stories that take place concurrently. Waking Athena is a short followup/prequel to Least of These.

Both are fanfiction and are not officially published books. Both are available free of charge at the fanfiction website. The author has made them available as ereader files to provide easy access for Hunger Games fans. Please share them and enjoy them!
