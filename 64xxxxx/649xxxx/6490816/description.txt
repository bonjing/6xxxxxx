Scooby-Doo! Mystery Incorporated (originally known as Scooby-Doo! Mystery, Inc.) is the eleventh incarnation 
of Hanna-Barbera's Scooby-Doo animated series, and the first incarnation not to be first-run on Saturday mornings. 
The series is produced by Warner Bros. Animation and Cartoon Network and is a reboot of the franchise.

Synopsis
---------
No., Episode title Overarching Mystery Clue(s), Villain, Original airdate, Ratings (in millions)

6 "Nightfright" None Nightfright/Arcus Fentonpoof June 7, 2011 N/A
 
Scooby and Shaggy win an essay contest to have dinner with Vincent Van Ghoul. 
They go to his mansion and are attacked by a monster who calls himself Nightfright. 
Shaggy calls the gang and the gang rushes over. The are chased by Nightfright and 
go into a room and lock the door. Fred makes a trap and asks Vincent to lure the 
monster into it. Finally, they catch Nightfright in Fred's trap. The end of the 
episode shows them eating dinner, Daphne devouring everything in sight because 
she has not eaten for hours.  

Spoken Language: English
