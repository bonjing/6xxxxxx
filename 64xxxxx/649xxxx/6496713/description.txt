Fifteen eager Food Star Finalists arrive at Hollywood's famous Grauman's Chinese Theater, where they meet each other for the first time and are welcomed by Bobby Flay and Giada De Laurentiis. 

For their first Camera Challenge, the finalists must create a breakfast dish that reflects their personality and culinary point of view. 

Their first Star Challenge finds them shooting promos for their shows as well as cooking for a panel of Hollywood's press, including Mario Lopez from television's Extra. 

The first finalist is sent packing.
