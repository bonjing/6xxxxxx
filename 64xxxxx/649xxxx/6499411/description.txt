The Encyclopedia of Networking 
Publisher: Sybex Inc | ISBN: 0782118291 | edition 1996 | PDF | 1268 pages | 6.40 mb

This thorough update to Novell's Complete Encyclopedia of Networking expands upon the original and provides a more comprehensive collection of data for all levels of network users. This Encyclopedia offers in-depth analysis of hardware, software, networking topologies, and network operating systems. Details on cabling, interface cards, shells, applications, standards, and protocols are also included.
