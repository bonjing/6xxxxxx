The Game:
John Doe is Arcade platform game.
The goal is to reach the castle at the end of the level.
On your way to the castle you will find mean sheep's, flaying dragons and jumping wasps.
You can kill your enemy's to jump on them, on this way they will disappear.
Try to collect all the coins you will find on your way, when you reach a amount off 40 you will get an extra live.
You start with 3 lives at the begin of the game, use them wise.


The Controls:
Arrow left = Turn to the left
Arrow right = Turn to the right
Arrow up = Jump
P-Key = Pause the game
