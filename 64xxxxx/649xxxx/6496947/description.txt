Plot Summary: After falling through a time-slip, Yusei Fudo
(who has just had his powerful card stolen by a mysterious stranger)
meets with Judai Yuki and Yugi Muto, who agree to help Yusei defeat the evil Paradox,
who is planning to destroy Pegasus before he can invent Duel Monsters.
Also know as Yu-Gi-Oh! 3D: Bonds Beyond Time.
