Plot


Black Tie Nights was a softcore drama anthology series which aired on Cinemax in the 

United States from 2004 to 2005.


This anthology show revolved around the adventures of Olivia Hartley and Cooper Snow 

who ran Black Tie dating service. They were assisted by their geeky sidekick, Ryan 

Lundy. Each week they would encounter new or returning customers and try to play 

matchmaker. The first season had two main subplots that continued in each episode. 

The first was the budding romance between Ryan and Cooper. The second was Olivia 

getting over the loss of her husband and dating again.
