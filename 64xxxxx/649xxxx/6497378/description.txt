On May 14th 2011, the Ukrainian football (soccer) team Shakhtar Donetsk celebrated its 75th anniversary. An 8-song Rihanna set in Donbass Arena (the team's home venue) was a centerpiece of the event.

1. Only Girl In The World
2. Disturbia
3. Don't Stop The Music
4. Shut Up And Drive
5. What's My Name?
6. Hate That I Love You
7. Love The Way You Like Part II
8. Umbrella

5,6 and 7 have featured verses on their studio versions which do not appear here.
