           Annihilator are a Canadian thrash metal band hailing from Ottawa, Ontario, originally established in Vancouver, British Columbia in 1984.The song "Only Be Lonely"  originally appears on the band's 1994 album 
"King of the Kill". The album  was recorded at Watersound Studios, Maple Ridge, Canada. 
           "By the Sword" is the first official single from the album "Slash" by current Velvet Revolver and former Guns N' Roses lead guitarist Slash. It features Andrew Stockdale of Wolfmother on vocals and was released as a digital single on March 16, 2010. It was released in the UK on March 28, 2010. U.S. Billboard Rock Songs - Peak position 41.
            "Guitar Heaven: The Greatest Guitar Classics of All Time", referred to as simply "Guitar Heaven", is the eighteenth studio album by Santana, released on September 21, 2010. The first single released from the album was The Beatles cover of "While My Guitar Gently Weeps" which featured vocals by India.Arie and cello by Yo-Yo Ma..
