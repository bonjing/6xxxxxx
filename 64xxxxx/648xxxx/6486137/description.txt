How To Prepare Your Curriculum Vitae
McGraw-Hill | January 11, 1999 | ISBN-10: 0844266310 | 192 pages | PDF | 1.22 MB

Strategies, examples and worksheets in this book show you how to write a powerful CV for any job in any field. It offers step-by-step guidance, explains what to leave in or out and how to work round complications, such as career changes, lack of experience and more.
