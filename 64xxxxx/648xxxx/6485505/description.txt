Dora and The 3 Little Pigs (2011)


Join Dora in 4 hilarious adventures. Dora needs your help to return to the
Land of Fairy Tales things that Fox has released, including bricks from
the three little pigs, and get everything back to normal!

1. Dora Save The Three Little Piggies
Dora and Boots are enjoying their favorite book, and everything is as they
remember until they hear the Piggies calling out. They find them calling
for help, and find out that their house is missing some bricks. It turns
out Swiper stole some of their bricks, now their house is in danger of
being blown down by the Big Bad Wolf. Dora and her pals try to fix
everything to make sure that the story ends the way it should.

2. The Mayan Adventure
Dora tells a Mayan story about two ball-playing twin brothers. The
brothers are great ball players, but need more players for their team for
a big Ball Game Championship. Dora's trip to the ancient Mayan kingdom
brings her on some fun and educational adventures, checking out beautiful
sites like the pyramids and animal constellations. Join Dora and her new
friends as they play ball and explore Mayan culture.

3. Bouncy Boots
The shoe shop makes a mistake and returns the wrong boots to Boots. He
gets the boots back from the shop and is excited to have his favorite
boots back, but finds out that when he puts them on, he bounces around.
It's fun for a minute, but now he wants his old pair back, and Dora sets
out to help him find his prized boots. Their friend Amelie teaches them a
fun hopscotch game that they play in France.
