Directed by Ahn Pan-seok, the film is the first to deal with the lives of North Korean defectors in Seoul. The film revolves around Kim Sun-ho (played by Cha Seung-won), who defected to the South with his family, while leaving his girlfriend Yon-hwa behind after promising to bring her to Seoul as soon as possible. In Seoul, he tries to save money to help her escape from the North, but one day, hears that Yon-hwa has married another man. He becomes hopelessly depressed but later tries to find a way to adapt himself to life in the South.
