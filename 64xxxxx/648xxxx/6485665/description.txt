Data Compression: The Complete Reference by D. Salomon (Repost)
Publisher: Springer; 3rd edition (February 26, 2004) | ISBN: 0387406972 | Pages: 900 | PDF | 7.81 MB

Data compression is one of the most important fields and tools in modern computing. This third edition of "Data Compression" provides a comprehensive, authoritative, and accessible reference for the many different types and methods of compression. Included are a detailed and helpful taxonomy, detailed description of the most common methods, and discussions on the use and comparative benefits of methods. The book's logical, clear, and lively presentation is organized around the main branches of data compression.
