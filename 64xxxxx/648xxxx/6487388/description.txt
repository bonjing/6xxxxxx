Gold Cobra is the upcoming fifth studio album by American metal band Limp Bizkit, scheduled for release on June 28, 2011 by Interscope Records. It is the band's first studio album since 2005's The Unquestionable Truth (Part 1), and the first from the original lineup since 2000's Chocolate Starfish and the Hot Dog Flavored Water.
