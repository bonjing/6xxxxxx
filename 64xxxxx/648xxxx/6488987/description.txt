Wiley - Beginning Android Application Development

Beginning Android Application Development pares down the most essential steps you need to know so you can start creating Android applications today.

The Android OS is a popular and flexible platform for many of todays most in-demand mobile devices. This full-color guide offers you a hands-on introduction to creating Android applications for the latest mobile devices. Veteran author Wei Meng Lee accompanies each lesson with real-world examples to drive home the content he covers. Beginning with an overview of core Android features and tools, he moves at a steady pace while teaching everything you need to know to successfully develop your own Android applications.
