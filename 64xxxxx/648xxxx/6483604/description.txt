
The series 'Battlestar Galactica' created by Ronald Moore and Christopher Eric James is a classic fantasy adventure. He was shot in 2004 by producers commit the same cast and creative team that worked on the mini-series from 2003, including stars Edward James Olmos, Mary MacDonald and Jamie Bambar.

The plot of the new series starts where the story ends in a mini-series. The second nuclear war against the robotic sayloni completed a full rout of the human race and all twelve human colonies are destroyed. A handful of survivors are facing refugees destined to clash with the cruel loss of his enemies. They are forced to cross the universe to find a new home. Their only hope for success is outdated spaceship "Battlestar Galactica", led by its captain - Commander Adama.

Commander Adama and President Laura Roslin of the colony managed fleet of wretched refugees in their desperate search for the thirteenth colony - the mystical planet Earth, which allegedly is lost forever. Hazards encountered along the way are many and further complicate the situation in which they are located. Saylonite pursue and attack them in space and their pervasive fighters are more subtle. But the problems of refugees do not end - they also have political conflicts and misunderstandings in their ranks. In the chaos of all these events are beginning to emerge evidence of the reality of the thirteenth colony "Earth" and Commander Adama's claims become more credible ...
