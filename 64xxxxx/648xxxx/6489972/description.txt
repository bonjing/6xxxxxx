This is the fourth studio album from the Christian pop punk band Stellar Kart.

It contains:

1. "All My Heart"
2. "We Shine" (Fee cover)
3. "Something Holy"
4. "Spirit In the Sky" (Norman Greenbaum cover)
5. "Everything Is Different Now"
6. "It's Not Over"
7. "Rescue"
8. "You Never Let Go" (Matt Redman cover)
9. "Until My Heart Caves In" (Audio Adrenaline cover)
10. "Like the Sun"
