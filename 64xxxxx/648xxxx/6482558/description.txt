Season 2, Episode 7 Aired: 10/23/2007

Long Way Home

25 year old Te Puke woman Judy Yorke, was a devoted and caring solo mother who adored her kids. In 1992, Judy asked her parents if they could babysit her daughter as she went to an orchard party that night. However, when she didn't arrive home and missed her daughter's fourth birthday party, her family realized something was amiss and reported her missing. Psychics Kelvin Cruickshank and Sue Nicholson attempt to ask her spirit to lead them to her killer, and provide some relief to her family.
