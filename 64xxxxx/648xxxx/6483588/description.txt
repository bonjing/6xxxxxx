The next installment in the Veber/Depardieu/Richard oeuvre.

This time our heroes find themselves both being told they are the fathers of the same son (the title vaguely translates as "ComDads") they never even knew about, in order to enlist them in finding him after he has run away from his uptight Parisian parents. Pignon, here, inflicts more problems on himself than on anybody else, less a Schlepprock, than a magnet for danger. Clearly he is still evolving...
