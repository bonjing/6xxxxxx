The Skilled Helper: A Problem Management and Opportunity Development Approach to Helping (9th Edition)
Brooks Cole | ISBN : 0495601896 | July 10, 2009 | 498 pages | PDF | 2.91MB

Emphasizing the collaborative nature of the therapist-client relationship, The Skilled Helper (9th Edition) is internationally recognized for its successful problem-management and opportunity development approach to effective helping, using a practical, three-stage model. In this new ninth edition, Egan continues to build upon the "positive psychology", solution-focused theme by adding insightful new discussions on evidence-based practice, research, and philosophical perspectives.
