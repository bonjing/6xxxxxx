

Eric Destler feat. Franka - On Fire

'On Fire' is Frankas new song and carrying single of the compilation "Papaya Day & Night 2011th," (On sale from Friday 17.06.2011). This is her first single in English. The song is written by her, and produced by Eric Destler, who has remixed many well known club Production names like Martin Solveig, Badura, Hanne Hansen and Dan Marciano. This is the first croatian summer single of this year. Enjoy it!!!
