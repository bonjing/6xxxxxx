Twist and turn your chains to make colorful matches of three or more to eliminate them from the board and build up a colorful linked world. Dive into each different game mode and score as many points as you can. Use exciting powerups like Wild Link, Freeze, Magnet, and Cross Link to clear each level and rack up a high score in Chainz Galaxy! 

System Requirements
 OS: Windows XP/Vista/7 
CPU: 1.6 GHz 
RAM: 256 
Direct X: 9.0 
Hard Disk: 139 MB
