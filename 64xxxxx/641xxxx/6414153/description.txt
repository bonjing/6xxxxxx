Best Movie Main Theme Film Score Soundtrack BSO collection (600 themes, by tonyx)

Updated March 18, 2011: added 100 main themes, cleaned up some ID3 tags and added cover art to all tracks.

I just love movie themes. yeah, yeah, everybody loves john williams and star wars music, but I also mean beyond that. I really love the more obscure and less-known movie themes as well. "less-known" is probably an understatement though. after you listen through this selection, you'll probably discover many tunes you always knew you had heard someplace, but didn't quite know where or in what connection. turn up the volume and let the invasions, fights, battles & conflicts begin!

although the title says "the best", it is only my personal selection you will find here. I never intended to create a collection to please everyone and these are simply my own personal favourite movie themes I've collected over the years. and yes, I mean YEARS. I started already around 1990 using a cassette tape and taping from the radio. when napster came, I was hard at work replacing my cassette collection with MP3's and now with ripping audio streams from the web and P2P, I pretty much have all the tunes I always wanted. sure, new movies are coming out all the time, but the law of diminishing returns is kicking in - it is quite unlikely, that the movie themes of the past year will be up to par with the best selection of the past 30 years, so I'll be taking it easy from now on.

in some cases, I've taken the liberty of cutting out my favourite parts of a tune. blasphemy, I know, but sometimes I felt it was a waste to listen to 7 minutes of background music just to get to that 2 minutes of action themes. I haven't used this too much though, so don't worry.

by the way, my primary reason for sharing this is to have a backup of my hard work of collecting movie themes in case all my DVD's an CD's are lost, stolen, broken etc. I really, really would not like to lose this collection and believe it or not, I've discovered torrents and P2P networks to be some of the more reliable mediums for restoring backups. so by releasing this, I am just creating one more backup of my collecion - now how selfish is that:)


enjoy!
tonyx
