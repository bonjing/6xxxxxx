Test Drive Unlimited 2 (TDU2) is a racing video game developed by Eden Games and published by Atari. It is the sequel to Test Drive Unlimited and the tenth game in the series. The game was released on 8 February 2011 in North America and on 11 February in Europe.[2]
The game suffered from many bugs during launch, particularly on PC.[3][4] Namco Bandai UK marketing boss Lee Kirton confirmed that a Test Drive Unlimited 2 patch 
would be released to address various issues in early March.
