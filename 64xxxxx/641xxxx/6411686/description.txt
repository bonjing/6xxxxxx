JOE ZAWINAL WEATHER UPDATE 
Studio: Geneon [Pioneer] 
Recorded : July 7, 1986 at The Philharmonic Hall Munich, Germany
DVD Release Date: September 20, 2005 
Run Time: 60 minutes
Format: xvid


Legendary synthesist Joe Zawinul is one of the most highly regarded and innovative figures in fusion history, combining elements of jazz, gospel, R&B, African and South American music. Weather Update is a follow up to the influential band Weather Report, and a precursor to the Zawinul Syndicate. The live production featured on this DVD is the only existing recording of Weather Update.
