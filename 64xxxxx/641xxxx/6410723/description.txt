Cryptic Fog Screensaver 1.0

Cryptic Fog Screensaver is a small, animated screensaver designed to bring the fog to your desktop.

A magical fog would delay you into the thicket. This screensaver is dedicated to strange places. In these places, inhabited by strange monsters and other amazing creatures.

Usually in those moments over the marsh or river arises magical mist. We want to invite you to enjoy this moment. You never forget the time spent in the magical mist.
