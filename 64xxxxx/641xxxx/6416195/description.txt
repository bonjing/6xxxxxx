                                Spark 360 Version 3.60 
                                                            

Xbox Live profile management made easy. With widgets, notifications, and more! Spark 360 is a full-featured, unofficial Xbox Live client for Android devices. It allows you to:

* Track your Xbox Live profile
* See your game history and achievements
* Send and receive messages (Xbox Live Gold users only)
* Receive friend and message notifications
* Manage your friends list, and find new friends
* View recent players list
* Show off your stats with gamercard widgets on your home screen
* Browse game details, download screenshots and set them as wallpaper
