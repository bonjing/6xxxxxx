Heroes of Might and Magic 3 COMPLETE:
Contains the original Heroes of Might and Magic III: The Restoration of Erathia,
plus the expansions Armageddon's Blade and The Shadow of Death.

This release does not need to be burned to cd.
It is complete with all music/voice/movies and works flawless from the harddrive.

Manuals from the games are included as pdf files in the game folder.
Have fun!!!


Install notes:

1) Extract the rar archive to a folder of your choice.
2) Run Heroes3.exe
3) Play

4) The map editor can be launched from h3maped.exe and the campaign editor from h3ccmped.exe.
