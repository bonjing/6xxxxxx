Sets of educational discs about New judging system was released by International Skating Union for better understanding on judgement process, marks setting principles and other judgement aspects of figure skating.

The Program components set contains seven DVDs. Each of them is about a corresponding mark type:
Interpretation;
Choreography;
Transitions;
Skating skills;
Presentation.

Each aspect is demonstrated by top skaters programs of last few years. It's made a little bit long but very clear.
