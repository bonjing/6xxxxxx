Yellow Admiral, The - Patrick O'Brian-viny

On stormy terms with his wife, Captain Jack Aubrey savors the prospect of going to sea again with his friend Stephen Maturin when an urgent dispatch orders him to Gibraltar: Napoleon has escaped from Elba! This high-seas adventure from the highly acclaimed Aubrey-Maturin series vividly details the atmosphere on board a British man-of-war during the Napoleonic Wars. "One of the most brilliantly sustained pieces of historical fiction writing.
