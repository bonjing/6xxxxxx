After the re-processing and re-manipulation of classic In Strict
Confidence album "Holy",Hecq (aka Benny Boysen) returns to weave
his futuristic electronics on Pzycho Bitch's latest album "The
Day After". His second strike for German label Minuswelt, "The
Day After" (The Hecq Destruxxion) is a tour de force of twisted
electronics - manipulated, deconstructed and re-constructed, an
exercise in cutting edge sound design utilizing elements of
industrial, dark electro and experimental briliance. Strictly
Limited to 555 copies only and housed in a custom metal tin.
