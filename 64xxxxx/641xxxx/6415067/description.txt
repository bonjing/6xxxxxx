The sights and sounds of the 1946 Indianapolis 500 mile race come back to life on this beautiful full color DVD.  This is the most extensive collection of rare, B&W and COLOR motion picture footage of the 1946 event ever made available on DVD.  

Included is the exciting start of the race, highlights from practice and qualifications, plus conversations with Wilbur Shaw, Ralph DePalma, Cliff Bergere, and the winner of the first 500 in 1911, Ray Harroun.

Running time 49 minutes.
