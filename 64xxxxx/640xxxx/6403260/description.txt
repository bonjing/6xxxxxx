Plot
Rahul Roy meets Anu Verghese and falls head over heels in love with her. The only problem is that Anu stays in an orphanage run by Arnie Campbell and he consider the orphanage as a holy place so romance is not allow there, in fact Arnie has other plans for Anu. While dancing in a party the police intervene between Rahul and Anu and both is forced to elope, but is later caught and Anu must undergo punishment, wherein she must leave the orphanage and stay with her alcoholic uncle, Peter. But Palu, Rahul's friend provide the right amount to Peter and he finally let go of her. Anu decides she must be independent. Watch as Anu becomes a famous model and Rahul a famous singer and how this will create a great distance between the couple.
