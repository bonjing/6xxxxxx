Elvis on Tour [2010, NTSC]

Elvis on Tour is the second documentary produced by Elvis Presley in 1972, shows shows, rehearsals and Elvis backstage in the U.S.. This work is always praised for showing, according to many people, and unfolded the career of Elvis for 70 years, that is, a synthesis of Elvis on stage \"70. \" Because of this, this documentary was honored with a Golden Globe. The production team has a name that would be popular in the world in film years later, Martin Scorsese. Elvis Presley was responsible for the musical direction of this work.
