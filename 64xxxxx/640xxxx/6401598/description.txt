Born This Way is the forthcoming second full-length studio album by American recording artist Lady Gaga. The album is scheduled for release on May 23, by Interscope Records. Preceding its release, the album's eponymous lead single "Born This Way" debuted on February 11, 2011, the second single "Judas" was released on April 15, 2011, the third "The Edge of Glory" on May 9, 2011.
 
The album's title was revealed as Gaga accepted the 2010 MTV Video Music Award for Video of the Year, while the release date was confirmed on her official Twitter page at the stroke of midnight on New Year's Day 2011.
