Jackie works as a surveillance operator. 
Each day she watches over a small part of the world, protecting the people living their lives under her gaze. One day a man appears on her monitor, 
a man she thought she would never see again, a man she never wanted to see again. 
Now she has no choice, she is compelled to confront him.
