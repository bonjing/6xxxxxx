Technical aspects :

The video was encoded according to AVCHD specifications so I guess a DVD-R readable by your Blu-Ray player is doable.

Picture :
I was unsatisfied by the offer on bitorrent on Stellet Licht. There was no H264 of the DVD.
Also, I have worked to find and use the "best" avisynth technology on the DVD source, and applied the strongest denoising/sharpening that wouldn't remove any noticable precision (and obv. thanx to high quality x264 encoding not loose anything noticeable there too).

Sound :
The AAC soundtrack is made from the DTS soundtrack, and with such high bitrates that one shouldn't be able to tell the difference (hence saving space and making the file mp4 compatible), it obvioulsy contains the six channels.

Subtitles :
I made the french subtitles as they were nowhere to be found on the web. Found and synched the others.
