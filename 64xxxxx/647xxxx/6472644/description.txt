VeggieTales: Lord of the Beans (2005)

DVDRip, 51min

Join the Veggies in this epic episode as they journey through a lesson in using your gifts! Meet a Flobbit, Toto Baggypants, who inherits a bean with amazing powers from his Uncle Billboy. Together with his Veggie cohorts, Toto forms the Fellowship of the Bean and embarks on a dangerous quest to find the answer to his question -- what is this incredible gift for? Join Toto and learn how important it is to use your gifts for good!
