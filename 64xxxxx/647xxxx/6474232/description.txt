                           Ritual (2001)

Runtime: 1 Hr, 38 Mins. (99 minutes)

Stripped of her license when a patient dies, Dr. Alice Dodgson (Jennifer Grey) moves to Jamaica and accepts a job ministering to a man (Daniel Lapaine) suffering from encephalitis and claiming to be hexed by voodoo curse.
