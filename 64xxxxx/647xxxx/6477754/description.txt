SRS Labs is a vital resource to consumer electronics OEMs and is the world leader in practical applications of the science of psychoacoustics. More than simply a manufacturer of audio products, we have studied both the sending and receiving of audio, and we design and create solutions that enable listeners to enjoy natural, restored sound from an incredibly wide variety of audio devices. Billions of people worldwide enjoy better, more natural sound with SRS Labs technologies.

Our portfolio of advanced audio enhancement, voice processing, and surround sound technologies provide a wide range of audio solutions that restore audio to what it was meant to sound like. Our solutions can be used to enhance sound in mono, stereo, and multi-channel surround, and our technologies also process human voice sources to improve intelligibility and clarity, particularly in mobile phones and home entertainment.
