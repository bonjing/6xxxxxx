The Platinum Collection (Sounds of Summer Edition) is a three-disc greatest hits album by American rock band The Beach Boys, released in December 2005 by EMI. It includes some of The Beach Boys' most popular songs from their early days like California Girls or Surfin' USA, but also songs from their later period, when their success started to fade, like Lady Lynda or Sumahama. Except for some tracks of the first two CDs, most before-mono tracks have been digitally remastered in stereo.
