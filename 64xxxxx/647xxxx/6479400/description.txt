Date: 	06-17-11
Host: 	George Noory
Guests: 	Jim Steinmeyer, Open Lines

In the first half, George welcomed magic historian and illusion designer Jim Steinmeyer for a discussion on the art of magic and researcher Charles Fort. Steinmeyer also performed a card trick that listeners could try at home.

The remainder of the program featured Open Lines, with a special line for people who have been "pushed to the brink."
