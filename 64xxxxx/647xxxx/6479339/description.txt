"Techno Fan" is a single from alternative-pop group, The Wombats. The single will be released in the United Kingdom on 5 June 2011 as a digital download on iTunes including three B-sides; "Avalanche", "Trampolining" and "Shock Goodbyes and P45's". This is the 4th single from the album This Modern Glitch, following "Tokyo (Vampires & Wolves)", "Jump Into the Fog" and "Anti-D". The single has peaked to number 60 on the UK Singles Chart.


The Wombats announced on 31 May 2011 their forth single from the album would be "Techno Fan" and the 4-track EP would include three unheard B-sides. It was released on 6 June 2011.


The music video for the song was uploaded to YouTube on April 19, 2011.
