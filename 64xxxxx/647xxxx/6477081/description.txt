RELEASE NOTES

At the end of the year 2063, Earth joined the Peripheral Galactic
Alliance as an equal member. Now, for the first time, Earth is
expecting a significant improvement of its situation.

Everybody believes that a new era begins bringing closer co-operation
with other civilizations. An era of development and prosperity, that
leads to the eventual solution of the political, economic and social
problems of the planet.

Nick Delios, ex private eye and now a governmental secret agent, when
accused of attempted murder against Annita Argyriou, the President of
the powerful Detronics S.A., he realizes that someone has framed him.
In his effort to find the truth he will find himself in the middle of
multiple conspiracy networks existing throughout the Peripheral
Galactic Alliance, threatening even its own existence.

Conspiracies II - Lethal Networks is a Full Motion Video (FMV) 3D
adventure game. Featuring plenty 3d puzzles and riddles, a great plot
with complicated conspiracies, funny dialogues and situations that
unravels the story in a unique way.

The game hero, Nick Delios, with friends all over the world,
experiences new adventures trying to save Earth, meeting new people,
confronting new enemies, revealing new conspiracies involving now
different planets of our galaxy.
