MOVIE SYNOPSIS :---

Dui Prithbii is based on Telegu Film Gamyam released in 2008. Koel Mullick is playing the role of a doctor who severes her engagement to serve people in rural areas. Jeet has to drive down the road to track Koel after the break up. Dev is playing the character of a thief who joins Jeet in his remarkable journey to find Koel.
