VeggieTales: The Ballad of Little Joe (2003)

DVDRip, 35min

Join the Veggies in this lesson on facing hardships. Meet Little Joe (played by Larry the Cucumber) and his rootin' tootin' French pea brothers on an adventure that will take them from an abandoned mineshaft all the way to Dodge Ball City -- with Little Joe's faith being tested every step of the way! 

Director:Tim Hodge
Writers:Phil Vischer, Robert G. Lee
Stars:Jacquelyn Ritz, Justin Barrett and Mike Nawrocki
