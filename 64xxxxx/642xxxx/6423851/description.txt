Swing Aerials: Tricks And Airsteps Volume 1 

Daniel Newsome + Tiffiny Wine 


When it comes to high energy performance, acclaimed champions Dan Newsome and Tiffiny Wine have repeatedly taken Swing and Lindy Hop to a higher level. They are also the founders, choreographers and coaches of 23 Skidoo, the 2006 and 2007 first place international champion swing team. Their name has become synonymous with exciting airsteps, lightning fast dancing, and a flair for pleasing the crowd.
