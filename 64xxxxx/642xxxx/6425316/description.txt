Cubase SX3 Description
Designed with the requirements and working methods of professional musicians, 
composers, audio engineers and producers in mind, 
Steinberg's flagship Cubase SX3 converges extraordinary sound quality, 
intuitive handling and a vast range of functions. 
Cubase SX3 provides creative tools that save you time at every link of the production chain, 
from the initial musical idea to final mastering.
