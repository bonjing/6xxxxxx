Fleet Foxes EP is a self-released EP album by the Seattle, Washington-based band Fleet Foxes. It was released in the Fall of 2006 around the Seattle area, and its success led to the eventual signing of Fleet Foxes to Sub Pop records. The album was produced and recorded by veteran Seattle producer Phil Ek.
