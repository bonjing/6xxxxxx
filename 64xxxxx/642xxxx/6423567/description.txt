Max Sharam, born Leanne Maree Sharam in Benalla, Australia in 1967.

In 1992 she performed her song "Coma" on the television talent show New Faces, winning her heat and attracting the attention of a number of record companies.

She subsequently secured a recording contract with Warner Music Australia in 1994.

Her debut album A Million Year Girl was released in 1995. The album went platinum and reached #9 in the Australian album charts.
