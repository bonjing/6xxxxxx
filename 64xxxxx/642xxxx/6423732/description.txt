A visually driven, supernatural, independent film about a girl named Cillian (Sarah Hale).  After Cillian suffers the death of her mother, she must go and live with her grandfather (Richard Malloy).  Cillian quickly finds herself lost in a lonely and mundane world, until Avery (Jason Zednick) grants her the gift of traveling through mirrors, quickly setting off a chain of events which will forever change Cillian's world.

Format: AVI

Directed by: Raven Thibodeaux

Awards:  Best of Show in the Accolade Competition (2010), Official Selection of the Red Rock Market Film Festival (2010) and the Southern Winds Film Festival (2010), and second place in the Narrative Feature category at the Beaumont Boomtown Music and Film Festival.
