Planet of the Apes (2001)

ORIGINAL TITLE: Planet of the Apes
YEAR: 2001
DURATION: 100 min.
COUNTRY: United States
DIRECTOR: Tim Burton
Screenplay: William Broyles Jr., Lawrence Konner, Mark D. Rosenthal (Novel: Pierre Boulle)
MUSIC: Danny Elfman
CINEMATOGRAPHER: Philippe Rousselot
Cast: Mark Wahlberg, Helena Bonham Carter, Tim Roth, Michael Clarke Duncan, Kris Kristofferson, Estella Warren, Paul Giamatti, Lisa Marie
PRODUCER: 20th Century Fox presents a Zanuck Company production





2029. In a routine mission, astronaut Leo Davidson (Mark Wahlberg) loses control of his ship and landing on a strange planet inhabited by a race of ape-like intelligence of humans and treat them like animals. With the help of a chimpanzee named Ari (Helena Bonham-Carter) and a small band of human rebels, Leo leads the enfrentamineto against the terrible army led by General Thade (Tim Roth) and his henchman, the warrior Attar (Michael Clarke Duncan). The key is to reach a sacred temple located in the forbidden zone of the planet, where they can discover the amazing secrets of the past of humanity and the key to its future.
