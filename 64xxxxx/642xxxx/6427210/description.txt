Bye Bye Bangkok , Movie (2011)

Cast and Crew :

Starring: Swastika Mukherjee, Silajit Majumdar, Rajatava Dutta, Biswajit Chakrabarty, Rajesh Sharma, Rudranil Ghosh, Kanchan Mallik, Anjana Basu, Kharaj Mukherjee, Kanchana Maitra, Sonalee Choudhury, Phalguni Chatterjee, Priya Karfa and Others.

Release Date: 28th January, 2011


Directed By: Aniket Chattopadhyay

Story : Aniket Chattopadhyay, Screenplay : Aniket Chattopadhyay

Produced by: R. P. Techvision (I) Pvt. Ltd. ( Kaustuv Roy )
:::

Story Line :

Bye Bye Bangkok revolves around a few couples, played by Anjana- Shilajit ( noted actor -singer) , Sonali Chowdhury -Kharaj Mukherjee ,  Kanchan Mallik-Kanchana Moitra , Neil Mukherjee-Locket Chatterjee. Incidentally  they all happen to be present in Bangkok. Here in Bangkok these couples get tempted to take a bite of the forbidden fruit resulting in  extra-marital affairs in hilarious circumstances.
