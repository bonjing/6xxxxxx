Sini Ada Hantu

Release Date: 10th February 2011
Language: Malay
Running Time: 98 mins
Genre: Comedy / Horror
Director: James Lee
Cast: Eira Syazira, Pete Teo, Mano Maniam, Baki Zainal, Patrick Teoh


Hilarious! A very horror comedy featuring 3 ghost stories that transcends racial background, truly reflects Malaysian mixed cultural life style. First seen in Malaysia. Showcasing the best talents (not necessary the most famous) from both young and old generation in Malaysia...
