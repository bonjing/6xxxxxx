Swing Aerials Volume 3: Advanced Air-Steps

When it comes to high energy dancing and teaching, national champion dancers Dan Newsome and Tiffiny Wine are known for taking Lindy Hop to new heights. Dan and Tiff are back with 14 more fantastic swing aerial moves. 
Aerials Volume 2 is all about flips! This DVD will show you 14 aerials from preparation to completion in a fun and easy learning environment. These exciting tricks and air steps will take your swing dancing to the next level. Whether in jam circles, competitions, or on the social dance floor, these aerials are fun and sure to impress!

Swing Aerials 3 is Region free and will work in DVD players world wide!
