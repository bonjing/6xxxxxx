Godzilla 1998


YEAR 1998
RUNNING TIME: 138 min.
COUNTRY USA
DIRECTOR Roland Emmerich
SCREENWRITER: Dean Devlin & Roland Emmerich (Story: Ted Elliott, Terry Rossio, Dean Devlin, Roland Emmerich)
COMPOSER: David Arnold
CINEMATOGRAPHER: Ueli Steiger
CAST: Matthew Broderick, Jean Reno, Maria Pitillo, Hank Azaria, Doug Savant, Kevin Dunn, Michael Lerner, Harry Shearer, Arabella Field, Vicki Lewis


The nuclear tests conducted by the French in the Pacific gave rise to the appearance in the ocean of a reptilian mutant, of enormous dimensions, located behind the attack on a Japanese fishing boat. However, the creature will be limited to living in water: Godzilla is directed through a fixed course to New York, in whose streets cause panic.
