Cairokee the first Arabic rock band in Egypt. Known for their unique mix betwen the power of rock and the magic of the orient and also for their lyrics that touches the daily life of people in Egypt.

Band Members:

Amir Eid (Vocals, Guitar)
Sherif Hawary (Lead Guitar)
Tamer Hashem (Drums)
Ahmed Bahaa (Percussions)
Adam El Alfy (Bass Guitar)
Sherif Mostafa (Keyboard).
