Le Vibrazioni is an Italian rock and pop band formed in Milan in 1999 that is still active. The four founding members, all born in Milan or its metropolitan area, are Francesco Sarcina, author and composer Stefano Verderi, guitarist and keyboardist, Marco Castellani, bassist, and Alessandro Deidda, drummer. After some years spent playing in Milan clubs without publishing any songs, the group manages to debut on the marked with the single 'Dedicato a te', which soon become a Disco di Platino.
