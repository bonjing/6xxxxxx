=================================
Biography of Rahat fateh Ali khan
=================================




From Bollywood unforgettables, 'Main Jahan Rahoon', 'Teri Ore', 'O Re Piya', 'Mann ki Lagan' & 'Jiya Dhadak Dhadak' to assisting his uncle, Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan for a collaborated album with American rock band Pearl Jam, Rahat Fateh Ali Khan has created his own space in the music market today.

He belongs to a family of musical geniuses. A great Sufi singer and an ardent practitioner of Qawwali, Rahat has been trained under the supreme supervision of late Ustad Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan. Rahat's family has an unbroken tradition of performing Qawwali for more than 600 years. He can be heard in numerous performances alongside his father (Ustad Farrukh Fateh Ali Khan) and uncle (Nusrat) in recordings from the 80s to 90s.

Rahat has also contributed to the soundtrack of the 1995 Hollywood film Dead Man Walking. In 2002, he worked on the soundtrack of Four Feathers in collaboration with the American composer of orchestral and film music, James Horner.
