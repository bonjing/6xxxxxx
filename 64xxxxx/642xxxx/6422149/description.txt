Hide My Movies 

The plugin to hide the videos of the PSP 

Installation: 

Copy the file in the folder hmm.prx setplugins located at the root of your PSP and add the line <ms0:/seplugins/HMM.prx 1> without the <and> in the 
vsh.txt file if the file does not create it 

Use: 

In the XMB press R TRIGGER button and round button down to hide your videos
