About:

The Cheetah Video Converter is an easy to use program for converting video and audio files to different formats. It can convert video from DivX, FLV, F4V, AVI, WMV, MPEG, VOB, MP4, 3GP, and MOV video files to FLV, AVI, WMV, MP4, MOV, and Animated Gifs. Video files can also be converted to MP3, WMA, and WAV files. The program can also convert audio from MP3, MP2, AVI, OGG and WAV files to different formats and bit rates..

System Requirements - Windows 2000/XP/Vista/ 7
