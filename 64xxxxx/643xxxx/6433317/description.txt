Plot
After drifter Tom Chaney (Josh Brolin) murders her father, 14-year-old Mattie
Ross (Hailee Steinfeld) hires alcoholic U.S. Marshal Reuben "Rooster" Cogburn
(Jeff Bridges) to help her exact revenge. The disreputable lawman still has
grit, though, and mounts an epic search. Joining the duo on their quest is a
Texas Ranger (Matt Damon) who's also hunting for Chaney in this updated Western
that received multiple Oscar nods, including Best Picture.
