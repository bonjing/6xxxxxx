Me and My Bass Guitar,Track from Victor Wooten's album A Show Of Hands.
Very funky bass slap,the original version (only bass)AND the version with 
Frenz featuring on drums.
Drums (snare,bassdrum,hihat,tom and crash) were digitally added with the 
audio-editor GoldWave in the same way  the (also uploaded) Salsa Meets 
Funk-songs were manipulated.
