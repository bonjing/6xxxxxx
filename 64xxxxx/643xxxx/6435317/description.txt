MU Online is a 3D medieval fantasy MMORPG, produced by Webzen, a Korean gaming company.

Gameplay
As with many MMORPGs the player selects a character and fights monsters to gain experience. MU is populated by a large variety of monsters, from simple ones like goblins and golems to frightening ones such as the Gorgon or Kundun.
