Enhanced remake of the 2009 crossover game Dengeki Gakuen RPG Cross of Venus.

One big villain is trying to rob all laughter from the light novels, so everyone in the top series, such as Toradora, To Aru Index and Nogizaka Haruka comes together to fight back. Every character gets their own fight moves that suit their personality and by combining cards, the characters get more abilities. So get ready to fight in Cross of Venus Special.
