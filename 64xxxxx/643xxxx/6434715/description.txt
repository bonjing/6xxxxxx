After months of search, I finally found the complete Animorphs and Everworld sagas by K. A. Applegate. The nasty part was, though they were all in PDF format, they the files themselves were a meta and content nightmare. So I fixed them and converted them to MOBI (because that works best for my Kindle). If you need them in another format, just DL Calibre and go for it. The meta-data should be correct for most other formats and the covers have been saved into the meta-data.

*note: The Megamorphs (4 in all) have been included *within* the series list since their stories actually occur during the series. I have placed them in their proper chronological space. The others (listed at the bottom) spanned so many timelines, I just didn't know where to put them, so they have been added as Beyond the Animorphs.
