Although CBS aired this as the fourth episode, 
it's actually the pilot -- told in flashback by
Dulcey Coopersmith when she first came to Cimarron City 
to take over her late father's restaurant / saloon.

Leonard Freeman was the original producer just for this episode, 
months before he started to develop Hawaii Five-O for the network.

Guest Stars Telly Savalas, Warren Oates, R.G. Armstrong, 
Andrew Duggan (first of 3 episodes as Major Ben Covington)
Robert Wilke, Hal Needham, and Seymour Cassel.
