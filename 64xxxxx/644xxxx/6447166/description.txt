+-----------------------------------------+
|Sandra Fleming Chronicles: Crystal Skulls|
+-----------------------------------------+

+----------------+
|Game Description|
+----------------+

For centuries, the legend of the crystal skulls has tempted explorers and adventurers from around the world. Now the race is on to retrieve them from their hiding places on lonely volcanic islands, deep in the snowy mountains of Nepal and the rainforests of the Amazon! Your quest will be dangerous - but not as dangerous as the dark secret you'll uncover along the way!

+----------------------------------+
|Instructions / System Requirements|
+----------------------------------+

No install or crack is required for this portable version. Extract the ZIP file and create a shortcut to the executable (EXE) to play.

- OS: Windows XP/Vista/7

- CPU: 1000 MHz

- RAM: 512 MB

- GPU: 32 MB Graphics Card (3D accelerated video card recommended)

- DirectX v9.0 or better
