|Game Description|
+----------------+

Do more, grow more and earn more than ever before with John Deere! Bale hay, mow fairways, plant soybeans or corn, spray or harvest corn, and more - it's up to you to do the job right. See what you can accomplish with 500 horsepower of Green Machine. Choose from more than 15 John Deere vehicles and implements including the 9860 STS Combine, 8530 Tractor and 4930 Sprayer. Complete each job with precision and efficiency to receive bonus Deere Dollars. A job well done is a job well paid in John Deere Drive Green!
