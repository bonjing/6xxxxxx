Summer 2009. Five paraplegics and a young able-bodied teenager light up the stage in front of an 
entranced audience of 8000 people. "Benda Bilili" - in English "See Beyond", is the name of this 
Kinshasa band which has acquired a global following. Chances of success were slim at first for these 
homeless handicapped artists who struggled to survive on the streets of their dilapidated capital. 
"Benda Bilili" is not a music film, it's the story of a dream that became reality. And a plunge into the 
streets of Kinshasa without a safety net.
