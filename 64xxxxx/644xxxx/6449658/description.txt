This is the first shoot she has ever done besides doing cam work. She has never been tied up and left at the whims of a stranger. Nerves are on edge and it shows as soon as I put my hands on her for the first time. It is apparent that she's not sure what she has gotten herself in to. 

Her nerves are getting the best of her so I focus on one thing at a time, not to overwhelm her on her first shoot with someone else in the room. First I find her pain threshold, and then decide to spend more time making her comfortable in her own skin. She has such a rawness about her...untouched, untrained, and scared. Just the way I like them.
