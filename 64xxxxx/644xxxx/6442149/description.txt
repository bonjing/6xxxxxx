ENGLISH
-------

Crocodile Clips develops and sells educational software and hardware. Our software is used worldwide, and is recommended by educationalists and governments for its "constructional" approach to teaching and learning. In some countries our software has been purchased for all state secondary schools.

As Crocodile Clips products are developed specifically for education, we focus on what teachers and students really need from the packages they use. Above all, we ensure that all of our products are both easy to use and engaging.

Our product range includes: Sumdog - free online maths games, Yenka - a set of simulation and modelling tools for science, mathematics, computing and technology, and Absorb - a set of online courses.

Crocodile Clips received a Queen's Award for Enterprise in 2000. The Award recognised the innovation demonstrated by Crocodile Clips in the development of its educational software.
