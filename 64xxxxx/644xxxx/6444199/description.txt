Story:
You and your wife are stranded in a city full of zombies. Survive the endless hordes of zombies by eating, sleeping, scavenging, and defending your injured wife until help comes...

Directions:
Install the latest version of Java and then run the executable file.

Tips:
Move using the WASD keys
Hold SHIFT to run
Hold ENTER to speed up time
Game window is maximizable and resizable
Click the red/blue square in the bottom-right corner to zoom in and out
