Scorpia Rising, the ninth and final novel in the Alex Rider series, was released March 2011. In the book, Scorpia is hired to return the Elgin marbles to Greece. Their plan includes the laying of a false trail to Cairo, killing Alex, and blackmailing London into returning the Marbles. MI6 falls for the trap and Alex is sent to Cairo, where, unbeknownst to Alex, Scorpia is pulling the strings.
