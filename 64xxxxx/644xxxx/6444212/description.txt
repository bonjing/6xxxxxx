Russell recorded his live DVD on September 23rd & 24th in London at the O2 Arena in front of over 30,000 fans.


This special DVD was shot with 11 cameras in full HD. RUSSELL PETERS: THE GREEN CARD TOUR Live from the O2 Arena is 30 minutes longer than the broadcast edit and features over 60 minutes of extras including; Deleted Scenes, Bloopers and Behind The Scenes footage.
