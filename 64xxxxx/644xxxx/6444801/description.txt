Release Notes

Deja 4 annees se sont ecoulees depuis l'accueil
enthousiaste des critiques de "Sink or Swim",
premier album de la formation. Harvee rebondit
maintenant avec un son plus brut et plus grincant
Le groupe presente "Furie", un album rock plus
depouille que son predecesseur. Realise par Howard
Bilerman (Arcade Fire, Wolf Parade, Basia Bulat) et
Murray Lightburn (The Dears), "Furie" est un
energique melange de morceaux francos et anglos

Four years have passed since the well received
Sink or Swim", their debut release, and Harvee
follow it up here with a sound that has more bite
and rawness. After recording sessions filled with
turmoil, what started out as a quintet soon became
a quartet, and the band finally presents "Furie", a
great work that was worth the wait. Produced by
Howard Bilerman (Arcade Fire, Wolf Parade, Basia
Bulat) and Murray Lightburn (The Dears), this
sophomore album is an energetic mix of English and
French songs
