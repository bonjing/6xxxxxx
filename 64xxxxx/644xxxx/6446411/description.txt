Robert Sylvester Kelly (born January 8, 1967), better known by his stage name R. Kelly is an American R&B singer-songwriter, arranger, performer and record producer. He is widely regarded as the King of R&B. Debuting in 1992 with the group Public Announcement, Kelly went solo in 1993. The discography of R. Kelly, consists of 14 studio albums, 2 compilation albums, 3 collaborative albums, and 46 singles.

Epic is a compilation album, released September 13, 2010 in Europe, through Jive Records and Sony Music. It is yet to be released in the United States. The album is a collection of Kelly's most epic ballads such as the hits The World's Greatest, If I Could Turn Back the Hands of Time, I'm Your Angel or I Believe I Can Fly. Also he recorded five new ballads and epic songs for the record including the official 2010 FIFA World Cup anthem and first single Sign of a Victory.  (wikipedia)
