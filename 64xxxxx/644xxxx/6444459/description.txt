

Steampunk FPS
#############



*This is a short indie game made by 3 people over the course of few months.*




Steampunk FPS is a first person shooter game setting in a steampunk enviroment. Goal of the game is to find your way to the mansion which lies on the opposing edge of the level from where the player starts. Player must battle through hostile soldiers or avoid them altogether. Player will also face a challenging puzzle which he must solve in order to find his way to the mansion.
