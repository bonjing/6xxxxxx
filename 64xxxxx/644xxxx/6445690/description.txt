2011 marks the 30th anniversary of the release of Blizzard of Ozz in the
U.S.. This landmark album has inspired new generations of rock bands and
fans. The new edition of this hard rock masterpiece showcases Ozzy's musical
collaborations with late guitar hero Randy Rhoads.The Blizzard of Ozz

B-Side), Goodbye To Romance (2010 Guitar & Vocal Mix) and RR- Previously
Unreleased - Outtake guitar solo from the session tapes for Blizzard Of Ozz
