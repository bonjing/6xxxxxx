Season 2, Episode 3 Aired: 9/25/2007

Last Orders

In 1993, devoted and loving father, Kevin O'Loughlin, witnessed the dark side of sunny Nelson, when he was viciously stabbed to death after a night out in town. Nelson police believe that more than one person killed Kevin, but they have been unsuccessful at trying to reveal the identity of his murderers. Psychics Kelvin Cruickshank and Deb Webber lend a hand to see what details they can unearth from this case.
