This DVD is divided into four parts:
physical preparation for labour and birth - information on diet, exercise and herbal medicine
emotional preparation - finding the right support person, understanding the father's role during pregnancy and birth
birth options - includes footage of births in hospital, birth centre and home, including a water birth
stages of labour  - an overview on the stages of labour in a natural birth.
Includes comments from an obstetrician, naturopath, midwife, herbalist and several childbirth educators.
Length: 88 minutes
Origin: Australia 1999
