Ethan Frome

From BBC Radio 4. Written by Edith Wharton; Full-cast dramatisation starring Dominic Mafham, Jessica Raine, Laurel Lefkow, Christopher Webster and Fenella Woolgar; Dramatised by Lin Coghlan.

A visitor to a New England town sets out to discover the nature of the tragedy that, some 20 years earlier, befell Ethan Frome, a mysterious local man struggling to make a living from the land while trapped in an unhappy marriage to a hypochondriac wife.
