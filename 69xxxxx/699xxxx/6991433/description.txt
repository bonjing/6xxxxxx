Drs. Joanne Stone and Keith Eddleman of Mt. Sinai Hospital, coauthors of the bestselling book Pregnancy For Dummies, host this informative DVD that demystifies the very mystical process of pregnancy by answering the many questions an expectant couple may have.Inspired by the book, the series uses fun graphics and humour to guide new parents through the exciting and emotional months from pregnancy to delivery to the first few months of life. Pregnancy For Dummies introduces you to real-life couples experiencing the challenges and rewards of pregnancy. Accompany them on doctors visits, shopping trips, and first outings with their new babies to see the joy of pregnancy and parenting through their eyes.
