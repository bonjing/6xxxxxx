My Zoo v1.0
Requirements: Android 1.5
Overview: Create your own animal wonderland in this great match-3 zoo game!


Create your own animal wonderland in this great match-3 zoo game! Play and build various shops, attractions and upgrade your enclosures! Try to collect all gems and place domestic and even exotic animals like the mighty T-Rex or Angry Birds in your Zoo! Compare your Zoo with casual players from all over the world in the global highscore and try to become nr.1! Enjoy and become the best zookeeper in the world!
