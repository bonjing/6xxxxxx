Solo a.k.a. (Cretan lover)(1980) by Jack Higgins 

From the bestselling author of The Eagle has Landed and The Eagle has Flown comes a superbly crafted, classic cat-and-mouse tale of murder, love, music, and revenge that sweeps across a colorful, international landscape. "Unadulterated macho adventure (and) suspense."--Houston Post.


Behind the image of a Cretan concert pianist lurks the reality of a ruthless killer. However, when his car knocks down and kills the daughter of an SAS soldier, he makes a rare and fatal error. For with a member of the SAS on your heels, it is only a matter of time.
