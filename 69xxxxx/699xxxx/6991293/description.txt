                                Release Notes
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  VHS rip of some old computer animation circa 1993, set to music composed
by John Serrie. Pure analog rip through composite video cable so the image
is a little soft on the more intricate scenes. The audio was captured through
standard RCA jacks, but is quite clean.
  Certainly, the graphics in this video pale in comparison to what can be rendered
now; what we see here can probably be created in realtime on a laptop. It\'s got a
nice ambient music track, though, makes for unassuming background media.
  Should recode to DVD without issue.
