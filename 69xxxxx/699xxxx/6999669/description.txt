1) Big City Adventure: London Story


Genre: Hidden Object | Puzzle


Fitzpatrick McGovern is long gone, but his mysterious lost legacy is still 
out there! Find it in Big City Adventure: London Story! Follow the tantalizing trail of clues he left behind and visit some of the best known locations 
around London in this incredible Hidden Object Puzzle Adventure game.

You'll be able to meet an eccentric cast of characters that can help you in 
your quest, but only if you can help them first. Track down the legacy in Big 
City Adventure: London Story!


Explore London
Meet eccentric characters
Find the McGovern legacy!


System Requirements:


OS: Windows XP | Windows Vista | Windows 7
CPU: 800 MHz
RAM: 256 MB
DirectX: 7.0
Hard Drive: 126 MB


Install and play.

2) Big City Adventure: London Classic



Genre: Hidden Object | Puzzle

Collect postcards
