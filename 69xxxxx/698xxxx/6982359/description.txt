In Orbit is the second album by Swedish singer September. This album was created under September's new label, Catchy Tunes. The album entered the Swedish Chart at no.17 on 3 November 2005, and peaked on the Finnish Album Chart at no.36. In Poland, the album was released with new remixes and videos with singles from album, peaked at no.10 (the highest peak of this album) and certified gold for sales of over 10,000 units.
In March 2008, In Orbit was released on the UK and US iTunes stores, but was removed only a few days later. (wikipedia)
