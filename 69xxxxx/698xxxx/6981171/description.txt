Feature, Parody, Comedy
-----------------------

Starring: Alec Knight, Angie Savage, Bill Bailey, Breanne Benson, Gina Lynn, 
Jack Lawrence, Lela Star, Lexi Belle, Otto Bauer, Samantha Sin

One of the most anticipated films of 2010 is finally here! Hustler Video`s newest parody, THIS AIN`T BAYWATCH XXX, is finally available! Watch as all of your favorite characters from the hit show finally get down and dirty in ways you only dreamed about! Remember watching the original show and thinking to yourself, When are Mitch and CJ finally going to get it on?! Well, you can put your queries aside because Hustler Video heard your pleas and cries of desire and made it happen.In this parody, CJ (Gina Lynn) and April (Breanne Benson) see a couple in the ocean (Lexi Belle andBill Bailey), whom they presume are drowning. Being the ultra talented lifeguards that they are, and along with the help of Mitch (Otto Bauer), they run down the beach to rescue the drowning pair. After saving the two from being swallowed up by the sea, they come to discover that the couple in the water wasn`t drowning at all. The bobbing up and down they saw from their lifeguard tower was actually an underwater BJ! Mortified at having mistaken the drowning for merely some harmless water sports, CJ apologizes for her misunderstanding. But April won`t let her get offthat easy, and after she torments her relentlessly and they duke it out with a catfight, Mitch feels forced to fire them both for unsportsmanlike conduct! But all is well in the end when CJ properly repents and gives Mitch the best apology he`s ever had!
