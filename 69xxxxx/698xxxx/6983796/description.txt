                         = HOW BRUCE LEE CHANGED THE WORLD = 
                         ===================================
                              




Bruce Lee, martial arts legend and on screen icon, starred in only 4 films before his tragic death in 1973 and was nevertheless one of Cinema s most famous and influential stars. For more than 35 years, Lee s legacy has changed the way martial arts and action films are made. Through the development of his own fighting techniques and philosophies he revolutionized traditional martial arts and enhanced its awareness all over the world. 


This programme takes an in depth look at Bruce Lee s life from his youth street fighting on the gang filled streets of Hong Kong to his domination of world cinema and the profound effect he had on the world he left behind after his death. With interviews from celebrities, film directors, friends and family, including Jackie Chan, John Woo, Raymond Chow, John Saxton, Donnie Yen, LL Cool J, Brett Ratner, Stan Lee and of course his wife Linda and daughter Shannon this programme offers a rare insight into one of the most influential Martial Artists of all time. 


How Bruce Lee Changed the World is also packed full of exciting action from some of his most memorable fight scenes in films such as Enter the Dragon, Fist of Fury and The Big Boss and from the TV Show The Green Hornet. Also included is rare footage of his most impressive feats, including the one inch punch and two finger press up. This is a remarkable celebration of Bruce Lee s life and legacy. The key to immortality is first living a life worth remembering. Also includes extended bonus interviews with Kareem Abdul-Jabbar, George Dillman, Ricky Hatton and Diana Lee Inosanto.
