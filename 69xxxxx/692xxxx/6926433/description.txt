Ghosts of the Crusades

History Documentary hosted by Jamie Lee, published by National Geographic broadcasted as part of NG Warrior Graveyard series in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Warrior Graveyard: Ghosts of the Crusades
An archaeological and forensic examination of six crusaders' bones brings to life the 1179 Battle of Jacob's Ford, revealing the wounds that killed the warriors in the Holy Land.
