Another year of trance music is coming to its end.
But before we fix our eye on the next, it is time to close down 2011 in style.
Each year, around December, Armin van Buuren honors another great year in music.
As a yearly tradition, he selects the best tracks of that year and mixes them into a strong 2 hour mix.
Expect an amazing mix of all the highlights of 2011,
carefully selected and blended into an epic trance masterpiece by soon to become the first extra
terrestial DJ on the planet Armin Van Buuren.
Take a ride into 2011 and remember all of its musical jewels.
