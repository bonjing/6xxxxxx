Guest Stars -- Forrest Tucker, Linda Cristal, Chief Dan George,
Linda Gaye Scott, M. Emmet Walsh, and Lee de Broux.

Although her role was a supporting one, this was lovely Linda Cristal's
first acting job after The High Chaparral was cancelled. She played 
a Mexican saloon girl in this episode.  David Dortort created and 
executive produced both series, which helped her get this part.
