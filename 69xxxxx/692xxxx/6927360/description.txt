Official Web Site:  https://market.android.com/details?id=girsas.wifiradar&hl=en or  http://wifi-radar.berlios.de/

Wanna monitor your home network signal strength? Are you simply wanting to try different networks? Waiting in the car and wanting to play around? Then WiFi Radar is for you!

Features :

* Lists all available wireless networks 

* Lots of info per network : 

* AP Name (SSID), 

* BSSID (AP Mac Address), 

* Channel, 

* RSSI / Signal, 

* Encryption / Open/Password-protected, 

* HT / CC,

* Last-Seen Time

* Graph for currently selected network signal over a period of time

* Auto-pilot mode : scan wireless network with a single click

* Advanced : Optionally change used interface

* Export results to a CSV for further processing

System Requirements:

Intel Mac, OS X.5 or better
