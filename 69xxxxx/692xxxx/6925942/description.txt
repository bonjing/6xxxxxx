Plot:

A suspense-thriller, 'Upaasna' is a film about two brothers Ram (Feroz Khan) and Mohan (Sanjay Khan), both of whom are lawyers. Mohan loves a girl named Shaalu (Mumtaz), but circumstances force her to betray him. Shaalu ends up killing a person and later meets with an accident which has been mistakenly committed by Ram. Ram takes her to his place and falls in love with her. The rest of the movie unravels the mystery of Shalu's past as the two brothers come across each other in a courtroom and put the pieces together to solve the thrilling puzzle.

This movie has some very nice songs of which the following two are my favs:
1. Aao Tumhe Main Pyaar Sikha Doon, Sikhladona...Mohd Rafi, Lata Mangeshkar
2. Darpan Ko Dekha Tune Jab Jab Kiya Singaar...Mukesh
