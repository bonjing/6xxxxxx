The Dark Knight 2008 1080p DTS-MA, 96/24 core

Ripped from blu-ray, the dolby true-hd track converted to dts master audio with a 96/24 core.

It is a lossy track, but only barely.  If your rig supports dts 96/24, then that's what you'll get.  If your rig only supports standard DTS, it will still work perfectly, just at the lower sample rate, and it still sounds great. 

Yes, the 1080p video track is huge, but it's well worth it on high res TVs.
