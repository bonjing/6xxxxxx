Christmas Day at Kirkby Cottage

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Anthony Trollope; Full-cast dramatisation starring Finty Williams, Julia McKenzie, Chris Larkin, John Rhys-Davies, Lizzie Bowling and Mary Wimbush; Dramatised by Philip Glasborough.

A young man believes Christmas is 'a bore' but is shown the error of his ways by his bright-eyed fiancee.
