Fun note- Page 3 in Indian newspaper supplements (notoriously 'The Times of India') feature not a picture of a topless model like in British tabloids, but are a bunch of photos/articles about celebrities and their parties.

Madhur Bhandarkar's movie is set in the world of celebrity coverage - the tale of Madhavi Sharma, an idealistic reporter sent to cover the city's happening do's and rub shoulders with the bigshot industrialists, Bollywood stars,fashion designers and others who frequent them. It takes a cynical look at the fake lives led by these people, and she ends up exposing the grime under the glamor.
