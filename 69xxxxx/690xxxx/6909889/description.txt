Plot:
"Kali Topi Lal Rumal" [1959] is an Indian Hindi film directed by Tara Harish. Starring Chandrashekhar, Shakila, Agha, KN Singh, Mukri and Kamal Mehra. Music is by Chitragupta. Lyrics by Majrooh Sultanpuri.

Its a story about a mans who loses his memory and then how his lady love helps him regain his memory.

The movie has some of the most melodious, superhit & immortal gems.
