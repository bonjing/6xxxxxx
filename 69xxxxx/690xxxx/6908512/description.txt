The Art of Stand-Up

Arts Documentary hosted by Alan Yentob, published by BBC broadcasted as part of BBC Imagine series in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

A two-part series on the art of stand-up comedy.


1)  To be or not to be 
Alan Yentob talks to comedians in Britain and America, exploring their backgrounds and influences and their passion for making people laugh.

2)  The journey 
Alan Yentob explores the evolution of stand-up and how it transfers to other mediums.
