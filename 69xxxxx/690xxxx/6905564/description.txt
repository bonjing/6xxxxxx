King of Christmas Lights

Arts Documentary hosted by Lauren Adams, published by Channel 4 broadcasted as part of CH4 Cutting Edge series in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

This Cutting Edge film explores the world of extreme Christmas decorating, meeting some of the UK's most obsessive, eccentric and lovable people who live for adorning their houses in festive regalia every year. Throwing a spotlight on what Christmas means in contemporary Britain, this programme finds out what motivates people to cover their houses in Christmas every year. Are these twinkly-lights-enthusiasts the last bastions of community and Christmas spirit?
