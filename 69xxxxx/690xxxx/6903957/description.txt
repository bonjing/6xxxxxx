Christopher Edward Chris Medina (born November 30, 1983) is an American singer born in Oak Forest, Illinois. Medina is most notable as a former American Idol contestant during the tenth season of  Idol and his single, What Are Words, which went to no.1 in Norway and Sweden and spent 19 weeks at number 1 combined. 11 in Norway and 8 in Sweden.
