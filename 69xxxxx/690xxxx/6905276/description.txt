Synopsis
Lorraine is a stressed out single mom harassed by an abusive ex-husband and struggling to raise her 8-year-old son. Her world changes in an instant
when she and her child are carjacked by a fugitive bank robber and taken on a desperate cross-country drive to retrieve his loot. But Lorraine refuses to be a victim and decides to take control and fight back.
