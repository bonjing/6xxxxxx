Released on November 21, 2011 this three volume box set spans their entire historic Mercury Records recording career. 15 albums all transferred to high resolution 96kHz/24-bit audio and digitally remastered.

EAC Logs included.

1975 Caress Of Steel
1975 Fly By Night
1976 2112

1982 Signals
1989 A Show Of Hands

1981 Exit...Stage Left
1980 Permanent Waves
1978 Hemispheres
1977 A Farewell To Kings
1977 All The World's A Stage
