Cost.................: FreeWare

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

With the upcoming release of C&C Red Alert 3, Electronic Arts has released the original Command & Conquer Red Alert as freeware. Red Alert is the prequel to the original Command & Conquer and takes place in alternative/parallel timeline in which World War II never occured. Instead the Soviet Union rises to power and draws the free world into an alternative World War II.
