Tintenfischalarm (2006)
Also Known As: Octopusalarm

Language: German 
Subtitles: English

This film by Austrian filmmaker Elisabeth Scharang is about Alex, who was born with ambiguous gender and shows his development into a person, who makes his own decisions. Elisabeth Scharang followed on camera Alex\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\'s life over a period of three years. 

One baby in every two thousand is of ambiguous gender. In Alex case, when he was two years old, doctors recommended the use of surgery to correct his natural aberration. After plastic surgeons had amputated his penis and testicles and created a vagina, Alex grew up in his small village as a girl who wears size XXL.
