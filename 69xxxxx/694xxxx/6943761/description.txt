Passage to Malaysia - Into the Wild

Travel Documentary hosted by Denise Keller, published by Discovery Channel in 2009 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

She's travelled all over the world as a model and VJ for MTV Asia. Now Denise Keller is changing track and celebrating her lineage via Passage to Malaysia. Shaped by adventurous explorers and proud natives throughout her colourful past, Malaysia is a land teeming with experiences to be savoured. Beautiful beaches, stunning mountains, hospitable people and delicious food are among the sights and wonders that await the curious traveler. Host Denise Keller will bring viewers on a journey to unravel the charms and pleasures of this tropical country. In PASSAGE TO MALAYSIA: INTO THE WILD, join Denise on a tour to East Malaysia, home to two UNESCO World Heritage sites: the Mulu National Park and the Mount Kinabalu National Park. With sweeping aerials, rugged trails and winding rivers, Denise goes on an action-packed adventure through the heart of Borneo's finest natural wonders. Denise encounters the wildlife of Borneo, where the Kinabatangan's banks teem with Proboscis monkeys, elephants and rhinos, before plunging into a white-water rafting expedition. Then Denise delves into a deeper exploration of Borneo's natural gems, and sees all the beauty of Malaysian nature

Presenter : Denise Keller
Producer & Director : David Moggie
Produced by Reel Networks Films for Discovery Networks Asia Pacific

Discovery Travel & Living Asia, Inc (2009)
