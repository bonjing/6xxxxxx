I Am Legion (French: Je suis legion) is French comic book series written by Fabien Nury, drawn by John Cassaday and published by Les Humanoides Associes, then in English by Humanoids Publishing/DC Comics and later by Humanoids / Devil's Due Productions.

During World War II, Nazis seek to capture and control 10-year-old vampire named Ana. Meanwhile, Allied forces try to uncover Nazis that have infiltrated their ranks.
