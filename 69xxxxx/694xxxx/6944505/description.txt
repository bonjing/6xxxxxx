This torrent contains all 29 songs from the insanely great 1993 movie "Dazed and Confused", which chronicles the day school gets out for a bunch of teenagers in southern Texas circa 1976. If there ever was a truly great "classic rock playlist", this wouldn't be a bad place to start.

All songs are encoded in v0 mp3, except one (it's just about the same anyway).
