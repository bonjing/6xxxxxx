In Style, published by IPC Media, is a monthly glossy magazine focusing on the world's most glamorous celebrities and their style. The British edition launched in 2001, following the success of the magazine in the US. The current editor is Trish Halpin, who was supposed that came on board in September 2006. The circulation of In Style is currently 180,000.

With the strapline "Your Own Personal Stylist", the magazine offers practical but stylish fashion and beauty solutions to its readers. It also focuses on celebrity, with stars instead of models as subjects of the fashion and lifestyle shoots.
