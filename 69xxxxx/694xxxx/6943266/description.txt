Personnel
John Sykes lead vocals, guitars
Marco Mendoza bass, vocals
Tommy O'Steen drums
Formerly the creative force behind Blue Murder, John Sykes' new trio adopts the name Sykes for this Japan-only release. The cover design is as impressive as the recorded material, which includes I Don't Wanna Live My Life Like You, released as a single.
