Foundations of Business, 2nd Edition provides a brief survey of 
the major functional areas of business including management, marketing, accounting, 
finance, and information technology, as well as core topics such as ethics and 
social responsibility, forms of ownership, small business, and international business. 

The text is filled with cutting edge content, including up-to-date information about 
the economic crisis as well as suggestions on how to manage personal financial planning 
in the midst of economic ups and downs. This second edition also includes 
two new appendices on Careers in Business and Personal Finance.
