Lamia

From BBC Radio 4. Written by John Keats; Directed by Susan Roberts.

Lamia is a serpent who has fallen for innocent mortal Lycius, a Corinthian youth, but knowing that she could never get near him in her current form, she strikes a bargain with the god Hermes.

Narrator ...... Paterson Joseph

Hermes ...... Jonathan Keeble

With original music by John Harle
