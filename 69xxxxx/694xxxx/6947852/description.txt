The Bomfunk MC's are a Breakbeat, hip hop and electro group formed in 1998 in Finland. The group's front man is the Finnish rapper B.O. Dubb (formerly known as B.O.W., born Raymond Ebanks) and the main producer is Jaakko "JS16" Salovaara.

In Stereo is the first studio album by Bomfunk MC's, released in 1999 through Sony Music Entertainment. The album's third single "Freestyler" became a chart-topping hit worldwide in 2000, reaching number one in eleven countries.
