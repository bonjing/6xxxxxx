The sleepy suburb of Lindencroft is under attack, and the Smith Family is the town`s only hope! Help the Smiths by deploying a wild arsenal of lawn ornaments, bug-fighting plants and other oddball inventions to stop the threat in the Smith`s backyard and beyond. Research new, more effective ways to protect your flowerbeds. Do you have the nerves to withstand an onslaught of ferocious fruit flies, aggressive ants, and slimy slugs? Then join the battle, and master Garden Defense!
