MS Office Professional Plus 2010:
=================================
Office Professional Plus 2010 is de meest uitgebreide versie van Office 2010 en bevat Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, OneNote, Access, Publisher, InfoPath, SharePoint Workspace en Lync 2010. Met Office Professional Plus 2010 krijgt u ook het recht om Office WebApps op uw eigen (SharePoint) server te draaien zodat u altijd en overal uw documenten kunt inzien, bewerken en delen met anderen. Install Notes:
==============
Run the setup in the folder (x86 or x64, depending on your OS)
Activate using on of the provided activation methods (you might have to disable you AV temporarily)
After activation run the SP1 setup, and you're good to go!
