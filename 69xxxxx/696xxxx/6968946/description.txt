FULL SOUNDTRACK FROM THE ROCKY HORROR PICTURE SHOW(1975) INCLUDING A LEAKED SONG CALLED 'MOON DRENCHED SHORES OF TRANSYLVANIA' THAT WAS INTENDED TO BE THE OPENING SONG FOR THE DIRECT SEQUEL, REVENGE OF THE OLD QUEEN.

ALSO INCLUDED IS THE ROCKY HORROR PICTURE SHOW(AVI) AND SEMI-SEQUEL SHOCK TREATMENT(AVI) AND THE RHPS AUDIENCE SCRIPT AND THE REVENGE OF THE OLD QUEEN DRAFT SCREENPLAY.
