Lily Mazahery (born October 10, 1972) is an Iranian-American lawyer, human rights activist,[1] and an expert source on Iran.[2] She is principal of Mazahery Law Firm and the founder and president of Legal Rights Institute (LRI), an international non-governmental organization (NGO) [Source: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lily_Mazahery]

In 2009, she was interviewed by TV's Anderson Cooper about the dramatic imprisonment, torture and eventual escape of Ahmad Batebi
