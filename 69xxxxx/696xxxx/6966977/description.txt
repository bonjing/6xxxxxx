aniPet Aquarium Live Wallpaper Full v2.4.11


Requires Android: 2.1+ 

Release Date: January 16, 2012

Create your own marine aquarium. Select 15 fish from 180 fish species to grow.
aniPet Aquarium is a live wallpaper simulates a marine aquarium with interaction.

* 20 fish categories, 180 fish species
* Up to 15 fish in the tank
* Able to feed fish and fish grows
* Matured fish may have babies
* Background change
* Landscape mode
* Many options available, such as fish speed, growth speed, food type, light animation, etc.

To use:
HOME/MENU/Wallpaper/Live Wallpapers/aniPet Aquarium

Works for phones with live wallpaper feature.


What's in this version:

Added 19 fish species, including blenny, rabbitfish, hawkfish, etc.
