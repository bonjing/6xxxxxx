The show revolves around a group of characters who come together to put on a Broadway musical based on the life of Marilyn Monroe. But before that can happen, the people who will try to bring it all together must deal with their own personal acts. The series will feature original music by composers Marc Shaiman and Scott Wittman.

Pilot Episode:

Noted songwriting duo Julia Houston (Debra Messing) and Tom Levitt (Christian Borle) get inspiration for a new Broadway show. Already it gets a producer, Eileen Rand (Anjelica Huston) and a director, Derek Willis (Jack Davenport). Meanwhile, a waitress named Karen Cartwright (Katharine McPhee) decides to leave her small town to finally make her theatrical dreams a reality, and a Broadway gypsy named Ivy Lynn (Megan Hilty) decides to try out for a lead role.
