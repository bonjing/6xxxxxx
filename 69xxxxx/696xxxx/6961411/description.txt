Product Details
---------------

Speed Launcher Pro replaces your default lock screen with customizable screen that gives you ability to connect your frequent contacts, launch favorite applications and manage your phone settings with just simple swipe.

Application gives you interactive rotatory dial like screen to use your phone in more smarter way. It gives you ability to use your lock screen to perform common tasks in style.

Even with all functionalities available on your home screen, application still respects your authentication. If you have enabled pattern/PIN lock, it allows user to enter it for unlocking phone.

Please provide your reviews below to spread the word about us. Do send us email on speedlauncherdroid@gmail.com if you are facing any issues, development team will actively look into it.

Operations that you can perform 
-------------------------------
+ Customize contact screen to configure up to nine contacts of your choice to call/message quickly. App reads your favourite/Star contacts from address book on first run. You can then call/message your buddies using your lock screen.

+ You can see call log and unread messages on call log mode. It displays you missed call/message time, contact details for you to quickly take an action on.

+ Customize app screen to configure your nine frequently used apps. Speed Launcher comes with default set of apps to get your started.

+ You can enable/disable android settings on settings mode. E.g. Enable/Disable WiFi/Bluetooth/Sound etc.

+ You can unlock your phone by just a swipe on lock button from any Speed Launcher mode.

+ Intuitive and consistent interface to interact with application.


App Permissions explained in detail
-----------------------------------

+ To show contacts on call screen app needs permission to read your address book
+ To change Android settings app needs permission for Bluetooth/Vibrate/WIFI/etc.
+ To open Camera app, Speed Launcher needs camera permission
+ To avoid showing up Speed Launcher screen during a phone call app needs to know if call is in progress.
+ To show app as lock screen, app needs permission for lock state

Note: Images for users shown in contact screen are picked from address book. To change users profile photo you need to edit users contact info in address book.
