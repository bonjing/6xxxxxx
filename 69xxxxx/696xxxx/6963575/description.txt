This book provides a basic but comprehensive information on the subject of Weapons of Mass Destruction. The book lists and discusses various biological, chemical and radiological agents and weapons. For each agent and/or associated weapon, the book describes what they are, and importantly, measures that citizen's can take to minimize the risks caused by exposure to them.
