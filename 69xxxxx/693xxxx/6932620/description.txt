Zlati Uhori (1979) Czechoslovakia

IMDB - http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0080175/

A movie about a little boy named Prdelka, who traveled with his father from Prague to the country during the Second World War. There, the boy became friends with a local fisherman and learned to catch the golden eels. Eventually his father and mother were arrested by the Nazis and the boy stayed with the fisherman.
'Golden Eels' won a first prize at the International Festival of the Prix Italia in 1979.
