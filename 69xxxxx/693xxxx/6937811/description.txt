Finally we cover Abraham, a mystic who spoke personally with God, a leader of men, a builder of nations, a pioneer and a warrior and Sarah. 
At the time she conceived her first child, the event being forecast by an Angel of the Lord. 
Three such Heavenly Messengers appeared in the course of events which befell Abraham and Sarah
