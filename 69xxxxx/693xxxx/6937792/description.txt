Not power on, blinks 6 times? 
A common problem are the two MCZ3001D ICs that are on the D board
IC8002 and IC6501 
(the main board laying flat on the bottom on the left when looking from the rear of the set).  
Buy replacements online MCZ3001DA

1080I on hdmi working?

Set OP / HDPT = 0 [this means to bypass mid settings for 1080 signals]

and setting HDMI = 1 [ on input 7]
