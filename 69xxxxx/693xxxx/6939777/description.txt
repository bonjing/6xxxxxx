kruhft - Songs For Beautiful Girls

A collection of soundtracks to movies yet realized, this album
provides both relaxing beauty and emotional intensity, just as the
those the album is dedicated to provide to us. Fans of Aphex Twin, Dan
Abrhams/Shuttle 358, and Mitchel Akiyama will enjoy this album.
