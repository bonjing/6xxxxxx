                SADE  - Feel No Pain  (1992)



         "Feel No Pain" is the second single from the English group Sade's fourth studio album, "Love Deluxe" (1992). It was released in the United Kingdom in 1992. The album peaked at #3 on the US Album charts and was certified Quadruple-Platinum (4x), but stalled at no.6 on the UK charts before being certified Gold by the BPI.
