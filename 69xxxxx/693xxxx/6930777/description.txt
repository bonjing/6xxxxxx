Synopsis: During the rescue mission Lt. Sonya Blade manages to escape and rejoin the others. The fight between Jax and Kano continues and Jax emerges victorious after delivering a skull crushing hit into Kano\'s right eye. All seems fine until a granade sends Sonya and himself to the ICU. Jax sustains significant damage to his back and arms. Kano escapes but his eye is replaced with a cybernetic one from Cyber Initiative.
