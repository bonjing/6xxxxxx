Title: Emergency: Fighters for Life
Genre: strategy / real-time / isometric
Released: 31st July 1998
Developer: Sixteen Tons Entertainment
Publisher: WizardWorks
Description:
Emergency: Fighters for Life is a real-time strategy game. You're controlling a group of cops, doctors and firefighters and you have to save as many people as possible from emergencies. The game contains a large variety of missions to solve; for example, you have to rescue people involved in a car accident, you have to put out a large fire in a building and you have to prepare a Formula 1 race!

Bonus content: manual, soundtrack.

How to play:
1) Download the game;
2) Mount or burn disc image;
3) Run setup.exe to install;
4) Play the game with the disc inserted.

! Warning
The game works in Windows Vista/7, but severe graphical glitches make in unplayable. Compatibility settings have no effect. Use Windows XP or older versions to play the game.
