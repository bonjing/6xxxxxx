Official Web Site: http://www.ecamm.com/mac/phoneview/

PhoneView is the Mac companion for iPhone, iPad and iPod Touch. Access file storage notes, iTunes media, voice memos, voicemail, photos, notes, SMS/MMS, contacts and call history data with one click. PhoneView also automatically archives your messages, call history and voicemails, so you can access them even when your iPhone is not available.

Features :

* Store any type of data.

* Access your music, videos, podcasts and photos.

* Play, export and archive voicemail

* Access notes, SMS, MMS, contacts and call history stored on the
  iPhone.

* Move files between work and home.

* Back up important data to your iPhone.

* Keep useful apps and installers with you at all times.

Bug Fixes

* Fixes an issue with iOS 5 support where thumbnails did not match up
  with items in Photos mode.

* Fixes an issue with iOS 5 support where certain items were missing
  from Movies mode.

* Fixes an issue where SMS messages and iMessages sent to multiple
  recipients were not displayed properly.

* Other minor fixes.

System Requirements:

iPhone, iPad or iPod Touch.
Mac OS X 10.5.8 or later.
iTunes 9.2 or later.
