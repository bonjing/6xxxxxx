English
Blessed was recorded live at the Sydney Entertainment Centre on March 3, 2002 by Darlene Zschech and the Hillsong Worship Team.
Blessed saw Marty Sampson step up to lead worship alongside Darlene Zschech and Reuben Morgan for the night.
Over 10,000 people from Hillsong Church attended the recording.
