Portapak: Tek is an ongoing project aimed at providing all the software a computer technician might find necessary on a portable flash drive. I've mucked with other portable application packs and found they either include a lot of pointlessness like media players and photo editors, or they don't include enough software to reasonably diagnose and repair a PC.

----------------------------------------------------------

Title:
PortaPak: Tek.

Format:
.RAR archive file.

Type/Genre:
Software / Repair.

Protection:
None.

Description:
A collection of portable software most technicians would find useful.

Features:
A varied plethora of utilities and softwares to aid in computer repair.

Installation:
Unrar directly to a 2GB flash drive or larger.

Over the next few months, I plan on releasing new versions of PortaPack: Tech, but I need some feedback. The only way I could imagine creating an end-all, beat-all release is by asking for suggestions from other technicians. If you know of a software title or two that you'd like to see included, tell me what need it fills and why it's better than what I already have included. I'll take all of that into consideration and think about adding your suggestion on next release.The goal is to make PortaPack: Tech as functional and as small as possible, so I'm going to prefer smaller apps and lighter-resource apps over big-name bloated software.
