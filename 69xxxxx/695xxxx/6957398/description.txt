An entertaining four part documentary series that reconstructs some of the most famous historical battles through unbelievable CGI animation. The transitions from historical footage of each battle to the computer-generated re-enactments are so fast paced and seamless, it makes the audience feel like they are actually participating in the battle themselves. 


Season 1 Episode 1: EPISODE: 1

Investigating the real and often unknown stories behind history's most significant battles. The first episode deals with the death of German tank commander Michael Wittmann in action on August 8,1944, near Cintheaux, France. Several different Allied units claimed responsibility for his killing - a controversy that has endured for more than 60 years.

Season 1 Episode 2: EPISODE: 2

By early 1942, all that stood between the Axis powers and victory in North Africa was the Mediterranean island of Malta. From June 1940 to December 1942, aircraft had attempted to bomb the island into submission and this episode examines the stories of Allied forces who defended Malta

Season 1 Episode 3: EPISODE: 3

The story of the Intruders, a group of airmen who were dubbed the 'Bandits of the Air' by the Nazis for their exploits in the skies above Europe's battlefields

Season 1 Episode 4: EPISODE: 4

A search for the lost graves of the International Brigades who were murdered by the fascist forces of General Franco during the Spanish Civil War
