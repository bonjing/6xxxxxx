The Wedding Band

Info:
The Wedding Band is the alter-ego of folk/bluegrass group Mumford & Sons (along with several friends), under which they released their limited tour EP, "The First Dance". The 10-inch release consists of songs that are regularly heard played out on the band's tour bus, but very rarely live on stage.
Line-up: - Mumford & Sons (Ted Dwane, Ben Lovett, Winston Marshall & Marcus Mumford) - Nick Etwell (Trumpet) - Callum Lindsay (Fiddle) - Jessie Quinn (Percussion) - Adam Stockdale (Lap Steel) - David Williamson (Trombone)
