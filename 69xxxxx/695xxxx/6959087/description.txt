Rules of Asylum

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by James Follett; Full-cast dramatisation starring Vernon Joyner, Manning Wilson, Francis de Wolff, Cyril Shaps and Caroline John.

Episode 1: A man who escapes from an asylum is apparently a threat to the state.

Episode 2: Valery is taken out to dinner by his interrogator - but is all quite what it seems?

Episode 3: How did Valery escape? Will Suskin ever uncover the truth?
