Deadfall

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by R.D. Wingfield; Full-cast dramatisation starring Bob Peck, Judy Berry, Stephen Thorne, Jim Reynolds and Alan Dudley.

Harold Davis is an explosives expert in the demolition business spending his days blowing up old factory chimneys. Previously, he was an operative for the Ministry of Defense involved in an assassination plot against a politician named Kabinda. His aid and special skills are solicited once again for a final assassination.
