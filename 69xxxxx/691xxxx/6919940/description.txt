Title: Soulbringer
Genre: RPG / action / 3rd-person
Released: 30th June 2000
Developer: Infogrames
Publisher: Infogrames
Description:
The game starts off with the story of a young man arriving in a town called Madrigal to find his uncle Andrus, who tells him about magic and his past. The protagonist is soon caught in the middle of a strange bandit activity around town. During the game, it is revealed that the hero is a reincarnation of a powerful being whose purpose is to defeat a group of ancient demons known as Revenants. Even though forgotten, the Revenants are secretly standing behind all major conflicts and problems of the world. The hero has to reawaken his past-life powers, travel to parallel worlds and ultimately trap the Revenants within the mystical Well of Souls.

How to play:
1) Mount or burn disc images;
2) Run SETUP.EXE to install;
3) Play the game without the discs.

! Warning
The game doesn't work on Windows Vista/7. Use Windows XP/98 if you want to play.
