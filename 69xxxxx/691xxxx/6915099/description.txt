Title: The Son of No One (2011)
Country: USA
Language: English
Film Genre: Action | Crime | Thriller
IMDB URL: http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1535612/
Director: Dito Montiel
Cast: Al Pacino, Channing Tatum, James Ransone



Plot or Synopsis
A rookie cop is assigned to the 118 Precinct in the same district where he grew up.
The Precinct Captain starts receiving letters about two unsolved murders that happened many years ago in the housing projects when the rookie cop was just a kid.
These letters bring back bad memories and old secrets that begin to threaten his career and break up his family.
