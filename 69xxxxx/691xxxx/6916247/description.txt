Saunders Comprehensive Review for NCLEX-RN 5th Edition

This program offers four ways to prepare for the NCLEX-RNÂ® examination:

Assessment, Study, Quiz and Exam modes.

There are also three audio lectures in this program: Acid Base Balance, Fluids and Electrolytes, or Pharmacology Strategies. You can listen to them now or download to listen to later from your MP3 player.

The Assessment mode delivers 75 specific questions. A 6-week calendar will be generated to help you target areas to study.

The Exam and Quiz modes allow you to select topics and categories to build a test randomly from the available questions. The Study mode generates all questions from categories and subcategories you choose. The Quiz mode generates 10 questions from categories and subcategories you choose. The Exam mode generates 100 questions randomly from all questions available in the program.

Review is available after completion of a Quiz, an Exam, or an Assessment. The Review displays your answers, the correct answers, rationales, strategy tips and references.
