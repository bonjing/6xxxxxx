Title: Harbinger
Genre: RPG / action / isometric
Released: 25th February 2003
Developer: Silverback Entertainment
Publisher: DreamCatcher Interactive
Description:
Harbinger takes place on a massive space ship inhabited by multiple warring races and a band of refugees. The player has the choice of three characters with their own unique quests, items, and full-game storylines: Human male - fights with guns and gunblades for ranged and melee attacks; Culibine female - channels energy through upgradeable gauntlets and uses a nova-like radial attack from another gauntlet; Gladiator male - big, slow and strong, uses a large arm cannon on one side and a set of melee blades on the other.

Bonus content: wallpapers.

How to play:
1) Download the game;
2) Mount or burn disc image;
3) Run setup.exe to install (it will start automatically if you have autorun enabled);
4) Extract Patch.zip archive to game folder (overwrite all files);
5) Play the game without the disc.
