Guy Ritchie and produced by Joel Silver, Lionel Wigram, Susan Downey, and Dan Lin. It is a 
sequel to the 2009 film Sherlock Holmes, based on the character of the same name created by 
Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. The screenplay is written by Kieran Mulroney and Michele Mulroney. 
Robert Downey, Jr. and Jude Law reprise their roles as Sherlock Holmes and Dr. John Watson, 
respectively, as the duo join forces to outwit and bring down their most cunning adversary, 
Professor Moriarty, played by Jared Harris. The film is specifically influenced by Conan Doyle's 
work The Final Problem, but it is an independent story rather than a strict adaptation.
