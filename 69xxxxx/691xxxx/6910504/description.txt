Storyline

Based on a true story, Killer Elite pits two of the worlds'
most elite operatives - Danny, an ex-special ops agent 
(Jason Statham) and Hunter, his longtime mentor
Robert De Niro) - against the cunning leader of a 
secret military society ('Clive Owen' ). 
Covering the globe from Australia to Paris,
London and the Middle East, Danny and Hunter 
are plunged into a highly dangerous game of cat 
and mouse - where the predators become the prey.
