Lost Boys Of Sudan

A POV documentary by Megan Mylan and John Shenk.

Winner of an Independent Spirit Award and named Best Documentary at the San Francisco International Film Festival. LOST BOYS OF SUDAN follows two teenage Sundanese refugees on an extraordinary journey from Africa to America, Offering a gripping and sobering peek into the myth of the American Dream.
