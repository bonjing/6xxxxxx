If you have installed a program on you computer and you have lost the serial number, this is the program to use.

Just double click the zip file.  Go to the "LicenseCrawler" folder. And double click the "LicenseCrawler.exe" file.  

Click "OK" to select the language.  

Then click "Accept".  
A "Save Settings?" window will appear.  
Click "Yes".  
Click "search". 
An advertising window will appear.  
Push it out of the way.  
Don't close it.

License Crawler will do its work with a list of the programs on your computer and their serial numbers.  You can copy and past the info into a txt file.

When you close the program choose "No" to save as an archive.
