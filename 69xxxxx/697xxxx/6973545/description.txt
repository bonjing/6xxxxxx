Feature, Parody
---------------

Director: Will Ryder 

Cast: India Summer, Faye Reagan, Brittany O'Connell, Kagney Linn Karter,
Jessica Bangkok, Madelyn Marie, Farrah Foxx, Barrett Blade, Scott Lyons,
Dino Bravo, Dane Cross, Eric Swiss, James Bartholet
 

The Bundy family goes wild winning $1,000,000 in the Illinois State Lottery
but when Al, Peg, Kelly & Bud are accused of fraud, fast money, wild times,
dirty blowjobs & hardcore fucking go out the window before Kelly bangs the
lottery official saving the family from financial ruin in this hilarious
award-winning sequel.
