Kathy Griffin: Hot Cup of Talk (1998)

Originally aired August 14, 1998. Topics include Griffin's acting stint in Awaiting the Renaissance, Celine Dion, Madonna, Griffin's encounter with former President Bill Clinton, and Hanson.

Bravo specials

Kathy Griffin: The D-List (2004)

Originally aired March 24, 2004. Topics include Britney Spears, Whitney Houston, Griffin's appearances on Hollywood Squares with Anna Nicole Smith and Little Richard, and hosting an AmfAR event with Sharon Stone.

Kathy Griffin: Allegedly (2004)

Direct to DVD release, later aired on Bravo. Topics include pitching a sitcom to NBC, the VH1 Vogue Fashion Awards, Gwyneth Paltrow, Scientology, the 9/11 celebrity telethon, appearing in the music video for "The Real Slim Shady" with Eminem, Griffin's trip to Afghanistan, Brooke Shields' wedding, and The View.

Kathy Griffin Is... Not Nicole Kidman (2005)

Originally aired August 3, 2005, preceding the series premiere of Kathy Griffin: My Life on the D-list. Topics include presenting with Ryan Seacrest at the American Music Awards, Anna Nicole Smith, Barbra Streisand on Oprah, Kelly Clarkson, and Clay Aiken.

Kathy Griffin: Strong Black Woman (2006)

Originally aired May 9, 2006. Topic include Star Jones, Griffin's incident joking Dakota Fanning had entered a rehab facility, Tom Cruise, Hurricane Katrina coverage, Griffin's encounter with Celine Dion, and Griffin's vacation in Palm Springs. For example, Sofi Ojeda.

Kathy Griffin: Everybody Can Suck It (2007)

Originally aired June 5, 2007. Topics include the Mel Gibson arrest, Lindsay Lohan, Club Hyde in Los Angeles, Whitney Houston, Rosie O'Donnell's R family cruise, the Creative Arts Emmys, and Ann Coulter.

Kathy Griffin: Straight To Hell (2007)

Originally aired November 29, 2007. Topics include Griffin's Emmy Award acceptance speech controversy, To Catch a Predator, Senator Larry Craig, Barbara Walters, Paris Hilton, Paula Abdul, Larry King's 50th Anniversary roast, Dr. Phil, The View, and Martha Stewart.

Kathy Griffin: She'll Cut A Bitch (2009)

Originally aired April 15, 2009. Topics include Taylor Swift, The Jonas Brothers, Griffin's encounter with Cher, Judge Judy, and the Prime-time Emmy Awards.

Kathy Griffin: Balls Of Steel (2009)

Originally aired November 3, 2009. Topics include Britney Spears, Taylor Swift and Kanye West on the MTV Video Music Awards, Jon and Kate Gosselin, Levi Johnston and Sarah Palin, Whitney Houston, Paula Abdul, Barbara Walters, Michael Jackson, Miley Cyrus, and the Prime-time Emmys.

Kathy Griffin: Whores on Crutches (2010)

Originally to be called "Diva in a Tipi". Originally aired November 2, 2010. Topics include The View, her playing on a slot machine, Paris Hilton, Tiger Woods and his affairs, television shows she frequently watches, Maggie's new book and The Real Housewives of New Jersey.

Kathy Griffin: 50 and Not Pregnant (2011)
Originally aired March 17, 2011. Topics include attending parties at the 53rd Grammy Awards, the Oprah Winfrey Network, attending a roast of Quentin Tarantino, Justin Bieber, Miley Cyrus, Steven Seagal: Lawman, Maggie Griffin, Sarah Palin, and performing in Knoxville for her previous special Kathy Griffin Does the Bible Belt.

Kathy Griffin: Gurrl Down (2011)

Originally aired June 22, 2011. Topics include Sarah Palin and her family, trying to pick up guys at MIT, her run-in with Michele Bachmann, hosting CNN's New Year's Eve Bash with Anderson Cooper, My Strange Addiction, Kirstie Alley, Phaedra from The Real Housewives of Atlanta, meeting Bill O'Reilly backstage at Glee, and Lady Gaga's parents.

Kathy Griffin: Pants Off (2011)

Orginally aired September 20, 2011. Topics include her mother Maggie, Kim Kardashian's wedding, Bruce Jenner, Casey Anthony, Nancy Grace, Marcus Bachmann, The Real Housewives of New Jersey and Orange County, performing at a maximum security prison, being pantsless during the 2011 Virginia Earthquake in New York City, dinner with Gloria Steinem, eating a pot brownie, and staying at Anderson Cooper's house.

Kathy Griffin: Tired Hooker (2011)

Originally aired December 20, 2011. Topics include Kim Kardashian and Kris Humphries' divorce, Nancy Grace on Dancing with the Stars, pajama jeans, forever lazy's, and ordering pizza with Cher.
