
A chronicle of the intersecting lives of four lost souls haunting the fringes of Coney 

Island's 60th Precinct, Missing Persons is a 3D-animated feature produced in 2003 and was 

shown at the Los Angeles Film Festival, Seattle International Film Festival, Flanders Int'l 

Film Festival and others.

It's...strange.
