Robinson Helicopter Safety Awareness DVD

Robinson Helicopter DVD.
Excerpts from the Robinson Helicopter Factory Safety Course.

 

DVD footage of the Robinson Helicopter Factory Safety Course presented by Frank Robinson.
This DVD explains the dangers of helicopter flight along with actual R-22 accidents from home videos.

Produced to help you understand what not to do during R-22 & R-44 flight, and the reasons why.

    Primary Causes of Fatal Accidents
    Low RPM Rotor Stall
    Energy Management and Rotor RPM Decay
    Weather including Wind, Turbulence, and Carburetor Ice
    Low G Mast Bumping
    Vortex Ring State
    Ships complete with DVD review ground test.

Run Time: 1Hr. 47 Min.
