iNet provides you with information about networks your Mac is connected to. Its very easy and user friendly design allows even the unexperienced user to get a profound and understandable overview of a network and the running services.

Courtesy of Lz0.

Whats New:

* Printing support for network scans

* Export As PDF

* Host count label in "Scan network" is back

* Airport Monitor now shows assigned icons/names for Airport Base
  Stations

* Airport Monitor not saving assigned names to WiFi clients under certain
  conditions

* Airport Monitor not displaying WiFi clients if more than 20

System Requirements:

Intel Mac, OS X.6 or greater
