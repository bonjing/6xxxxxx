Holy Quran For Nokia Symbian60 v5 Mobiles

This application is compatible with Nokia N97, Nokia X6, Nokia 5230, Nokia 5530, Nokia C6, Nokia C7, Nokia 5235, Nokia 5800, Nokia N8 and Nokia 5233 Mobiles. Read the Holy Quran in Othmani font, search words, bookmark Ayah or Sura using a multi-lingual interface and listen to the Quran narrated by famous reciters. Use an easy way of Quran memorization. Learn Quran Tajweed through multimedia content and view the recitation rules in colors. Switch between Urdu and Arabic meanings.
