Craig Owens, former singer/screamer of Chiodos, has moved on to newer and bigger things with his new band, Destroy Rebuild Until God Shows (D.R.U.G.S.). This is their first album release. It is the deluxe version with 12 songs, 1 music video of \"If You Think This Song Is About You, It Probably Is\", and a 19 minute Mini Documentary. 

D.R.U.G.S. will be featured in the 2011 Vans Warped Tour
