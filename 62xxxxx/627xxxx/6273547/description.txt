My Sisters Hot Friend - Allie Haze 

March 28, 2011

Allie arrives at her friend's house to pick her up so they can practice tennis, but she's not home. Her friend's brother, Rocco, tells Allie that his sister went to the mall, and should be home in about an hour or so. Allie's miffed that her friend ditched her, but decides to wait for her there. Rocco's not interested in talking tennis with her, but he is interested in hitting on her. Soon enough he's serving her his ace, and she's helping him with his stroke.
