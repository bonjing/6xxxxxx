 Trainer notes
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~

 NUMPAD1 - Infinite Health
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 This option will give you an infinite amount of health.

 NUMPAD2 - Infinite Ammo
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 This option will give you an infinite amount of ammo.

 NUMPAD3 - Infinite Suit Energy
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 With this option your will be given an infinite amount
 of suit energy.

 NUMPAD4 - Super Speed
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 This option will let you run around with the speed of light.

 NUMPAD5 - Super Jump
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 This option will let you jump much higher than usual. Make
 sure to have infinite health enabled.

 NUMPAD6 - Grenade Master
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 This option will let you take control of any grenade explosion in 
 the game. You can do great things with this. For example:
 throw a bunch of grenades on the ground. Hit numpad 6 and they
 will not explode. Than when enemies walk you can let them explode
 into pieces by pressing numpad 6 again.

 NUMPAD7 - Cloack Mode
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 This option will keep you cloacked even when shooting your
 gun.

 NUMPAD8 - Bullet Impact
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 This option will increase the impact of your bullets.
 Every object you shoot in the game will fly through 
 and explode into pieces.

 F1 - F2 Save / Restore Position
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 This option will give you a nice teleporting system.
 Simply hit F1 to save your current position. Use
 F2 to restore it back.

 NUMPAD9 - One Hit Kill
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 Enable and hover your mouse over your enemy and you will be able
 to kill him with one shot.

 NUMPAD0 - Slow Motion Enemies
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 Enable and hover your mouse over your enemy and they will walk
 in slooowwwmotion.

 F3 - Super Throw
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 With this option enabled you can throw every object you pick up
 much and much further than normal.

 F4 - Super Accuracy
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 With this option enabled your weapons will shoot super accurate.

 F5 - Super Zoom
 ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
 With this option enabled and pressing right mouse button you can
 view much further than usual. You can easily look around enemy spots
 to see what happens. You can also shoot your enemies from a very far
 distance.

 4. Toggle desired Options on/off
