The lust and depravity of modern man has lured the Demon King to the surface 
world. His monstrous minions roam the streets, sniffing out nubile victims and 
devilish delights. But their debauchery is short-lived - the Vampire Queen awakens 
to slay her natural enemies. With a bloodlust that rivals her deadly, preternatural powers, she 
will hunt them down. In a city of steel and science, an ancient feud is about to reignite!
