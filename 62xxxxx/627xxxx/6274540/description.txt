Reba Russell Band's eighth CD aptly Titled "8". Partially a tribute to Mr. James (Jim) Luther Dickinson, the band includes songs from Mr. Dickinson's play list such as "Asshole" and "Hitler Lives". Also on this CD, songs written by Delta Joe Sanders, Joe Schicke, Willie Dixon and Don McMinn, along with many more written by Reba , Wayne and the Band. As always this recording is filled with various styles of Delta/ Memphis Blues and a bit of Jazz thrown in.
