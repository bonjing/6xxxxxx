SQS Supastars is an Indian Pop Band based in Mumbai. The group consists of Sanam Puri (lead vocals), Samar Puri (lead Guitar and vocals), Venky (Bass Guitar and vocals) and Quesh (Drums and vocals). The band became an instant hit amongst Indian teenagers with Hawa Hawa and Behka as their lead songs. They are also known as 'Supastars SQS'.
