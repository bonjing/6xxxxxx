Bloodthirsty arcade shooter in the world of Stickman. Our hero will shoot out his own extremities in order to survive. He has a weapon loaded with lead and alien plasma, which will surely defeat the enemy if used with skill. An online table of records will help to record the accomplishments in this world of madness.

System Requirements:
- Operating system Microsoft Windows 98, XP, Vista, Win7
- Pentium 3 and above
- 256 MB RAM
- Video card with 32 MB memory
- The sound device compatible with DirectX 9
