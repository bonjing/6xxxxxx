Unabridged recording of "God Emperor of Dune", the fourth book in the saga of Dune, written by Frank Herbert, and published in 1981.

This recording was made in 2008, and is of much higher quality than the previous available version.

Readers: Simon Vance, Scott Brick and Katherine Kellgren.

32 files (~30 minutes each). MP3, 80kbps, 44kHz.
