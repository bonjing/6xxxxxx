Chessmaster 7000
Version 1.0.0

*** General Information ***

If you experience any problems with this version of your Chessmaster
7000 program, before you contact Technical Support, make sure that
you can outline the procedure by which you experienced your problem.
What were you trying to do? What happened? What had you done with
Chessmaster before the problem occurred? What other programs were
running at the time? Etc.... These are all potential questions that
Technical Support might ask you, and your ability to answer these
questions will greatly increase the likelihood that your problem will
be resolved.
