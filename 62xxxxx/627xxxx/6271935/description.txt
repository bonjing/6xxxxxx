The Outside Child

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Nina Bawden; Read by Zelah Clarke; Abridged by David Ian Neville.

Episode 1: Jane happily lives with her two aunts - until discovering a secret is being kept from her.

Episode 2: Jane unearths clues to her past and makes up her mind to track down her secret family.

Episode 3: Jane and Plato do some sleuthing in London in search of the children - and the truth.

Episode 4: Jane sets off alone on the most important journey of her life and makes a shock discovery.

Episode 5: Jane's journey of discovery and forgiveness draws to a close.
