The Disintergration Machine

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Arthur Conan Doyle; Full cast dramatisation starring Bill Paterson, Gunnar Cauthery and Joanna Munroe; Dramatised by Robert Lloyd Parry; Produced by Fiona Kelcher.

The Professor and his sidekick, Peerless Jones, pay a visit to Latvian scientist Theodore Nemor. They must investigate rumours that Nemor has created a Disintegration Machine which hostile foreign powers are keen to acquire.
