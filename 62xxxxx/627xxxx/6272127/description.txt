The Strange Case of Dr Jekyll and Mr Hyde

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Robert Louis Steveson; Read by Tim Pigott-Smith; Adapted by Morag Lyall; Produced by David Jackson Young.

In the course of his scientific researches, Dr Jekyll develops a potion that enables him to transform himself into his monstrous alter ego, Mr Hyde, a cruel and sinister figure who roams violently around London. But when connections begin to be made between Jekyll and Hyde, Dr Jekyll finds it increasingly difficult to maintain his secret life.
