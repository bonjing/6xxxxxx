Nightmare Town

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Dashiell Hammett; Read by Stuart Milligan; Adapted and Abridged by Neville Teller.

Episode 1: Arriving in desert boom town Izzard, Steve Threefall finds that all is not what it seems.
Episode 2: A hostile reception sends Steve Threefall into the arms of Nova Vallance.
Episode 3: As Steve ponders the wrongdoings of desert boomtown Izzard, events take a sinister turn.
Episode 4: Facing more danger and intrigue, can Steve finally unravel the town's dark mystery?
