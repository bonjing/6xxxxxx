Released on EMI, it is an interesting addition to the "moan and groan" genre. The performances are credited to Les Vibrations, Nathalie, Andrea, Nichole, Christine and Serge - whether that is THE Serge I can't say but the credit is on Gainsbourg's Je Taime Moi Non Plus so it's possible.

In any case there's nice sparse guitar, drum machine and wordless vocals with lots of whispering and other more intimate sounds on reworkings of favourite themes like Emmanuelle and some equally enjoyable originals.
