Plot Summary:

 Next in the four-part Marvel Anime project and broadcasts after the Iron Man anime. Wolverine is a mutant,
possessing animal-keen senses, enhanced psychical 
capabilities, three retracting bone claws on each hand and a healing factor that allows him to recover from virtually 
any wound, disease or toxin at a accelerated rate

The first episode of the series brings us to a time where Logan is drawn back to Japan to rescue Mariko from her father.
At this point in his history, it's to set up his great Japan 
adventure that helped truly define the character in the 80's where he gained his concepts of honor and family.

Synopsis:
--------------

Episode 10 is Titled "Shingen"

On arrival at the entrance to the Dragon palace Logan and Yuriko are met by the mutant samurai Kikyo, However,
Due to the Logans exhausted state the Assassin does not feel that any fight between them would be unfair 
and his moral code forces him to delay the battle once more. At this point Shingen appears on an extendable balcony far above 
and Releases the AIM upgraded Statue robot Vadhaka once more. Kikyo Abstains himself from the battle due to the employment 
of another assassin and sits down to await the outcome as Vadhaka Tries to Crush Wolverine who finds it a much more formidable opponant this time. 
Koh spots Shingen, the son of his Enemy, Kurohagi, and advances but shingen orders Vadhak and his Guards to attack Anyone at the Palace entrance. 
Seeing the Guards open fire on the civilian rebels is too much for Kikyo who Now acts against Shingens men disarming them and Even attacks Vadhaka
After it Strikes Min who is defending a fallen Koh.
 Can Wolverine and Kikyo manage to defeat the statue between them?
