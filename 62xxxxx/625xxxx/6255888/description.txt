Wiki:
Now That's What I Call Music! 63 or Now 63 was released on 10 April 2006. The album is the 63rd edition of the original (UK) Now! series, ending a run that started with the first Now album in 1983. It was also released on Compact Disc and via Digital Download.
Now 63 features five songs which reached number one on the UK Singles Chart. They are "No Tomorrow", "Thunder in My Heart Again", "It's Chico Time", "That's My Goal" and "JCB Song".
