Renoir's look at bourgeois life in France at the onset of World War II. An assorted cast of characters - the rich and their poor servants - meet up at a French chateau.

Director:
Jean Renoir

Writers:
Jean Renoir (scenario & dialogue), Carl Koch (collaborator)

Stars:
Marcel Dalio, Nora Gregor and Paulette Dubost

Dual audio:

#1 Original French audio
#2 Comments in English

Subtitles: English (idx sub); Spanish, Portuguese (srt).
