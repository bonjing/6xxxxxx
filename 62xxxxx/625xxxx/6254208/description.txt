Walmart has traditionally paid hunger-wages to its hourly workers, forcing many of them to rely on welfare to make ends meet; thus, depleting the social services budgets of the communities burdened with a Walmart Store.


This was Walmart's 2004-2010 Hourly Workers pay plan. It is essentially the same as the current one.


Included in the torrent is also a script given by Walmart to all its management and hourly employees as part of the company's attempt to derail then Sen. Obama's presidential bid in 2008.
