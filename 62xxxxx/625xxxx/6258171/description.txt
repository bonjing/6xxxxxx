Puzzle Hero is an addictive and exhilarating puzzle-RPG (role playing game), in which you defeat more than 50 mystical 3D monsters, while you charge through picturesque environments. Hero in you must save your kidnapped brother, Tristan, from the evil spell of sorcerer Zalock, the ruler of Kindgom of Fog, and his Army of Darkness. Outwit the puzzle to battle the mystical monsters guarding the road to Kingdom of Fog. Increase your power by gathering gold and using it to shop for magical amulets, vast selection of weaponry, armor, and clothing accessories.
