Thornapple

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Ruth Rendell; Full cast dramatisation starring Zac Fox, Elizabeth Spriggs, Colleen Prendergast, Juliet Aubrey and Shaun Pye; Adapted by Yvonne Antrobus; Directed by Conor Lennon.

Smitten by poisons, James is captivated by ruthless single mum Mirabel on a visit to her wealthy aunt.
