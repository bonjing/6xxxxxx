Stahlhammer (German for "Steel Hammer") is a Neue Deutsche Harte band from Austria that formed in 1992. They incorporate elements from hardcore, industrial metal and symphonic metal into their songs. The band has to this date produced six albums, where the majority of songs are in German. Their latest album is Opera Noir, which was released in March 2006.
