This is one of Joey Sturgis' solo productions. For those of you who don't know who that is, he's the guy who produces albums for the bands The Devil Wears Prada, We Came as Romans, Asking Alexandria, Emmure, etc.

This is probably the most beautiful experimental piece I've ever listened to.
