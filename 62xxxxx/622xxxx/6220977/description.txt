Minimum System Requirements

- Windows 98/ME/2000/XP
- Intel Pentium III or AMD Athlon 800MHz Processor or Intel Celeron or AMD Duron 1.2GHz Processor
- 128MB RAM
- 4X CD-ROM Drive
- 1.6GB Hard Disk Space
- 32MB DirectX compatible 3D Accelerated Video Card
- DirectX 9

- Windows 98/ME/2000/XP
- Intel Pentium 4 or AMD Athlon XP 1.8GHz Processor
- 256MB RAM
- 16X CD-ROM Drive
- 1.6GB Hard Disk Space
- 64MB DirectX compatible 3D Accelerated Video Card
- DirectX compatible Sound Card with Surround Sound
- DirectX compatible Joypad, Joystick or Wheel
- DirectX 9
