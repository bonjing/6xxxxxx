Hollys World is an American reality-based television series that debuted on E! on December 6, 2009. Initially a one-off special, success in the ratings lead to E! ordering an eight-episode season that began airing on June 13, 2010.

Hollys World follows former Girl Next Door Holly Madison as she sets out to make her mark on the strip. She stars in the Vegas show Peepshow at Planet Hollywood.
