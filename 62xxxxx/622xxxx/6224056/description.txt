The native language of more than 20,000 Eskimo people who live in Southwestern Alaska is presented in this grammar of Central Yup'ik.

This volume is not only a comprehensive textbook for students but also a complete reference guide, and can be used for self-study.

The 24 chapters include vocabulary lists,grammatical explanations with examples and exercises. Annotated Yup'ik readings are keyed to the chapters.

Two cds are included - pronunciation of the Yup'ik sounds and the vocabulary words in the book.
