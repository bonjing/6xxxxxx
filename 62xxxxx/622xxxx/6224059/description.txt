How To Spot A Shill.





When you are dealing with a shill, an agent of
the enemy, or a highly publicized figure who is taking the fight to the top for you,
the number one way to root them out is to watch for the 2% bullshit they throw
out with their message. It's ALWAYS in there. 98% of what they say will be SPOT
ON, accurate as can be. You will think they took the words from your very mouth,
or concede that you couldn't have said it better yourself, but if you are not
careful you will not notice the subterfuge.
