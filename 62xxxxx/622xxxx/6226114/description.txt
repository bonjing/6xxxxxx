High Stakes Poker - Season 7 Episode 1

High Stakes Poker is back for Season 7 with New host, Norm MacDonald.  Sitting at the first table are returning pros Doyle Brunson and Antonio Esfandiari. They are  joined by the 3rd woman in HSP history, Vanessa Selbst, who comes in with over 3 million dollars in career earnings.  The pros are challenged by amateurs Robert Croak, the creator of SillyBandz; Bill Klein, a retired businessman and philanthropist; and billionaire entrepreneur Phil Ruffin, who owns the Treasure Island Hotel and Casino in Las Vegas.

Air Date: 26 February 2011
