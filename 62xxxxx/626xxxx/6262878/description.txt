Sports fanatic Anastacio da Silva goes to Porto with his wife and two children to see a championship match. They stay in the home of the wealthy Barata family, whom they met while on vacation. The Baratas believe that Anastacio and his family are also from the upper class, a situation which is complicated when the Barata's son Eduardo falls in love with Anastacio's daughter Juju. The two decide to marry in Lisbon, forcing Anastacio to maintain the charade, at no small financial expense.
