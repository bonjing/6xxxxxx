From BBC Radio 4. Three short stories written by Stacy Aumonier; Adapted and Abridged by Neville Teller.

The Brown Wallet

read by Mark Heap.

With contrary forces struggling within his mind Giles Meiklejohn takes a penknife to a brown wallet found in the back of a taxi.

A Source of Irritation

read by Mark Addy.

Kidnapped by a crashed German fighter pilot, elderly farmer Sam Gates soon finds himself mistaken for a spy, trapped behind enemy lines and - worst of all - late for his dinner.

The Room

read by Matthew Horne.

Finding himself alone in the world James Wilbraham Waite dreams of escaping The Room when his landlady knocks on the door.
