Lynyrd Skynyrd - Street Survivors [Deluxe Edition] (1977/2008)
---------------------------------

Lynyrd Skynyrd is an American rock band,formed in Jacksonville,
Florida in 1964.

Street Survivors is the fifth studio album by Southern rock band,
Lynyrd Skynyrd,released on October 17, 1977.
