Deadpool has to secure a deadly weapon in the Savage Land. It turns out to be the head of the Marvel Zombies Deadpool with which he teams up to fight Hydra.
 
The cover to each issue is an homage to famous film posters, including Jaws, Scarface, Aliens, and Pretty Woman.
