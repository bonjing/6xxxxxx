Urban Sickness Audio: USA1008

Artist: Tim Besamusca Featuring Lisa Fitzgibbon & Chrysa T
Title: Running Away EP


Tim Besamusca enlists the help of resident USA vocalists Lisa Fitzgibbon and Chrysa T in his latest creation for Urban Sickness Audio.

Spanning Breaks, Dubstep and House, Tim injects his potent fusion of Electro and Trance into the mix to create a vibrant sonic journey of heavy bass lines and sweeping synth textures.
