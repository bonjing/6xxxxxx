Rocco has come home from work because his little sister is worried about her best friend Samantha and wants him to check on her. She is playing a video game in the middle of the day without a care in the world. Rocco tries to get her to start accepting responsibility but she just doesn't want to listen. But she is glad he cares enough about her to come check on her. They hug and then get lost in the moment before doing what they always dreamed about with each other.
