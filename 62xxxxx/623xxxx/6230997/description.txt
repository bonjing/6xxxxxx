This Album released in 2011 by Enty3way is a phenomenal take on sound art and music and revolutionizes the sound of hip hop music today this album takes you on a visionary journey through time and space. The album goes from Bar-b-ques to Child abuse, From a great summer to the mind of a serial killer.
Purchasing this classic album will open up your mind and bend your view of the future.The beats are dynamic, dramatic & epic.
