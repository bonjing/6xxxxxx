A genre-bending tale of a boy trying to fulfill his unrequited love for a girl who has risen 
from the dead. It tells the story of twin brothers Patrick and Carol Darling, newly graduated 
from high school and struggling to come to terms with the mysterious disappearance of their 
friend, the bright and beautiful Wendy Hearst. When a drive through the countryside 
surrounding their suburban community leads to the discovery of Wendy's mysteriously animated 
corpse, the boys secretly transport the un-dead Wendy to an empty house in hopes of somehow 
bringing her back to life. As the sweltering summer pushes on, they must maintain the 
appearance of normalcy for their friends and family as they search for ways to revive the 
Wendy they once knew, or, failing that, to satisfy their own quests for love amongst the 
living and the dead.
