S.. Sasucha... is a story of relationships. It is a thriller which stays around a woman and her deceased mother-in-law.After the marriage the woman and her groom move to their new house which is adjacent to the older one, after which the mother-in-law\'s potrait suddenly shows changing expressions. This is when the woman starts getting visions and dreams which eventually materialize.S..
