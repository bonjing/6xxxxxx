Show Summary:
Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah is a sitcom that airs on SAB TV since 28 July 2008 which airs from Monday to Friday 8:30 pm IST. The show is produced by Neela Telefilms and the series writers are Raju Odedra and Rajen Upadhyay. It is based on the column Duniya Ne Undha Chasma written by Indian columnist and journalist Taarak Mehta. The serial is based in the Gokuldham Society. In August 2010, it beat Baalika Vadhu to become the most watched TV show in India.
