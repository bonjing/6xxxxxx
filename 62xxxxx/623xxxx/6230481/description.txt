Jane Eyre

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Charlotte Bronte; Full cast dramatisation starring Sophie Thompson and Ciaran Hinds; Dramatised by Michelene Wandor; Directed by Cherry Cookson.

Ep.1 - Jane, the Child

Unhappy Jane applies to be a governess at Thornfield Hall and meets Mr Rochester.

Ep. 2 - Jane, the Governess

As she settles in at Thornfield Hall, Jane's feelings grow for charismatic Mr Rochester.

Ep. 3 - Jane, the Fugitive

The wedding plans of Mr Rochester spark bewilderment for governess Jane.

Ep. 4 - Jane, the Wife

Jane settles into village life - but still she thinks of Mr Rochester.
