Dreadful Day of Judgement

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Ruth Rendell; Full cast dramatisation starring Danny Sapani, Bryan Dick, Ralph Ineson and Stuart McLoughlin; Adapted by Yvonne Antrobus; Directed by Conor Lennon.

Three lost souls are thrown together over the Christmas period when they find themselves clearing up an abandoned graveyard. As time goes on, their personal demons loom larger, and something eventually has to give.
