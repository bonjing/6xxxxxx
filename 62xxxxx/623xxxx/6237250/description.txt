  of the DFX2 gameplay, from the enhanced ballistics to the innovative 
  in-vehicle spawn system, to be carefully coordinated to support a 
  fully-balanced experience where teamwork is deadlier than individual 
  firepower, and in DFX2, intelligence and creativity is rewarded as much
  as fast-twitch reflexes.
