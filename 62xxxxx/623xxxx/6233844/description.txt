A great Super mario 64 hack pack with over 30 roms including not released yet roms in beta of completely finished!

To open in project64 you need to configure your emulator like that:

1.open project 64
2.load your rom
3.go to settings
4.check unhide advanced settings and close and reopen settings
5.goto rom settings and change RDRAM size to 8mb
6.reload the rom
7.you`re done!!
