Oracle Open Office 3.3.1
Based on OpenOffice.org
Contains: main installer (version 3.3.0 build 9552) + service pack 1 (updates the product to version 3.3.1 build 9572).

After Sun was bought by Oracle, Sun StarOffice 9 was renamed to Oracle Open Office 3. It's a modified, non-free version of OpenOffice.org.

This release includes setup files for Solaris (x86, SPARC), Linux (x86), MacOS X (x86) and Windows (x86).

Multilanguage version (ar, de, en-US, es, fr, hu, it, ja, ko, nl, pl, pt, pt-BR, ru, sv, zh-CN, zh-TW).
