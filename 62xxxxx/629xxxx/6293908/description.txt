Ancient Metal Religious Tablets

Date: 04-02-11 
Host: Ian Punnett 
Guests: David Elkington, Charles M. Higgins 

Appearing in the middle two hours, egyptologist and author David Elkington discussed an extraordinary new find of ancient metal religious tablets that could change the world's view of biblical history (related article). 

In the first hour, scientist Dr. Charles Higgins, who is on a quest to build intelligent machines with help from insects, revealed the science behind the new film Source Code. 

The final hour of the program featured Open Lines.
