Eng

Like the original version of the game, Counter-Strike: Source team confront each other counter-terrorists (CT, Special Forces) and a team of terrorists (T) in a series of rounds.
Each round is won by performing a specific mission (such as the undermining of a bomb or rescuing hostages) or after the destruction of all members of the opposing team.
Gameplay is similar to that of the Counter-Strike. Most of the weapons are also identical weapons in Counter-Strike 1.6, with the exception of the shield, which was available to counter-terrorists. In later versions, buying rounds was carried out automatically in the early rounds, and when buying new weapons. Cartridges are now free, just give full ammo for buying weapons, they can also be returned to fill the place of purchase.
