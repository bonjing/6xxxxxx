Mercado, the March 2011 release, is the epitome of elegant eCommerce integration, sporting three shopping solutions; in addition to its refined visuals, style and structure.
The Mercado theme has a unique design with a dedicated sidepanel, with more prominent styling, perfect to act as a focal point. Built on the Gantry Framework, Mercado features a whole range of controls over its layout and design elements, allowing for easy use and swift modification.
