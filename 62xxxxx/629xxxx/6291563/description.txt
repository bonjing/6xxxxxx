1964 saw the birth of a very British institution. Spanning over four decades, 
Top of the Pops has produced many classic moments in pop culture.

Digging deep within the darkest depths of the BBC's archive, this compilation 
offers some memorable performances from 1964 through to 1975 from the likes of 
the Rolling Stones, Tom Jones, Status Quo, Procol Harum, Stevie Wonder, 
Queen and the Kinks, and opening the vintage vaults to rare performances 
from Stealers Wheel, Julie Driscoll, Peter Sarstedt and the Seekers.

So sit back and witness once again where music met television..
