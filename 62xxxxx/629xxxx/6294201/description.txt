Discover Arabia!!

The adventure continues. Following hot on the heels of Around The World in 80 Flights and Pole to Pole the odyssey of exploration reaches the sun drenched Arabian Peninsula and the Middle East. Discover Arabia puts you in the pilot seat with a series of flights in this expansion pack for Flight Simulator 2004 and Flight Simulator X that unveil the exotic mystery and unforgettable vistas of some of the most dramatic landscapes on Earth. With your own personal aircraft, route and itinerary provided by us you need only pack your imagination for this once in a lifetime experience!
