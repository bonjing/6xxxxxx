Visual Dictionary in 5 languages: English, French, German, Spanish, Italian
Publisher: De Agostini | 2006 | PDF | 400 pages | 622 Mb

This unique and effective learning tool features more than 6,000 objects and situations of everyday vocabulary, using more than 1600 images and 
to provide the translations in five languages. Easy to use and nice to browse, the Visual Dictionary in five languages and is ideal for students, 
tourists, traders and all those who want immediate access to languages.
