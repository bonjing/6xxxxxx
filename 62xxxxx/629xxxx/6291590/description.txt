Platform: WII
Genre: Educational Adventure
Developer: Black Lantern Studios
Origin: PAL
Languages: English

Resumen:
Ni Hao, Kai-lan combines educational elements of learning Chinese language and culture with game play and action, and features familiar voice actors from the animated show. Kids can play alone or with a friend, and enjoy 10 fun-filled mini games, including: Butterfly Catching, Kite Flying, Dragon Boat Racing, Bubble Popping, Badminton, Soccer Challenge and more.
