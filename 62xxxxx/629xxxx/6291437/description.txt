The DVD in the deluxe package includes the video to the first single "I Die For You Today" and a documentary titled "Audiovision" which is there in a normal version and also in a 3D version. The band held an album release party, where they played a short unplugged set, at the Quasimodo Club in Berlin on the evening of 18th November, 2010 to which their closest fans and friends were invited.
