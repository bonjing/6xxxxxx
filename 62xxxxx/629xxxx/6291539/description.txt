James Ellis reads five original French stories adapted to Irish settings.

From BBC Radio 7. Recorded in front of a live audience at The Ulster Folk and Transport Museum.

Episode 1 - The Devil

Old Granny Greer outwits a miserly son by striking a deal with his ailing mother.

Episode 2 - Scully's Goat

What befalls an unsuspecting goat when she escapes to pursue her freedom?

Episode 3 - My Uncle Julius

The Davenport family's boat fails to come in, in more ways than one.

Episode 4 - A Miller's Tale

A priest goes to desperate lengths to entice his erring flock back to the fold.

Episode 5 - The Umbrella

When a man's new umbrella is ruined, his wife cunningly replaces it within budget.
