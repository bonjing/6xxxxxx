YoWindow is a weather application. A new kind of a weather application. YoWindow displays weather conditions over a nice and peaceful landscape. So you can watch the weather looking at the landscape. Naturally - just like looking out of your window.




INSTRUCTIONS :-


1) AFTER DOWNLOADS COMPLETE,OPEN THE FOLDER

2) CLICK ON YOWINDOW.EXE AND INSTALL THE APPLICATION,USING ON-SCREEN INSTRUCTION.

3) AFTER INSTALLATION COMPLETES,OPEN THE APPLICATION

4) CLICK ON HELP AND SELECT REGISTER TO REGISTER THE SOFTWARE.

5) COPY THE SERIAL IN SERIAL.TXT

6) ENJOY!!!!!
