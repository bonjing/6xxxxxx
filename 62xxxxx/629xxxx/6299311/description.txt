Info: 
Power Video Cutter is a digital video converter/cutter. The main function of Power Video Cutter is to convert video files from one format to another and cut a part of file into new video file. The program supports AVI, MPEG-1/2, DVD/SVCD/VCD, DAT, ASF, WMV, Divx, xVid, MOV, MP4, M4V, MPA, 3GP, MKV, RM, RMVB, FLV, SWF video formats. 


System Requirements 

* Operating System Microsoft Windows 98/Me/NT/2000/XP/2003/Vista 
* DirectX 8.1 or higher 
* QuickTime Player (if you want convert mov, mp4, 3gp files) 
* Real Player (if you need convert rm, rmvb file)
