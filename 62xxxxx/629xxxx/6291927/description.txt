From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Ian Fleming; Full cast dramatisation starring Imogen Stubbs, Alex Jennings and Eric Potts; Dramatised by Sherry Ashworth.

Ever seen a car that can fly? Swim? Think for itself? Well, neither have the Pott family - until the magical Chitty Chitty Bang Bang comes into their lives. But dangerous times lay ahead. And when the Pott family comes up against Joe the Monster and his henchmen, they desperately need help.
