Oneira

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Robert Easby; Full cast dramatisation starring Lyndsey Marshal and Peter Marinker; Directed by Liz Webb.

Episode 1 - The Big Chill

A museum guard's quest to learn the secrets of the Universe with a 400-year-old man.

Episode 2 - Sleight of Mind

An Electricity Company official interrogates the museum guard, but is he an alien?

Episode 3 - A String of Time

The museum guard ends up in the Texan desert with a dead comic and a 13th century friar.

Episode 4 - The Thing with Two Cappuccinos

The museum guard is chased by multiplying android coffee bar staff.

Episode 5 - Mind the Gap

The museum guard ends up literally talking to herself, and finds her other self very rude.
