Season two: 1984

 8 "Servant of Evil" Jeffrey Scott November 5, 1983 (1983-11-05)

Right on his birthday, Bobby's friends are kidnapped by Venger and brought to a dungeon which is virtually escape-proof. His only hope of rescuing his friends is a person who has been coerced into becoming Venger's gaoler (prison guard). 
__________

 9 "Quest of the Skeleton Warrior" Buzz Dixon November 12, 1983 (1983-11-12)

A war-bird riding knight cursed into undeath seeks out the five children to recover an ancient artifact from the tower of his old order. But inside the tower, the children must confront and overcome their greatest fears. 
__________

10 "The Garden of Zinn" Jeffrey Scott November 19, 1983 (1983-11-19)

During a fight with a dragon creature, Bobby is fatally poisoned. The only cure can be found in the garden of Queen Zinn, who is willing to give it to them, provided that Eric becomes her husband. But this request is not as innocent as it seems. 
__________

11 "The Box" Jeffrey Scott November 26, 1983 (1983-11-26)

A sorceress friend of Dungeon Master possesses a chest which could bring the children back home, but once again Venger does not want the children to leave his grasp. 
__________

12 "The Lost Children" Jeffrey Scott December 3, 1983 (1983-12-03)

The five children encounter another band of children stranded in the Realm as they are. Their father possesses a ship which could take them home, but he is now Venger's prisoner, and the children must infiltrate his stronghold to bail the man out. 
__________

13 "P-R-E-S-T-O Spells Disaster" Jeffrey Scott December 10, 1983 (1983-12-10)

Caught in a giant's castle, the children's only hope for freedom is Presto, who has eluded capture but is still as bumbling with his magic hat as ever. In aiding his friends Presto also rescues the young of one of the few remaining good dragons left in the realm. 
__________

14 "The Girl Who Dreamed Tomorrow" Karl Geurs September 8, 1984 (1984-09-08)

The children find a cart from the Dungeons and Dragons ride and attempt to use it to return home. Before they can board it, they find a young girl named Terri who rode the cart into the realm. The girl happens to have a magical necklace which contains power that can predict the future through dreams. The children set out to find a magical portal found at the end of a deadly maze that can take them all home. 
__________
