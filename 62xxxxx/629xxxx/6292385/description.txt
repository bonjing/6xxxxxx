The Falcon is a Chicago-based punk rock supergroup. The band features The Lawrence Arms members Brendan Kelly (guitar and vocals) and Neil Hennessy on drums, as well as Alkaline Trio's Dan Andriano (vocals and bass). Todd Mohney, former member of Rise Against, played guitar on the 2004 EP but could not attend the recording session for the band's first full length Unicornography[1], and his spot was temporarily filled in by Kelly and Hennessy.

Included are the following:

2004: God Don't Make No Trash -or- Up Your Ass With Broken Glass 
2006: Unicornography
