
Mind Power
----------
Date: 03-12-2011
Host: Ian Punnett

Guests: Dr. Andrew Newberg, Patrick Hammer, James Chiles


Ian Punnett welcomed neuroscientist Andrew Newberg for a discussion on how meditation and prayer help memory and improve the aging brain.

In the first hour, meteorologist Patrick Hammer estimated how radioactive fallout could potentially spread from Japan across the planet. Next, researcher James Chiles addressed worst case scenarios should a full meltdown occur at the damaged Japanese nuclear power plant.
