Olalla

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Robert Louis Stevenson; Full cast dramatisation starring Paul Blair, Richard Green, Simon Tait and Carol Ann Crawford; Dramatised by Marty Ross.

During the Peninsular War in Spain, a wounded Scottish soldier is sent to stay in a remote house in the hills. The soldier falls in love with the daughter of the house, but the family harbours a terrible secret.

Part of The Darker Side of the Border series.
