The famed live jam session by two of the greatest musicians ever to have played the blues on electric guitar - Albert King and Stevie Ray Vaughan! This video footage has never-before been available until now!

Although it never aired, this jam session between the legendary blues guitarists Stevie Ray Vaughan and Albert King was recorded in 1983 for the Canadian music show IN SESSION.
