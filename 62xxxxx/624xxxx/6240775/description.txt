Her physical beauty is stunning and her voice is the champion of two San Remo Festivals. Her best tracks are all assembled on this stellar CD. Included are both the original version of "E Poi" and a previously unreleased verson as well as the previously unreleased "Marzo" (March). This is Italy's fastest selling release of 2002.
