FLAC / LOSSLESS

Britney Spears releases her seventh studio album titled Femme Fatale. The iconic global superstars title Femme Fatale is a tribute to bold, empowered, confident, elusive, fun, flirty women and men. Its not a conceptual album, Spears is letting the music speak for itself. Shes worked hard on it for two years and she considers it to be her best album to date, true to where she is now personally and professionally.
