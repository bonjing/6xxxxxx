So here it is. 12th edition of the Best Of House series bringing you more mind blowing tracks from the top of the top. I got all nostalgic during compilation of this edition, so you'll find quite a few Acid House/Techno and early Progressive and Hard House tracks in it, besides the decent selection of Jackin and Disco House and here and there a touch of Tech-House. As is mostly the case, most tracks in 320kbps or V2, with few older ones in 192kbps. Enjoy and don't forget to support the artists!
