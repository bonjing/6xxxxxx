The Captain of the Polestar

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Arthur Conan Doyle; Full cast dramatisation starring Alec Heggie, Nick Underwood, Brian Pettifer and James Bryce; Dramatised by Marty Ross.

A Dundee whaling ship becomes trapped in the arctic. Strange cries sound across the ice at night and a figure is glimpsed on the floes. Is it the Captain's lost love come to reclaim him?

Part of The Darker Side of the Border series.
