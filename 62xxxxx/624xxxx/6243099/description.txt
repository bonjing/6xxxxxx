.hack//Quantum is an OVA series, part of the .hack conglomerate.
 The series will consist of three episodes and is being presented by Bandai Visual company. 
Animation is produced by Kinema Citrus (Tokyo Magnitude 8.0) with director Masaki Tachibana 
(Tokyo Magnitude 8.0, Noir) taking charge of the animation. .hack// writer Tatsuya Hamazaki 
is writing the OVAs and video game and anime composer Ko Otani (Shadow of the Colossus, 
Blade of the Immortal) is composing the music for the series. CC2 confirmed that Quantum 
is not related to the CGI movie tie-in 
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Synopsis:
---------------

Episode 2 "wired Prisoners"

Hermit replaces his list of player details with an updated copy, while studying the PKed players he found inside 
the black hole he fell into he activates the original copy which Isumi/Sukaya had picked up.The list leads Isumi and Lori to Tokyo and 
a meeting with an NAB investigator looking into the mysterious coma cases linked to "The World", she turns out to be the Mage leader of the guild 
which had placed a bounty on Sakuya. The investigator tells them about her involvement in another incident where children were trapped inside "The World"
but in a coma in real life 5 years before and shows them some of the people known to have been stuck wandering "the World" in other incidents 
She Explains how CC Corp, the creators of "the World" are covering up these coma cases by moving them all to their own private hospitals, 
Isumi's friend Iri among them, and of how the original creator of "The World" dreamt of a utopian society where mankind were all equal in a virtual world.
Hermit reunites with Sakuya but neither mentions their knowledge of the list and the next day Hermit spots a meeting 
of Isumi, Lori and the Investigators guild in which they discuss the reason for the list and it's owner and diclose some bad news at which point he leaves.
As they are explaining that the list is a search for a donor match for a transplant a match is found and suddenly chaos erupts.
