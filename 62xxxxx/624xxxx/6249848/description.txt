This e-book contains both of the famous literary works of Jerome K. Jerome. The books "Three Men In A Boat" and its sequel "Three Men On A Bummel" are present in this e-book.

Three Men in a Boat (To Say Nothing of the Dog), published in 1889, is a humorous account by Jerome K. Jerome of a boating holiday on the Thames between Kingston and Oxford.

Three Men on the Bummel(also known as Three Men on Wheels) is a humorous novel by Jerome K. Jerome. It was published in 1900, eleven years after his most famous work, Three Men in a Boat (To Say Nothing of the Dog).The three companions this time is on a bicycle tour through the German Black Forest.
