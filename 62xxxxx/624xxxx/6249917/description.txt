"Jam (Turn It Up)" is a single released by American television personality Kim Kardashian. The song is for charity purposes and Kim confirmed that she will not be releasing any albums. The song was first premiered on March 2, 2011 during On Air with Ryan Seacrest. The song was also released to iTunes on March 2, 2011, a portion of the proceeds from the sales of the song will be donated to St. Jude Children's Research Hospital.[3] The single made its television premiere during the March 6 episode of Kourtney and Kim Take New York.
