Naughty Rich Girls - Lily Carter 

Actors: Lily Carter, Xander Corvus

Release Date: January 07, 2011

Lily and Xander are supposed to be going to a big year end party for their second date together. Heading to the garage she discovers her purple Lamborghini is not there! Her assistant was to have it washed and waxed, and it's not back. Being the spoiled rich girl that she is, Lily has a complete meltdown, and rejects every idea Xander has about alternate transportation. Finally agreeing to a limo, he calls to arrange it, and then has to deliver the news that it's going to be 90 minutes before it will arrive, whereupon she has another meltdown. It may only be their second date, but Xander figures 
the best way to kill some time, and get this rich bitch smiling is to get it on. Luckily for him she finally agrees to one of his ideas and leading him back into the house, they start their second date the best way possible, sex first!
