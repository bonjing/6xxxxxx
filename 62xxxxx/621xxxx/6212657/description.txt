Psysive Presents: FS2004: Eiresim Dublin EIDW (RIP)

Dublin is the result of 2 years of development and is the most realistic rendition of the airport to date. Attention to detail has been the key behind developing Dublin Airport and all recent structures have been included with the scenery (e.g. all major developments including Pier D, Pier E and the new Terminal 2). Updates are released on a regular basis also which allows for the most up-to-date and realistic experiance when flying into one of Europes largest, and busiest airports. 

Scenery Features
Docking system for 6 aircraft type.
Highly detailed virtual replica of the airport.
Both animated and static ground vehicles.
Airport Enhancement Services (AES).
Impressive frame rates.
Photo realistic day and night textures.
Realistic runway and taxiway lighting.
3-D Approach lights.
3-D Papi lights.
Realistic Navigation aids.
Realistic Ground Markings.
Extensive manual with information about the airport.
Afcad included.

System requirements for FS2004
Microsoft Flight Simulator 2004 (Version 9.1)
Pentium 2, 6 GHz 1 GB RAM (2 GB recommended)
Graphic card with 256 MB RAM
