
My Friends Hot Mom - Claudia Marie 

Actors: Claudia Marie, Michael Vegas

Release Date: January 07, 2011

Michael has come over to his buddy's house after coming home from his family vacation. Not to see his buddy, but in order to give a gift of English tea to his buddy's mom, Claudia. Claudia is super excited, because she never gets any gifts or presents, and thanks Michael for thinking of her. Michael admits that he thinks about Claudia all the time. She blushes, then decides to give Michael a present of her own.
