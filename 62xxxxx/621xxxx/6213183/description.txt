Avril Lavigne's fourth studio album, Goodbye Lullaby, is set for release by RCA Records on March 8, 2011. Working with longtime collaborators Deryck Whibley, Evan Taubenfeld and Butch Walker, as well as songwriter/producer Max Martin, Lavigne continues to share her personal experiences through her writing and music with a more organic and raw sound. Lavigne also wrote the song Alice for Tim Burton's critically acclaimed Disney Film Alice in Wonderland.
