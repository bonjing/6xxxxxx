Autograph was an American hard rock band from Los Angeles, California. Active on RCA from 1984 to 1989, they sold 1.5 million records and were awarded gold with their "Sign In Please" album. Sign In Please 1984


Sign In Please is the 1984 debut album by the American rock band Autograph. The album featured their signature song, "Turn Up the Radio," and their other minor hit, "Send Her to Me," both of which had accompanying videos.

"Turn Up the Radio" was featured in the opening credits of the 2010 movie Hot Tub Time Machine, the video game Grand Theft Auto: Vice City as well as the Obsidian RPG, Alpha Protocol, while "All I'm Gonna Take" was featured in Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Stories.
