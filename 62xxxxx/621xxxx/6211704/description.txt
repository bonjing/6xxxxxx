Waiting for "Superman" is a 2010 documentary film from director Davis Guggenheim and 

producer Lesley Chilcott.[2] The film analyzes the failures of American public 

education by following several students through the educational system.

The film received the Audience Award for best documentary at the 2010 Sundance Film 

Festival.[3]The film also received the Best Documentary Feature at the Critics' 

Choice Movie Awards in a tightly fought competition.
