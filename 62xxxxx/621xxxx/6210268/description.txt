Here's a terrific collection of rare audio by the Monty Python team,
including some early work which doesn't otherwise exist on video.
[And some that does.]

These are all audio only!
Most are MP3s, some are AC3 and AVI audiofiles.

Including:

Hastily Cobbled Together For a Fast Buck- Unreleased Python Album
[Rudyard Kipling, Otto Suicide Song]

Eric Idle's Radio 5 [Nine Episodes 1973-1974]
Sketch comedy and music with Eric Idle and Lyn Ashley.
A precursor to Rutland Weekend Television.

At Last the 1948 Show- Many rare clips from otherwise lost episodes,
rare episode soundtracks and the album
[John Cleese, Graham Chapman, Marty Feldman, Tim Brooke-Taylor,
Aimi MacDonald]

The Frost Report - many full episodes, many otherwise missing
[About 22 full episodes with John Cleese]

Out of the Trees [Graham Chapman, Douglas Adams, poor quality]

A Liar's Autobiography Audiobook - Graham Chapman

Graham Chapman - Six Pack of Lies

The Pythons By the Pythons Autobiography by Bob McCabe

Bonus Tracks from Recent Album Rereleases
[Teach Yourself Heath, Courtroom Sketch]

Do Not Adjust Your Set - Many Bonzo Dog Band Performances
and Rare Episode Soundtracks [Michael Palin, Eric Idle, Terry Jones]

same backing tracks.
