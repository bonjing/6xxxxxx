cary grant and constance bennett star as george and marion kerby, a fun loving couple who find themselves in limbo as ghosts after an unfortunate car accident. deciding that heaven is just one good deed away, they decide to turn their attention toward their good friend cosmo topper. but topper is one stodgy banker, and it'll take all the high jinks the kerbys can muster to haunt topper into loosening up and living it up. b/w 1937
