A re-imagining of the cult classic British science fiction series The Prisoner, a man known only as Six (James Caviezel) awakes to find himself captive in an enigmatic locale called the Village. The administrator of the Village, Two (Sir Ian McKellen), is determined to crack him in order to gain access to the knowledge which he possess. However, Six is just as determined to escape.
