Full Movies Used

Nightmare On Elm Street 1 and 7
Friday the 13th 2009
Freddy vs Jason

I went a little out with this edit initially when i started it wanted to make it a freddy vs pinhead vs jason but as my memory refreshed hellraiser are more
psychological horrors so i had to custom make a few scenes with pinhead and the cinobytes throughout the movies chasing and watching freddy so scenes from all 8 hellraiser movies
have been used and scenes from NMOES 4.5 and 2

Now on to edit starts of with NMOES 1 and friday the 13th running side by side then continues on to NMOES 7 from whcih i have completely removed the element
that NMOES 7 showed freddy was fictional and a movie so it continues on as nancy growing up and freddy haunting her again.Then moves on to freddy vs jason
a lot of cut scenes have been added through out freddy vs jason with pinhead and i have added a custom ending so the original ending of freddy vs jason where jason rises out of the lake
with freddy's head is gone i used the alternate ending with a scene that i made image per image you will notice all the cut scenes i have added through out edit as you will see pinhead
a lot of times kicking freddy's ass and vice versa.Enjoy if you are old school true horror fan.
