 http://www.bootmed.com/

BootMed is a Live CD that was made to help those not familiar with Linux to repair or recover their Windows PC from a Live CD.  BootMed comes with many different kinds of tools that can help you: fix virus problems, undelete files, recover your pictures and documents, image damaged drives, etc.  More importantly it comes with step by step instructions complete with screen shots, that are meant to guide the average computer user through repair and recovery.
