This is the official Mercedes-Benz Model 140 STAR Classic Service Manual Library DVD Part Number P-2700-140-04

How to use the W140 official workshop manual:
This DVD is only compatible with Adobe Reader 9 or earlier
1) Extract "W140 SCL.ISO" from "W140 SCL.7z" using 7zip or Winrar
2) Burn or mount "W140 SCL.ISO" (you can't just extract files, PDFs have a DRM)
3) Copy the Ops.api file in the Acrobat\Reader\plug_ins\ folder of the DVD to your plug_ins folder of your Reader (usually in C:\Program Files\Adobe\Reader 9.0\Reader\plug_ins or C:\Program Files (x86)\Adobe\Reader 9.0\Reader\plug_ins if you've got a 64 bits edition of Windows)
4) To open the ETM (schematics) you will need to download and install Autodesk DWF Viewer (free)
5) To start the manual, just launch the Classic\home.htm file on the DVD
(Keep the DVD mounted/in your drive, else PDFs files will ask you a password)
