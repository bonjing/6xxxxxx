Story
====== 
Team Prime live on Earth as a pre-emptive measure against the return of the Decepticons,
 forming an alliance with three teens as Megatron returns having discovered a rare element 
that grants him rule of life itself. The show delves into what it is to be a Prime, 
and though in continuity with Exodus and War for Cybertron, its Earth-bound backstory 
is reminiscent of the live-action film series.

The Autobots go in search of Cliffjumper when his life signal suddenly reappears, 
unaware that Megatron used the dangerous Dark Energon to momentarily revive Cliffjumper before 
striking the reanimated husk down. Arriving to an Energon mine, 
the Autobots fight their way through Eradicons before finding the still-living remains of 
Cliffjumper in his final moments and later escaping when Starscream rigs the mine to blow
. After going through disinfection after her horrific experience, with Ratchet getting a sample of 
their fallen comrade's strange fluid, Arcee takes Jack home to serve as his guardian should the Decepticons attack him.
 While Ratchet unknowingly bring a machine to life with the strange fluid, 
Megatron stabs himself with a shard of Dark Energon to master its power.

First Appearances: Agent Fowler, June Darby
