
The film shows part of the history of skateboarding in Brazil, focusing on the early '90s, when the skate industry collapsed after an huge economic crisis. Motivated by love for the sports, friends, teenagers at the time, joined forces and released a home video that would change their lives forever. The tape was called "Dirty Money ". The video was an instant hit, traveled the country, and inspired thousands of young people, becoming the cornerstone for the reconstruction of skateboarding as a sport and lifestyle in Brazil. Formed one important names like Fabio Cristiano, Alexandre Vianna, Marcio Tarobinha, Bob Burnquist, among others.
