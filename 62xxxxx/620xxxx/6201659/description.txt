F&P InfoSmart software for F&P Icon CPAP machines


Installation:

1. Close all open programs before installation. Note: You must be logged in as a local administrator
2. Place the CD in your CD-ROM drive, and follow the on-screen instructions.
3. If installation does not start automatically, open InfoSmart.exe from the CD.
4. Follow the on-screen instructions.
5. Open up the F&P InfoSmart Client icon from the computer's desktop.
Login to F&P InfoSmart with Username: Admin and Password: password.
