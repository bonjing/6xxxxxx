Plot Summary:
 Next in the four-part Marvel Anime project and broadcasts after the Iron Man anime. Wolverine is a mutant,
possessing animal-keen senses, enhanced psychical 
capabilities, three retracting bone claws on each hand and a healing factor that allows him to recover from virtually 
any wound, disease or toxin at a accelerated rate

The first episode of the series brings us to a time where Logan is drawn back to Japan to rescue Mariko from her father.
At this point in his history, it's to set up his great Japan 
adventure that helped truly define the character in the 80's where he gained his concepts of honor and family. 


------------------------------------------------

Episode 7 is Titled Statue~Vadhaka

 Logan and Min try to make their way through the city of Madripoor
but Kurohagi has activated a giant robot statue called Vadhaka and ordered it to kill Logan. 
They also have to deal with the many loacal thugs who are all out to claim the reward for killing 
The famous Wolverine. Yukio catches up with the pair and aids them in evading the giant but is injured and 
left behind when Min leads Logan into the Underground world of the Madripoor resistance fighters.
The leader of the Resistance Tells Logan the History of his fued with Kurohagi and the involvement of Shingen and reveals 
his relationship to Yukio.He then asks Logan to aid him in the Attack on Kurohagi's HQ 
which they have planned to coincide with the wedding celebations.
