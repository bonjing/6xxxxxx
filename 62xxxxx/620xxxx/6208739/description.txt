Bob Gale screenwriter of classic comedies like BACK TO THE FUTURE and
USED CARS directed this unique comic fantasy. In it James Marsden stars as
Neal a young man freshly graduated from high school and unsure of what to do
with his life. Torn between his fathers vision of himself and his own budding
dreams Neal gets a push in a strange direction when he suffers a blow to the
head and begins hallucinating. Sent on a journey by a mysterious man named
Ray Neal drives down a nonexistent highway to deliver a package only to have
his ideas of reality turned inside out.
