Airlines is a game that teaches the gamer the art of the Airline industry, the game is for anyone not necessarily for those interested in air travel. The gamer can fly some of the most sophisticated airliner jets in the world and use the controls to fly around some of the most wonderful locations around the world. Gamers can experience flight on the Boeing 738 as well as trying their hand at the airbus.
Some of the most wonderful locations that the gamer is likely to visit include alicante in Spain, Tenerife as part of the canary islands and Puerto plate in the beautiful Caribbean.
