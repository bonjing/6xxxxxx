Who You Are was one of the most anticipated releases of 2011 due to Jessie J winning the Critics Choice award at the 2011 Brit Awards and topping the BBC s Sound of 2011 poll. On Twitter, Jessie J confirmed that it took her 6 years to completely finish the album - it was completed on 19 January 2011. The song "Casualty of Love" was released to the US iTunes store on 22 February 2011, but has yet to be confirmed as an official single.
