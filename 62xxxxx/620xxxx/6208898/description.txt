Roman mythology is based heavily on the Greek myths, & the two cultures share many of the same gods & goddesses, under different names. However, some Roman myths are based on real, historical figures. This book includes stories from the complex culture that was ancient Rome. Each chapter is followed by a Question & Answer section which covers themes, symbols & characters; & Expert Commentary which makes for great discussion. Wonderful illustrations placed in the text tell the stories as well.-- These books explore myths of different cultures and expand the reader's mind with fascinating stories and useful historic background.
-- Every chapter is enhanced with a question and answer section and expert commentary by noted scholars.
