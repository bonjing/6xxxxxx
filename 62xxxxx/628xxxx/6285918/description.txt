2010 three CD collection. BGT
Americana traces the history of
Americana, from its origins as a
catch-all term for US roots based
folk, country, soul, rhythm & blues
& rock & roll to its definition
today as alt-country. Features
tracks from many of the key
American recording artists of the
20th and early 21st century such
as: Wilco, The Band, Steve Earle,
Waco Brothers, Meat Puppets, Aretha
Franklin, Al Green, Emmylou Harris,
Johnny Cash, Odetta, Willie Nelson,
Blind Willie Johnson, Leadbelly &
Woody Guthrie Compiled by Max
Reinhardt - presenter of BBC Radio
3's Late Junction & the compiler of
the Americana-defining albums
Beyond Nashville', Way Beyond
Nashville' & Further Beyond
Nashville'
