Collection of Windows NT4 most essential software - latest versions available.

Most of it requires having Service Pack 6 installed.

Contains:

DirectX 5 (From Windows 2000 beta, never officially released for NT4, works !)
ATI drivers (Both official and not, for all Radeon cards up to X850)
nVidia drivers (Official, for all cards from TNT 2 to GeForce 6800)
Internet Explorer 5.5 SP2 (Standalone installer)
Internet Explorer 6 SP1 (Very latest, but requires internet connection)
Mozilla Firefox 2.0.0.11
Adobe Reader 6
Windows Media Player 6.4
Windows Media Player 7

Get the most of your NT4 :) !
