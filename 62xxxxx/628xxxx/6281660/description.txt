Disney animators take on the classic Grimm Brothers story of Rapunzel (Mandy Moore), a long-locked 
beauty imprisoned in a secluded tower by evil hag Mother Gothel (Donna Murphy), who needs the 
rejuvenating powers of Rapunzel's tresses to remain young. When a bandit on the lam (Zachary Levi) 
helps Rapunzel escape, the old crone plots to recapture her and end her budding romance with the 
thief in this Golden Globe nominee for Best Animated Feature.
