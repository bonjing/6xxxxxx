Trance Fix - Afterhours.FM End of Year Countdown 2010 - Day 1

==================================================================================
Afterhours.FM End of Year Countdown hosts 300 DJ's

Misc: Afterhours.fm keep up their annual online festival which hosts 300 livesets from the top jocks on their online radio, over a period of 15 days to enclosed this year.
 
In December of 2007, AH.FM was the first internet radio station to organize an event of such magnitude that brought together 180 of the worlds best Djs in an event that lasted 7 days.

This year AH.FM is back for the third time with a line-up of the best 300 DJ's from all corners of the world in an event that will stretch over 15 days, from December 18th until January 1st 2010.
==================================================================================
