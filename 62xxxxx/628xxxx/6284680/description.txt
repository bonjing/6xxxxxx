Dungeons & Dragons is an animated television series based on TSR's Dungeons & Dragons role-playing game. A co-production of Marvel Productions and TSR, the show was popular in the United States and ran for three seasons on CBS. The show's story editors were Hank Saroyan (who voice directed the series) and Steve Gerber, both of whom contributed episodes and had a firm hand in the writing of the series.
__________

01 "The Night of No Tomorrow" Mark Evanier 	September 17, 1983 (1983-09-17)

Right after their arrival in the Realm, the children encounter Merlin the Magician and learn how he saved the town of Helix from a herd of marauding dragons. 
Presto decides to become Merlin's apprentice, but in his attempt to find a spell which could send his friends and himself home, he unleashes the dragons upon Helix once more.
__________
 
02 "The Eye of the Beholder" Hank Saroyan September 24, 1983 (1983-09-24)

Dungeon Master tasks the children to bring down the deadly Beholder. Their only ally in the struggle is Sir John, a cowardly knight who is soon blackmailed by Venger into leading the children into a trap.
__________
 
03 "The Hall of Bones" Paul Dini October 1, 1983 (1983-10-01)

While fending off another of Venger's attacks, the children's weapons inexplicably fail, their only hope of recharging them lies in the Hall of Bones, an ancient tomb for the bravest of warriors. 

But Venger learns of their plans to reach the tomb and tries his best to get his prospective prizes into his hands.
__________
 
04 "Valley of the Unicorns" Paul Dini & Karl Geurs October 8, 1983 (1983-10-08)

An evil wizard named Kelek seeks the powers of the Unicorns for his own use. 

Uni is kidnapped by him, and the children must hurry to save their little protegee.
___________
 
05 "In Search of the Dungeon Master" Jeffrey Scott October 15, 1983 (1983-10-15)

Evil warrior Warduke manages to capture Dungeon Master, and the children set out to free him before he is delivered to Venger.
__________
 
06 "The Beauty and the Bogbeast" Jeffrey Scott October 22, 1983 (1983-10-22)

The children encounter a band of enslaved humanoids called Bogbeasts, and Eric of all people must lead them to freedom.
__________
 
07 "Prison Without Walls" 	Steve Gerber 	October 29, 1983 (1983-10-29)

The children stumble upon gnome village enslaved by Venger and his orcs and must free their sorcerer. 

However, Venger has the gnome sorcerer captured within a prison from which there is no apparent escape.
