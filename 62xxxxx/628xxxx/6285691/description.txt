The soundtrack to director Steve Barron's 1984 comedy starring Lenny Von Dohlen, Virginia Madsen and the voice of Bud Cort. Released on Virgin, it features the hit "Together In Electric Dreams" by Human League vocalist Philip Oakey and soundtrack deity Giorgio Moroder, plus nine other tracks, including cuts by E.L.O.'s Jeff Lynne, Culture Club & Heaven 17.
