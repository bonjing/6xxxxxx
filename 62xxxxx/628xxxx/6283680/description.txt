The daily routine, hectic as it is, is thrown into further disarray one day, when a high ranking Hospitaller comes to inspect the facilities. What follows this seemingly ordinary inspection is thrillingly played out by a wonderful cast of actors.  

The Fortress stars Clement Sibony as Captain Thomas Cortmain. The Noble Lady Constanza, who dwells in the castle near the fortress is played by Louise Pasteau.
