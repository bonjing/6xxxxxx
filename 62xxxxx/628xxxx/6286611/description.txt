Started in Charlotte, North Carolina, Bruised But Not Broken is a group of devoted Christian individuals who love to use their talents for their Lord and Savior. Although the members are only 15 and 16 years old, they are hitting the scene with a unique blend of hard-hitting metalcore with a techno twist that is captivating crowds every show they play
