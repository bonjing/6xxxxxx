The Thunderthief is John Paul Jones\'s second solo album, following his debut Zooma. It features his first solo attempts at lyric writing and singing, for which he employs a range of styles to convey the mood of the non-instrumental songs. Atmosphere ranges from ethereal to raucous and the album displays both the versatile compositional skills and virtuoso musicianship of its creator.
