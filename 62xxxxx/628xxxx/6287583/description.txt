Tatoeage specialist Jake Sawyer reist de hele wereld af, op zoek naar nieuwe

American tattoo artist Jake Sawyer wanders the world, exploring and exploiting

his first glimpse at the exotic world of traditional Samoan tattoo (tatau), 
and, in a thoughtless act, unwittingly unleashes a powerful angry spirit. In 
his devastating journey into Pacific mysticism, Jake must find a way to save 
his new love, Sina and recover his own soul.
