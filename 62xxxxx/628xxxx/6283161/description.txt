In the new Story Mode, follow the ascent and fall of heroes.
In Dynasty Warriors 7, experience a new Story Mode and follow
the captivating story of our Kingdoms battling for rule of
China. Experience the individual tales of the Shu, Wu, Wei and
the new Jin kingdomes as they shape China from the warring chaos
at the end of the Later Han period to the division of the Land
into Three Kingdoms. Take your faction through this key point in
history and tell the story of the Dynasty that rose to seize power
and unify the country once again. Immerce yourself in the epic
scale of each Kingdom's story, viewing events from the eyes of a
different character in each stage. Now is your chance to live
through an intense historical drama of greetings and goodbyes,
friendships, feuds and rivalries
