Basque is the ancestral language of the Basque people, who inhabit the Basque Country, a region spanning an area in northeastern Spain and southwestern France.
It is classified as a language isolate and is the last remaining pre-Indo-European language in Western Europe.

Beginner's Basque + audio
Aurrera! A textbook for studying Basque volume 1
Standard Basque a progressive grammar
A Student Grammar of Euskara
