Synopsis:
---------

 The Spirit of Vegas
(Translation: The God of Poverty)
 
While Sam and Dean are in Las Vegas trying to "earn some money", 
a chandelier falls down and kills the dealer right in front of their eyes. 
Since then, Dean is met with misfortune after misfortune. 
It turns out that Dean has been cursed by the God of Poverty, 
a Japanese god who is said to get attached to a human and cause him/her misfortune until their death. 
Can the brothers run away from the growing number of misfortunes?
