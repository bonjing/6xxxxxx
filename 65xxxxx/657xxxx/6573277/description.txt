LSD Dream Emulator is an obscure video game released alongside a group of cds and a book/journal in 1998 in Japan on the  Playstation. This obscure set was based on the dream journal of Osamu Sato, a staff member at Asmik Ace Entertainment, had been writing for a decade. The PS1 game has gained a small cult following after many popular youtube lets plays.
