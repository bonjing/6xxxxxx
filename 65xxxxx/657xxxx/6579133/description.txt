Dark Tranquillity is a melodic death metal band from Gothenburg, Sweden. They are one of the longest-standing and most renowned of the original Gothenburg bands, and pioneers of the melodic death metal genre. 


This is the full studio discography, including all re-releases, bonus tracks, and more.
