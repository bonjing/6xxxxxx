Berlin\'s Paul Kalkbrenner is on Essential Mix duties.
Kalkbrenner is one of Germany\'s biggest electronic music artists, headlining huge open air concerts and playing to over 100,000 people on his last European tour. He even starred in the 2008 film \'Berlin Calling\'!
This Essential Mix is a live set of Kalkbrenner\'s own productions and remixes, including tracks from his new album \'Icke Wieder\'.
