Elwyn Hartley Edwards - Encyclopedia of the Horse
Crescent Books | 1990 | ISBN: 0517224038 | English | 247 pages | PDF | 47.1 MB

This magnificent encyclopedia, a comprehensive survey of the exciting world of the ridden horse, is the complete book for the hose enthusiast. Specially designed for easy reference, it is an authoritative guide to all aspects of riding. Included are sections on the development of the horse throughout the world, the whole range of equestrian sports from the thrill of the rodeo to the dignity and grace of the dressage arena, and a detailed look at horse care and management covering such topics as health, breeding and training.
