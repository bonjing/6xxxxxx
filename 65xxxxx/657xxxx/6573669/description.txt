Blade and Motoko hire a small boat and make their way through the Phillipine islands to a small Island the locals 
believe to be Cursed called Isla del fuego or "Fire Island", a likely cover for a Vampire hideout. Meanwhile,
Deacon Frost entertains a Pureblood Vampire guest, a nessenger from the Vampire "Nobility" who warns him the 
nobility feel he is becoming too conspicuous. The messenger is also there for reasons of his own, his interest in 
Existence and Deacon Frosts experiments with Vampire DNA which is the source of Frosts own immense power.
 With the aid of local guardians the Blade and motoke storm the Vampires hideout but Frost is too pwerful for Blade 
and incapacitates him and sets one of his creations on the natives killing 2 of them forcing the rest to flee and 
drag blade with them, the surviving guardian then confronts Blade about his own vampire heritage.
 The next night they return and lure Frosts creature outside to attack it but it suddenly releases 
the 2 dead guardians who have been changed into flying vampire monsters as well, 
the remaining guardian battles them while Blade battles with Frosts Creature...
