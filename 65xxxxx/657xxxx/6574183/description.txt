                             Green Lantern(2011)

Test pilot Hal Jordan lives a carefree life with fast fighter and even faster relationships as their primary interests until the day he was recruited against his will to become a Green Lantern, a member of a force with superhuman powers whose life is devoted to protect the universe. Hal doubt that he is the right man for the job but when alien forces threatening the world there are only two choices: to flee or to fight.
