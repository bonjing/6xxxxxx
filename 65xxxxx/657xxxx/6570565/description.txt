From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written and Dramatised by Alexander McCall Smith; Full-cast dramatisations starring Claire Benedict as Precious Ramotswe.

Episode 1: The Daddy

Owner and founder Precious Ramotswe takes on her first case - and a new secretary.

Episode 2: The Bone

Precious seeks the truth about Nandira Patel's boyfriend - and to solve a bone mystery.

Episode 3: The Maid

Precious Ramotswe tackles a doctor's erratic behaviour and takes on a new case.

Episode 4: Tears of the Giraffe

Precious Ramotswe gets a surprise, and her case of the missing American remains unsolved.
