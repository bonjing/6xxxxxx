On August 13, 2007, 2K Games released a free PDF book entitled "BioShock: Breaking the Mold" containing concept art from the creation of BioShock for download in high resolution. The artbook includes a forward written by BioShock's director, Ken Levine which lists the graphic artists who were influential in the design of the game. Within the artbook, concept art shows the creative progression of the design for the game logo, the iconic enemy, the weapons, and the locations of BioShock.
