Perspectives on Astronomy: Mars-Scape or Earth-Scape? 
Br ks C le | ISBN : 0495113522 | January 31, 2007 | 378 pages | PDF | 55,4 MB 

Authors Mike Seeds and Dana Backman personalize the history of the universe by placing you at the center of the latest chapter in a grand and amazing story. You will learn how we are the latest link in the "great chain of origins." And, by emphasizing the role of the scientific process, Seeds and Backman will help you also understand how analyzing scientific evidence not only answers the question "How do we know?," but provide deeper insights into our place in the universe as well.
