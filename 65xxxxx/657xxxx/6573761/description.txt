 W.rld Sci..ific Publishing C.mpany | 1994 | ISBN: 9810212984, 981021295X | 510 pages | PDF | 8.22 MB 

 The material for this volume has been selected from the examination questions for graduate students, for the past 20 years, of the following institutions: University of California (Berkeley), Columbia University, the University of Chicago, MIT, State University of New York at Buffalo, Princeton University and University of Wisconsin. This volume comprises of 310 problems and is divided into three parts: Newtonian mechanics; analytical mechanics; and special relativity.
