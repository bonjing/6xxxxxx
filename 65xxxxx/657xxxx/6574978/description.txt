The Angry Video Game Nerd is a Internet TV series based on a fictional character created and portrayed by James D. Rolfe. In each episode, the nerd reviews a terrible video game and rants about it while using dirty language. Mike Matei helps Rolfe by playing characters in certain episodes (Bugs Bunny, The Joker etc.) Kyle Justin sings and plays the theme song written by Rolfe.
