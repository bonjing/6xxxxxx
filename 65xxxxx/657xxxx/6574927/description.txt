Overview

TriDef 3D software enables the viewing of movies, photos and games in 3D on a wide range of stereoscopic 3D displays.

TriDef 3D includes DDD's unique 2D to 3D conversion software enabling existing 2D photos, movies and DVDs to be enjoyed in dynamic 3D. Blu-ray and HD DVDs are not supported.
