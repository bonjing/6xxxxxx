=========
A B O U T
=========

Emily's cats are on holiday! Visit them one after the other in Paris,
London, New York and Moscow. Each cat has nine lives, or  levels  can
you make it to the end?


===============
F E A T U R E S
===============

- "Emily the Strange" exclusively on PC and Mac

- Exhilarating skateboard action

- 36 levels in the style of "Emily the Strange"

- Level design based on the characteristics of Emily's cats:
  Neechees "World of Chaos", Miles "World of Creativity",
  Sabbaths "World of Fire" and Mysterys "World of Stars"

- Extended PC level editor lets you play your own levels


===================
System Requirements
===================

Processor: 1 Ghz
Memory: 256 MB
Video: Video card with 64 MB compatible with OpenGL (not below the GeForce 2 MX / Radeon 9000)
Sound: DirectX Compatible Soundcard
Free DB: 200 MB
DVD-ROM: Not required

I N S T A L L  N O T E S
========================

Copy "Setup.exe" & paste it where you want to install the game.

Run setup.exe.
