I transferred this to DVD years ago, but I figured with the "Early Works" sets coming out, it would surely have been released officially on DVD by now.  I can't find any edition of this, and the transfer here is pretty usable compared to some I've seen (and done myself).

At just under 30 minutes, it covers a lot of ground, in a very Greenaway way, numbered, lettered, narrated and with a Michael Nyman score.

A guide through bathing and toiling areas in London and Oxfordshire, with an expert and quite a bit of humor.
