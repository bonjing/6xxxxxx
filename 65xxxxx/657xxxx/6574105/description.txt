Lotus is a compilation cd featuring many of the now hard to find tracks of Ishq which have released over the last 10 years with various labels on compilation albums. The album also features one new unreleased track. The music itself highlights Ishq's highly emotive and melodic side and as a compilation this album brings together some of their best tracks released over the last 10 years onto one album for people who do not wish to buy or cannot find these rare tracks anymore on separate albums.
