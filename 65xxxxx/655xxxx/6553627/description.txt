QIIME (canonically pronounced "Chime") stands for "Quantitative Insights Into Microbial Ecology". QIIME is a microbial community analysis toolkit developed in Python using PyCogent, and can be used for microbial ecology studies based on traditional and high-throughput sequencing methods.

The best source of information on QIIME is the QIIME homepage: http://qiime.sourceforge.net/
