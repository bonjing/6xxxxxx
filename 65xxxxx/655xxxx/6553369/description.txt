Moshing Samurai is a 8-Bit, Nintendocore / Hardcore / Metal band based in Glasgow, UK. Born with love for both video games and metal music, members of Moshing Samurai decided to form a band that will represent their passions.

They have released 2 records.

Atlantis Tapeworm Incident [DEMO] 2009
Atlantis Tapeworm Incident [EP] 2010

And the newest longplay album - The Future Of Law Enforcement
