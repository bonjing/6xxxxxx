The Strategic Project Office: A Guide to Improving Organizational Performance 
Publisher: CRC Press | ISBN: 0824707508 | edition 2001 | PDF | 272 pages | 1.28 mb

Describing the initiation, design, execution, and control of a strategic project office, this book provides step-by-step instructions for establishing a PMO. The author emphasizes cost management, cultural change, risk assessment, resource allocation, and skills tracking to increase project value, organizational efficiency, and productivity.
