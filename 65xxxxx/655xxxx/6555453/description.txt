The Strategic Project Office, Second Edition 

Publisher: CRC Press | English

The second edition of this award-winning reference provides step-by-step instructions 
for establishing and maturing a project management office (PMO). Concise and easy to 
read, The Strategic Project Office, Second Edition covers the four primary areas of 
knowledge and practice regarding the PMO: governance and portfolio management, resource 
optimization, organizational change, and performance measurement.
