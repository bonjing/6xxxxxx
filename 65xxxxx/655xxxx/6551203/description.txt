Summary:
Teenage Dream is the third studio album by American pop singer Katy Perry. The album was released in the United States on August 24, 2010, and debuted at number one on the Billboard 200, selling 192,000 copies in its first week. Teenage Dream has spawned five singles, all of which have achieved massive chart success. 
 "Teenage Dream" was released on August 24, 2010, in the United States as a physical release by Capitol Records. A deluxe version was made available on August 27, 2010 in the US, and consists of two CDs with the twelve original tracks, plus two tracks from 2009 on which Perry had appeared as a featured artist. The deluxe version also includes remixes of "California Gurls" and "Teenage Dream".
