Plan Like a Grandmaster 
Publisher: B.T. Batsford | ISBN: 0713458305 | edition 1988 | PDF | 264 pages | 3.14 mb

By a Soviet Grandmaster, trainer and theoretician, this book contains an analysis of how to formulate a plan in the opening and early middle game of chess, with 350 examples. By the same author as "Contemporary Approach to the Middle Ground".
