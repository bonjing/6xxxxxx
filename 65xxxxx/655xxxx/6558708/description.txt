Gardening series. Alan Titchmarsh shows how to create a garden that is perfect for a party. He reveals how to build an elegant bridge for the guests, how to plant around seating areas and how to use dramatic garden lighting. Plus Matt James and Valentine Warner join forces to create an alternative to the traditional barbecue - building a firepit and cooking a stuffed leg of lamb.
