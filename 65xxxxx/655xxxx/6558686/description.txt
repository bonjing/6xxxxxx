"Freek-a-Leek" is a hit song recorded by Petey Pablo in 2004 for his second album Still Writing in My Diary: 2nd Entry. It was produced by Lil Jon. The single peaked at #7 on the Billboard Hot 100 in May 2004. It is featured on the playlist of the video game Midnight Club 3. Backing Vocals heard are from Ciara.
