                              
                          A.Walk.To.Remember.(2002).



Every time spring comes to North Carolina, Landon Carter thinks back on his high school and Jamie Sullivan. Landon hung with gangs inside the school, and he and his friends had nothing but contempt for the confident Jamie. But when Landon ends up in the school's drama group, as punishment for a prank, he meets Jamie on her own terms. Their encounter arouses passionate feelings, feelings he did not know existed. Their meeting came to change his life forever.
