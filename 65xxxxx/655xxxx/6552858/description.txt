Title: Panya Renu
Year: 2011
Genre: Comedy
Director: Bin Banluerit
Country: Thailand
Audio: Thai
subtitle: none
Cast: Petchtai Wongkamlao, Salinee Duangta, Sitida Hongsa, Supachai Polrassami, Yeong Lookyee

Plot:
Panya Renu is a comedy set in a Northeastern Thailand village. It tells the story of a schoolboy who forms a traditional Pong Lang Band with his friends and aspires to win the coveted place in the provincial competition. Renu is a girl who has a crush on him, but she faces a complication when a bus carrying Bangkok students breaks down near their village and the rich city kids have to spend the night with the rural poor ones.
