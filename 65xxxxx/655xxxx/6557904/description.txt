Architecture 1 is the first of 5 DVDs in the box-set collection, Architecture - The Complete Box Set, 2009.

****************************************

The documentary series charts the most ambitious architectural creations of the 19th and 20th centuries, featuring remarkable archive material and interviews with the greatest architectural minds. From Paris to Dessau to Nimes, the series offers a unique and fascinating insight into the greatest architectural achievements of our time, including the stunning Alhambra, Johnson Building and Paris Opera Garnier.
