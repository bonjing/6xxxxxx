Love Letter is the tenth studio album by American R&B recording artist R. Kelly, released December 14, 2010 on Jive Records. It was written and produced entirely by Kelly. A departure from his previous work's contemporary sound and sexually-explicit themes, Love Letter incorporates classicist soul music influences and features chivalrous lyrics concerning love and forgiveness.
