31 "Armor Wars" Kevin Burke, Chris "Doc" Wyatt Kevin Burke, Chris "Doc" Wyatt  August 10, 2011  205 

When Stane builds duplicates of the Iron Man armors called the Guardsmen (consisting of Force and Shockwave, 
Tony must reveal they are frauds. But, this causes the civilians to start hating Tony. Later that week, 
Obadiah Stane holds the Stark International Guardsmen Expo where he will reveal the Firepower armor. 
Pepper finds out the identities of Force and Shockwave, revealing they are criminals. 
Tony reveals this to the world, and with the help of Rhodey defeats Force and Shockwave. 
Obadiah makes up a lie on television that Force and Shockwave made up fake identities to get 
jobs at Stark International in order to use the armors.
