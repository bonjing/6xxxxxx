This is a collection of rules-light Role-Playing Games, as made popular by Microlite20.

These systems are for people who prefer to focus on the story, or who like to keep things fast and simple.

Most PDF's in this collection were bookmarked by me.

Included in this Collection:
* Basic Storyteller
* Core Elements Toolbox
* Challenges & Champions
* Microlite20 RPG Collection Spring 2010 Revised
* Microlite74 (+ supplements)
* Microlite75 (+ supplement)
* Microlite supplements from DominoWriting
* Microlite Storyteller
* Mini Six
* Spylite: Cyberpunk
