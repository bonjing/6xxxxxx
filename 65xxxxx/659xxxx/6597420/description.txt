For english-only speaking:
This book is in german language. English title would be: TV-Monopoly - The Inside Story (2002). Roger Schawinski's first hand account on how he tried to break the SRG media monopoly in Switzerland. Meet the players who have the power to legally prevent any kind of competition. Schawinski first tried with TeleZuri, then with Tele24, to create an independent private television, yet to no avail. Filled with insider information and unknown facts, the author also shows ways to improve this situation that cannot possible be in the public interest. 215 pages.
