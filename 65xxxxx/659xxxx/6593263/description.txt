Extreme Lives 
Explore the shocking, subversive and controversial side of human nature Extreme Lives Season examines some of the most confronting behaviours, rituals and beliefs from around the world in a season of special documentaries. From a woman emotionally attracted to a building to a gender-bending subculture in Japan, a mother of babies made entirely of vinyl to ultimate beauty enthusiasts; these are the people living lives in the extreme.
Extreme Lives: Addicted Beyond Control
Dependency doesn't just mean drugs or alcohol: follow four people as they struggle to break their all-too-real addictions, including incessant shopping, promiscuous sex and even computer gaming.
