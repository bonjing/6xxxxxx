Become the Captain America, super soldier definitive as you battle the army of Red Skull in this exciting and original action-adventure game in the third person, set in the murky days of the Second World War. Use the legendary shield of Captain America to stop the enemies from a distance, returned fire and interact with your environment. Immerse yourself in battle with your mighty shield and live own superhuman feats. You have at your disposal all the force of the world's first super soldier.
