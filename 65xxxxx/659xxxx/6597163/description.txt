Description of the Game :
Ben 10 Ultimate Alien: Cosmic Destruction is the fifth video game in the Ben 10 game series, based on Ben 10: Ultimate Alien. The game revolves around Ben traveling across real-world locations including Tokyo, Paris, the Great Wall of China, the Amazon Rainforest, and Rome to find ancient Galvan artifacts to save the Earth from total destruction by a mysterious cosmic storm.
