Watch the Throne is the debut studio album by The Throne, an American hip hop duo composed of Jay-Z and Kanye West. The album was released on August 8, 2011, by Roc-A-Fella Records, Roc Nation, and Def Jam Recordings. Recording sessions for the album took place at various recording locations and began in November 2010. The first single, titled "H.A.M", was released in January 2011 with the following single, "Otis", released on July 20, 2011. Jay-Z and West will promote the album with a 
concert tour during October to December 2011.
