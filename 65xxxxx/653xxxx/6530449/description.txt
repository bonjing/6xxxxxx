                 
                         '25 Years Of Trojan Records'


This double-disc set from Trojan includes classic ska, rocksteady and reggae tracks from Desmond Dekker the Aces, Jimmy Cliff, Lee Perry the Upsetters, the Melodians, Toots the Maytals, Bob Marley the Wailers, Ken Boothe, John Holt and many others. 
Though there are quite a few Jamaican classics missing from this history of the music, all the tracks included are quite solid. (AMG)
