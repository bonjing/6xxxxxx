Green's Functions in Quantum Physics 
 Publisher: Springer | ISBN: 3540288384 | edition 2006 | PDF | 495 pages | 3.55 mb

 The new edition of a standard reference will be of interest to advanced students wishing to become familiar with the method of Green's functions for obtaining simple and general solutions to basic problems in quantum physics. The main part is devoted to the simplest kind of Green's functions, namely the solutions of linear differential equations with a -function source.
