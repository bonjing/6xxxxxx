One of Willie's greatest recordings, and an audiophile lovers dream.... hard to find and OOP, this was part of Classic Records single sided 45 RPM series, pressed on 200 gram Quiex vinyl. That's 800 grams of vinyl for 37 minutes of music. I compared this to my previous go-to, the Mastersound gold CD. This is much more open and transparent, you can hear every detail in the recording.
