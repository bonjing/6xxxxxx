Ilan Chet, "Wolf Prize in Agriculture"
 Publisher: World Scientific Publishing Company | ISBN 10: 9812835849 | 2009 | PDF | 1143 pages | 16.8 MB

 This specially compiled, one-of-a-kind volume contains exemplary contributions from Wolf Prize laureates. In agriculture, there is no higher prize than the Wolf Prize. Thus, the reader has the opportunity to learn from the very top in various aspects of this field. Indeed, the book includes a list of publications and the most important papers in plant and animal breeding, genetics, biochemistry and plant protection, biotechnology, as well as chemistry and the physics of soils. One can learn about the development of a particular field of agriculture, at its highest level, in this volume through the scientific activities of its world-class contributors
