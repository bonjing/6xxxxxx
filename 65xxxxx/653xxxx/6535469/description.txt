Cardiovascular Physiology 
 Publisher: McGraw-Hill | ISBN: 0071701206 | edition 2010 | CHM | 304 pages | 3,1 mb

 The goal of this unique review is to give you a working understanding of the key concepts of cardiovascular physiology. Concise but thorough, Cardiovascular Physiology focuses on the facts you need to get a solid big picture overview of how the cardiovascular system operates under normal and abnormal situations.
