Plot:
Arjun Bhagwat (Emraan Hashmi), is a ex-policeman, who worked for Goa police. He is hungry for money and earns his bread by doing all wrong and good things and an atheist, he doesn't believe in God but regularly visits church to donate fat bundles of money to orphans. In this process to earn more money, he meets a Goan gangster Sameer, who has been going through the bad phase of his business due to disappearance of his hookers. Sameer knows the investigative mind of Arjun and offers him huge amount to locate the missing girls.

On the other hand, Priya (Jacqueline Fernandez) is a model, who is in a confused relationship with Arjun.

During his investigation, Arjun finds a cell number which linked to missing girls. He decided to send a dummy candidate to catch the culprit.

Here comes Reshma (Sulagna Panigrahi), who is a college girl but involved in prostitution to feed her family. Sameer sends Reshma to after calling on that number. It is an antagonists' house, Dheeraj Pandey (Prashant Narayanan). Dheeraj is psychopath killer. He brutally kills women and does the same with Reshma, but leaving her alive in a deep well where Pandey disposes all the girls he killed. Reshma soon manages to escape from there as Pandey is in police station and reaches a temple nearby.

In between the investigation Arjun and Dheeraj lock horns and land in police station. Dheeraj is investigated, trying to confuse the cops, finally accepts his crime to a police officer (Sudhanshu Pandey). However, Arjun has just one night to prove Dheeraj being a serial killer.

Arjun fails to do so and Dheeraj comes out of police station as free bird with the help of one of his friend. They both go to the temple which they follow, where Pandey kills his friend, the pandit and also finally Reshma.

Pandey targets Priya next and waits in his home, but Arjun reaches there followed by Priya for a photoshoot unknowingly to kill her. Arjun and Pandey indulge in fight as few police officers reach at the place later. However even though police requests Arjun not to kill Pandey, Arjun kills him followed by few bullets by a police officer. The movie ends with Arjun at the Church, accompanied with Priya.[/COLOR][/B][/QUOTE]
