The Poof Point is a classic Disney Channel Movie from 2001

Release date: 14 September 2001

Plot: This is a movie about two scientists who invent a time machine. A critical part falls out of the time machine before they get into it to test it. Nothing seems to happen at first, but when they wake up the next day they are regressing in age(physically and mentally). Their kids seem to notice something strange is going on, judging by their behavior. Soon enough, the children realize that it is the machine that has changed their parents.
