Enjoy The Game!

Embark on a dangerous journey with Tess, a lonely young girl, as she is transported to a world that lies within her imagination in Sphera, a new hidden object adventure from Sandlot Games!

Mocked mercilessly by her older brother for her eccentricity and the large glasses that sit upon her tiny head, Tess desperately wants to escape her lonely life. When she finds a mysterious marble, Tess is swept into a strange world where a walrus-like creature named Zuchary rules with kindness. When his adopted son, Jacob, turns against him, Zuchary reaches out to Tess for assistance. In return, she will be allowed to return home.
