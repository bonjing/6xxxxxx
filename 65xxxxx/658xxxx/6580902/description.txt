Super Mario Bros. is a 1985 platform video game developed by Nintendo, In Super Mario Bros., the player controls Mario (and in a two-player game, a second player acts as Mario's brother Luigi) as he travels through the Mushroom Kingdom in order to rescue Princess Toadstool from the antagonist Bowser.

The Super Mario Bros. theme, officially known as the "Ground Theme" is a theme originally found in the first stage of the Nintendo Entertainment System video game Super Mario Bros. The theme has a calypso rhythm and usually receives a corresponding orchestration in games whose sound synthesizers can imitate steel drums.
