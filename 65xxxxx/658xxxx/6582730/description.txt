From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written and Dramatised by Alexander McCall Smith; Full-cast dramatisations starring Claire Benedict as Precious Ramotswe.

Episode 1: The Chief Justice of Beauty

Mma Makutsi takes on a case for the Chief Justice of Beauty.

Episode 2: The Confession

Precious Ramotswe must find two women wronged in the past by her client, Mr Molefelo.

Episode 3: The Kalahari Typing School for Men

Precious learns about her competition, and Mma Makutsi embarks on an enterprise.

Episode 4: The Admirer

Precious makes an unhappy discovery and Mma Makutsi finds a fan at the typing school.
