60 Minute Revision Guide To Life In The UK Citizenship Test by Susanna Ting (2011 ISBN 978-1-4477-4422-1).pdf

If you are preparing for the Life In The UK Citizenship test, and you are struggling through the rather thick official handbook, then this revision guide is for you. Whether you are waiting for the bus, or waiting at the dentist, this book is designed to let you use the time to quickly revisit the basic facts that you need for the test. All the information in the book is in a concise, easy-to-remember, simple format. There are no mock tests. Just the simple bare facts.
