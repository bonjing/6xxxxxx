It's Thor as you've never seen him before, brought to you by master storyteller Paul Jenkins. In "Blowin' in the Wind", illustrated by industry legend Mark Texeira, Thor must diffuse a tense hostage situation with a man whose son died attempting to fly like a super-hero. Can Thor save the lives of the innocents using his mind instead of Mjolnir? Then, in "Ask Me Tomorrow", breakout artist Pascale Alixe brings to life the story of a Catholic priest on death's door who consults with the God of Thunder about life, the universe and everything. Two gripping Thor tales from Marvel's greatest!
