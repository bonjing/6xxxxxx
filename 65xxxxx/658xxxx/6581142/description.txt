Information

Earth Investigated: Killer Lakes
Since the death of 1,700 people near a lake in Africa, scientists have uncovered a terrifying series of hazards in lakes across the world. With contributions from some of the world's foremost scientists, we examines the newest developments and theories and uses moving human-interest stories, personal experiences and the latest computer-generated graphics
