The Very Best of 10cc is a 1997 compilation album by the band 10cc. The album comes with an exclusive essay written by Chris White especially for it. It was released in the UK with 18 tracks and in the USA with 15 tracks (adding For You And I and deleting four tracks: Une Nuit A Paris; Under Your Thumb; Wedding Bells; and, Neanderthal Man. The album features all of the highest charting material from the band's career and also features several songs by Godley & Creme and (in the UK edition) Neanderthal Man by Hotlegs, acts closely associated with 10cc. The album charted at no37 on the UK Albums Chart.
