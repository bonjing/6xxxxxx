The Mind of Mr JG Reeder

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Edgar Wallace; Read by David Horovitch.

Episode 1: The Poetical Policeman

Tackling a strange 1920s London bank robbery and death - Mr JG Reeder thinks like a criminal.

Episode 2: The Troupe

Mr JG Reeder tackles a jewellery fraud with a link to a transatlantic visitor to London.

Episode 3: The Green Mamba

Mr JG Reeder tackles a jewellery heist of emeralds and hounds a notorious villain.

Episode 4: The Strange Case

When a government minister dies, Mr JG Reeder sets out to uncover the truth.
