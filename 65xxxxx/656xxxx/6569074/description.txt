Scooby-Doo! Mystery Incorporated (originally known as Scooby-Doo! Mystery, Inc.) is the eleventh incarnation 
of Hanna-Barbera's Scooby-Doo animated series, and the first incarnation not to be first-run on Saturday mornings. 
The series is produced by Warner Bros. Animation and Cartoon Network and is a reboot of the franchise.

The gang is attacked by a hi-tech assassin called the Obliteratrix. This leads the gang to investigate the mystery of 
the Cursed Treasure further with the assistance of H.P. Hatecraft. Along the way, they discover the fate of the 
original Mystery Incorporated. In the end Angel Dynamite (Cassidy Williams) traps the Obliteratrix and they solve the mystery.

Note: The entire Jonny Quest crew make a cameo appearance Destroido lab workers during Alice May's flashback narration. 
Jonny Quest is from Hanna-Barbera Productions. Angel Dynamite reveals herself to the rest of the gang. The Dusk Teen Vampire 
Romance book series that is mentioned is a parody of the Twilight series.
