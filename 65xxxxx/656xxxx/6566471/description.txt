Episode list for: "The Othersiders"
 
Season: 2
 
Year: 2009
 
Episodes: 10
 
 
Haunted or not? The Othersiders continue to collect evidence and cast their
verdicts with all-new investigations in Season 2. Uncover the dark inner
workings of an eerie saw mill, some not-so-sweet scares at the sugar factory, 
and creepy criminal activity at Alcatraz! Get ready for more unexpected 
encounters as the team explores the unknown.
 
 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
 
Season 2, Episode 1: Camp Gilmore
Released October 07, 2009
 
The team visits a summer camp that was built on a Native American burial ground.
 
 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
 
Season 2, Episode 2: La Purisima Mission
Released October 14, 2009
 
The Othersiders search one of the original Spanish missions for the spirit of a 
greyhound.
 
 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
 
Season 2, Episode 3: Santa Maria Sugar Factory
Released October 16, 2009
 
The team looks for a paranormal connection to the many accidental deaths at a 
local sugar factory.
 
 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
 
Season 2, Episode 4: Santa Anita Race Track
Released October 21, 2009
 
The Othersiders try to round up the ghosts of three famous horses at the 
Santa Anita racetrack.
 
 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
 
Season 2, Episode 5: LA Power Plant
Released October 23, 2009
 
The team looks for the spirit of a scuba diver who accidentally drowned in the 
power plant.
 
 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
 
Season 2, Episode 6: Union Hotel
Released October 26, 2009
 
During their investigation of the Union Hotel, the team tries to confirm reports
of cowboy and bandit spirits, strange piano playing, and a hidden room.
 
 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
 
Season 2, Episode 7: Santa Barbara Courthouse
Released October 27, 2009
 
The team investigates the Santa Barbara Courthouse where a man who was convicted
and hanged for killing his girlfriend supposedly haunts the grounds.
 
 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
 
Season 2, Episode 8: Preston Castle
Released October 28, 2009
 
The Othersiders visit Preston Castle, which was once used as a reform school in
the 1800's, and investigate reports of the apparitions of boys who lived there. 
 
 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
 
Season 2, Episode 9: Camino Saw Mill
Released October 29, 2009
 
The team heads to the abandoned Camino Saw Mill to see if the ghosts of the
workers who have tragically died there are haunting it.
 
 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
 
Season 2, Episode 10: Alcatraz
Released October 30, 2009
 
In their biggest, farthest, and scariest challenge to date, the Othersiders get
exclusive access into the infamous Alcatraz prison. During their investigation,
they explore the Civil War-era dungeon and old prison cells, including the one
that housed the legendary mobster Al Capone, who reportedly haunts the prison
walls while playing his banjo.
