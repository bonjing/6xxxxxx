This is the 2010 F1 simulator from Ferrari. In this game, you race with the 2010 Ferrari F1 originally driven by Fernando Alonso and Felipe Massa in the 2010 Formula 1 World Championship.

You can choose between 3 tracks:
-Mugello, Italy
-Nurburging, Germany
-Fiorano, Italy

And you can race with 1 car, 3 drivers:
-Car with Felipe Massa helmet
-Car with Fernando Alonso helmet
-Car with white helmet.

You can choose between:
-Practice session, you can drive how you want it.
-Hotlap, don't go off-track or your lap won't count.

If you think it's too easy, try it with Traction Control and Brake Assist off, and perhaps with manual shifting.
