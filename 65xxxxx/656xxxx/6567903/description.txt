Descreption :
Novaspace is a German Eurodance project which, between 2002 and 2004, covered several 1980s hits, such as "Time After Time", "Guardian Angel" and "Beds Are Burning". They have reached the Top 20 in Germany, Austria and Australia. Novaspace, which was originated in 2002 by the record producer Felix Gauder, was first fronted by the singer Jessica Bohrs, who was later replaced by Jenny Marsala.
