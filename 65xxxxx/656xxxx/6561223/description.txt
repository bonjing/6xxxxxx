ZD Soft Screen Recorder 4.1.3.0 

ZD Soft Screen Recorder is the ultimate screen recording/sharing software for Windows. It captures what you see on screen and what you hear/say simultaneously, and saves them as compressed video file in real-time. It is usually used to create software demos, capture streaming videos and record game plays. External capture device such as webcam is also supported. Further more, you can share your screen with a remote PC through video chat software such as Windows Live Messenger.

You can use ZD Soft Screen Recorder to:

+ Create demo videos to demonstrate how to use computer software.
+ Capture streaming videos to hard disk for local playback.
+ Record your game plays to show how cool you are in the game.
+ Share your screen with a remote PC through video chat software.
