Springer Texts in Statistics (Updated June 2011)
Springer Texts in Statistics (STS) includes advanced textbooks from 3rd- to 4th-year undergraduate courses to 1st- to 2nd-year graduate courses. Exercise sets should be included. The series editors are George Casella, Stephen Fienberg, and Ingram Olkin.

NOTE: This is not a complete collection. There are some missing titles.
