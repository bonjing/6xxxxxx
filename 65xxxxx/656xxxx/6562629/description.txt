Destroyed By Nature
Hard-hitting amateur, CCTV and news footage reveals how some of the world's biggest natural disasters took away people's lives, homes with tsunamis, earthquakes and mega-storms looming large in our memory, it seems Mother Nature is not feeling very nurturing towards us Earthlings of late. And the worst could be yet to come. In Destroyed by Nature, Nat Geo reveals what it's like to be caught in the middle of devastating weather attacks. Using a combination of CCTV, amateur and professional footage and using harrowing first-hand accounts, we're taken into the heart of the storm to see what terrors our planet is capable of unleashing on us. And the forecast for the future? It's looking gloomy. Time to batten down the hatches.
