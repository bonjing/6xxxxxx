This is a 2 CD set of MJ & BJ's Morning Show, based out of Tampa, Florida's WFLZ 93.3 FM which was released on the eve of the millenium celebration in the year 2000. The two CDs are a madcap compilation of some of their best on-air comedy bits and prank phone calls. Many prank calls by their hilarious Crotchety Old Man and Mumbleman characters.
