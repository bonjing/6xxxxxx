                      Reach PC [Hyperdrive25]
                      =======================   

Ever wanted to be a boxer? Fulfill your dreams in this out-of-this-world boxing game. 

Boxing is in the ring and as well as outside it! You have full freedom to do what you fancy. Destroy to your opponent as well as his manager. From bare knuckle fights,
backroom brawls and traditional fights this one has it all!

Create your player from scratch, hire the manager you want. As you win fights you will increase your boxing skills as well as your poplarity. Training improves your boxers skills and as you rise through the ranks, more prestigious and lucrative offers await you.

Get your boxing gloves on, this one is going to be intense!  _____________________________________________________________________________________

Installation: Double-click the setup file to begin. 

Have fun!!!
