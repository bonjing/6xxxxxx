This torrent contains the exact contents of the Memory-stick distributed at the end of the 52th International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) Amsterdam 2011.

It contains Photo's of the Opening and Closing Ceremony, the Excursions and the Group Picture.
Furthermore are the Daily News Magazins and Video's included.

Most of these pictures/movies/news can also be found on the official website:
