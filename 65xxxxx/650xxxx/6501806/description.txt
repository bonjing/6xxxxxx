The Hireling

From BBC Radio 4. Written by L.P. Hartley; Full-cast dramatisation starring Simon Day and Lisa Dillon, with Kenneth Cranham as the narrator; Dramatised by Judith Adams.

Former Sergeant Stephen Leadbitter works as a chauffeur with car for hire.

He despises his clients, until the young, widowed and immensely rich Lady Franklin hires him to take her on trips to cathedrals which she visited with her late husband. Lady Franklin has been in mourning for her late husband for two years, and is struggling to return to normal life.

She shares her burden with Leadbitter and he tells his own, fictional, story which grows to plague the inventor.

Two alien classes are put on a collision course.
