On a mission at the edge of the desert, a girl is born of an
Aboriginal mother and an Alghan father. A dark skinned, green
eyed, forbidden beauty - she is a reluctant traveller between
the worlds of her parents. This is the story of Jila (Alice
Haines). Conceived against her mother's will, born between
worlds, abandoned by love, cheated by death, removed from her
faith and from her place
