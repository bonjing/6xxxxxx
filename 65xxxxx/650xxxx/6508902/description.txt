Corporate Entrepreneurship and Venturing 
Publisher: Springer | ISBN: 0387249389 | edition 2005 | PDF | 168 pages | 8,8 mb

Corporate Entrepreneurship and Venturing is positioned at the crossroads of the strategy and entrepreneurship fields. The common theme is how and why corporate entrepreneurship and corporate venturing can contribute to innovation and strategic renewal in large established companies, in particular it explores ways to balance exploitation and exploration in established companies.
