The third DVD in Sandra's popular "Belly By Sandra" instructional series dives into a crucial part the bellydance choreography: The Classic Veil Intro. As the start of a carefully considered solo, the veil intro is a highly structured prologue that showcases your dance skills and sets the relationship with your audience for your entire performance. Done properly, the veil intro touches a wide range of styles and rhythmic changes, demonstrating your knowledge and mastery of bellydance fundamentals. In this video Sandra uses one of her own veil intros, building it up step by step into a complete choreography. The result is a comprehensive roadmap to creating a Classic Veil Intro for your own bellydance solo!
