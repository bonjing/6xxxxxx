Dietitian's Guide to Assessment and Documentation
Publisher: Jones & Bartlett Publishers | ISBN: 0763778516 | edition 2010 | PDF | 224 pages | 1,8 mb

Dietitian s Guide to Assessment and Documentation takes an investigative approach to nutrition assessment reviewing the collection and analysis of evidence to determine the real nutrition-related problem, understanding the underlying problem and its effect on nutritional status, and providing intervention to address the problem - thereby improving nutritional health. This text will help students learn how to: Formulate interview questions; Create goals and patient teaching strategies; Interpret lab data; Write a care plan; Document in a medical record.
