Whether extracting ingredients from a block of ice or using a Swiss Army knife as a lone
cooking utensil, three chefs are pushed to their physical and mental limits in each episode
of Extreme Chef.

101 - Rock the Block
In the first challenge, the competitors must rummage
through a garage to find a hidden wagon, stroller and
grocery cart, and then wheel it to the back of a pickup
truck to grab unmarked canned goods and prepare a meal
in 30 minutes.
