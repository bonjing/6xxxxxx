Luxury isn't just a question of expensive and the beautiful 
objects for the rich and the powerful. It has always been 
much more, and much more important, than that,

came to be forbidden, and others embraced. A simple 
luxury like meat could unite could unite the democracy,

Sparta was replaced by the Macedon of Philip II, father

suppressed and still affects our ideas of luxury today.
