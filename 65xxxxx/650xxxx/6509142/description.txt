The second volume in Sandra's popular "Belly By Sandra" DVD series, "Vol. 2: Isolations and Combinations" will rapidly improve your bellydance technique with engaging and fun routines that help find the dancer in even the newest of students. This extended length 2 hour, 20 -minute DVD starts with all-new isolations and then combines them into short rhythmic sequences that feel more like dancing and less like simple exercises. By practicing and mastering these combinations you will build a vocabulary of techniques that are the foundation of real-world choreography and performance.
