Description: 
------------ 
Air Conflicts: Secret Wars is an arcade flight simulation scenarios set in World War I and II. During the 7 campaigns the player has to fly and fight through over 48 missions. Before taking off, the player can choose between different types of aircraft, each with different strengths and weaknesses. 
Among the missions the player has different objectives, such as patrolling, secretly, Hit & Run, the bombing and escort. Objectives are presented as dialogues between different characters. After some missions, the player can improve their skills of maneuvering the aircraft easier, have more stamina or his wingman driven. In addition, the player can choose between two control schemes. Arcade and simulation. The first of them makes the game easier to control, while the second gives more options to control the aircraft. 

Along with the game's campaign also features several multiplayer modes, local and Internet for up to four players.
