Eyes Open is the fourth album by Northern Irish/Scottish alternative rock band Snow Patrol. It was released 1 May 2006 internationally, and 9 May 2006 in the United States. The first European single, "You're All I Have," was released on 24 April 2006; the first U.S. single was "Hands Open" and the first Australian single was "Chasing Cars."
