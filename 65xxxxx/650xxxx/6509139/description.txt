TrailerBursting out of Manchester in the Eighties,Simply Red pioneered a distinctively British soul sound that captivated music lovers for 25 years.
Now, as the final curtain comes down on the Simply Red era, this concert gives fans a last chance to re-live highlights of a career that yielded over 50 million album sales and a raft of chart-topping singles.
Filmed at Sydney Opera House on the Farewell Tour in October 2010, it captures a group at the peak of their powers.
Backed by a magnificent six-piece band, frontman Mick Hucknall's vocal performance shows once again that he has one of the finest coices of his generation.
