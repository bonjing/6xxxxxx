Merzbow is the main recording name of the Japanese noise musician Masami Akita. Since 1979 he has released in excess of 350 recordings.

Merzbox is a boxed set of CDs by Japanese noise musician Merzbow, released in 2000 by Extreme Records. The set consists of 50 CDs, spanning Merzbow\'s career from 1979 to 1997. Thirty of the discs are reissues from LP, CD, or cassette. The remaining twenty are previously unreleased albums.
