Four of the sexiest and most sophisticated girlfriends L.A. has ever seen are back in GIRLFRIENDS Season 4. See ambitious lawyer Joan (Tracee Ellis Ross), her sharp-tongued assistant Maya (Golden Brooks), free-spirited Lynn (Persia White), best friend Toni (Jill Marie Jones), and their friend William (Reggie Hayes) as they deal with hot forbidden romance, self improvement and a very itchy first year of marriage!
