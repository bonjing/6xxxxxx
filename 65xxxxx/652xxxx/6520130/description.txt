Trapped Under The Ice

Nature Documentary hosted by Qariie Marshall, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC I Shouldn't be Alive series in 2006 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

A father son rafting trip in Alaska turns into a disaster when they are dragged under the ice and forced to split up to search for help in the wilderness.
