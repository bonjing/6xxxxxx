Four of the sexiest and most sophisticated girlfriends L.A. has ever seen are back in GIRLFRIENDS Season 6. See ambitious lawyer Joan (Tracee Ellis Ross), her sharp-tongued assistant Maya (Golden Brooks), free-spirited Lynn (Persia White), best friend Toni (Jill Marie Jones), and their friend William (Reggie Hayes) as the girlfriends are on the verge of losing a dream, losing their patience and for one, possibly losing her child. Only with laughter, strength, and friendship with they find a way to keep what's theirs and help each other through it all.
