 This book constitutes the proceedings of the International Conference on Signal Processing, Image Processing and Pattern Recognition, SIP 2009, held as part of the Future Generation Information Technology Conference, FGIT 2009, held on Jeju Island, Korea, December 10-12, 2009.

 The 38 papers presented in this volume were carefully reviewed and selected from numerous submissions. The topics covered are from multifaceted aspects of signal processing, image processing and pattern recognition.
