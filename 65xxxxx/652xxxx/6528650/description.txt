James Douglas Hamilton - The Truth about Rudolf Hess - Unabridged

Read By: John Cartwright

Duration: 10:38

Description

Hess's flight to Britain in 1941 was one of the most bizarre and intriguing episodes of the war. Here many of the myths are exploded, and the development which led to the flight and Hess's approach to the Duke of Hamilton are explored. New light is thrown on the importance of Haushofer, envoy to Hitler and Ribbentrop an adviser to Hess, and the fascinating story is placed in true historical perspective.
