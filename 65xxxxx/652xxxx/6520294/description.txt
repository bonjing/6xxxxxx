Robinson's career as a writer started out in comic books with several stints on indie comics. After that he wrote screenplays, several of which were produced, optioned or in development (including the screenplay version of The Didymus Contingency). His switch to book writing came with The Screenplay Workbook in 2003. He has since written five novels, three are published, Raising the Past and The Didymus Contingency and his third novel, Antarktos Rising, also from Breakneck Books.
