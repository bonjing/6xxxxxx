Is a Canadian children's television show starring Hammy Hamster and other animals.
The series was created by David Ellison and Paul Sutherland from 1995 to 1998, was produced for YTV and Channel 4; 65 episodes of that series were made. It is ranked 79th in the UK Channel 4's 2001 poll of the 100 Greatest Kids' TV shows.
