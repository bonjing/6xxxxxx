Season 2, Episode 4 Aired: 10/2/2007

Taken for a Ride

After a night out in Auckland city, 54 year old photographer and building inspector Simon Buis was savagely beaten and kicked to death in a suburban park by unknown culprits. His murderers have still not been found. Simon's murder shocked his friends, family and the wider art community, who want answers and want to see justice prevail. Psychics Deb Webber and Sue Nicholson ask Simon's spirit for answers surrounding his death.
