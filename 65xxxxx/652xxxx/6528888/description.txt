Four of the sexiest and most sophisticated girlfriends L.A. has ever seen are back in GIRLFRIENDS Season 5. See ambitious lawyer Joan (Tracee Ellis Ross), her sharp-tongued assistant Maya (Golden Brooks), free-spirited Lynn (Persia White), best friend Toni (Jill Marie Jones), and their friend William (Reggie Hayes) as they are chased in Manhattan, challenged with new careers, opening new digs, and experiencing the joys and pains of motherhood.
