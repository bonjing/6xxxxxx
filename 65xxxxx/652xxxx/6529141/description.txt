Kid Vs. Kat

Kid vs. Kat is a Canadian flash animated television series developed and produced at Studio B Productions, created and co-directed by Rob Boutilier. The show premiered on YTV in Canada on October 25, 2008 and in The U.S on Disney XD on February 13, 2009. The story features a 10-year-old boy's constant battle with his sister's Sphinx cat which, in reality, is a cybernetic alien.
