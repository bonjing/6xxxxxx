Gardening series with Alan Titchmarsh. In this episode, Alan adds some magic to an outdoor space by showing how to get the look of a fantasy garden. He demonstrates how to build enchanting winding pathways, how to place sculpture and how to dress elegant archways. Plus Charlote Uhlenbroek reveals how to fill the garden with birdsong and Matt James visits a reclamation yard in search of something special for his garden.
