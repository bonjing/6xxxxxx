Album: Eleni Dimou - Life Is A Woman (1993)
Format: mp3 @ 320 kbps CBR
Booklet: Complete Scans included
Label: SONY Music
Genre: Greek love ballads, jazz

Eleni Dimou is one of the most sensuous voices of the Greek pentagram. This album is an hymn to the women with wonderful ballads. The composer, Mihalis Kapoulas, in his arrangements uses both electronic and conventional instruments. The mood is jazzy. Enjoy 12 nice love ballads.
