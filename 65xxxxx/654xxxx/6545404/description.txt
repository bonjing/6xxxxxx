Tom Jones at T In The Park 2011-07-08 BBC TV

Tom Jones live at T In The Park, the biggest music festival in Scotland, in Balado, Kinross-shire. The whole set was not shown on TV (if you want to see the full set list see www.bbc.co.uk/tinthepark) but this is a good selection of a very fine show.
