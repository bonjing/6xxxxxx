Take a stroll down memory lane with six of the best children's programmes from the last 30 years. Compiled onto one handy video, sit back and enjoy 116 minutes of pure nostalgia. You'll be humming the theme tunes in no time!

Episodes included:

Chorlton & The Wheelies: Chorlton and the Silence Spell (1977), Spot the Happiness Dragon (1979)

Dangermouse: Dangermouse Saves the World Again (1982), The Four Tasks of Dangermouse

Duckula: The Ghost Castle of McDuckula (1988)

Jamie and the Magic Torch: The Flying Rabbit (1977)

Rainbow: Naughty Zippy (1991)

Button Moon: A Day Trip to Button Moon (1983)
