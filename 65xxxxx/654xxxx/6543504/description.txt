Civil Engineering Formulas 2nd Edition by Tyler G. Hicks
Product Description
Instant Access to Civil Engineering FormulasFully updated and packed with more than 500 new formulas, this book offers a single compilation of all essential civil engineering formulas and equations in one easy-to-use reference.  Practical, accurate data is presented in USCS and SI units for maximum convenience.  Follow the calculation procedures inside Civil Engineering Formulas, Second Edition, and get precise results with minimum time and effort.  Each chapter is a quick reference to a well-defined topic, including: Beams and girders Columns Piles and piling Concrete structures Timber engineering Surveying Soils and earthwork Building structures Bridges and suspension cables Highways and roads Hydraulics, drams, and waterworks Power-generation wind turbines Stormwater Wastewater treatment Reinforced concrete Green buildings Environmental protection.
