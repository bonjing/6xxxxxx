WildC.A.T.s/X-Men is a crossover event by Image Comics (from Jim Lee's WildStorm)

 and Marvel Comics in 1997 and 1998 that featured WildStorm's WildC.A.T.s meeting 

Marvel's X-Men. Originally released in four individual comics, each representing a 

different age.

Golden Age - By Scott Lobdell and Travis Charest

Silver Age - By Scott Lobdell,Jim Lee and Scott Williams

Modern Age - By James Robinson,Adam Hughes and Mark Farmer

Dark Age   - By Warren Ellis,Mat Broome,Bret Booth and Stan Parsons
