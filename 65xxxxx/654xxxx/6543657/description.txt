Alan Titchmarsh shows viewers how to get the most from their gardens. In this edition, he reveals how to identify annuals and perennials, and the best way to plant flowers to achieve a blaze of colour and scent. He also demostrates how a sail can be used to create an amazing seating area. Plus Valentine Warner makes chocolates with lavender and Charlotte Uhlenbroek visits a beautiful butterfly garden.
