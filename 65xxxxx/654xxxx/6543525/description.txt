Hot Mix Asphalt Materials, Mixture Design & Construction 2nd Edition by Freddy L. Roberts

Product Description
This college textbook is unique in that it was underwritten by an Education Foundation supported by practitioners of the Asphalt Industry. The text was compiled & written by the staff of the National Center for Asphalt Technology (NCAT), located on the campus of Auburn University, Auburn, Alabama. The textbook was written to serve as a primary reference for Civil Engineering & Construction Management courses having to do with pavement materials, pavement design, & pavement management. It is also an excellent supplemental text for Engineering courses on construction materials. The textbook serves as the foundation for the Professor Training Program run by NCAT. The 9 chapters cover the life cycle of asphalt cement from the refinery to the production of Hot Mix Asphalt (HMA) pavements. Included is a chapter on aggregates, Mix designs, production & placement equipment, testing, specifications, quality assurance & recycling. Latter chapters discuss how pavement performance is measured & contains an overview of maintenance & rehabilitation practices. Order direct from The National Asphalt Pavement Association.
