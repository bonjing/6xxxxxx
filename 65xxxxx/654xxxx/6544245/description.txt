[b]TVO - Allan Gregg in Conversation, April 03 2009: Susan Pinker - The Sexual Paradox[/b]

Women far outnumber men in university and there are more women in the workforce than ever. So why are there still so few that make it to the top? In a provocative new book, [url=http://www.susanpinker.com/]Susan Pinker[/url] argues that the gender gap has more to do with biological differences, than with discrimination. Pinker is a psychologist and columnist for the [i]Globe and Mail[/i]. Her book is called [url=http://www.susanpinker.com/book.html][i]The Sexual Paradox: Extreme Men, Gifted Women and the Real Gender Gap[/i][/url].
