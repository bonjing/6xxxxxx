Hold Back the Night

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Adam Baron; Full-cast dramatisation starring David Morrisey, Danny Webb, Cheryl Campbell, Harry Myers, Rachel Stirling and Adjoah Andoh; Dramatised by Doug Luice; Directed by Janet Whitaker.

Episode 1: Ex-cop turned private-eye Billy Rucker scours hot and humid London for runaway teenagers.

Episode 2: A fresh discovery in the London hunt for a runaway makes Billy Rucker a murder suspect.

Episode 3: Billy suspects murder victim Lucy and her friend Donna were mixed up in something nasty.

Episode 4: Private-eye Billy uncovers the full horror that has wrecked the lives of Lucy and Donna.
