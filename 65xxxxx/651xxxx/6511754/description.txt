Band:The Enid 
Genre:Symphonic Prog Rock
Year:2011
Origin:UK

The Enid is a British rock band founded in 1973 by Robert John Godfrey, Stephen Stewart and Francis Lickerish. Another early member was William Gilmour, who subsequently founded his own band Craft and now plays keyboards in Lickerish's band Secret Green.
