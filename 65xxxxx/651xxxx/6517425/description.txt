MAME 0.142u6 to 0.142 CHD Downgrade (Rollback CHDs)

by Lovok

Usage:
1. Copy all 0.142 Rollback CHDs over your 0.142u6 CHD Collection
2. Delete all zero size CHDs from your CHD Collection, then delete all empty directories from your CHD Collection
3. Now your 0.142u6 CHD Collection is downgraded to 0.142 CHD Collection
