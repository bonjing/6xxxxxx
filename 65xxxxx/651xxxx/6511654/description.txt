2 Duncton Quest

Read By: Gene Foad

Duration: 36:10

Description

Tryfan, son of Bracken and Rebecca, has been entrusted with a special task: to accompany the old scribemole Boswell to the Holy Burrows of Uffington, and to carry the seventh Stillstone to its rightful resting place. But a dark evil is spreading over the land and when they arrive they find signs of death and destruction ... and one frail survivor - Spindle. An epic tale of good and evil in the secret world of the moles.
