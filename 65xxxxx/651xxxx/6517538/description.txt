I\'m a big fan of the Romanian/Russian musical classic Rock \'n Roll Wolf (Med 
Grimm og Gru in Norwegian or Ma-ma in Russian). With all it\'s charm and 
technical prowess, what really makes this production shine is arguably the 
consistently splendid music. The musical is positively brimming with a great 
variation of exuberant, smart and joyful songs and themes.

Alas, the English version has never surfaced on DVD, neither has anyone 
taken up the brilliant idea of making an official soundtrack album. I 
decided to do my best to rectify this, and this is the result. Probably the 
closest you\'re gonna get to an OST.

The source I\'ve used to make this soundtrack is a TV rip from Norwegian 
Broadcasting. Therefore, don\'t expect top notch hifi quality. 

However, considerable effort is put into improving the clarity and 
crispness of the sound as much as possible. A process commonly referred to as 
\'remastering\', I guess. Hopefully you\'ll agree that the wonderful sounds of 
Rock n\' Roll Wolf is worth the effort.

With all that in mind, please enjoy this release. I certainly do!
