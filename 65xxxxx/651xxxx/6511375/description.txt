Predavanje o Aleksandru Makedonskom - dr. Jovan I. Deretic

Mesto: Poljoprivredni Fakultet u Novom Sadu, 2005.

Originalni naslov predavanja: Lesandar Veliki [napomena: lesandar = dar sume]

Alexander the Great of Macedonia, lecture, hidden history, in Serbian. Controversial but not more than mainstreem history.

Cited works: Gordon Childe, Ivan Gundulic, John Fine (prof., univ. michigan), Ivo Vukcevic (prof., univ. santa barbara), Enc. Britannica,... including overwhelming  ancient sources.
