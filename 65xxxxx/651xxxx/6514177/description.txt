Mock the Week is a British topical panel game, hosted by Dara Ã“ Briain. It is made by independent production company Angst Productions and made its debut on BBC Two in 5 June 2005.

Created by Dan Patterson and Mark Leveson, the same people responsible for the comedy game show Whose Line Is It Anyway? Mock the Week is usually described as a cross between Whose Line Is It Anyway? and Have I Got News For You.

At the end of each series is a clips show that features highlights and outtakes from the most current shows (this trend started due to the London 7/7 terrorist attacks that prevented most of the guests and production crew attending the recording, and so after positive feedback for the first clips show, was repeated for subsequent series.)
