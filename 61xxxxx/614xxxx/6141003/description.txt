Katherine Marie Heigl is an American actress. She is best known for her role as Dr. Izzie Stevens on ABC's [b]Grey's Anatomy[/b] and her starring role in the movies Knocked Up, 27 Dresses, and The Ugly Truth.

Heigl married Josh Kelley in Park City, Utah, on December 23, 2007.
