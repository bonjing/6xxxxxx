Midsummer Rock is a television film based on the Cincinnati Pop Festival. The TV version consisted of 90 minutes air time and featured Alice Cooper, Mountain, Grand Funk Railroad, The Stooges, and Traffic.

It was filmed on June, 13, 1970 at Crosley Field in Cincinnati, Ohio. The show was based off the 1970 Cincinnati Pop Festival. On television it lasted 90 minutes including commercials with 5 five acts being The Stooges, Alice Cooper, Traffic, Mountain, and Grand Funk Railroad. The film itself was never released on DVD or VHS but it does occasionally air on TV or is exerpted from time to time.
