PLOT

Carrie, a beautiful college student is in love with her boyfriend Mark but is feeling the stress of the relationship as Mark demands all her attention. A final blowout fight leads to the pair parting ways. Newly single, Carrie begins a sensual relationship with fellow student, Jack. Only after the great sex does she realise she misses Mark but when she goes to Mark's apartment hoping for reconciliation, she finds him engaging in an erotic romp with Ava, a hot brunette. Stunned, Carrie tries to forget Mark which leads her into numerous lustful adventures.
