Justice League: The New Frontier is a 2008 direct-to-video animated film adaptation of the DC Comics limited series DC: The New Frontier. The film was written by Justice League writer Stan Berkowitz, with Darwyn Cooke, the writer and artist of The New Frontier, serving as story and visual consultant.

The video has received a rating of PG-13 for violent content/images and was released on February 26, 2008.[2] It is the second in the line of DC Universe Animated Original Movies released by Warner Premiere and Warner Bros. Animation; with the first release being Superman: Doomsday and the next release being Batman: Gotham Knight. The film made its broadcast premier on October 18, 2008 on the Cartoon Network.
