Latin Adultery - Diamond Kitty 

Actors: Diamond Kitty, Danny Wylde

Release Date: February 03, 2011

Diamond and her husband are staying in a bed and breakfast recently inherited by Danny. When the two of them bump into each other in the sitting room, he inquires about her stay, and they get to talking. Turns out she's accompanied her husband on a business/pleasure trip, and today he's going to be away in meetings. Left alone, she wants to know if there's anything fun to do in town. Danny reports that it's totally dullsville, and aside from bird watching there isn't much to do. Well, she may be married, but if there's nothing to do, and she's got a good looking man in front of her, well, work with what you've got. Making a pass at him, Danny tries to fight her off, but he's no match for a bored, sex-crazed, fiery Latina on holiday.
