=====================
D E S C R I P T I O N
=====================
UberIcon creates a more customizable atmosphere on your desktop by extending
Windows to perform new effects when you launch your icons and folders. The
effects are plugin-based to give you control over the look and feel.

===============
F E A T U R E S
===============
-Improves the look of Windows
-Extrememly efficient
-Plugin-based for complete customization
-Integrates with your Desktop, Explorer and most software
-Unicode compliant
-Simple plugin SDK for creating new effects
-And best of all...its FREE
