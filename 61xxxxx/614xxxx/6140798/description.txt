Publisher: Falcoware Software Company

Freeware


Fight nasty pirates! Recently, Gloomy Jack betrayed his crew, so they staged a mutiny
and threw his ass overboard! How dare they? But he survived. The traitors have landed
on the island to celebrate, but little do they know, Gloomy Jack has followed them! The main goal of the game is to figure out how to kill the enemy. There are several methods to carry this out. Use your wits to get revenge on the traitor pirates. Nice graphics and music will help you swim in a cruel but interesting pirate world.


SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS:

Windows 95/98/ME/2000/NT/XP/2003/Vista/W7, 64 MB RAM, Pentium-133 MHz, 7 MB hard disk space
