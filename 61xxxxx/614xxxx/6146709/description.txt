The Growing Pains of Adrian Mole

Adrian Mole (Gian Sammarco) continues the journey through adolescence which began in The Secret Diary Of Adrian Mole. His parents, George (Stephen Moore) and Pauline (Lulu) are now separated. Pauline has become involved with Mr Lucas (Paul Greenwood), while George now has a relationship with Doreen Slater (Su Elliot). Adrian still worries about his acne and is still obsessed with the lovely Pandora Braithwaite (Lindsey Stagg).
