Lorna Doone

From BBC Radio 7. Written by RD Blackmore; Full cast dramatisation starring David Schofield, Alison Pettit and Janet Dale; Adapted by Barry Letts; Directed by Brian Lighthill.

Ep. 1: John Ridd meets and falls in love with Lorna Doone.

Ep. 2: Carver Doone tries to starve Lorna into marrying him, but John Ridd comes to her rescue.

Ep. 3: Lorna is taken to London, and John thinks he has lost her for ever, until fate brings them together. But Carver Doone, John's enemy, has murderous plans.
