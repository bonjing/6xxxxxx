For students new to the rules of writing, or those who need a quick and easy refresher, this book provides 20 lessons that promote an in-depth understanding of writing in a short amount of time. This skill builder includes full tutorials in easily confused word pairs and using verbs for strong, active writing, a pretest to diagnose strengths and weaknesses, a posttest to measure progress, hundreds of practice questions, and more!
