ABOUT THIS SET

protocol:legacies is a tribute. 

Trance has explored many avenues of sound leaking into and influencing countless 
other genres. Its impact on world music cannot be mistaken. This set is a tribute 
to all who have created, spun, promoted, purchased, danced to, tripped to, fell in
love with, fell in love to, had sex to, yelled at, broadcast, simulcast, grew up 
with, never grew out of, and has a soft/hard/psychotic spot for trance of all shades.

With this set we aimed to leave a lasting impact on the listener, and firmly 
understood the only way to achieve this was to shatter the limitations of 
traditional mix set media. 

Unprecedented in mix-set culture, this 10 HOUR MP3 ONLY behemoth contains no less 
than 102 tracks, and pulls together multiple aspects of trance into one piece -with 
tracks that are dance floor favourites, nostalgic triggers, popular anthems, acidic 
tinglers, dark brooding emotional pieces, progressive rollers, and even some lesser 
known gems and rarities. 

With a set of this magnitude, we naturally came across unpredictable technical 
issues; some of which are still residual in the set, so bear with it, they are minimal.

We believe that music is meant to be free to infect the ears of and inspire the hearts 
of everyone, so your obligation is to keep this set alive by passing it along, copying 
it, playing it, and burning it; thereby spreading the gift. We also believe in giving 
credit where credit is due, so whenever you pass it along, please include this file 
and its track list with the mix set.
