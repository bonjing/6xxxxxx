XENOPHOBIA WHY?
vSpy vSpy\'s timely release during the Australian Bicentenary year of white-mans habitation of the island: Terra Australis. This album contains some poignant tunes that were relevant to the changing times in Australia & the world. This album was recorded at Rhinoceros studios in Surry Hills, Sydney, with Les Karski & David Hemmings on the control board. The band was at it\'s height in popularity in Australia and the strong themes in this record were taken on the road internationally for the first time after it\'s release.
