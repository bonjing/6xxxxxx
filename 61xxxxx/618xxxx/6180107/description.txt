Description
iDemo is a tool that simplifies real-time demonstrations of iPhone apps and games. iDemo connects to your iPhone over WiFi and mirrors your iPhone screen on your desktop, in real-time â€“ kinda like Steve Jobs during his keynote presentations.


System Requirements
In order to use iDemo, you need a computer running one of the following Operating Systems:
- Mac OS X 10.5 or higher
- Windows XP Service Pack 3 or higher (including Windows Vista & Windows 7)
and a jailbroken iPhone running one of the following applications:
- ScreenSplitr
- Display Recorder

IMPORTANT NOTE: iDEMO ONLY WORKS WITH JAILBROKEN IPHONES AT THE MOMENT.
