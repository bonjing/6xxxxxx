Spy vs Spy are an Australian ska/pub rock band from Sydney formed in 1981. They became known for tackling social issues through their music, including racism, inequality and homelessness. A powerful live outfit: they made their mark in Australian music as a lively 3-piece band who released 6 studio albums and various compilations. This album was released by Sony Australia, the only album featuring Mark Cuffe on drums, recorded at 301 studios Sydney
