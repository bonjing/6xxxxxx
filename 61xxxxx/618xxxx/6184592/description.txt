FOUR FRESH LEMONS
Spy vs Spy are an Australian ska/pub rock band from Sydney formed in 1981. They became known for tackling social issues through their music, including racism, inequality and homelessness. A powerful live outfit: they made their mark in Australian music as a lively 3-piece band who released 6 studio albums and various compilations. This is their our EP recorded in 1982 with Michael Vidale and released on Green Records
