UNRAR WITH WINZIP OR WINRAR
1) Open the 'Theme' folder. which you download
2) Drag both 'Future of Dark.theme' and the 'Theme' folder to 'C:\Windows\Resources\Themes\'
3) Right click on desktop -> Personalize...there you should see in Installed Themes.Click on it to apply theme.
