Glassjaw is a four-piece rock band from Long Island, New York. The band is fronted by vocalist Daryl Palumbo and guitarist Justin Beck. They have been influential in the progression of the underground music scene in the eastern United States and United Kingdom and are known for their intense live shows.  The Coloring Book EP was given to fans during the 2011 tour.
