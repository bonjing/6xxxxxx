From BBC Radio 7. Written by Thomas Hardy; Full cast dramatisation starring Janet Haw; Directed by Nick McCarty.

Episode 1:

When shepherd Gabriel Oak meets Bathsheba, a cycle of passion and pain begins.

Episode 2:

Farmer Boldwood starts to play a major role in the tale of Bathsheba Everdene.

Episode 3:

Farmer Boldwood and Gabriel Oak continue to vie for Bathsheba Everdene's affections.

Episode 4:

Bathsheba's defences are breached to devastating effect upon meeting a dashing soldier.

Episode 5:

Sergeant Troy's cruel treatment of Fanny comes back to haunt him.

Episode 6:

Still courted by her would-be suitors, will Bathsheba ever find love?
