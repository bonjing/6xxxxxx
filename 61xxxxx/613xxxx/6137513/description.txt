Bible Stories (Dramatized) for children.  To purchase the tapes or CDs, please visit the following website:  http://yourstoryhour.org/

This is the MP3 format.

This is the second in the series which contain the following stories:


B13-1 Dangers in the Desert.  Israelites face dangers and challenges in the wilderness.
B13-2 The Ten Commandments.  God gives the Isralites the 10 Commandments and Moses goes up Mount Sinai.
B14-1 Miriam the Great.  Moses\'s sister, Miriam, becomes jealous.
B14-2 The Man of Patience.  Moses not allowed to go into the promised land.
B15-1 The Unfaithful Prophet (Part 1).  Balaam employeed to curse Israel.
B15-2 The Unfaithful Prophet (Part 2).  Baalam\'s plans to curse Israel are defeated.
B16-1 The Thief Who Defeated an Army.  Joshua and the Israelites defeat Jericho, but the next battle is lost because Achan took items from Jericho, against God\'s command.
B16-2 The Longest Day in History.  The day the sun stood still.
B17-1 Conquering Canaan.  Joshua\'s leadership in establishing Israel. 
B17-2 Mighty Gideon.  Gideon defeats the Midianites.
B18-1 The Champion of Champions.  The life of Samson.
B18-2 The Story of Ruth.  The story of the faithful daughter-in-law, Ruth.
B19-1 The Wayward Sons.  Eli\'s wicked sons.
B19-2 The Youngest Priest.  Samuel\'s ministry as a child.
B20-1 The Two Battles of Aphek.  The ark of the conveant is captured.
B20-2 The Tall, Strong, Handsome, Young Man.  Saul is chosen to be the first king of Israel.
B21-1 King Saul Saves a City.  Saul battles the Ammonites.
B21-2 The Crown Prince on a Secret Mission.  Prince Jonathan and his armor bearer attack the Philistines.  Saul disobeys God\'s commands and Samuel tells him his kingdom will taken away and given to a neighbor.  God sends Samuel to annoint David.
B22-1 The Boy and the Giant.  The story of David and Goliath.
B22-2 The Deadly Plot.  Saul begins to plot David\'s death.  David and Jonathan become friends.
B23-1 The Sword of Goliath.  David asks Ahimelech for Goliath\'s sword.
B23-2 David Escapes.  David escapes for his life from Saul.
B24-1 King Saul and a Cave.  David spares Saul\'s life when he has a chance to kill him.
B24-2 A Wicked Rich Man.  The story of Abigail, David and Nabal.
