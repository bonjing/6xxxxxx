Cody Robert Simpson (born 11 January 1997) is an Australian pop/R&B singer-songwriter from Gold Coast, Queensland, who is currently signed to U.S. record labels Atlantic Records and Poe Boy Music Group



4 U is the first extended play (EP) release by Australian singer, Cody Simpson. It was released on December 21, via Atlantic Records. The songs are mostly pop/R&B influenced. [1]

The album's lead single, "iYiYi", was released on June 1, 2010.
