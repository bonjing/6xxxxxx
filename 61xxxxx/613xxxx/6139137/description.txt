House of orphans



A family moves in to a house that used to be an orphanage back in the 19th century. This orphanage was owned by a very rich couple who both were highly respected in the area, but what people don't know doesn't hurt them. The couple sold the children to be workers, used for sexual purposes, and some were even killed. The couple who ran the operation were revealed and executed, but before they died, the woman pronounce a curse,on both the city and the orphanage. The orphanage burned down, but the foundations remained. On that basis, they re-built the house again

In 2005, when this family moves in, strange things start to happen as the teenage girl of the family and her friend stumbles upon a locked door in the basement.
