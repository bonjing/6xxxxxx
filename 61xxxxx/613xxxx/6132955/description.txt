A is a 1998 Kannada movie directed by Upendra. It starred Upendra and Chandni as the lead couple.

The movie was dubbed into Telugu, and remade in Tamil as Adavadi.

It was the first film of Upendra as a hero, as well as Gurukiran's first film as music director.

The film was a hit and broke some records, earning Rs 200 million.

It was described by a reviewer as "loud and disjointed, like the ramblings of a delirious mind, but made a lot of sense". Its design also received some praise.

It provoked some controversy over anti-women remarks and was alleged to contain autobiographical elements
