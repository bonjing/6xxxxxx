Lou Reed - Berlin (1973/1981) RCA Records
-----------------

Berlin is a 1973 album by Lou Reed, his third solo album and the follow-up
to Transformer.
In 2003, the album was ranked number 344 on Rolling Stone magazine's list
of the 500 greatest albums of all time, though the publication called the album a "disaster" 30 years prior.
