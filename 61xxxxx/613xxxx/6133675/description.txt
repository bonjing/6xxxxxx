The Fixx - The Sign of Fire [live promo].VOB



Very HQ rare live promo music video. "The Sign of Fire" is a song by The Fixx, from their second studio album Reach the Beach, released in 1983. The album reached number eight on the American albums chart, their highest to date there. It also contains their most well known and highest charting single, "One Thing Leads to Another", which reached number four on the Billboard Hot 100.

The Fixx are an English rock band formed in London in 1979. They are one of the few bands to have enjoyed significant success outside their country of origin, notably in the United States and Canada, yet remain almost unknown in their home land. 

Their other hits include "Red Skies", "Stand or Fall", "Saved by Zero", "Sign of Fire", "Are We Ourselves?", "Secret Separation", "Driven Out", "How Much Is Enough?" and "Deeper and Deeper", which was featured on the soundtrack of Streets of Fire. "A Letter to Both Sides" appears on the Fletch soundtrack.

The Fixx are recording their 10th studio album "Something Ahead of You" due for release in April 2011.
