Be the first to try and enjoy the best hit - Farm Mania: Hot Vacation! Our friends Anna, Bob, Grandma and Grandpa are coming back again! This time while being on vacation they decided to take part in the Best Farmer competition. Help them to prove everyone that they are the best!
