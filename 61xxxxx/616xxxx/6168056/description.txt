Crysis 2 is an upcoming first-person shooter video game developed by Crytek and published by Electronic Arts, due for release on Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3, and Xbox 360 in March 2011. It is the sequel to the 2007 video game Crysis and its parallel expansion game, Crysis Warhead.[2] The story was written by authors Richard Morgan[3] and Peter Watts,[4] who has also written a novel adaptation of the game. It will be the first game to showcase the CryEngine 3 game engine.
