"Born This Way" is a song by American recording artist Lady Gaga. It was released as the lead single from her forthcoming second studio album Born This Way on February 11, 2011. Written by Gaga, the title track was produced by herself, Fernando Garibay, and DJ White Shadow.


The video was shot the weekend of January 22 - 24, 2011, and is described to be a "profound, so inspiring and so incredibly beautiful" music video.
