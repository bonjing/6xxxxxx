Storyline
Since closing the door to a violent past,
 quiet and thoughtful Moses Stanton everyday existence is running a small neighborhood store,
 and watching over his daughter who doesn't know he exists.
 When a young man, Malik, comes in claiming to be a black Muslim that is doing good for the neighborhood,
 Moses takes him on as a partner but soon realizes that Malik is nothing but a drug dealer seeking
 to destroy the neighborhood and Moses's daughter.
 Therefore, Moses must become the man he used to be in order to save his beloved neighborhood and his daughter.
