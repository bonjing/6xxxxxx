Mum's Cooking for Lads Away from Home | 160 Pages | PDF 

Cutting the gastronomic umbilical cord doesn't necessarily imply canteen catering or steady diets of junk food for those men living on their own. This pocket-sized guide written by Dominique Ayral and wittily illustrated by Jean-Pierre Cagnat shows that cooking well can be economical and enormous fun. It is full of hints and tips to add those personal touches of color, originality and zest that will impress dinner guests and friends alike!
