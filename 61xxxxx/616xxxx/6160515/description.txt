2010 Demetrius Holloway

Part 3 is a cold-blooded examination of the Eastside drug wars of the 80's... "Last Man Standing" starts the investigation tracing the origins of the Eastside drug lords that capitalized on criminal activities by turning them into family enterprises...with men like Maserati Rick, Demetrius Holloway and Whiteboy Rick, this documentary is a sweeping saga of bloodshed, betrayal & big business.
