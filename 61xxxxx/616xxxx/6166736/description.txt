Description
'The Blue Max' is a raging war time thriller featuring spectacular aerial combat sequences. It's the story of Bruno Stachel (George Peppard), a cold, ambitious German combat pilot in World War One. As brave as he is ruthlessa, he excels in combat, wins the highest medals, The Blue Max, and becomes a national hero...

'The Blue Max' is among the best aviation films ever made with outstanding photography by Douglas Slocombe ('2001: Space Odyssey), spectacular dogfights and a dramatic score by Jerry Goldsmith.
