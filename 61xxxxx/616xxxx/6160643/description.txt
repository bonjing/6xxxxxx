Whiteboy Rick White Boy Rick, "The King Rat" Breaks His Silence...Richard Wershe was a teenage drug dealer during the 1980's that became widely known as White Boy Rick...at the age of 15, Rick was meeting with Detroit Police & federal agents at local restaurants to sell them info on crooked cops, city officials & big time drug dealers...during this time, White Boy Rick became one of the underworlds top prodigies associating with city officials like Mayor Coleman Young, & Detroit homicide detectives. Drug enforcement officials ended relations when there was no more info needed from him..at the age of 17, he was convicted of drug trafficking and has been serving a life term since 1988.
