Description
The unexplainable attack on the residents of Bodega Bay by birds of all shapes and sizes is the subject of this classic Hitchcock thriller. Thousands of real birds and nearly 400 trick shots make reality of the seemingly impossible, and lend an air of sheer terror that you will never forget. Based on the novella by Daphe Du Maurier and written by Evan Hunter, The Birds is Hitchcock at his very best.
