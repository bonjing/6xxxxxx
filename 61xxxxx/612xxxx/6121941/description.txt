This all-star reunion features vintage sixties footage and live performances by some of the greatest Motown stars. Michael Jackson sings 'Billie Jean' and teams up with The Jackson Five. A medley of hits are sung by Marvin Gaye and Stevie Wonder. Smokey Robinson joins Linda Ronstadt to sing 'Tracks Of My Tears'. The all-star line up includes the historic reunion with Diana Ross and The Supremes. This programme captures the magic of Motown sound yesterday, today, and forever.
