Description: This social romantic comedy explores the issues young Arabs face in today's Middle East with dating, love, unemployment and more. Rida, a young college graduate, has been trying for months to get a job, without any viable prospects. Due to a sudden family crisis, he is forced to work at the Cairo train station selling tickets. There he meets Malak, a college student with dreams of a career and a happy life, and their relationship becomes the backdrop for social issues, cultural obstacles, and the hopes and dreams of all young Arabs.

Starring: Karim Abdel Aziz, Mona Shalabi, Lotfi Labib, and Said Tarabeek.
