Epeidi den noeitai katoxyrosi stin Elliniki mas Glossa.

Ayto to Leksiko sas prosferetai dorean.



Sevasteite ayto to gegonos kai MIN to svisete apo ton ypologisti sas oste na mporei kai o epomenos patriotis mas na to apoktisei dorean opos kai eseis.




To leksiko ayto einai aparaitito se kathe Ellina, idiaitera se kairoys toso dyskoloys gia to Ethnos poy dexetai syntonismeni kai epimoni alloiosi tis Istorias toy, tis Glossas toy, tis Thriskeias toy, ton Paradoseon kai ton Aksion toy poy to kratisan zontano xiliades xronia.

Oso perissotero tha milame sosta Ellinika, toso tha isxyropoioymaste san Fyli kai Ethnos.

I meleti tis Ellinikis Glossas apotelei kathikon.
