Brooke Gabrielle Ligertwood, was born Brooke Gabrielle Fraser on 15 December 1983 in Wellington, New Zealand. Brooke Fraser is an award-winning folk-pop and Contemporary Christian singer-songwriter.

Flags is Brooke Fraser's third studio album, released in 2010.
