Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan, probably the most famous Qawalli and popular folk singer from pakistan. Gaining the adoration of millions of fans across the world and of notable musical talents such as Peter Gabriel and famously Jeff Buckley.

Here he was captured live in London just months before his untimely death. Savour the whole experience of his magical concerts and detailed insight with an interview with NFAK himself.

Songs include:

Allah hoo
Akeeyan Udeek Diyan
Kina Sonah Tenu
and many more


Songs sung in Farsi, Urdu, Hindi and Punjabi Subtitled in Englished throughout
