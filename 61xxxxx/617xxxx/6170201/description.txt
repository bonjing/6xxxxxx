A miserable widowed housewife goes looking for her daughter one day and faints in a belly-dancing cabaret. She is gradually drawn to this art-form, and anonymously becomes quite a star. She likewise has a brief fling with the drummer, before he goes off to propose to a woman his own age.

Meanwhile her daughter has fallen in love with a fine upstanding man, a drummer, and she is bringing him to meet her mother...

Re-seeded from somehere or other. This is one of the best African movies.
