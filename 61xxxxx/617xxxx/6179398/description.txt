RSM is young and energetic band that represents a new wave of Polish dark electronic projects.

Their music does not follow the usual dark electro patterns, but instead it takes inspiration from many other idustrial genres. The outcome of those explorations is fresh and aggresive dark electronic music.

"Realizuj Swoje Marzenia" (Translates to "Follow Your Dreams") is packed with action and very dancefloor-worthy. The release is complemented by remixes.

Halotan Records
