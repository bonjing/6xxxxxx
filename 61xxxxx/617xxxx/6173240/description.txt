Our World - Somalia's Forgotten Suffering  2011 02 05   BBC World News

180.07 MB/21 mns/xvid

Zeinab Badawi travels to parts of Somalia rarely seen by the outside world, and to neighbouring Djibouti, to witness the suffering of those who have fled the 20 years of violence.


Refugees fleeing the violence of southern Somalia reach Puntland and Somaliland only to be subject to unrelenting poverty and sexual violence....others risk being transported to Yemen, Saudi Arabia and on to the Gulf States only to fall into slavery.
