Description
Three horror stories seen through the eyes of a cat. Written by Stephen King.  A stray cat is witness to strange and suspenseful events in the lives of humans in three different cities. In New York, Richard Morris is trying to quit smoking. In Atlantic City, Cressner, a powerful professional gambler makes a mortal bet. In Wilmington, NC, the ten year old girl Amanda is threatened by a troll, living inside the wall of her bedroom. 

Adapted by Stephen King himself from three of his own stories, CAT'S EYE is a gem of black humor film, combining elements of suspense, comedy and horror.
