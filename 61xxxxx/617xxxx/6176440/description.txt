The French Lieutenant's Woman

From BBC Radio 7. Written by John Fowles; Full cast dramatisation starring Jonathan Firth, Kelly Reilly, Elizabeth Spriggs and T P McKenna, with John Hurt as the narrator; Dramatised by Graham White; Produced by Peter Kavanagh.

A young fossil-hunter in Lyme Regis is intrigued by a mysterious woman in black who stares out to sea. He learns about her disgrace after an affair with a French sailor, but this whets his appetite to find out more about her.
