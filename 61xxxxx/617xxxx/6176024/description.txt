Alfred Hitchcock Presents was an American television anthology series hosted by Alfred Hitchcock. The series featured dramas, thrillers, and mysteries.
Alfred Hitchcock Presents, 25 minute Episodes, aired weekly at 9:30 on CBS on Sunday nights from 1955 to 1960
