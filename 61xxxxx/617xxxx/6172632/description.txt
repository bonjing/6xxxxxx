This book is in greek language, so the comments are in same language too.


H meleth ths istorias bebaiwnei oti ta politeumata-ka8estwta anatrepontai me treis tropous:
- apo epanastasin,
- apo polemo kai
- e3 aposun8esews.
Epi paradeigmati to ka8estws tou Tsarou anetraph me epanastasin, to nazistiko ka8estws me polemo kai to sobietiko ka8estws e3 aposyn8esews.

Sto biblio auto e3etazetai h anatroph politeumatos-ka8estwtos apo epanastasin kai analyontai ola ta sxetika me to koinwniko fainomeno twn epanastasewn, pou apoteloun istorika gegonota.


Meta tous polemous, to perissotero aima xynetai se epanastaseis. Epomenws oi deiloi na mh diabasoun auto to biblio, dioti den tous afora.
