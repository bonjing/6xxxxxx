Ministry of Sound present their annual guide to what's going to be big in club land over the forthcoming year. Clubbers Guide 2011 kicks off with the BBC Sound Poll Nominated Tips for 2011 Wretch 32 with 'Traktor' & Nero 'Me & You'. We celebrate the comeback of the almighty Eric Prydz with 'Niton' and Radio 1 playlist records by Yasmin, Danny Byrd & Magnetic Man to name but a few
