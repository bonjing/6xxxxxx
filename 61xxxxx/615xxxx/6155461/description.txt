Bejeweled Twist Review
PopCap's Bejewled is considered the cornerstone of the casual game genre. Bejeweled Twist is the latest evolution of the series. Instead of swapping pairs of gems, you now rotate quartets to set up gem matches. It upends the flow of the game, adding a wrinkle of long-term strategy to the fast-paced match three puzzler. The DSiWare Bejeweled Twist is a pared down edition of the PC original, stripping away a handful of game modes and leaving the player with just a single-player run and two-player battles over the Nintendo wireless connection
