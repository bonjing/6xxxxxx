The magic of Engelbert Humperdinck is captured on this amazing DVD release filmed live in front of an adoring crowd. Join the legend as he takes you on a musical journey performing some of his unforgettable hits such as 'Quando Quando', 'How To Win Your Love', 'The Last Waltz', 'Dance The Night Away' and 'Release Me'. The show is both memorable and moving, displaying a quality that could only be Engelbert Humperdinck, the man who has rightfully earned the title 'The King Of Romance'.
