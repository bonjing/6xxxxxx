              Fast and Loose


Season 1 Episode 4



Fast and loose is a BBC television series on BBC Two. Guests take part in numerous sketches in which each comedian inhabits a certain character or movie genre. The current series will be eight episodes long and is hosted by Hugh Dennis.

The style and format is very similar to Channel 4's Whose line is it anyway?

Guest include Greg Davies, Justin Edwards, Pippa Evans, Humphrey Ker, Marek Larwood and Laura Solon and former guest on Whose Line Is It Anyway? Wayne Brady.
