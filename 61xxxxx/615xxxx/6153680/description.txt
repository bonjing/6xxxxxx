Speedy Soft FotoBeschriften v4.1.1 Build 2.2.11 Bilingual
The program developed from the need to provide photos with decorative labels with different design possibilities. The program is very compact. One dosnt only have to go on several windows or menu levels or move again and again by lining up several steps with new surfaces and selections toward result.


The inscription is meaningfully for photos which one would like to :


- bond into photo albums
- pass on to others
- look at together in the circle of friends
- attach to mails
- attach to letters
- publish in the Internet
- use in a photo calendar
- use in so-called photo books
- publish in photo pools in the Internet
- publish in Facebook or in other forums
- protect with the publication by a watermark
- use with specific text effects in DIGISTUDIO
presentations
- show in simple slide shows on a digital
picture frame
- show in simple slide shows on the
television
