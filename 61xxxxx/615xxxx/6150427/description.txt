Almukhtar Al-Thaqafi: The avenger of Hussain Bin Ali Episode 2

The life and times of the Arabian military leader who Avenged Imam Hussain Bin Ali Bin Abi Taleb, by killing all who had part in the murder of Imam Huassain and his companions in Karbala.
The work is an Arabic dubbed Epic Iranian Series that stretches over 20 years from the time of Mu'awiah.
