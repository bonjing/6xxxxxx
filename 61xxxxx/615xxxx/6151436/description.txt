This DVD captures The Manhattan Transfer in the studio as they sing five, classic American standards. These exclusive, intimate performances feature the group's trademark vocal harmonies with piano accompaniment. the Manhattan Transfer recorded these songs at the legendary Henson Studios in Los Angeles during the group's 35th Anniversary celebration. 

Tracks: 

1. Route 66
2. Embraceable You
3. On The Sunny Side Of The Street
4. Someone To Watch Over Me
5. A Nightingale Sang In Berkeley Square
