DAZ 3D Studio & Poser scenes with .pp2 files (props) and png files (icons).8 images with suggestions how to place the objects.

* Drag and drop the "Runtime" folder over the content folder in : x:\your Daz main folder\content.


* The files are in "Props" area in DAZ;only few(2) files(props object) will be seen in Scenes and Props>Interior. 

* If you rename or move folders dont forget to open .pp2 files with Notepad,seach for "Runtime" word every time and retype the new location,click yes when ask to overwrite.If you rename the icons or .pp2 files try to have same names,no need aditional edit only if you rename the folders.

* To locate/delete files/folders go to your daz main folder\content\Runtime\libraries\Props\House of Nosferatu .
