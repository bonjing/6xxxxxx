The Dutroux Compendium
Dutroux_Compendium.7z

A collection of video, audio, pictures and text publications about and related to the Dutroux affair.
This collection or "compendium" was for the most part compiled by an anonymous internet user or group of users (oh my gauwd I love teh internets!). As the original torrent lost interest (I remained the only seeder) I decided to stop seeding and make a new "release" of the compendium. I (re)structured the files, removed some duplicates and added new content. I also archived all files into an 7-zip archive to save bandwidth and make the whole thing more manageable.

See the file list below.

7-zip (for extracting the archive) can be downloaded here: http://7-zip.org/
(7-Zip is open source software. Mostly GNU LGPL licensed. Not only is it free, it's the best archiver currently around.)

Warning: Archive is 6.3 gig, unpacked it's about 6.7 gig. Even by today's standards that's a lot of bits and bytes. Make sure you have enough free space.

Good luck researching!

Warning 2: This stuff is not for the faint hearted. I warned you. I mean it.
