It's been a busy year and Skylar thinks it's about time for a vacation for her and her husband. She proposes the idea to him in the morning, saying they both need to get away from the office and kids for a while, and could use some fun in the sun in the Bahamas. To help sway him, Skylar takes advantage of his morning wood, and rides him all the way to the Caribbean.
