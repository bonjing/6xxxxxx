Luxor 5th Passage is a computer game, released by MumboJumbo in 2010. It is the 5th game in a series, being the sequel to Luxor, Luxor 2, Luxor 3, and Luxor: Quest for the Afterlife. This game's plot-line is based around the Egyptian gods, and your quest is to beat the levels in order to defeat the god Set. To play, you must hit strings of moving spheres with the matching colour. If 3 or more in a row of a colour is formed, then all three or more are destroyed. This game celebrates the 5th anniversary of the creation of the original game, Luxor, thus the name "Luxor 5th Passage".
