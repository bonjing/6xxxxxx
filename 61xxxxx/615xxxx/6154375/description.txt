Britannica Word Search
Endless Word Search fun!

You've never seen a word search like this before! Britannica Word Search has fun, challenging puzzles for everyone, stuffed with exciting twists and varied play. Plus, with multiple modes and categories you can enjoy fast-paced action, relaxing gameplay, and everything in between.

Features

    * Single or Random Categories: Choose your favorite category to play, or let the game randomly pick from all categories
    * Multiple Game Variations: Choose from over 500 puzzles with customizable play options
    * 4 Exciting Game Modes:
    * Levels Mode: Win points, beat the clock, and gain access to the next level of new puzzles.
    * Frenzy Mode: The computer will re-scramble your puzzle after you colve it-and shorten your time limit
    * Times Mode: Race the clock to see if you have what it takes to be the ultimate word-finder
    * Relaxed Mode: Play puzzles at your own pace.
