   A period action adventure set in the days when a healthy paranoia about 
   witches was considered de rigeur, Knights Contract follows the tale of 
   Heinrich, a cursed executioner of witches, and young girl Gretchen,
   a reincarnation of a witch whom Heinrich previously had the job of 
   be-heading and who returned the favour by cursing him eternally.
