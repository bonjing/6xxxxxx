Mechtogan Mayhem

After Drago's new powers endanger everyone in Interspace, Dan and Drago decide to go to a private sector of Interspace for some intense training. They also hope to unlock what is causing their nightmare. While they are training a mysterious group comes across them and forces the two into battle. Can Dan and Drago over ome this latest challenge, or will they find Drago losing all of his powers to this new mysterious force?
