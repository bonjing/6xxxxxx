I Am Emma Humphreys

From BBC Radio 4; Written by Shelley Silas; Full cast dramatisation starring Joanne Froggatt, Stephen Critchlow, Delroy Brown and Susan Jameson; Directed by Claire Grove.

The true story of Emma Humphreys, who in 1985, aged 16, murdered her pimp, Trevor Armitage, who had found her homeless on the streets of Nottingham.
