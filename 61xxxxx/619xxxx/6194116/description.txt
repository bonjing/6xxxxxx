I will simply say that this is THE box to have if you happened to be a Twins fans, or simply
enjoy those 80's tunes you listened when you were young. Each CD is Remastered with an
excellent quality while suplemented with extra songs. Besides, the 2nd disc for each set is a
real gift for us fans who craved for these rare versions and remixes [in my case, living in
Argentina, it was just like a treasure hunt to find those remixes back in the old days].
Listening again to all these tunes, after so many years [25 years to be precise] 
it's like rediscovering the band again.
