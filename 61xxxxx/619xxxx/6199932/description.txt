Lord of War is a 2005 action-drama film written and directed by Andrew Niccol which stars Nicolas Cage.

It was released in the United States on September 16, 2005, with the DVD following on January 17, 2006 and the Blu-ray Disc on July 27, 2006. Cage plays an illegal arms dealer with similarities to post-Soviet arms dealers Viktor Bout and Leonid Minin. The film was officially endorsed by the human rights group Amnesty International for highlighting the arms trafficking by the international arms industry.
