Microsoft Office Publisher is the tool within the Microsoft Office Suite
responsible for the layout of printed and electronic publications in various formats.

Unlike a word processor, the layout with Microsoft Office Publisher
is oriented visuals, allowing you to easily add images and graphics
along with text in columns perfectly aligned with the other elements of each
page.

Microsoft Office Publisher 2010 has become an important application in
Office. So now also incorporates ribbon interface (Ribbon) for further
integration with other programs and, of course, for greater accessibility and
customization of features by the user.

Creativity is an important part in the layout. However, Microsoft Office
Publisher also provides design work through their grids and templates
color schemes. Thus, creating any type of document is a process and structure
fast and uncomplicated.

The advantages of Microsoft Office Publisher 2010 also show themselves in their choice of
export. Thanks to the formats supported, you can export documents
directly in JPG or PDF. However, work still needs to be compatible
input formats Indesign or QuarkXpress.

FEATURES:

NEW

* New customizable interface Ribbon
* Document Templates, grilles and color schemes
* Layout accessible to anyone
* Export to different formats


Microsoft Office Publisher supports the following formats:
Admission: PUB, TXT, MHT, HTML, RTF, DOC, DOX, WPD, WPS
Output: PUB, TXT, GIF, JPG, BMP, DOC, DOCX, WMF, PS, XPS, HTML, MHT, PDF

Processor: 500 MHz
