Crescent is an album released by Gackt on December 3, 2003. It is conceptually linked to its predecessor Moon and comes with booklets for both records (Moon did not originally contain one). Crescent also features a duet with L'Arc~en~Ciel vocalist Hyde ("Orenji no Taiyou") with whom Gackt co-starred in the 2003 movie Moon Child.
