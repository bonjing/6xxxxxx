Hawkwind - 25 Years On - Limited Edition Box Set (1994)
------------------------------------------------

Hawkwind are an English rock band, one of the earliest space rock groups.
Their lyrics favour urban and science fiction themes. They are also a
noted precursor to punk rock and now are considered a link between
the hippie and punk cultures.
