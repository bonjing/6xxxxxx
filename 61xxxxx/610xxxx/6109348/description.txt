THX 1138 LaserDisc Rip by me ogvh5150

Xvid Codec and 256kbps mp3 audio. Captured to huffy codec. Post-Audio processing was done in Sound Forge 5. Two track mono Audio. 

I included the "Buck Rogers" intro since I think it was George Lucas' idea to show the viewer the contrasts of the "bright" future as opposed to a "dark" one where there is a state controlled society. A society where sexual intercourse is banned and there is medication for everything that ails you. And a state sponsored religion.
