Created by a dedicated team of Mac developers, Corel Print House 2000 for Macintosh is a powerful, yet easy-to-use home publishing solution. Design spectacular greeting cards, calendars, banners, and more with thousands of customizable templates and more than 80,000 graphics. Use the photo-editing tools in Corel Photo House 5 (also included) to touch up digital images and add them directly to your projects; then publish to paper or the Web with ease. Complete with templates from PaperDirect, Avery, and Kodak, this all-in-one package makes graphics projects a breeze.
