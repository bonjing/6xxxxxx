This fourth and final installment of the 14-year-long Rite series collects together the first (and, until now, lost) 1990 kraut funkathon "Too Stone" with the latest 2005 unsignposted funkathon "So Tough". Meditational music that's also good for sex and ritual, driving and deep theta meditation. 8-page booklet includes explanation of the Rite series manifesto, modus operandi and musical aspirations.
