Color Management for Digital Photographers For Dummies

Shooting great photos is only half the battle tweaking the details in your digital darkroom makes the difference between so-so images and spectacular ones. This book is packed with examples to guide you through understanding colour basics, correcting brightness and contrast, getting the exact tone and hue, and creating perfect prints in full colour, of course!

- Shows digital photo enthusiasts how to ensure that the colour in an image file is accurately represented and reproduced, from camera or scanner to monitor or printer

- Packed with colour management solutions that will help novices create picture-perfect images

- Discusses the main components of colour management including understanding light, matching digital images to displays and prints, working with colour spaces, calibrating a printer, and using colour management tools

- Familiarizes readers with the various colour management tools that help calibrate consistent picture quality
