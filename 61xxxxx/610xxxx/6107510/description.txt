The premiere episode of the talk radio show Prometheus Unchained with hosts Dale Everett, Neal Conner, and Stephanie Murphy. The show deals with GLBT issues from a liberty perspective.

This episode we discussed what the ongoing weekly show will be about, how we chose the name, the Peaceful Assembly Church in Grafton, NH and their policy on gay marriages, a bill to get the NH government out of marriage, and whether there is a higher incidence of atheism amongst gays and lesbians.
