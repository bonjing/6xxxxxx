The bulk of the first and second discs were recorded at Chicagos New World Music Center, during the 1994 Counterparts tour and the 1997 Test for Echo tour. The third disc is taken from a performance at the Hammersmith Odeon in London during the, A Farewell to Kings tour in 1978.

The album is dedicated to the memory of Neil Pearts daughter Selena, who died in a car accident in 1997, and his wife Jacqueline, who died of cancer 10 months later. The text, Suddenly... You were gone... from all the lives you left your mark upon. In loving memory of Jackie and Selena, is printed inside the foldout cardboard sleeve. The lyrics featured are from the song Afterimage, from their 1984 album Grace Under Pressure.
