Music from the 1972 classic movie "Deliverance".
In 1955, Arthur Smith composed a banjo instrumental he called "Feudin' Banjos" and recorded the song with five-string banjo player Don Reno. Later the composition appeared in the popular 1972 film Deliverance as "Dueling Banjos"
played by Eric Weissberg and Steve Mandel.
