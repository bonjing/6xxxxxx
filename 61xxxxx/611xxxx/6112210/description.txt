The Changeling
by Thomas Middleton and William Rowley

First broadcast on BBC Radio 3 on 20091129

A Jacobean classic, set in Alicante, Spain, in the 1920s.
Beatrice-Joanna is due to marry Alonzo e Piracquo, until she falls in
love with Alsemero and seeks the help of her father's man, De Flores.
