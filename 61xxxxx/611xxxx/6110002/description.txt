A collection of 40 B-side tracks from Alison Moyet's solo career vinyl and CD single releases. 

These tracks were either never included on any of her albums or they were not included on an album at the time (some of them were re-recorded and placed on later albums). There are no Yazoo B-sides included in this collection - only the songs that Alison has released as a solo artist. 320 audio quality.
