The first two films of E. Elias Merhige's experimental 'philosophical horror' trilogy (He has not yet, at current, made the third). Both films are in avi format.


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

~~~~~~~~~ BEGOTTEN (1990) ~~~~~~~~~ 
God disembowels himself with a straight razor. The spirit-like Mother Earth emerges, venturing into a bleak, barren landscape. Twitching and cowering, the Son Of Earth is set upon by faceless cannibals. 

Running time: 78 Minutes

~~~~~~~~~ DIN OF CELESTIAL BIRDS (2006) ~~~~~~~~~
Din of Celestial Birds is a trip through the evolution of consciousness.

Running time: 15 Minutes
