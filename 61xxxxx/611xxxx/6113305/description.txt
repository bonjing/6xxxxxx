This year's Million Dollar Cash Game will be filmed in High Definition and will take place at an exclusive venue in London on the8th and 9th September. It will be an invitation only event, with the world's poker elite being personally invited by sponsor Full Tilt Poker. Season 5 will broadcast on Sky Sports from January 2011.

James Hopkins, Founder and Managing Director of Emblaze Productions, talked about the fifth season of the show on the Emblaze Productions press release earlier this year. "We are delighted that Full Tilt Poker have continued their association with the Million Dollar Cash Game and we are expecting the strongest player line up yet for season 5".
