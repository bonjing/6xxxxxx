Armando Iannucci's Charm Offensive is a radio comedy programme broadcast on BBC Radio 4 which it 

shared with The News Quiz & The Now Show. 

It is hosted by Armando Iannucci and each week a panel of comedians make satirical and surreal 

observations on current events.



In April 2007, Series 2 was awarded a Sony Radio Award in the comedy category. The judges felt 

that this was a great example of brilliant observation with surreal twists, taking the listener 

on an unpredictable journey and bringing a fresh slant to the format."
