Amazon.com - The ICONS series on the Green Umbrella label is a range of compilation CDs offering recordings from various dates and venues of some of music's most famous artists. This Triple CD offers many of the popular pieces from operas in which Pavarotti performed, including those by Donizetti, Bellini, Mozart, Puccini, Verdi and Rossini. Many are taken from live performances, as is evident from the rapturous applause which greets their ending, and some feature one or more accompanying artists, which sadly are not mentioned (nor are the orchestras or choirs) in the sleeve credits.
