Checking on her neighbor, Brooklyn Lee finds that Mr. Pete is pushing boxes around and in the process of moving. He lost his loan with bank, so he's leaving under less than favorable circumstances, but Brooklyn is also taking it hard because she's thought about fucking him for some time. Quick on her feet, she figures out a way to brighten both of their days. She quickly gets her feet in the air, he pushes her box around, and she takes it even harder. Moving doesn't have to be a bitch.
