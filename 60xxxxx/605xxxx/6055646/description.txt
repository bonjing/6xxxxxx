Critically acclaimed debut-album of Dutch garagerock-band New Killer Stars.

Produced by Mark Robertson, bassplayer and producer of Th' Legendray Shack Shakers.
Recorded at the famous Echo Mountain Studio in Asheville, NC and the Stainless Steel Studio in Nashville, TS.
