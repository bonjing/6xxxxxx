Govi's fascinating blend of global grooves and lush guitar melodies is his signature style that has deeply touched listeners since his debut in 1988. On SAFFRON & SILK, his sensuous electric and acoustic guitars, spiced with the exotic sounds of Indian sitar, Greek bouzouki and Turkish oud, capture the romance and mystery of ancient trade routes. With alluring melodies and a modern flair, SAFFRON & SILK is a unique journey that is sure to please old and new fans alike.
