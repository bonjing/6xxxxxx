Feature #1
House of Dark Shadows                          http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0065856/

House Of Dark Shadows, based on the very popular TV Gothic soap opera, follows the 

life (or is that AFTERlife) of Barnabas Collins. Recently unleashed from his 

coffin by local drunk, Willie Loomis, the vampire (Barnabas) goes on a killing 

spree, while at the same time charming his present day family members. In the 

process he meets local girl Maggie Evans and notices that she looks exactly like 

his deceased fiance Josette. Barnabas assumes that she is the reincarnation of   

Josette, and plans to make him his unholy bride for eternity.


Feature #2
Night of Dark Shadows                          http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0067491/

A painter and his wife move into a home and find themselves plagued by ghosts and 

spirits of his ancestors that used to be witches.
