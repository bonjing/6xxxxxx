Roxanne Hall, Chastity Lynn, Seth Gamble, Zoe Voss, Wolf Hudson, Mickey Mod


Sweet Sinner's award winning series returns with volume 3 as older/younger passions again unite. Gorgeous older blonde Roxanne Hall takes young daughter Chastity Lynn on vacation to a summer cottage - and to escape the pain of a recent divorce. But when Chastity meets hot teenage boy Seth Gamble, Roxanne is overcome with lust for the dangerously sexy young stud. Chastity invites Seth and his single dad Mark Wood over for dinner, and Wood falls for Roxanne on sight, but Seth and Roxanne only have eyes for each other. Will Roxanne come to her sense and settle for Seth's dad? Or is her passion for a younger lover too strong to resist?
