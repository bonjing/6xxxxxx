Syphon Filter (PAL)
A terrorist organization steals a top-secret virus known as Syphon Filter, and they plan to use it to extort money from the world's governments. Only special agent Gabe Logan can stop them and keep the world safe. Take control of Logan has works his way through parks, subway lines, a museum, and a military compound. Along the way, Logan will be able to utilize a large number of weapons including a taser, machine guns, shotguns, and grenades. If he runs across an enemy wearing armor, he will have to take them out with a quick shot to the head. While blasting the bad guys is great fun, there are times when stealth will make a task much easier. Stop the terrorists and return the virus to the proper authorities in SYPHON FILTER.
