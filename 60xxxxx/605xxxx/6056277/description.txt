Channel 4
Monday, December 20th, 2010
9:00pm to 10:00pm


Introduced by his two daughters, Poppy and Daisy - and with the help of some seasonal animation - Jamie Oliver explains how to cook the recipes that will guaranted a stress-free day. After making easy work of succulent turkey, luscious gravy and roasted veg, the chef Jamie follows up with a winter pudding bombe, DIY chocolate truffles and mince pies. All washed down with Jamie's own recipe for mulled wine.
