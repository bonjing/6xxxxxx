Copenhagen: Elements of Life World Tour
is a DVD of Tiesto's Elements of Life World Tour November 10, 2007 performance 
at Parken Stadium in Copenhagen, Denmark.
The DVD was released March 7, 2008.

It was released on DVD and Blu-ray. 
Both double disc versions cover over four hours of Tiesto's concert.

With more than 25,000 people present, the atmosphere captured on video resulted in 
a fantastic impression of what the Elements of Life World Tour is about.
