Deep_Frieze
By: SleepResearch_Facility
Genre: Dark Ambient
2007

The album is described as "Ultra deep, glacial Dark Ambient, based around Antarctic co-ordinates". Each of the five tracks is associated with a particular location in Antarctica.

"...there is a certain comforting warmth in the encroaching slumber of hypothermia..."
