At Jesus' Side(2009)- Christian animated movie with Romanian subtitles

The teachings of Jesus come to life through the story of five misfit animals on a journey of love and forgiveness.

A vagabond street dog named Jericho follows Jesus throughout the last week of His life. After Jesus' arrest at Gethsemane, Jericho rallies an epic charge of animal friends to save Jesus from His fate.

Jericho's grief at the Crucifixion becomes joyous celebration when he witnesses Christ's resurrection from the dead.

Features the voices of Lucas Grabeel and Roma Downey.

At Jesus' Side is a journey of love and forgiveness that will quickly become a favorite for the entire family.
