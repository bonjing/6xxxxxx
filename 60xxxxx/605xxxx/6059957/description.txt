Ballville The Beginning games NEW



Traveling orbees has broken their spaceship on a far undiscovered planet. Explore this wonderful world and build a new city for them in this innovative match 3 game! Help the brave captain to find the missed crew members, solving interactive hidden objects and breathtaking mini games. Use skills of each orb to achieve the goal faster. Find out a lot of interesting details about this bouncing folk in Orbopedia. Join orbees in their dangerous adventures and discover lots of new. You will love this new world!

System Requirements:
OS: Windows XP/Vista/7
CPU: 1.0 GHz
RAM: 256 MB
DirectX: 8.0
Hard Drive: 110 MB
