Orgullo, Pasion Y Gloria: Tres Noches En La Ciudad De Mexico is a live CD by American band Metallica. 
This was recorded during three concerts at the Foro Sol in Mexico City on 4, 6 and 7 June 2009 during World Magnetic Tour 2009 tour promoting their 
album Death Magnetic.
