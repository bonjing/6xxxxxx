The Complete Season 1 of The Cleveland Show, an American Animated television series created by Seth MacFarlane, began airing on September 27, 2009 and ended after 21 episodes on May 23, 2010.[1] Production of the 22 episode season began in May 2008 and was expected to begin broadcast in January 2009 but was later pushed back to September 2009.
The DVD was released as a "Complete Season" featuring all of the aired episodes. It was released in the Region 1 on September 28, 2010, and was released in the Region 2 on October 11, 2010.
