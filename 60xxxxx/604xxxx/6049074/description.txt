Nova Natura means new nature, a pure nature that everyone dreams about to live in some day. After months of preparation out artists have given this vision life in this music trip. Nova Nature is a radio show, broadcast on 5 radio stations around Greece, hosted by Nick Miamis aka Side Liner. Its purpose is to present the beauty of chilling sounds and promote their magic to a wider audience.
