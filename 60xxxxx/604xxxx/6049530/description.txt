After a life spent seeking pleasure and decadence, Casanova seeks his one true love, Henriette. 

This is a great TV Miniseries, featuring Peter O'Toole and David Tennant. Released by the BBC it is a great comedy and drama that draws you in and bonds you with the character. You will watch this over and over again as it is one of the best performances of the two title actors. Seed and enjoy this is a hard to find version of the classic tale Casanova.
