The return of the dating show which helps lucky lads and ladies find love. Each male contestant faces 30 single women, who turn their lights off if they don't fancy a date as the candidate tells the ladies about himeslf. Tonight's show features a personal trainer from Reading, a frisky farmer from Scotland, a cage fighter from Norwich and an architect from Hampshire. This year each couple is whisked off to an exclusive dating destination to see if they can find love in the sun. Presented by matchmaker Paddy McGuinness.
