Unabridged and read by Christopher Lee. 3 hours 42 mins duration. 3 Cassettes. (Fraunhofer CBR 32 kbit/s 22050 Hz Mono, jetAudio 6.2+).


In Christie's celebrated courtroom drama, "The Witness for the Prosecution", ladies man Leonard Vole is accused of wooing a spinster Emily French to win her heart and control her money.

A vicious blow to the woman's head ended the brief affair, and her handsome suitor was promptly arrested. Vole's trial is complicated by the damning testimony of his only alibi - his wife!

Other mysteries included in this collection are "The Red Signal", "The Fourth Man", "S.O.S" and "The Mystery of the Blue Jar".
