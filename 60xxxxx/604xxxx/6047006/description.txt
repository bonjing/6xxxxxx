Air Raid is a 3D shooting action game presented in the most graphical, realistic battle simulation that offers up an adrenaline-pumping, action-packed experience for every game player. Air Raid begins with an impressive cinematic introduction that puts you in first person view behind an anti-aircraft gun on the bow of a World War II battleship. From a 40mm Anti-Aircraft fixed deck gun aboard the battleship, the players mission is to blast away at storms of enemy attackers to save the ship.
