Golden Earring - Long Blond Animal [TopPop 1980].VOB



Very HQ very rare uncensored tv show promo performance. AVRO's "TopPop" was the first weekly pop program broadcast on Dutch television. It ran from September 22, 1970 to June 27, 1988.

"TopPop" was mainly filled with pop artists in the top pop studio, playing to their latest hit. During the years of 1970-1974, 1978-1981 and 1986-1988, the artists were selected by the choice of the viewer. In the years 1974-1978 and 1981-1986, the National Charts were used instead.

"Long Blond Animal" is a song from Prisoner of the Night, an album by Dutch hard rock band Golden Earring, released in 1980.
