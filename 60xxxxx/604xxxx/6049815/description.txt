Ultramarines: The Movie is a 70-minute sci-fi thriller CGI movie set in Games Workshop\'s fictional Warhammer 40,000 universe and based around the Ultramarines Chapter of Space Marines. The screenplay is to be written by Black Library author Dan Abnett.

Terence Stamp, Sean Pertwee and John Hurt head the cast of voice actors.
