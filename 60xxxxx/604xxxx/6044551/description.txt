Around the World in 80 Days

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Jules Verne; Dramatised by Terry James; Starring Leslie Phillips, Jim Broadbent, Yves Aubert et al.

Episode 1: An Overhurried Departure

Eccentric gentleman Phileas Fogg's new valet Passepartout is hoping for a quiet life.

Episode 2: Distractions in the Jungle

Phileas Fogg and Passepartout buy an elephant in India and come to the aid of a Princess.

Episode 3: The Missing Acrobat

Passepartout sets off for Japan alone. How will Phileas Fogg and Aouda catch up with him?

Episode 4: Burning to get Home

Fogg takes drastic action over the Atlantic, but will he get to the Reform Club in time?
