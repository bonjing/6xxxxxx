Mystery Science Theater 3000 - MST3K - Complete 35 DVD Collection

This is a complete collection of 35 DVDs with every episode ever made (except for the first three KTMA episodes of which no fan copy is known to exist.)

Codec is DivX or XVid. The size of each episode is ~700 MB.

Source: Most episodes are from the "Digital Archive Project" (DAP) http://www.dapcentral.org , some are commercial DVD rips, some I just got. The old DAP-releases were converted to XVid for usability.

I've been collecting them for some time and these are the best quality versions I could find until now.

Check out Mike Nelson, Bill Corbett and Kevin Murphy's new project: THE FILM CREW ( http://www.filmcrewonline.com , all episodes are available onand RiffTraxand http://cinematictitanic.com/

Covers for every DVD are included (PDF for easy print out and PSD if you want to change them).

This release is compatible with the 35 DVD collection I postet a couple of years ago. So if you have these DVDs already you can move them into the folder with this torrent, rehash and continue seeding.
