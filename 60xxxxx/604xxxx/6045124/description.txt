Classic British comedy.
		
35mm, black and white, 85 mins
 
Director	Marcel Varnel
Production Company	Gainsborough Pictures
Producer	Edward Black
Screenplay	John Orton, Val Guest, Marriot Edgar
Original story	Frank Launder
Photography	Arthur Crabtree
Cast: Will Hay (William Porter); Moore Marriott (Jeremiah Harbottle); Graham Moffatt (Albert Brown); Sebastian Smith (Charles Trimbletow); Agnes Laughlan (Mrs Trimbletow)

An incompetent stationmaster is posted to an apparently haunted station in Northern Ireland, where he and his equally inept assistants stumble across the activities of a ruthless gang of gun-runners.
