Masterpiece of Argentine cinema showing the struggles of downtrodden frontier labourers circa 1910. The approach is more forthright than you'd get from Hollywood. Director-Star Hugo del Carril was something of a revolutionary in real life.

One of the delights is hearing so much Paraguayan harp music (it is set on the Paragaguayan frontier, where they are gathering yerba mate).
