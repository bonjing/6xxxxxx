Tomas Kalnoky is the lead singer, guitarist, and songwriter for the bands Streetlight Manifesto and Bandits Of The Acoustic Revolution. He is the former lead singer and guitarist for third-wave ska band Catch 22 but left after only one album, 1998's Keasbey Nights.
