La Boum (US title: The Party) is a 1980 French language motion picture comedy directed by Claude Pinoteau and starring Claude Brasseur, Brigitte Fossey, and Sophie Marceau. The movie was an international box-office hit. The music was written by Vladimir Cosma. Richard Sanderson sang the song "Reality".
