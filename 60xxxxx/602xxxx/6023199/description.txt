Description: 

Frank West, a freelance photojournalist on the hunt for the scoop of a lifetime, pursues a juicy lead to a small suburban town only to find that it is being overrun by zombies! He escapes to the local shopping mall thinking it will be a bastion of safety, but it turns out to be anything but. It's a true struggle to survive the endless stream of enemies, but with full reign over an entire shopping center, Frank can utilize anything to fight off the flesh-hungry mob.  

Features:

  -  Survive in a shopping mall overrun with an army of undead where everything
     and anything is a weapon.
  -  Move environmental objects like tables or garbage cans. Chow down on various
     "mall food" to revive health.
  -  Snatch items from different stores to use as weapons, including golf clubs,
     fry pans and more!
  -  Context sensitive controls pop up as need allowing you to perform complex
     actions with a tap of the button.
