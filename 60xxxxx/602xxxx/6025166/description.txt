Imagenomic Portraiture

Portraiture plug-in allows you to retouch digital photos. The plugin allows to remove skin imperfections, while maintaining the texture and other important portrait details such as hair, eyebrows, eyelashes, etc. In the plugin settings you can specify the force of impact, mitigation, level of detail and sharpness adjust, change the tone, brightness and contrast. A unique feature of the program is the built-in automatic mask that helps you quickly and automatically detect a range of skin tones in an image, and, if desired, you can manually configure it to provide the best results and unprecedented accuracy and performance. Portraiture comes with presets for the effect settings at the touch of a button, and you can create your own sequence of actions, customized to your specifications.

Features:
Processing images in any format
Integrated support for ICC
Support for RGB, LAB, CMYK and Multichannel
Support for using special presets
Several modes preview the result
