Although the bitrate is less than in my DS9 release, the video quality is even
a bit better for this VOY rip because I used higher encoder settings (and the
average DVD quality is maybe a little better too).

The DVD material is not perfect though.  Interlacing is very erratic: In some
episodes, progressive video changes to interlaced in the middle of a scene.
Most episodes use very much interlacing, some others (most notably the first
season) have only a few seconds of interlaced video, if any.  I got very
good results by using the yadif deinterlacing filter for all episodes.

Also, on the DVDs, the pitch of the English audio was too high due to PAL
speedup.  I did a pitch adjustment, so now it sounds fine.
