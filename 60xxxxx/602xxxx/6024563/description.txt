"Christmas Album" was released for Christmas 1981 in Germany ("Mary's Boy Child - The Christmas Album" in the UK). This album became the basis for several Christmas compilations of the group: "The 20 Greatest Christmas Songs Of The World" in 1986, "Happy Christmas" 1991 and "The Most Beautiful Christmas Songs Of The World" in 1992.
