Award-winning actress Claudine Barretto and former real life couple Anne Curtis and Richard Gutierrez will stir your emotions in this extraordinary drama directed by Mac C. Alejandre.

Ciara (Claudine) goes to America to better provide for her sister Julia's needs.

Back in the Philippines, Julia (Anne) lives a fast life and hooks up with a photographer named Storm (Richard).

The two also make it to the U.S., only to find out that life there is a struggle.

To help Storm obtain an immigrant status, Ciara, who is now an American citizen, agrees to marry him.

But this supposed marriage in paper alone turns into something else.

Caught in this heartbreaking situation, Ciara, Storm and Julia will have to choose between love and loyalty.
