Hitchens-Dembski Debate November 2010

Creationist, William Dembski made such a poor showing of this debate against atheist Christopher Hitchens that the 

Prestonwood Baptist Church, hosting the debate has begun filing DMCA notices against YouTube channels displaying and 

commenting on the debate.
Prestonwood Baptist Church has made it clear that this debate was intended to further the cause of preparing religious 

arguments against evolution and not to further questioning or truth.

This torrent is posted with the intention that the debate be exposed; and that it be made available to anyone who wants to 

compare the perspectives defended in the debate.


Prestonwood Baptist Church has also filed claims seeking to remove commentary videos from channels in which the debate was 

analyzed. Notable among these the YouTube Channels of AronRa and GreatBigBore.


This torrent contains the full debate, in its entirety and additional commentary by Christopher Hitchens.
Analysis commentaries provided by AronRa and GreatBigBore.
