For 15yr old Steve & his 13yr old sister Tamara , city life comes to a tragic end when their mother is killed in a road accident

Their parents divorced, their fathers whereabouts unknown, the kids go to live in the country with their uncle, the local cop Mike Henderson.

They quickly learn that country life in a sleepy coastal town is not so easy. gradually they make friends - firm friends - with cowboy & his gang. They share their adventures, their triumphs & disappointments, & they learn to love their new life
