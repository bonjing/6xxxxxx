Murfy Maths is the action-packed maths puzzle game where it is up to you to solve the sums!

Pick the numbers and use the symbols to solve the goal for each maths puzzle.

In Arcade Mode, advance through many levels of puzzles, whilst in Blitz Mode you are up against the clock. Mission Mode will throw different goals at you.

System Requirements:

OS: Windows XP/Vista/7
CPU: 1.2 GHz
RAM: 512 MB
DirectX: 9.0
Hard Drive: 74 MB
