          Chrono Rage is a fast paced shoot them up for the PC for      
          casual as well as hardcore gamers. Chrono Rage confronts      
          the player with a pixel swarm coming from the past and        
          ready for battle. Great dexterity, nerves of steel and        
          blazing reflexes are the weapons needed to tame this game.    
                                                                        
          Chrono Rage features:                                         
                                                                        
              * 20 time trial challenges                                
              * 1 survival mode                                         
              * 2 bonus modes                                           
              * 14 original enemies                                     
              * 21 achievements to unlock                               
              * XBox controller support                                 
              * Double analog gamepad support
