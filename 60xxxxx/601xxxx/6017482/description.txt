Stephen Mulhern brings audiences live and exclusive access to the Street as part of the 50th anniversary celebrations. This programme features unprecedented access to the show and includes immediate reactions to the week's shocking storylines from cast, crew, viewers and famous fans. Stephen is joined in the studio by Corrie cast members, TV critics and celebrity guests, while viewers can phone in and get direct access to the stars of the soap. Plus there is an exclusive look at how the tram crash was captured on film.
