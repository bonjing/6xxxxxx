Another chance to see where 50 years of TV history began with the very first episode of Coronation Street, originally broadcast on 9th December 1960. Newcomer Florrie Lindley takes over the reins of the corner shop, siblings Dennis and Linda cause headaches for their mother Elsie Tanner and Kenneth Barlow clashes with his father Frank.With Betty Alberge, Patricia Phoenix, Philip Lowrie, Anne Cunningham, and William Roache.
