Michael McIntyre Live And Laughing is the debut DVD of Britain's fastest rising comedy star.

Having stormed the TV shows "Live At The Apollo", "Have I Got News For You", "Mock The Week" and "The Royal Variety Performance", Live And Laughing was the mesmerising first show of Michael's sell out five night run at the legendary Hammersmith Apollo.

Michael McIntyre is electrifying, his energy, charm and pinpoint observations leave the packed house baying for more.

Michael McIntyre Live And Laughing also includes Michael's appearance on BBC1's Live At The Apollo.
