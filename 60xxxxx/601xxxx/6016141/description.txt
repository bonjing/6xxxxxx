Based on novel by Kaminaga Manabu.
Shinrei tantei yakumo aka psychic detective yakumo tells a story about a college student, Saitou Yakumo who born with different eye colors.
His red left eye gave him a special ability to see ghosts and spirits.
He believes ghosts and spirits are bound to earth because of a certain 'cause' and simply by eliminating that 'cause' will those spirits rest in peace.
Being asked for help by Ozawa Haruka, together they began the investigation.
