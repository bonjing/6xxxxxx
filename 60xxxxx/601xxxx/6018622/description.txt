Belle prepares the castle for Christmas against Beast's wishes, trying to bring him happiness for the 
season. Forte, a pipe organ, fears that Belle's plans may eventually bring about an end to the curse... 
the curse that brought him more importance in the Master's life. Forte uses Fife, a flute who
