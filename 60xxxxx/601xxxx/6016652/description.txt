CrimeCraft: Bleedout - is a multiplayer third-person shooter that combines dynamic shooting, with character development and interaction with other players inherent in modern online games. In the game there as battles between players and the battle with the bots and the performance of tasks.


Supplement BleedOut: Weekly episodes - each week a new episode is available, continuing the exciting story. The duration of passage of the missions will be 2-3 hours.
