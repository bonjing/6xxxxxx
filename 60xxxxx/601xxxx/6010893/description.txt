Besieger is Russian RTS game

For installation you don't need anything 
just start Strategy.exe for the english version or Strategi.exe for the Russian version

Multiplayer: (16) LAN, Internet

Minimum system requirements:

Windows 98/ME/XP/2000; CPU: 1.0 Ghz AMD/Intel Processor or Equivalent; RAM: 256 MB; Video: 32 MB Direct3D Compatible Video Card  (Geforce2 equivalent or higher); Sound: DirectX 9 Compatible Sound Card.
