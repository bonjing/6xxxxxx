This Morning I Met A Whale

From BBC Radio 7. Written and Read by Michael Morpurgo. Unabridged.

At sunrise, young Michael spots a whale on the shores of the Thames and thinks he must be dreaming. But the creature is real and brings a message for him - humans must mend the damage they are doing to the planet before it is too late.

Can he fulfil his promise to tell others when neither his teacher nor his classmates believe his story?
