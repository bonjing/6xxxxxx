Sean Croxton - Gluten Sensitivity and Celiac Disease with Dr. Thomas O'Bryan.










Dr. Thomas O'Bryan visits Underground Wellness Radio to discuss the physiologic and neurologic effects of wheat and other grains on the entire body. Topics will include the difference between gluten sensitivity and Celiac disease, the effect of gluten on the brain and nervous system, and the surprising variety of symptoms  of gluten sensitivity that are outside of the intestines. Dr. Tom's DVD, "Unlocking the Mystery of Wheat & Gluten Intolerance", can be found on his website, www.thedr.com. Hosted by Sean Croxton of Underground Wellness.
