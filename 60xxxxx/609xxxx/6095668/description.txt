From BBC Radio 7. Written by Charles Kingsley. Full cast dramatisation starring Julia McKenzie, Oliver Peace and Timothy West. Dramatised by Berlie Doherty; Directed by Janet Whitaker.

A young chimney-sweep who, cruelly treated by his master, runs away and falls into a stream, where he is magically transformed into a water baby. Two fairies, one harsh and one kind, take him on a series of adventures.
