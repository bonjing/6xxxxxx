A House to Let

From BBC Radio 7. Written by Charles Dickens, Wilkie Collins, Elizabeth Gaskell and Adelaide Anne Procter. Full-cast dramatisation starring Marcia Warren and Brian Croucher; Directed by Ned Chaillet.

Episode 1

Victorian tale of a woman and an unsettling sign of life in a derelict house.

Episode 2

Will discovering the history of the house solve the mystery of the strange watcher?

Episode 3

The derelict house mystery deepens when manservant Trottle finds people inside.

Episode 4

Trottle makes a disturbing discovery in the derelict house - in a locked room.

Episode 5

The derelict house finally reveals its sad secrets.
