The Boondock Saints (1999)

Fraternal twins set out to rid Boston of the evil men operating there while being tracked down by an FBI agent.


Director: Troy Duffy
Writer: Troy Duffy
Stars: Willem Dafoe, Sean Patrick Flanery and Norman Reedus

The Boondock Saints II: All Saints Day (2009)

The MacManus brothers are living a quiet life in Ireland with their father, but when they learn their beloved priest has been killed by mob forces, the duo return to Boston to bring justice to those responsible.

Director: Troy Duffy
Writers: Troy Duffy (screenplay), Troy Duffy (story
Stars: Sean Patrick Flanery, Norman Reedus and Billy Connolly
