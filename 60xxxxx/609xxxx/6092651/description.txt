This is a deconstruction of the 1998 Universal Home Video DVD of Dune (1984).  The feature is a 1.5 GB XVid AVI with 5.1 English and stereo French AC3 audio tracks.  English and Spanish subtitles are in separate sub/idx files.

Extras from the DVD are included in separate AVI files; one is a three minute trailer, the other includes the DVD menus, with production notes and cast and filmmaker credits.

I have never been happy with any of the available torrents of David Lynch's original 1984 2:16 cut of Dune, including one I made from DVD myself last year. Since then I have gotten better with the tools, so I decided to try again.  This version is cropped properly, so I got a much better picture with a smaller bitrate.
