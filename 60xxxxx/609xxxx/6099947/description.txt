Since it's a nice round value, I figured I'd do another small special. This time, the focus is on Jackin' House. In order not to make it too monotonous, I've added some underground Disco House and "regular" Deep House as well, but the track selection for those was carefully chosen to match the mood, speed and funkiness of Jackin' House. Also, I've added back two tracks that were present in the previous release but were bad recordings (one had some nasty pops somewhere at the middle while the other had its panning shifted towards the left channel too much and was a bit of boomy). The tracks in question are "Late Night Alumni - I Know You When (Members Only Mix)" and "Deep House Souldiers - Summer Love (Pastor Bobby Mix)" so in this release you'll find clean, 320kbps versions of them :)

As usual, most tracks in 320kbps or V2 with some older ones in 192kbps. Enjoy!
