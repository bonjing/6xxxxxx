   Release Notes:

   Dragged into Archeology by his uncle, Dan decides to take matters into   
   his own hands. He discovers ancient temples with long lost treasures     
   and menacing traps. Dan follows the clues found in the temples which     
   leads him on a trip across the world, bringing him ever closer to        
   making a sensational discovery. You will help Dan navigate past the      
   traps, collect treasure and reach the bottom of the temples where Dan    
   can find the treasure map that leads him further on his journey.         
                                                                            
   Addictive casual platform game with a twist!                             
                                                                            
   Make your way down the towers of treasure collecting Gems and gold!      
                                                                            
   Avoid the deadly pitfalls and traps in randomly generated levels!        
                                                                            
   Choose Diamond Dan or Diamond Ann and use their unique bonus moves!      
                                                                            
   Travel the world and discover 4 unique themes: Inca/Gothic/Persian and   
   a secret location!                                                       
                                                                            
   Polished gameplay and high production values!                            
                                                                            
                                                                            
   RIPPED: Nothing                                                          
                                                                              
   Install Notes:
         
   1. Unpack the release with WinRAR or equivalent                          
   2. Install by running unl-ddan.exe                                       
   3. Start game with dd.exe                                                
       (if you want to play in german, use the german.bat )                 
   4. Enjoy!
