Amia is a college professor that finds pleasure in humiliating Jordan, one of the students, in front of the entire class. Knowing that he's not a strong reader, she has him to read a question from the text book. Jordan is completely embarrassed. When the class is over, he decides to stay and ask Amia why she treats him the way she does. Jordan has been patient enough with Amia and decides that she needs to be punished.
