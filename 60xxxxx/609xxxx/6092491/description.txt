Bing Satellites starts the year with an EP of ambient piano songs. These four songs are based around generative loops where each note is played on a loop, the length of that loop varying according to its pitch. They are sparse and beautiful.

Bing Satellitesis a prolific ambient shoegaze musician and producer from Manchester, UK. 
Bing has released several EPs and album on BFW, including a collaboration with OnSpiderPark, a collection of remixes and an ever-growing collection of live, improvised ambient music entitled Twilight Sessions. 
He also hosts a radio show on RebelRadio.FM - playing an eclectic selection of the best ambient, shoegaze, punk and electronica from around the world.
