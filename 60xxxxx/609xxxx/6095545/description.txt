The Doppelganger

From BBC Radio 7. Written by JCW Brook. Full cast dramatisation starring Nigel Anthony, Emily Richard and Elizabeth Lindsay. Produced and Directed by Ian Cotterell.

Adam & Jane are on a make-or-break second honeymoon; but at Oxford station, Adam sees his possibly dead mother and then at the hotel he meets Beth, a 25 year old girl with the mind of a child of seven, who looks like Jane...
