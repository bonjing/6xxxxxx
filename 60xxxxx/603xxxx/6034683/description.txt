This is the superb 1999 version of Charles Dickens' A CHRISTMAS CAROL starring Patrick Stewart. At the time this was filmed, Stewart was already famous for having performed the role of Scrooge for many years onstage. Here, it is give a full theatrical treatment in one of the best and most faithful adaptations ever filmed.
