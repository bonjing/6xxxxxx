    There were 2 reasons to make such thing. First, here are only 2 sources of official patches, IGN site (lack latest updates) and Atari site (speed sucks). They will not function forever, so it's best to be ready to face it when it happens. Second, it's just convenient to have all you need in one package. Simple as it is.
