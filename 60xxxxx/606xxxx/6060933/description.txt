Concluding part of the festive culinary programme, which blends live action with animation. Jamie Oliver prepares a selection of his favourite festive food, including his grandmother's recipe for Boxing Day brunch, and Caribbean-inspired jerk ham, as well as pancakes, strudels, Asian salads, clementine jelly and refreshing party drinks.
