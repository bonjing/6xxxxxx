Rokstarr is the second studio album by British R&B artist Taio Cruz. It was released on 12 October 2009 in the United Kingdom and on 11 June 2010 in the United States, as the singer's U.S. debut album. The album was originally titled T.W.O. (This Way Out). The album's first single, "Break Your Heart", with a remix featuring American rapper Ludacris, was a success worldwide, topping the charts of several countries, such as United Kingdom, United States, Australia, Canada and more. The second single, "No Other One" was released only in the UK and peaked at number forty-two. "Dirty Picture", featuring American singer Ke$ha, was released as the third UK single and was a top ten hit in the UK and Ireland. The album received mixed to average reviews from contemporary music critics. It debuted and reached number fourteen on the UK Albums Chart and number eight on the U.S. Billboard 200.
