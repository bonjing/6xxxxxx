A Christmas album by Ohio underground legend John Valentine. Turn up your bass and let your speakers bump for the Holidays for this album which has been called "the most depressing Christmas album I've ever heard" (Mixba Magazine) as well as "gloriously creepy" (Quilted Tattoo Weekly).

The Christmas Canon is worth the download alone.
