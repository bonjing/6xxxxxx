Summary\\Plot
xxxxxxxxxxxxxxx

A New York City ballet company is preparing for the production of Swan Lake, choosing to cast a newcomer as both the White and Black Swan. Dancer Nina Sayers (Portman) competes for the part alongside several other young dancers, including Lily (Kunis). Nina lives with her overbearing mother Erica (Barbara Hershey), a failed dancer who tries to control every aspect of her daughter\'s life.
