Perhaps best known as the motormouthed pimp Money Mike in Ice Cube's blockbuster hit Friday After Next (2002), the Cincinnati-born African American comedian, television star (The Boondocks, Nick Cannon Presents: Wild N' Out), and movie actor Katt Williams returns to his hometown for a night of comedy in the stand-up performance film Katt Williams: Live. In this raunchy, uncensored routine, Williams free associates on everything from sex, to fatherhood, to his presuppositions and disillusionment about life in the City of Angels. Alphonzo J. Wesson directs this eighty-five minute set.
