Plot:
-----
A group of friends are sailing on the yacht when a storm blows them off 
course and damages the boat. They soon land on a deserted island where 
they find a deserted, fungus covered wreck. They then find out that it was 
a research vessel examining the effects of radiation on plant and 
animal life. However, they also discover a strange fungus dubbed Matango. 
They also find out that the crew ate some of the fungus and were somehow 
driven mad. The group then goes about trying to find food and water 
without succumbing to the lure of the mushrooms. However, one by one 
they soon are driven by their hunger and desires to eat the mushrooms 
with disastrous effects.
