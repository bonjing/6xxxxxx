Description :
Christmas  Puzzle  offers  classic match-three fun with a seasonal twist Match ornaments, snowmen, candy canes and more  as  you  collect  gold  coins and clear tiles on 42 holiday-themed  levels.  Earn the  ability  to  use  six powerups  that  can  clear  entire rows or columns with a click  of  your  Christmas mouse. And decorate a virtual house  with  beautiful lights and ornaments. With bright, colorful  graphics  and  lively  holiday music, Christmas Puzzle  will  fill  you with the spirit of the season and the days and nights ahead with delight.

CPU 1.0 GHz
RAM 512 MB
DirectX 8.0 or higher

Install and play.
