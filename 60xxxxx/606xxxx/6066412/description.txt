Manmadan Ambu is a 2010 Tamil romantic comedy film directed by K. S. Ravikumar.[1] Written by Kamal Haasan, it stars himself alongside R. Madhavan and Trisha Krishnan in the lead roles,[2] whilst featuring Ramesh Arvind, Sangeetha, Manju Pillai and Urvashi among others in supporting roles. The film features music composed by Devi Sri Prasad, with several songs written and sung by Kamal Haasan himself, whilst Manush Nandan and Shaan Mohammed made their debut as cinematographer and editor.
