Vaishali [1988]

Writer : MT Vasudevan Nair

Director : Bharathan

Starring : Sanjay, Suparna Anand, Nedumudi Venu, Geetha, Babu Antony, Ashokan, Sreeraman, Parvathi

Plot : A young Brahmin named Rishyashrungan, who doesn't know what a female is, should do rituals to have rain in a country. Vaishali was appointed for bringing Rishyashrungan to the country by seducing him...
