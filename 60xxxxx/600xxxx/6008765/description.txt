The No. 1 Ladies' Detective Agency is a series of ten novels byauthor Alexander McCall Smith. 

The agency is located in Gaborone, capital of Botswana. Its founder is a Motswana woman, Mma Precious Ramotswe, who features as the stories' protagonist and main detective. 

The episodic novels are as much about the adventures and foibles of different characters as they are about solving mysteries. They have been adapted for radio and television.
