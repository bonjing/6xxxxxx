DRMBuster is an all-in-one media application to convert any type of purchased or copied audio
and video files, both protected and non-protected, to commonly used formats foruse on dozens 
of playing devices e.g. iPod, iPhone, Zune, any portable MP3 and MP4 players, CD and DVD 
players, mobile phones and many others. DRMBuster introduces the unique technology 
maintaining extremely effective PC utilization in order to perform high conversion speed and
reliable conversion results.
