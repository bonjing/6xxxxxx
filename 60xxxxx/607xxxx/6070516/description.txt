Description :
Finding yourself in a real fairytale is every child's dream a chance to feel the magic, to command the power of nature or even to perform heroic deeds. Catch a glimpse of a world where dreams come true and fairytales are real in Lamp of Aladdin, a unique Hidden Object game! Revisit the story of Aladdin as he finds a magical lamp that makes his dreams a reality! Insidious traps, wild magic, and a cast of colorful characters await our heroes.
