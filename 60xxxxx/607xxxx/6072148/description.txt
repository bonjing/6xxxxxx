Damian ,Jr. Gong, Marley is the youngest son of Bob Marley. His album, Halfway Tree won a Grammy for best Reggae album in 2002. In 2006 he won two more Grammys. Best Reggae album and Best Urban/Alternative performance for Welcome To Jamrock. He is the only Jamaican Reggae artist in history to win two Grammys in the same night.

1996: Mr. Marley
2001: Halfway Tree
2005: Welcome To Jamrock
2010: Distant Relatives (with Nas)
