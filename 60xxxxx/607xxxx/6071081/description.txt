Description :
Join the journey from penguin farm to icy Antarctica in Penguins` Journey, an exciting tile-based Brain Teaser game. Build bridges between the islands to help your Penguins island hop and make it home safely. If your penguins fall into the water, it is up you to save them by throwing life preservers. As each level is completed, the challenge becomes more difficult with more tiles, increasingly intricate bridges, pesky creatures presenting obstacles and less time.
