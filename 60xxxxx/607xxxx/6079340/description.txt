Missing Persons - Words [promo].VOB



HQ rare promo music video. "Words" is a song from Spring Session M, the debut album by American new wave band Missing Persons, released in 1982. The title of the album is an anagram of the band's name. Tracks from this album: "Words", "Windows", "Destination Unknown", and "Walking in LA" all had videos created and all received heavy play on MTV and AOR and modern rock radio. 

The visual effects used in this music video for "Words" were unusual for the time, making it popular on the fledgling cable TV channel MTV.

The singles "Words", "Mental Hopscotch", "Destination Unknown", "Walking in L.A." and "Windows" met with varying success, especially in the local markets of Los Angeles, New York, and San Francisco. The group toured and its shows were well attended.
