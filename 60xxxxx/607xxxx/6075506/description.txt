Recorded at the Odeon Theatre in London between 18 and 20 June 1977 and the Apollo Theatre, Manchester between 16 and 17 July 1977 and Released in the Autumn of 1977. It was 10cc's first live album.

Featuring  Graham Gouldman, Rick Fenn, Eric Stewart, Paul Burgess and Stuart Tosh and Tony O'Malley.
