KISS - Top2000 a GoGo
30-12-2010 (broadcast date)
18-06-2010 (recording date)


Top2000 a GoGo is a Dutch tv-program about
the best 2000 songs the people voted for.
All the songs are broadcast on radio2 from
2000 to number one.
The number one of 2010 was Hotel California 
from the Eagles.
The tv-show starts at 26-12-2010 and the last
one is at 31-12-2010.
On every show they have some short highlights
of one of the songs/bands.
