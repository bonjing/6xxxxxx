Tae Bo Cardio Workout
Get ready to burn calories and fat, while toning and strengthening your body. Tae Bo Cardio focuses on learning how your body operates in an aerobic capacity, applying weight training, flexibility, kicks and punches, while revealing this secret: it is the mind and will that control the body, and not the body that controls the mind and will. It's everything you've been looking for -- and more! (Approx. 44 mins.)
