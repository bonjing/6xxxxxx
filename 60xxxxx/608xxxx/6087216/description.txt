When Rodders receives an unexpected windfall, it strikes Del as the perfect chance for a trip of a lifetime to sunny Florida. However, despite sun, sea and 79 TV channels, the boys soon find trouble. Luckily their new friends - a top-notch Italian-American family - are around to help...

Back in the Nag's Head the last thing Raquel, Trigger and Uncle Albert expect to see is Del's mug on the evening news; on trial as a gangland leader! Will Del realise he's the Godfather's double before he and Rodney get an offer they can't refuse?
