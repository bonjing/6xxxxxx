Santiago Alvarez, founder of the Cuban film school and for years director of Noticiero ICAIC, toured various regions of the world to portray the events and significant personalities of the second half of the twentieth century. April in the Year of the Cat was a portrayal of the long struggle of the Vietnamese people for their independence in various historical periods, also covering its rich cultural tradition, in the soundtrack as well as the images. This film is itself an important witness to the final victory of the North Vietnamese Army, the U.S. withdrawal and the country's reunification after the fall of Saigon in the days of April 1975.
