SYNOPSIS

This excellent documentary on H.H. The 17th Karmapa is packed with interesting 
footage and material. With images ranging from his childhood at Tsurphu Monastery 
in Tibet, to visits to Bodhgaya and Sarnath in India, to his participation in the 
Kagyu Monlam, and wonderful clips of the 16th Karmapa. It also features several 
teachings by His Holiness and footage of Thrangu Rinpoche and Gyaltsap Rinpoche.
