Format: Monkey's Audio

The music here is often beautiful, haunting, delicate, and brutal. It is also vital music that doesn't hold on to any record label apron strings. Nels Cline is one of the most interesting guitarists working today. Recommended for adventurous listeners.

Musicians: Nels Cline (electric guitars), Woodward Lee Aplanalp (electric guitar), Carla Bozulich (electric guitar, sampling keyboard), Alex Cline (drumset, percussion), Bob Mair (electric bass guitar, electric guitar). G.E.Stinson (electric guitars), Zeena Parkins (electric harp), and Wayne Peet (clarinet, fake mellotron).
