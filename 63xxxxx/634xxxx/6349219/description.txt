Salsa Meets Funk.All original tracks are full of percussion but...no real drumbeat. Until now.
Frenz joins many salsa/latin jazz artists digitally by adding original,very funky beats to their music,making it a colloboration of salsa and fusion. The added sounds are mostly bassdrum,snare,hihat and crashes from real drumsets, and very nicely integrated into the songs with Goldwave,an audio editor.
