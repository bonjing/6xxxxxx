Presented in its original wide-screen format to preserve the spectacular action scenes, Airport '77 is highlighted by the work of Oscar-winning effects wizard Albert Whitlock. With George Kennedy recreating his role of Joe Patrioni from the original Airport and Airport 1975, this third installment of the successful series was directed by Jerry Jameson.
