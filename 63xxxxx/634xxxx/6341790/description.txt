UNIVERSAL HORROR
=================

A collection of 18 classic horror films from Universal Studios.

The unique collection includes some of the most memorable landmark films ever; thrilling classics that established a major genre.

Starring renowned actors Boris Karloff, Bela Lugosi and Lon Chaney Jr., in the roles and films that made them screen legends.


DRACULA COLLECTION
--------------------------------

01. DRACULA (1931) - with Philip Glass Score
02. DRACULA (1931) - original Spanish Language Version (with English subtitles)
03. DRACULA'S DAUGHTER (1936)
04. SON OF DRACULA (1943)
05. HOUSE OF DRACULA (1945)
