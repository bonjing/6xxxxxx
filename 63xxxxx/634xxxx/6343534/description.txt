Description
This epic about a mid-air crash and a desperate rescue effort features an all star cast, including Gloria Swanson, playing herself in her final film, and George Kennedy, recreating his role as Joseph Patroni.

En route from Washington, DC, to Los Angeles, Columbia Airlines Flight 409 is struck by a small plane, leaving its pilot, Capt. Stacy (Efrem Zimbalist, Jr), blinded and its co-pilot (Roy Thinnes) dead. Can chief stewardess Nancy Pryor (Karen Black) keep the giant aircraft aloft whilst her lover, former jet pilot Alan Murdock (Charlton Heston), is lowered by helicopter through the gaping hole in its nose? Helmed by veteran director Jack Smight, this star studded sequel to the Academy Award winning 1970 blockbuster contains twice the thrills, drama, and suspense of the original Airport.
