Solution Manual - The Science and Engineering of Materials 5th Edition by Donald R. Askeland and  Pradeep P. Phule

Product Description
Covers not only ferrous and non-ferrous metals and alloys, but also ceramics, composites and construction materials. Many new examples and illustrations are provided and there is greatly expanded coverage of fracture toughness and crack growth rates. Extensive new material on intermetallic compounds, and the kinetics of phase formations, discussed in respect to ceramics, polymers and metals.
