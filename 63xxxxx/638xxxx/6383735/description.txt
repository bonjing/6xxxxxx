London Underground in pictures (Kim Rennie)
==========================================================================

The London Underground in pictures - features rolling stock developments on Tube. Also included - London Overground, Docklands Light Railway and London Tramlink.

Includes many special workings and 'first day' events.

All photos taken from high-resolution digital images in good lighting conditions. Many of them have been included to the 'London Underground Review of 2009. A rolling stock photobook' and it's next 2010 edition.

==========================================================================

Author: Kim Rennie (http://www.flickr.com/people/24772733@N05/)
Date: 1989 - 2011
Quantity: 6807
Format: jpg
Quality: 255 X 200 - 1024 x 1024
