"Man Down" is a song performed by Barbadian recording artist Rihanna, taken from her fifth studio album Loud (2010). Written by Shama Joseph, Timothy & Theron Thomas , Corey Jackson Carter and Shontelle Layne and produced by Shama "Sham" Joseph, the song was sent to US rhythmic and urban radio on May 3, 2011 as the fifth US single. The song received positive reviews from the critics, calling it a comeback of Rihanna to Caribbean-tinged rhythm. The song has reached a peak of fifty-seven on Billboard Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs Chart.
