"I Wanna Go" is a song by American recording artist Britney Spears from her seventh studio album, Femme Fatale. "I Wanna Go" is a dance-pop and Hi-NRG song with a whistled melody and stuttering vocals, in which Spears sings about losing inhibitions.

"I Wanna Go" received mixed reviews from critics. Some praised the track for being effective and highlighted its hook, while others called it repetitive and dismissed the vocals as processed. "I Wanna Go" has charted in the lower regions of the US Billboard Hot 100 and the Canadian Hot 100, and has also topped the South Korean International chart for three consecutive weeks.

On May 13, 2011, It was confirmed via Spears' official websites that "I Wanna Go" will serve has the third single off Femme Fatale.
