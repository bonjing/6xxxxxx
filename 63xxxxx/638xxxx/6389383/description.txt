Conflict between Science & Religion - A Collection:


The main entries in this upload are the two classical works:

 -  History of the Conflict between Religion and Science (by: John William Draper, 1875), and

-  History of Warfare of Science with Theology in Christendom (by: Andrew Dixon White, 1896).
