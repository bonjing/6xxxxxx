BluePrint Simulations SPIM scenery by Pilot_7983

Installation:

1. Create a Folder in the main flight simulator 9 directory, called "BluePrint Simulations".
2. Copy and paste this ("Jorge Chavez Intl (Lima, PE) SPIM") folder into the "Blueprint Simulations" folder we created in step 1.
3. Start Flight Simulator and go into the Scenery Library, and "Add Area". 
4. Go into the BluePrint Simulations Directory once clicked "Add Area", and SINGLE click the "Jorge Chavez Intl (Lima, PE) SPIM" folder. Click Ok/Select.
5. Press Ok, and restart flight simulator.
6. Enjoy!
