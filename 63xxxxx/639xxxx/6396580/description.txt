Bill Sheffield was born in Atlanta, Georgia in 1951. He has been performing for over 32 years. Bill has appeared in concert with Muddy Waters, T-Bone Walker, B.B. King, Big Mama Thorton, Stevie Ray Vaughn, the list goes on and on. Bill has recorded and gigged with guitar great, Roy Buchanan plus his own bands, The East Side Blues Band, The XL's, Cool Breeze, and The Ringtail Rounders.

Bill is well known as a seasoned singer-songwriter with over 80 songs in his repertoire. The material, just like Bill's voice, is powerful and evocative.
