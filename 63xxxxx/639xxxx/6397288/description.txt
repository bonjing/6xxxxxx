"Right There" is the third single from American recording artist Nicole Scherzinger's debut album, Killer Love. It will be released by Interscope Records on May 17, 2011 in the US and on June 5, 2011 in the UK. The song was written by James Scheffer, Ester Dean, Frank Romano and Daniel Morris.
