DJ Screw: Soldiers United 4 Cash

This is the DJ Screw documentary. The untold story of DJ Screw and the Screwed Up Click. Features tons of interviews from SUC members, Screw's family, etc. If you like Screw music, this is a must see. This is a must see for ANY hip-hop music fan.

RIP DJ Screw, Pimp C, Fat Pat, Big Mello, Big Moe, HAWK, and all the fallen brothers.
