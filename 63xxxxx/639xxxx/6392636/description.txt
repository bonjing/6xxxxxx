Chevrolet TPI and TBI Engine Swapping

By Mike Knell

Chevrolet TPI & TBI Engine Swapping Guide shows how to install Tuned-Port injected and throttle body injected Chevy V8s into older, non-fuel injected vehicles. The information provided will also help with LT1 engine swaps. It covers the electrical system (and how to use the factory wiring harness), fuel systems, air cleaner and ducting, smog laws, overdrive transmissions, how to buy engines, and much more! It also has a chapter on Astro van V8 conversions.

An OK technical manual with instructions (18 Chapters and over 200 pages) that detail how to install an Electronic Fuel Injected engine into just about any car or truck.
