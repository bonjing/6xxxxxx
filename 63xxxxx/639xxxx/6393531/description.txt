.RAR or .ZIP files are compressed archives. Most modern operating systems have built in functionality to open .ZIP files, but .RAR files require some free third party software to decompress. Any of the following software can help you decompress an archive, and all are free or shareware.

PC: 7 Zip
PC: WinRAR
Mac: UnRARX
Mac: The Unarchiver
