Bridges began performing when he left school shortly after his 17th birthday on the small stage doing stand up comedy gigs at The Stand Comedy Club in Edinburgh and then throughout the rest of the UK. At 18 he reached the final of Channel 5's So you think you're funny competition at the Edinburgh Fringe.[1] He performed at the 2008 Edinburgh Festival Fringe. Bridges appeared on Michael McIntyre's Comedy Roadshow on the BBC, where he performed at the Edinburgh Playhouse.[2][3]

His debut DVD, titled Kevin Bridges: The Story So Far... Live In Glasgow, was released on 22 November 2010.[4]

In 2010, he took part in Channel 4's Comedy Gala, a benefit show held in aid of Great Ormond Street Children's Hospital, filmed live at the O2 Arena in London on 30 March.
