	Busty cougar Deaxuma comes to visit old friend Nica for the weekend, and confesses her new affair with teenage lover Frederick Way. Nica is shocked, but can't deny that Deauxma seems happier than ever. Nica then meets up for lunch with friends Michelle and Magdalene and shares the details of Deauxma's exploits.
