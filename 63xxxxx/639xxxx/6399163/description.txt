Another perilous mission awaits the International Rescue team in this film based on the 1960s British TV 
show starring Gerry Anderson's supermarionation puppets. The team is off to save the day when the Zero-X, a 
manned spacecraft headed to Mars, encounters problems during take-off. The intrepid rescuers ensure that the 
second mission runs smoothly, but are forced to tackle a dangerous situation in the last phase of the mission
