Become a master gardener in Blossom, the puzzle game that challenges you to irrigate your way to a bright, happy garden. Sluice the water from the watering can through a system of pipes to reach the flowers and make them bloom. You'll have to rotate the tiles with pipes, flowers, and that all-important watering can to make each part of the grid connect together and fill up every tile.
Can you master the art of Blossom? Soon you'll be a fantastic florist, watering your way through over one hundred increasingly challenging levels.

* Make flowers blossom by channeling water to them
* Rotate pipes, flowers, and watering cans to solve the puzzles
* Take on the challenge of over 100 levels of increasing difficulty

OS : Win XP/2000/Vista/Win 7
