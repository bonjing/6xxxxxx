FlexiMusic Audio Editor is a professional and home user digital audio editor that can be used to play, edit, mix sounds/tracks, record, analyze sounds and apply various special effects such as Equalizer, Echo, Change Speed, Reverse effect, Fade Out/ Fade in audio, Remove Noise, Pop/Clicks removal, Stretch/Shrink, timing control recording features, work on single channel and etc. Audio can be recorded from cassettes, albums, radio, audio streaming from internet and digitally copied from audio CDs. Conversion between different audio file formats, or between different sound quality levels
