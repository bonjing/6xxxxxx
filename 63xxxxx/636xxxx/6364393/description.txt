Raavan is a 2010 Hindi film directed, written and produced by Mani Ratnam. 

It stars Abhishek Bachchan, Aishwarya Rai and Vikram in the lead roles while Govinda, Ravi Kishan, Nikhil Dwivedi, Tejaswini Kolhapure and Priyamani feature in key supporting roles. 

It was simultaneously released in Tamil as Raavanan with a slightly different cast, which would also be dubbed into Telugu and other regional languages. 

The film score and soundtrack is composed by A. R. Rahman.

The film was released on 18 June 2010.
