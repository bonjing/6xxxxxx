|Plot/Storyline|
+--------------+

Two young girls, Satsuke and her younger sister Mei, move into a house in the country with their father to be closer to their hospitalized mother. Satsuke and Mei discover that the nearby forest is inhabited by magical spirits called Totoros (pronounced toe-toe-ro). They soon befriend these Totoros, and have several wonderous adventures.
