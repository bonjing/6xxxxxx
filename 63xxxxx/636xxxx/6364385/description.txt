Aladin is a Bollywood film directed by Sujoy Ghosh of Jhankaar Beats fame. 

Amitabh Bachchan, Ritesh Deshmukh, Sanjay Dutt and Jacqueline Fernandez appear in pivotal roles in the movie. 

Indias top art director, Sabu Cyril designed the sets of this film.

The film uses the name and plot of the famed Aladdin, while the story itself has nothing to do with India. 

Certain elements of the story, such as the name of the princess, Jasmine, were actually taken from Disneys animated adaptation of the tale.
