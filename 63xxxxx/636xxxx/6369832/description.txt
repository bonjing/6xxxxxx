Genre: Shooter
Thison-rails shooter will provide plenty of excitement for gamers looking for a shooter fix. You have a host of lethal weapons at your disposalincluding homing missiles, lasers, and cluster bombs. Another uniquefeature of this game is the fact that you have control of your mech,even though the game is on rails. What this means is that you can dodge incoming fire and missiles or rotate in search of the best shot.Control-wise, a targeting system has been implemented with both manualand automatic lock-on firing, which puts you in complete control. Thereis also a special gallery section that allows you to check out all thebig mechs in the game.
Game Features

19different zones filled with a variety of objective-based missions, 360degrees of 3D rendered graphics, customize and upgrade equipment andweapons
