The Haibane Renmei soundtrack shows a great deal of intellectual brilliance in its backing, and even more musical diversity within. The sound varies from bittersweet & melancholy to bright and cheerful. Whether or not you've watched any of the anime series, this musical score paints vivid images from one piece to the next.
