"Super Bass" is a song by Trinidadian rapper Nicki Minaj. It serves as the fifth single from her debut studio album, Pink Friday. It was originally a bonus track on the deluxe edition of Pink Friday, but was released to radio on April 5, 2011.In an interview with American radio station 102.7 KIIS FM, Minaj indicated that Taylor Swift's unsolicited promotion of the song influenced the decision to release it as a single.
