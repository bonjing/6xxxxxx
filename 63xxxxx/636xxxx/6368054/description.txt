Goblin is the second studio album by OFWGKTA member Tyler the Creator.
It has been confirmed to be released on May 10, 2011 through XL Recordings.
The album features vocal contributions from fellow OFWGKTA members
Hodgy Beats, Frank Ocean, Jasper Dolphin, Taco, Domo Genesis and Mike G.
