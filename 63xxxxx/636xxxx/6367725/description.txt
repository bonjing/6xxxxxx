501 Math Word Problems was designed to provide students with review and practice for the math word problems that are found on many standardized tests, including high school exit exams, the PSAT, SAT, and GRE, and professional and civil service qualifying exams. The book's 501 problems are organized  into six sections: Miscellaneous Math, Fractions, Decimals, Percents, Algebra, and Geometry. The book teaches both basic and advanced math concepts and every problem comes with a complete answer explanation. For problems that require numerous steps, a thorough step-by-step explanation is provided.
