Tony Tammaro, stage name of Vincenzo Sarnelli, (born January 7, 1961 in Naples) is an Italian parody singer/songwriter. He composes songs about "tamarri", rough people that imitate the manners of high society, from which his stage name is derived.

Tammaro started his career in 1989, releasing Prima cassetta di musica tamarra (Italian: First tape of "tamarra" music).

In 1997 Tammaro returned to the studio to record the album Monnezzarium and released 'O trerrote as the first single. Tammaro released his greatest hits album Tutto Tony Tammaroin 1999. It is a double album and each of two CDs is made up of twentyfour songs.

Tony Tammaro and his band released The Dark Side of the Moonnezz in June 2005. Moonnezz is a phonetic pun since it sounds like "Munnezz'", the Neapolitan word for garbage.
