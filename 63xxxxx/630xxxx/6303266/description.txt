
AoA Audio Extractor provides you a handy tool to extract audio/sound or background music from video files.

With AoA Audio Extractor, you can easily extract audio tracks from AVI, MPEG, MPG, DAT, WMV, MOV, MP4, 3GP video files and save them as MP3, WAV and AC3 formats.

It can also be used to save any portion of the audio of a video file.

Features:

* Convert AVI, MPEG, MPG, DAT, WMV/ASF, MOV, MP4, 3GP to MP3
* Convert AVI, MPEG, MPG, DAT, WMV/ASF, MOV, MP4, 3GP to WAV
* Convert AVI, MPEG, MPG, DAT, WMV/ASF, MOV, MP4, 3GP to AC3
* Batch conversion support.
* Ability to save any portion of the audio of a video file.
