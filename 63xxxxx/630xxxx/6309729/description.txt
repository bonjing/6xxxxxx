Released in 1998, the Sailor Moon Memorial Song Box is a collection of BGM (Background music) used throughout the Sailor Moon anime TV series, including the movies and specials.  It is 10 discs long with two discs each for the first and final seasons.  

This collection was reprinted back in 2000, but is out of print and VERY rare now.
