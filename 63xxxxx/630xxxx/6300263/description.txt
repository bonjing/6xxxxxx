In Driver - you are the Wheelman!?. Grab the wheel - Welcome to the criminal underworld. You're a cop named Tanner and you're undercover - deep cover. Posing as a getaway driver for hire, you'll be ripping it up through the mean streets of the wildest urban jungles: New York, Los Angeles, San Francisco, and Miami. Tailing unsuspecting victims. Picking up "shipments." And, of course, getting away from the big heists. You'll be doing it all (and then some) if you wanna make the big bust. The only things you need to leave behind are a trail of blown red lights, dazed pedestrians, and burnt-out cop cars!
