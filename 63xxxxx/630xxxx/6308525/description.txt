Rockford is a computer game developed by Arcadia Systems in 1988 and distributed by Electronic Arts for the Atari ST, Amiga, Commodore 64, Atari 8-bit, and IBM micros and compatible machines.

PLOT:
The player is on a treasure hunt, and visits five worlds to collect treasures without getting crushed by giant rocks.

The game was reviewed in 1989 in Dragon #141 by Hartley, Patricia, and Kirk Lesser in "The Role of Computers" column. The reviewers gave the game 4 1/2 out of 5 stars.
