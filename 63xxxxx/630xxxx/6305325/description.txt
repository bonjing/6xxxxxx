Eddie, Sarah-Lynn, Maggie, Michael and Sonya-Lee are a group of friends who each have their own special talent. They play together, learn new things through big discoveries and laugh a lot, because that is what best friends are for!

This DVD includes ten, five-minute episodes centered around lessons on numbers and creativity.

Little People make learning extra fun and colourful for young children.
