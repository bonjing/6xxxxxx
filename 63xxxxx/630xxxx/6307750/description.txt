This collection of mostly Science Fiction and Fantasy novels and collections 

includes several authors where I have spent many hours searching every corner 

of the internet for all of their books, then converting the files from 

multiple formats-- including some extensive editing of plain text files-- 

into EPUBs.  For those authors, this is the first time you can find all (or 

all that can be found anywhere on the internet) of their books in one place, 

in one format.
