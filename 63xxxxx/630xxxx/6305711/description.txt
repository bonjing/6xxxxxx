The Vanilla Ice Project is a reality TV show about renovating houses on the DIY Network. It is hosted by rapper and general contractor Vanilla Ice[1] who has experience with home improvement and flipping since the 1990's; buying houses in his early twenties and getting more into home improvement starting in 1998[2]. The season is focused on renovating a house in Palm Beach, Florida with each episode dedicated to a different room in the house.
