Buddy Rich - At the Top

Speech by Buddy Rich and his Orchestra on Feb. 6, 1973 in Hall nightclub The Top of The Plaza in Rochester (Rochester), New York remains one of the most popular and beloved shows ever recorded by Buddy. At that time he was 55 years old!
In addition to the performances in the beginning there is a story about his life, activities and, of course uplifting, his comic sketches. The video also includes a rare solo Buddy Rich, unaccompanied, and was last executed by them on television in May 1978 and 3 bonus not less rare video.
