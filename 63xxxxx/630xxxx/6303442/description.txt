China Farm: A video game for learning Mandarin Chinese symbols and vocab

-------------
Version 
-------------
1.3

-------------
Features:
-------------

    * Good supplement for beginning/intermediate Mandarin Chinese students
    * 720 vocabulary words and growing
    * Useful for learning learning new vocab, and symbol recognition
    * Strongly reinforces learning by putting the language in a varied and exciting context
    * Play competitively with other players over the internet, or against the computer
    * Good supplement for students already learning Chinese

-------------
To Play:
-------------

    * Install Java if you don\'t already have it
    * Double click \"China Farm.jar\"
