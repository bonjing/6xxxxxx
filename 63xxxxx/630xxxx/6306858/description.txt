SYNOPSIS

Based on true events, "Mountain Patrol: Kekexili" tracks the heroic efforts of a small group of Tibetans struggling to save the Tibetan antelope, or chiru, from extinction. This true story pits the Tibetan mountain patrol against a band of ruthless poachers who have killed one of their men and have left behind a trail of slaughtered antelopes. And if do-gooders stand in the way of their hunt for the elusive antelope, these gun-toting poachers won't hesitate to shoot anyone.

This film was shot on location in Kekexili (Tibetan: Achen Ganggyal) 

NB: Despite some people viewing this as some sort of Chinese propaganda here and here, we uploaded this film mainly because it is about a group of young Tibetans.
