H.M.S. Surprise

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Patrick O'Brian; Full cast dramatisation starring David Robb, Richard Dillane, Jon Glover and David Timson; Dramatised by Roger Danes; Directed by Bruce Young.

Naval battles, political intrigue and romantic rivalry are all to the fore in this tale set in 1804-5 in England, India, and on the high seas.

Episode 1: Captain Jack Aubrey engages the Spanish and the French but falls victim to enemies at home.

Episode 2: Jack Aubrey is ordered to convey a British ambassador to the East Indies - but in Bombay Stephen Maturin meets Diana Villiers, the one woman Jack would avoid at any cost.

Episode 3: Tragedy halts the ship's mission to the East Indies.
