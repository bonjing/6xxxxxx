Consumer safety has taken, over the last decades, an ever-growing and important position in our society, in particular in developed countries. This book focuses on some of the key issues in this field which touches virtually everyone including child seats, firearms, food safety, and plastics. In addition, laws and federal agencies and their activities are reviewed and discussed.
