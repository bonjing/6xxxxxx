BEGINNING
ELECTRIC BLUES GUITAR
ANDY ALEDORT
Guitar Tuition VHS


Learn the essentials of playing electric blues guitar from one of the genre's most renowned and respected educators. This VHS covers twelve-bar blues form, the blues scale, major and minor pentatonics, combining scales, shuffle rhythm, pick harmonics, seventh chords, and more must-know topics. Andy also includes an inspiring performance section, revealing the unique approach of several blues masters, including B.B. King, Stevie Ray Vaughan and Eric Clapton.
