Sense and Sensibility

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Jane Austen; Full cast dramatisation starring Jane Leonard and Abigail McKern; Adapted by Louise Page; Directed by Vanessa Whitburn.

Episode 1: Barton Cottage

It's 1810. Widow Mrs Dashwood and her three daughters are forced to move to Devon.

Episode 2: Lucy Steele

Willoughby's departure leaves Marianne distraught - and Elinor gets shocking news.

Episode 3: The Truth About Willoughby

Rumoured to be engaged to Willoughby, Marianne is still pursued by Colonel Brandon.

Episode 4: Delaford, House and Parsonage

Elinor is pained by thoughts of Edward marrying Lucy Steele - and Marianne gets sick.
