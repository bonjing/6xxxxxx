Leaves of the Dead (a Julie Enfield Mystery)

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Nick Fisher; Full cast dramatisation starring Imelda Staunton, Geoffrey Matthews, Ross Livingstone, Janet Moore, Christopher Scott, Alex Lowe, Shelley Dixon and Stephen Thorne; Produced by Richard Wortley.

A maniac is at large in and around the British Museum, committing gruesome murders, Ancient Egyptian style.
