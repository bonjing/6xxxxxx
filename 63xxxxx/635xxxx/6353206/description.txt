William The Conqueror
Released 3 Nov 1982
Romanian title: Cucerirea Angliei

Directors:Sergiu Nicolaescu, Gilles Grangier
Writers:Marcel Jullian (dialogue) & Serge de la Roche (dialogue)

Language:French, subtitles Romanian
Genre:Action   History
Country:France    Switzerland    Romania 
Company:Cine TV

This great film describes the legendary life of William, bastard of Edward. Renegade as a child, William  will be forced to hide for five years, among the peasants. Years after this, pushed by ascension he will become a tremendous Norman duke, then he will will be crowned as first Saxon king and founder of a dynasty.
