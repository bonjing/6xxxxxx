The legendary Irish folk group The Clancy Brothers' 1970 out-of-print album, Welcome to Our House. This is one of only three albums that all four Clancy Brothers (Bobby, Liam, Tom, and Paddy) made together. Bobby Clancy had replaced Tommy Makem in the group and was himself later replaced by Louis Killen.
