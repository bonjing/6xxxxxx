.: Plot :.

Battlestar Galactica is a three-hour miniseries. It was the first part of the Battlestar Galactica reimagining based on the 1978 Battlestar Galactica television series, and served as a backdoor pilot for the 2004 television series. 

After a 40-year armistice in a war between the Twelve Colonies (the homeworlds populated by humans) and the Cylons (human-created robots), the Cylons launch a surprise nuclear attack intended to exterminate the human race. Virtually all of the population of the Twelve Colonies are wiped out. Most of the human defense force is either rendered ineffective or destroyed due to Number Six, a Cylon in the form of a human woman, who has seduced the famous scientist Dr. Gaius Baltar and exploited their relationship to gain access codes under the cover of an insider contract bid, in order to introduce malware into the military computer network, rendering it vulnerable to cybernetic attack.....
