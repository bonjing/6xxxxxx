This DVD preserves a very special production of Puccini's opera Turandot which was staged in Beijing's historic Forbidden City. Performed by the artists of Florence, Italy's Maggio Musicale Festival, this version was staged in cooperation with Chinese director Zhang Yimou, with the orchestra under the direction of Zubin Mehta and starring Giovanna Casolla, Sergej Larin and Barbara Frittoli.
