Choice of Straws

From BBC Radio 4. Written by E.R. Braithwaite; Full-cast dramatisation starring Harry Hepple, Luke Norris, Gugu Mbatha-Raw, Ellie Haddington and David Hargreaves; Dramatised by Roy Williams; Directed by Claire Grove.

1960, London's East End: twins Jack and Dave Bennett are a happy-go-lucky, rootless pair. If they do occasionally rough-up a black guy it's just a game to them - until a victim in Whitechapel fights back and Dave pulls a knife.
