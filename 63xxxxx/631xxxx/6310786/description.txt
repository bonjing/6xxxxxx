Lynyrd Skynyrd - Gold (1998/2006)
---------------------

Label: Geffen/Universal
Catalog#: 0602498540930

The Essential Lynyrd Skynyrd, reissued as Gold, is a two-disc compilation
of Lynyrd Skynyrd in the peak years of its classic lineup (1971-1977).

    * In 1998, it was released as The Essential Lynyrd Skynyrd by MCA.
    * In 2006, it was re-released as Gold by Geffen (which had absorbed MCA)
               with identical content and a slightly different cover.
