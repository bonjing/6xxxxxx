While out on tour to revive her stalled career, once-popular country singer Kelly 
(Gwyneth Paltrow) falls for emerging newcomer Beau (Garrett Hedlund) and tries 
desperately to hide their burgeoning romance from her ever-present husband and 
manager, Ed (Tim McGraw). Written and directed by Shana Feste, this musical drama 
co-stars Leighton Meester as a former beauty queen trying to break out as a songstress.
