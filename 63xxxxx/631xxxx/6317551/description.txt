Teren Delvon Jones, better known as Del the Funky Homosapien or Del tha Funkee Homosapien, is a hip hop artist. He is a major part of the group, Hieroglyphics, and was also a guest performer on the 2001 Gorillaz album. He is cousin to the seminal west coast rapper Ice Cube.
