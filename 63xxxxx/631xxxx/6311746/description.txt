Kratt's Creatures is a high-energy, jam-packed wildlife adventures series! Join Martin Kratt and Chris Kratt on their creature mission, This creature-crazy zoologist/biologist team takes you in search of fascinating creatures all over the planet. Fifteen-year-old computer whiz Allison Baldwin comes at you from the Creature Club with incredible creature facts and her cartoon sidekick, Ttark, chimes in with fun facts and wisecracks. So go exploring with brothers Chris and Martin on wildly wonderful adventures and meet all your fellow creatures.
