- Release Notes ------------------------------------------------------------- -

   Year One is a collection of NSN's early self-released EPs along with five
   unreleased tracks.

   After achieving wild success by posting early tracks and engaging his growing
   fan base via MySpace, Christofer Drew has since signed with Warner Bros.,
   debuting an eight song album What Is Love? quickly followed up with Harmony,
   which debuted in the Billboard 200, selling upwards of 20,000 records in its
   first week of release. He headlined the 2010 Alternative Press Tour and The
   Vans Warped Tour. Never Shout Never was also named 2010's "Artist Of The
   Year" by Alternative Press.

   Now, with all of the success and sold out shows, Drew is looking back at his
   salad days with Year One, which showcases the beginning stages of NSN. The
   release includes the Me & My Uke EP, "30 Days" single, the Demo-shemo EP,
   along with five exclusive tracks and other tracks.
