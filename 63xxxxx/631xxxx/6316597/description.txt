Bell X1 - Bloodless Coup (2011)








Bloodless Coup is the fifth studio album by Irish band Bell X1. It was released in the Republic of Ireland, Germany, Belgium and the Netherlands on April 1, 2011 and in the United Kingdom on April 4, and April 5, 2011 in North America. The album was produced by Rob Kirwan and recorded in Westmeath, Ireland and while touring Europe and North America.

The album has received favorable reviews in their native Ireland. State described the album as \"rejuvenated\" and Gold Plectrum describing the album as \"...a real return to form from Bell X1 and leaves us with future hope of similar releases\".
