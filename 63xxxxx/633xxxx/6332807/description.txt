RSS Xpress allows you to read RSS feeds versions 0.90, 0.91, 0.92, 1.0, 2.0.1, and atoms. The application includes the essential functionalities for using RSS feeds and even more: very easy interface but complete possibility to organize your feeds in folders; new messages are saved locally so that you can browse them offline; you are informed about new messages by a pop-up windows and a sound; possibility to import and export feeds in the OPML format; search engine to find easily old messages in your feeds; auto-update; tab-browsing navigation; possibility to customize the application; compatible with feeds protected with username and password; support proxies.
