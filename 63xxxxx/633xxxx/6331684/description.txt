Cash Cash is an American band from Roseland, New Jersey. Cash Cash consists of Jean Paul Makhlouf, Alexander Luke Makhlouf, Sam Frisch, and Anthony Villacari.


Love or Lust is the second studio album by American Powerpop band Cash Cash, released on April 19, 2011. Victim of Love was released as the album's first single on April 5, 2011.
