Indio Solari (born Carlos Solari, c. 1949) is an Argentinean singer and musician. He was the leader of the mythical band "Patricio Rey y sus Redonditos de Ricota" between 1976 and 2002.

He left "Redonditos" and then started his first solo work in 2004 together with "Los Fundamentalistas del Aire Acondicionado"."El perfume de la tempestad" is the last album of the trilogy beginned in 2004 with "El Tesoro de los Inocentes (Bingo Fuel)", followed by "Porco Rex" (2006).
