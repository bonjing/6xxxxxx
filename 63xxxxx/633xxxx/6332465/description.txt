Recommended System Requirements

- joystick
- light gun

Installation Notes
- Run MameUI32 or MameUI64 if you have a 64-bit system.
- click on \"file\" -> \"audit all games\". after it has checked the roms, click \"close\".
- click \"view\" -> \"refresh\". if you don\'t do this operation, you won\'t be able to play at any game or you\'ll see only a minimun part of the roms.
- after choosing the game you want to play, click O and K, two or three times and it should start.
- insert the coins with 5, choose the number of the players with 1 or 2.
- to set your joystick or light gun, go to \"option\" -> \"default game\" -> \"controller mapping\"
- to adjust the difficulty press F2
- if there are games that are too fast, press F8
