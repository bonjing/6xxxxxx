      ENCYCLOPEDIA DRAMATICA - THE JANUARY 2010 PANIC GRAB

In January of 2010, Archive Team took a 12gb snapshot of the entirety of the
infamous Encyclopedia Dramatica - just in case. A little over a year later, 
"just in case" happened as ED became OHINTERNET, a slimmed, cleaned up shadow
of its former self. Well, we'll just leave this here.
