A Piece of Justice (an Imogen Quy Mystery)

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Jill Paton Walsh; Full cast dramatisation starring Barbara Flynn, Rosemary Leach, Robert Harper and Colin Starky; Adapted by Neville Teller; Directed by Janet Whitaker.

Biography is usually a safe profession. Even rather sedate. But more than one biographer has found that writing about the late great mathematician Gideon Summerfield leads to a hasty retreat. Or something more deadly...
