Turkish Grand Prix.

Jake Humphrey presents coverage of the Turkish Grand Prix, the fourth race of the 2011 Formula 1 season. Reigning champion Sebastian Vettel has set the early pace in this year's drivers' title race but has unhappy memories of the Istanbul track, having crashed out of the race last year after a collision with his Red Bull team-mate Mark Webber.
