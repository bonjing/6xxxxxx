Flaminio Maphia is the name of a band of Italian hip hop. It was launched in Rome in 1994 by G-Max, and MC Rude G. I Flaminio compose with the Sardinian group La Fossa, the crew Patto di Sangue.

Per un pugno di euri is an album of hip hop Italian Flaminio Maphia, published in 2005. In it is a remarkable change from the gengre rap and hip hop to a genre a bit 'less hard, especially in which the pop songs Che idea! and Federica. This change is probably due to the great success that pop music began to recover from that period and which were the leading experts in Article 31.
