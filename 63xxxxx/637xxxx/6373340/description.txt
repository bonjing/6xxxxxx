The Continuous Call Team - Ray Hadley, Steve Roach, Darryl Brohman and Bob Fulton - has been a radio powerhouse for 25 years on 2GB and the Macquarie Radio Network . The team have exclusive commercial radio rights to the National Rugby League (excluding Monday Night matches) and the games are broadcast on Friday nights, Saturdays and Sundays, with a talkback/humour programme broadcast on Saturday and Sunday afternoons. Their first ever CD release contains 2 discs of classic Continuous Call Team skits!
