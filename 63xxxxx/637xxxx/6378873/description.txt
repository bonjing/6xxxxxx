For over 30 years the coffeeshop was the Dutch symbol for unique tolerance and freedom. Since several years the cannabis business seems to have lost its innocence. The decriminalization policy, which turned the front door of the coffeeshop into an ordinary door or a store, does not regulate the produce and supply of cannabis. This is now sent to the backdoor, putting the coffeeshop on the line of the upper- and underworld.

The highly discussed documentary Teledoc: Nederweed enters the backdoor of the coffeeshop underworld. A world where nothing is certain, but where idealism and pleasure seem to lose from greed and violence.
