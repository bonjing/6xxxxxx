Luigino Celestino Di Agostino (born 17 December 1967 in Turin, Piedmont), better known by his stage name Gigi D'Agostino, is an Italian DJ, remixer and record producer.

Il Grande Viaggio, published in 2001, is a compilation of mixed Italian DJ Gigi D'Agostino that includes some of his unpublished and other pieces by various artists.
