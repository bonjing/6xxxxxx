Shredder (Mac App Store)

 

Description

 

A simple app for the periodic timed secure deletion of web browser caches and cookies. Can also shred files, folders and free disk space.

 

Manual

1. Unzip and move .app to Applications folder
2. Open Keygen and select the Shredder app in Step 2
3. Click on Serialize
4. Enjoy!!!
