Overview:

As it does so, it attracts obsolete and unnecessary information, and gradually becomes cluttered and fragmented. With the growing of the registry, it can degrade the performance of the whole system and cause many weird software problems.

In short, Registry Clean Expert will help you get rid of the bloat in the Windows registry and achieve a cleaner, much faster computer. The new version now supports registry defrag / compact, privacy protection and auto update feature.
