JAWS Screen Reader V12.0.1158 Portable [mp3pro1986]

JAWS Screen Reader is an application that helps blind people to use their computer. It combines speech and braille output to help reading the screen.
This version is the Portable version. It can be used from an USB Drive or from any folder on your harddisk. No Need to install, just run and go.

1. Unpack.
2. Run jaws12.bat.
3. Enjoy!
