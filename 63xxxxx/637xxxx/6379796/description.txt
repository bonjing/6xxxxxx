Synopsis
-=-=-=-=-

43 "This Man-Thing, This Monster!  (Six Against Infinity, Part 3)" May 8, 2011 217
 
Iron Man finds himself stranded on a world of monsters. There he meets Man-Thing and 
Werewolf by Night and battle Dracula and a gang of mummies. Later it is revealed that 
Werewolf's girlfriend was turned into a vampire prior to their arrival. 
As Iron Man tries to figure out how to leave to battle Dark Surfer, 
the trio of monsters declare they will form the "Supernatural Hero Squad" 
to save innocents on their world.
