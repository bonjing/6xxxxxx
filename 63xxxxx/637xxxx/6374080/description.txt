42  "Days, Nights, and Weekends of Future Past!  (Six Against Infinity, Part 2)" 216

After being thrown in to another dimension by Dark Surfer, Falcon and H.E.R.B.I.E.

 With help from a elderly Magneto, and a strong Reptil, Falcon, and H.E.R.B.I.E. try to convince
