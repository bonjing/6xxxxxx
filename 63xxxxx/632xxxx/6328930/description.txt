SCWU is a fighting game that makes you return to the time of old school fighting like Street FIghting.
The game as a lot of madness and parodies. The music is nice and gameplay amazing.
The computer can allways turn out a headeach sometimes, but nothing more. Still, a great fighting game for who likes parodies.

This game is free and you should not pay for it.
