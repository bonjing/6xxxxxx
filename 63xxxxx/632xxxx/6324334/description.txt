Virtual Reality
Date: 04-16-11 
Host: Ian Punnett 
Guests: Jim Blascovich, Jeremy Bailenson, George Hardy 
Jim Blascovich and Jeremy Bailenson, two pioneering experts in the field of virtual reality, joined Ian Punnett to reveal when we all might be living in the Matrix. 

In the first hour, actor George Hardy talked about how he went from small town dentist to the cult movie star of Troll 2, a horror film chronicled in the new documentary Best Worst Movie.
