A simple yet exciting indie game. Test your reaction time by dodging the falling boxes. Collect shooting bonuses, invulnerability bonuses and more. Every fallen box will lead you to deadly spikes, so beware! Online table of records allows you to compare your reaction times with players from around the world.

System Requirements:
- Operating system Microsoft Windows 98, XP, Vista, Win7
- Pentium 3 and above
- 256 MB RAM
- Video card with 32 MB memory
- The sound device compatible with DirectX 9
