discription
You and your fireteam take a small step into the future, Operation Flashpoint: Red River depicts a fictional conflict with contemporary geopolitical themes, which will unfold over three distinct acts in both single player and drop-in/drop-out co-operative play for up to four players online.  Faced with new counter-insurgency combat and the returning threat of the Chinese PLA, players will feel the tension, brutality and carnage of modern conflict from the perspective of a marine fireteam on deployment in Tajikistan, thousands of miles from home.
