This is the official Froyo update for the Virgin Mobile Samsung Intercept. This only works with a retail phone that has NOT been rooted in any way. This update is applied without rooting the phone.

Hash values: 

MD5: c5a5205f30c421a7ec40597efca8d763
SHA1: 54dbc743fd5b12973db6ee5c207f723e1906481f


How to upgrade:

1. Rename 54dbc743fd5b.update-SPH-M910_OTA_DI06ToEC07.zip to upgrade.zip
2. Copy upgrade.zip to the root folder of your microSD card.
3. Shut down your phone.
4. Hold down the volume key, the send key and the stop key at the same time. If you do this in the right sequence, you will boot the phone into restore mode.
5. Use the trackpad to select the choice that will apply update.zip to your phone.

I have updated two phones so far, each took less than ten minutes and no data was lost. The only thing I had to do was to login back into my Google account.
