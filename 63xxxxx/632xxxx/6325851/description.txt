Kaveri facilitates text processing in major south Indian languages giving special focus to Malayalam.
The following are the tools packaged with Kaveri:
Spell checker for Malayalam,
Simple typing system for Tamil, Malayalam, Kannada, Telugu and Hindi,
Bi-lingual User Interface,Transliteration system of major South Indian Languages,
English-Malayalam Dictionary,
English-Malayalam translation aid,
Unicode Malayalam Fonts and Ethnic Screen Savers etc.
