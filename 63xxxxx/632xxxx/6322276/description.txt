In a small village in Rajasthan, India, a woman named Shanichari was born - so named as shortly after her birth her father died; & her mother, Euli, ran away with a rich lover, abandoning her. When Shanichari grew up she was married to a male named Ganju who lived with his ailing mother. Shanichari gave birth to a boy and named him Budhwa. The local Landlord, Ram Avtar's son, Lakhsman Singh, would like Shanichari to live with him as his mistress, but she must first abandon her husband and son, which she is unable to do. Years later, Ganju gets killed after consuming holy water from a temple - Shanichari is unable to weep; Then her ailing mother-in-law passes away - Shanicharni is unable to weep; When Budhwa get's married to a local prostitute, Mungri, who becomes pregnant, and then subsequently aborts the child - Shanichari is unable to weep; When Budhwa runs away from home, never to be seen again - Shanichari is unable to weep...
