A 5 track bonus CD that came with the first European release of the Doppelganger album. Tracks are virtually (if not completely) identical to the versions on Pubic Fruit. Except 'I Speak Your Every Word', which has a few rap verses added. 

1 Die like a dog
2 Frozen
3 No escape from heaven
4 Blindfold
5 I speak your every word (with JC001)
