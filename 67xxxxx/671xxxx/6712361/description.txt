Three Country Stories (series 1)

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by H.E. Bates; Read by David Neal.

Time

Three old men sit and watch the world go by, but one has a youthful secret of vitality.

Chaff in the Wind

A lost man needing directions has a chance encounter in a cold, windswept field.

The Maker of Coffins

Every Sunday an awkward, fiddle-playing coffin-maker comes to play for his mother.
