Paul McCartney - Good Evening New York City

Good Evening New York City is a CD/DVD double live album by Paul McCartney consisting of material performed over three nights as the inaugural concert at New York City's Citi Field, 17, 18 and 21 July 2009, part of his Summer Live '09 concert tour. 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Good_Evening_New_York_City

NOTE: This upload does not contain the DVD. 

All songs performed by Paul McCartney, Paul'Wix' Wickens, Rusty Anderson, Abe Laboriel Jr and Brian Ray. Piano and additional vocals on 'I saw her standing there": Billy Joel
