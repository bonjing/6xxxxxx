From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Richard Adams; Full-cast dramatisation starring Simon Chandler, Tom George, Stephen Tomlin, Christian Rodska, Ian Brooker, Peter Meakin, Sean Connolly, Robert Lister, Colin Rote, Susan Jeffrey and Alison Belbin; Dramatised by Neville Teller.

Episode 1: When prophet Fiver predicts doom for their warren, not all the rabbits will listen.

Episode 2: The rabbits try to rescue General Woundwort's females to establish their own dynasty.
