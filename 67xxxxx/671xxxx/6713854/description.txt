Master of Ceremonies, is the fourth album by Yonkers-bred rapper Styles P. The album is scheduled to be released October 4, 2011.The album's guest list features rappers such as Lloyd Banks, Rick Ross, Busta Rhymes, Pharoahe Monch, Pharrell Williams and Styles' group The L.O.X.. Producers on the album include the veteran beatmaker Pete Rock, Statik Selektah, West Coast veteran Warren G, Dipset producer ARAABmuzik, and more. The lead single off of Master of Ceremonies, is "Harsh", which features Rick Ross and Busta Rhymes. A second single has also been released. The song is called "It's OK", which features his long time companion Jadakiss.
