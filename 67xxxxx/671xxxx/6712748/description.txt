Fred Dibnah is unique, he is well known to the British Public through his BBC Television series "A Year With Fred".

This DVD offers an insight into the world of Fred Dibnah, from chimney felling to a visit to the world's largest steam rally.

You will enjoy Fred's wonderful sense of humour as he takes centre stage and captivates his audience with his anecdotes and witty reminiscences.

Scene selections include:

Monton Chimney Drop
Astle Park Steam Rally
The Caernarfon Engine
Bolton Chimney Inspection
The Workshop
Dibnah Delivery Service
The Great Dorset Steam Fair
The Farnworth Chimney Drop


Enjoy and Seed!!
