Adaptation of a controversial mobile arcade on your PC. By controlling the cube, you have to get to the portal, collecting as many bonuses. The game has hundreds of levels, minimalist design, simple operation and excellent soundtrack to the style of 8-bit.

Features:
- Platform in retro style!
- The classic 8-bit Soundtrack!
- Steam leaderboard.

System requirements:
- Operating system: Microsoft Windows XP | Vista | 7
- Processor: 1.5 GHz
- Memory: 512 MB
- Hard disk: 100 MB
- Sound system: compatible with DirectX 9.0
- Video: 256 MB
