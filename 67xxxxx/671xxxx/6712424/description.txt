Sinners Never Sleep is the 3rd full length record from English alternative rock band You Me At Six. The album is released through Virgin in the UK, Australia and New Zealand regions. The first single to be released from the album is "Loverboy". You Me At Six broke the news via their website. The single will have a video which is in the making, artwork has been revealed and the single is set to be released on 23 September 2011.
