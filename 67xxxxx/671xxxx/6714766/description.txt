Episode Details:

24 "Satan's Offspring"

Rin and his companions manage to flee from Satan who starts destroying the True Cross Academy with his flames. Knowing that Satan still stands beside the Gate because Rin has the power to destroy it, Shura comes up with a plan to distract him to give Rin an opening to do it, while the other students help evacuate the school. Their plan fails when Shiemi intervenes trying to dissuade Yukio with no success, but Rin manages to have his brother regain consciousness and expel Satan from his body. However, the gate keeps getting stronger and it will not be long befor Gehenna and Assiah merge into one single realm.
