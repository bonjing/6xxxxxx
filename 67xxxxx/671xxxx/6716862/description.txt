Ashes & Fire is the forthcoming thirteenth studio album by Ryan Adams, due for release on October 11, on PAX AM and Capitol. Recorded with producer Glyn Johns, Ashes & Fire marks Adams' return to recording following the disbandment of backing band, the Cardinals, in 2009. Norah Jones will feature on several tracks.[1]
 
The album is preceded by the single, "Lucky Now", and will be Adams' first release with Capitol Records
