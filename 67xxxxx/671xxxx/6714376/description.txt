Great Adventures: Lost in Mountains Game

A world-renowned scientist is reported missing under hazy circumstances - along with his most recent discovery, a powerful formula that could have dire consequences if it fell into the wrong hands. Now it's up to his courageous daughter to recover both of them... Gameplay is focused and will be greatly appreciated by anyone who love detective stories or mysterious puzzles. The original concept, state-of-the-art graphics, wide range of puzzles and amusing soundtrack will keep you entertained for hours!
