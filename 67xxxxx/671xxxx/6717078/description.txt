These are the original master tracks by Jakob Schmid for the 2008 PC game Lost
Empire: Immortals:

1-trailer-2008-01-28.flac           - Used in the story trailer.
2-maintheme-2008-01-30b.flac        - Used in LOI main menu.
3-ingame-allraces-20071219a.flac    - Played during gameplay (1)
4-trailer-gameplay-2008-02-12.flac  - Used in 'Colonize' trailer.

1) Note that the in-game music track is just an example bounce, as the LOI in-game
music is mixed on the fly during gameplay.
