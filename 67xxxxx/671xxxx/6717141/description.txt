London, England native Jamie Woon, the son of background vocalist Mae McKenna, debuted in 2006 with a startling folk-soul a cappella update of The Wayfaring Stranger.  He released his second proper single, the moody Night Air, in 2010. In 2011, on the heels of placing fourth in the BBCs Sound of 2011 poll, Woon released his debut solo album, Mirrorwriting, on Polydor Records.
