Roger Ruskin Spear's 2, post Bonzo Dog Doo-Dah Band, solo albums.

"Electric Shocks", released in 1972 and "Unusual", released in 1974.

Both taken from UK copies of the original vinyl LPs - United Artists UAS29381 and United Artists UAS29508.

Restored using CEDAR De-Click, but no other enhancements. Tracks run together as continuous audio, with no fades between tracks.

44.1k, 16-bit FLAC files coded with MediaMonkey.

Includes JPEG scans of front and back covers and record labels of both albums, plus PDFs of the sleeve-notes.
