About The Game:

Alibi in Ashes is new game of the point-and-click Nancy Drew adventure game series by Her Interactive, named in a teaser trailer at the end of The Captive Curse. A local contest turns into an arson scene and now deceit smolders among the charred ruins of the River Heights Town Hall. Police have several suspects, but well-placed incriminating evidence and poisonous local gossip compelled authorities to make only one arrest: Nancy Drew. Can you, as the teenage detective and her closest friends, catch the real arsonist and extinguish the accusations among the embers?
