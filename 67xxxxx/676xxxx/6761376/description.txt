Great guide to the coinages of the Roman Empire in the 3rd century AD. Part 3 of what will become a 5 or more part total review of the coinages of the Roman Empire. Not totally complete in all types but major types are illustrated and priced in dollars US and pounds British so one can get a ballpark figure of what to pay if assembling a collection. Recommended for all serious coin collectors who wish to assemble a collection of ancient Roman coins.
