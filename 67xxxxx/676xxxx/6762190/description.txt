Private Gold #113 - College Girls Vs. College Guys



Cast :   Mandy Dee, Aleska Diamond, Abbie Cat, Carmen Black, Roxy Taggart


Set in a beautiful villa in the woodlands of Colorado, college girl sorority group `Taf Kavla Epsilon` have a lot of new candidates who want to join their sorority club. So Britney, the head of the college girls gets together with Jason, head of the college guys, organize a secret sex ambush on the girls to help them audition the sorority`s new members. Watch as the new applicants will do whatever it takes to make the grade.
