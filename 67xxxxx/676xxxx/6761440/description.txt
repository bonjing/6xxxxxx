CDC - Voices In Harmony 4CD set plus bonus selections 320k (musicfromrizzo)

This was obtained on the net as a 4CD set published as from the Compact
Disc Club offerings.

Much of it is from Classical or operatic productions however there is some
beautiful traditional stuff such as the Kalinka and other similar stuff - 
what sheer beauty of the spirit! There's also great songs that are often done
by Jose Carreras and Sarah Brightman (the latter included as a bonus equally 
I say!)

Some song titles had French versions such as "The Red Army Chorus" 
artist notation etc - so we fixed it for everyone's benefit in this
particular case. Many titles don't indicate artist in fact
but rather the Opera or Classical production the song is from.

Enjoy! I note this is posted on a torrent however we obtained it elsewhere
as we subscribe to sources that provide us content for torrent postings for
everyone's benefit in our style and with added bonus additions that make the
offering even more meaningful in the first place

Enjoy!
