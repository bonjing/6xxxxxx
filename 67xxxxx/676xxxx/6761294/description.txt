The Dying Wish

From BBC Radio 4. Written by Rosemary Kay; Full-cast dramatisation.

Fran and her partner Abe are befriended by a lonely old woman, Joy, who lives in the flat above. Joy persuades them to perform an ancient ritual after she's died. They unwittingly agree without realising the terrifying consequences of their action.
