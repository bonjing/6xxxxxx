Prayer Mask

From BBC Radio 4. Written by David Pownall; Full-cast dramatisation.

In 853 a young Lieutenant Richard Burton disguised himself as an Afghani pilgrim and undertook an expedition to Mecca. His aim was to explore the Holy of Holies. He chronicles this journey fraught with dangers and misadventure. But can he hide his identity from his two young guides?
