This is a fairly complete collection of Doctor Who Extras
.
Also included are misc. audio files - soundtracks, and odds and ends.

There is a pretty complete collection of Doctor Who Magazines - up to March 2011, along with some comics, stories, books and other documents.

And the 4 Doctor Who Adventures games are here and able to be installed and played outside the Uk.

And the current version of several programs needed to play the Divx, Xvid, MPG, MP4 files, and read CBZ and PDFs are in the tools folder.

So you have:
Doctor Who Magazines
Doctor Who The Movie
Various Doctor Who comics
Some documentaries about Doctor Who
Youtube videos on the intros and The Doctor's regenerations
Soundtrack files and audio books
Doctor Who NDS game
Doctor Who Adventures PC games - all 4 episodes
Doctor Who RPG Books
Plus some other odds and ends I've found


Enjoy and Seed!!
