Latino, Euro Ballroom dance


There is some great music here for your next party or to start your own Club
even for those that want to better enjoy and appreciate life through the
unique great gift of ballroom dance experiences

There are many major artists included here such as Julio Iglesias
Celine Dion, Dean Martin and Tom Jones, along with lesser
known names giving us great renditions equally to some of what have been
standards - such as Besame Mucho and more!

Enjoy and quite the punk music tolerance in your life for a happier
more fulfilled life - sienpre!

Michael Rizzo Chessman
