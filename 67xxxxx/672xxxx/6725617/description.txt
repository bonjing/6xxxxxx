"Hector Badge Of Carnage" is a fun-tastic point-and-click adventure game, which has been divided into three episodes...

Episode 1 --&gt; We Negotiate With Terrorists.
Episode 2 --&gt; Senseless Acts Of Justice.
Episode 3 --&gt; Beyond Reasonable Doom.

This game is a true entertainer! Its a must-play for adventure game lovers. This game is totally animated and graphics is just awesome... cartoonish. Cm'on, now don't call it a child's game...! You gotta use your common sense and wit to succeed through all the 3 episodes. (I suggest you NOT to view the walk-throughs uploaded for no good in youtube.) The story/plot is nice too. You only play detective inspector Hector in 1st episode; but in the other episodes Lambert, a lame inspector, asissts him. You can very interestingly swap characters, i.e., Hector and lame Lambert! So enjoy a full-fun-pack adventure series provided to you. I hope there will be more Hector games coming up for us very soon.

Genre: Comedy|Crime|Adventure|Animation.
