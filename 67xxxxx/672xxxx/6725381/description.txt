'The Lost Prince' is the story of Prince Johnnie, the younger son of Queen Mary and King George V who was hidden away to spare an uncomprehending family and public from his severe epileptic fits and autism. From writer/director Stephen Poliakoff, this is a moving drama set against the looming First World War, featuring stunning costumes, idyllic sets, and the beguilling atmosphere of immensely significant world events, seen simply through a child's eye.
