Lifted is the story of Henry, a middle school student and talented singer, whose happy family life is disrupted when his father, a Marine, is sent to Afghanistan, and despite all his obstacles including losing the family home and being forced to move in with his grandfather who hates his music, is inspired to enter a teen singing competition in the hopes of winning and escaping a world that is falling apart. Lifted is an amazing and inspiring story that sheds light on the lives of millions of American families who are serving our country.
