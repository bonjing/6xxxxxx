Joining Jools on the third show of the series is the UKs female folk star, singer-songwriter
Laura Marling, with songs from her current LP A Creature I Don't Know. Legendary American
singer and showman Tony Bennett performs a couple of songs at the piano from his newest
collection of great American songs.

Also, from Nashville, The Silver Seas, who formed when producer Jason Lehning met singersongwriter
Daniel Tashian, son of 60s garage rock legend Barry Tashian of The Remains.
They perform songs from their pop rock album Chateau Revenge, which has been described
as reminiscent of late-70s AOR recordings. From Jamaica, Jools is joined by reggae singer
Little Roy, who has recorded an album of Nirvana songs entitled Battle For Seattle, reworking
and giving the likes of Lithium and Come As You Are a new twist and life.

Also in the studio, US duo Joy Williams and John Paul White - aka The Civil Wars - who met
at a Nashville songwriter session and have recently supported Adele on her UK tour.
