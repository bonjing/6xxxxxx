Lady Death: A Medieval Tale (2003 complete) and The Wild Hunt (2004 unfinished)

Lady Death was originally published by Chaos comics before the publisher went bankrupt.  This torrent contains all the lady Death that was published at Crossgen before it too went bankrupt. Lady Death is currently owned by Avatar Press

The medieval world is threatened by a near-immortal race called the Eldrich. Humanity's last hope for survival is Lady Death, an outcast who is half-human, half-Eldrich, and all attitude! Blood of both clans, she belongs to neither. Bearing a sword of vengeance, Lady Death roams Medieval Europe in search of her place in the world.
