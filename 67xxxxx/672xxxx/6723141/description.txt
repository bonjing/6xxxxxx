Legends-The Enchanted #GN



Named Best Graphic Novel of 2010 by Horror News.
Violently ripped from the pages of fairytale folklore, a new breed of warriors emerge: the Enchanted. When their half-wooden, half-mechanical warrior comrade, Pinocchio, is killed by the evil Hag, the Enchanted begin to lose their immortal powers and strength. Follow Red Hood the wolf hunter, Jack the troll killer, Goldilocks the mercenary, and psychic exterminators Hansel and Gretel on their quest to destroy their nemeses, the Hag and Squire, who both wish to see them all dead.
