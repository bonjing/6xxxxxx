This is a collection of most of M Night Shyamalans movies with the exception of Praying with Anger which I still cannot find.  I searched for a collection of his movies and just couldnt find one so I put this together.  I figure I cant be the only one who would want that!


Movies contained, in order of release:

- Wide Awake (1998)
- The Sixth Sense (1999)
- Unbreakable (2000)
- Signs (2002)
- The Village (2004)
- Lady In The Water (2006)
- The Happening (2008)
- Avatar The Last Airbender (2010)
- Devil (2010)
