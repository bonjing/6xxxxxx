Jake Humphrey presents live coverage from the Suzuka Circuit, where the drivers battle for positions on the grid ahead of the 15th round of the season (Start-time 6.00am). Sebastian Vettel dominated here last year, securing pole position before going on to win the race from Red Bull team-mate Mark Webber. Commentary by Martin Brundle and David Coulthard, with analysis from Eddie Jordan.

BBC1 London 5:00am-7:30am (2 hours 30 minutes) Sat 8 Oct
