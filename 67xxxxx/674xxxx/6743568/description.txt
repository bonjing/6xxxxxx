Janes Addiction were one of the most hotly pursued rock bands when they gained notice in Los Angeles in the mid-80s, with record companies at their feet. Flamboyant frontman Perry Farrell, formerly of the band Psi Com, had an undeniable charisma and an interest in provocative art, and Janes Addiction played a hybrid of rock music: metal with strains of punk, folk, and jazz.
