SYNOPSIS

The Unwinking Gaze offers a unique, behind-the-scenes insight into the recent 
working life of Tibet's would-be saviour and revered world icon the Dalai Lama.

Filmed over a period of three years with exceptional access showing the daily 
agonies of the Tibetan leader as he tries to strike a balance between his Buddhist 
vows and the realpolitik needed to placate China. David and Goliath is played out 
in front of us as the world's emerging superpower and the Dalai Lama walk a 
tightrope over an issue of global importance.

The Unwinking Gaze is not 3 years in the life of the Dalai Lama. It is his life's 
work in 3 years. This film takes you inside the Titanic struggle of one of the 
great spiritual and political figures of our time as he tries to lead his people 
to a peaceful resolution with China.
