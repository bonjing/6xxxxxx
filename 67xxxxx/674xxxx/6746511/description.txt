"Night Of The Living Shred" is a bombastic demontration of Stump's over-the-top arsenal of blazingly fast neo-classical riffs, heavy rhythms and overall phenomenal guitar technique. "Night Of The Living Shred" is the follow up to 1993's "Guitar Dominance" and incudes Stump's tributes to Stevie Ray Vaughan ("Texas Chainsaw Boogie") and Jimi Hendrix ("The Jimi Stomp"). A true "facemelter"!
