The Doors are quite simply one of the most influential groups in the whole of rock. They spawned some of the greatest music of the 1960s and their legacy extended way past the early death of Jim Morrison, most notably in the music of the 1970s new wave group, The Stranglers (compare the middle section of 'light my fire' with 'walk on by' for a notable example).
