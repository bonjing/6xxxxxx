The album, 'We Don't Need to Whisper' by Angels and Airwaves.

We Don't Need to Whisper is the debut album by American rock band Angels & Airwaves. It was released on May 20, 2006 in Australia, on May 22 in the UK, and on May 23 in North America.

Track listing

1. Valkyrie Missile
2. Distraction
3. Do It for Me Now
4. The Adventure
5. A Little's Enough
6. The War
7. The Gift
8. It Hurts
9. Good Day
10. Start the Machine

Bonus tracks: The Machine, It Hurts (Live) and Do It for Me Now (Live).
