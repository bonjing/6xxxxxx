Genre: Heavy / Power Metal
Origin: Sweden

Albums
2001 - Castle Of Yesterday
2002 - Thunderblaze
2004 - Demon Wings (EP)

The band started a musical career in 1999 and consists of Joel Linder (drums, ex Majestic), Jonas Blum (vocals, ex-Majestic), Jonas Reingold (bass, also Flowerkings and Midnight Sun), Thomas Blum (keyboards) and Peter Espinoza (guitar, ex- Nasty Idols) and Lasse Boquist (guitars).
