The Floating Cemetery

History Documentary hosted by Scott Moore, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC Bone Detectives series in 2008 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Scotty visits the island nation of Palau in Micronesia to examine a skull uncovered by archaeologists that appears to be evidence of a 3000-year-old murder.
