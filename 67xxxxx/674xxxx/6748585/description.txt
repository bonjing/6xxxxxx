Title: Cafe Mahjongg
Genre: strategy / puzzle / mahjongg
Released: 19th September 2007
Developer: Jenkat Media
Publisher: iWin
Description:
Order up a tall latte and immerse yourself in the wonderful world of coffee at Cafe Mahjongg. Match tiles to earn your favorite coffees from around the world! Try out the House Blend for a story adventure or Espresso if Free Play is more your flavor. Customize and create your own unique blends - upload your favorite picture and use it as a background wallpaper while playing your favorite mahjong tile set. With over 120 original levels, Cafe Mahjongg is your gaming and coffee fix.

How to play:
1) Download the game;
2) Run Cafe_Mahjongg_Setup.exe to install;
3) Launch the game using the main executable.
