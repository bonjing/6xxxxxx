Formula.1.2011.Korean.Yeongam.Grand.Prix.Qualifing

BBC1 London 5:00am-7:30am (2 hours 30 minutes) Sat 15 Oct



Plot
Jake Humphrey presents live coverage from the Korea International Circuit, where the drivers battle for places at the front of the grid ahead of the 16th race of the season (Start-time 6.00am). Sebastian Vettel took pole position here last year, but failed to finish the race, with Fernando Alonso claiming victory. Commentary by Martin Brundle and David Coulthard
