Phillip Jackson (Jack Thompson) is a man who seemingly has it all: a successful police career, a comfortable apartment and a beautiful, devoted and sexually uninhibited girlfriend (Rose Ashton). But Phillip is about to lose everything he holds dear, perhaps even his mind. Somewhere, a psychotic serial killer is feeding women to death and broadcasting the full horror of their deaths on the internet. Phillip's investigation leads him into a gruesome and unimaginably perverse world of sexual depravity and death where the lines between victim and killer are blurred...
