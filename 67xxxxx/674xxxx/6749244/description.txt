Empire is the second album by British indie rock band Kasabian, released in August 2006.
The album went onto #1 in the UK Albums Chart upon its release and was preceded by the
release of new single "Empire" on 24 July 2006.

The album was recorded over two weeks after touring with Oasis.According to Tom Meighan in
an interview on the album with the NME in early 2006, "Empire" is a word used by the band
to describe something that is good. To date the album has sold over 1 million copies
worldwide, including more than 600,000 in the UK.
