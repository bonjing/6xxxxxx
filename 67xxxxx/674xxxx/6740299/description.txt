Guide courageous rescue workers as they deal with the aftermath of devastating storms, catastrophic earthquakes, and disastrous wildfires! Race against the clock to clear away debris; collect resources for rebuilding hospitals, bridges and power stations; and save people and animals from certain peril. Collect power-ups to increase your speed, steer seven powerful vehicles over rugged terrain, and strive for the Master ranking on 45 levels of thrilling time management action. The only tools you will need will be your mouse and the ability to figure out the quickest way to restore everything to normal. Can you save the town before Mother Nature wipes it off the map?
