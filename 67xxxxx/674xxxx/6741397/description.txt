  Game information:
  =================
   
  Thirteen years after the original game, Kalypso and b-Alive bring you
  Airline Tycoon 2, the long awaited sequel to the legendary economic
  simulation above the skies! Run your own airline, craft unique airplanes
  and evolve your company from a run-down cheap carrier to a global player.
  Airports worldwide are waiting to be explored! But beware: your rivals
  never rest! Mean opponents like the nasty Tupolevsky and the beautiful
  Natalie Childman also strive to become the world greatest airline tycoon!

  Features:
  ~~~~~~~~~
  * Dynamic economic and flight system: The world of Airline Tycoon 2
    is constantly changing!
  * Airports worldwide, from Moscow to Venezuela Â– every single one with
    realistic conditions and passenger volume
  * Individually customizable airplanes: With extensive editors, clunky
    chassis become unique, luxury travel machines!
  * Be in the action: Navigate your avatar around your 3D home airport,
    order your employees around and expand your airline. You might even
    meet some VIPs at the airport bar!
  * Challenging economic simulation: Plan flights all around the world,
    coordinate arrivals and landings, make a profit and improve the image
    of your airline.
  * Four playable avatars with defined strengths and weaknesses
  * A mix of random events bring variety to the game: The pope wants
    a special flight? Civil unrest in the third world? Volcanic ash
    clouds above Europe? Only if you act fast and properly, will you
    benefit from these events.
  * Sabotage: Annoy your opponents with devious actions! But beware!
    Your enemies will take revenge on you!
