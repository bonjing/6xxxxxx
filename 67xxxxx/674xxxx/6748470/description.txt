Love Lift Us Up - 40 Legendary Love Songs (2 CD) 320k (musicfromrizzo)

There's some greatest songs here, some sung with interesting tone
of voice such as a favourite of mine - "All by myself" (though best done by
Celine Dion thus far in my own view) - the rendition on this CD is great though
by an unknown group of male singers. Speaking of which several songs on this
2CD offering are done here by some unknown talent especially black talent
in what is a clear style of interpretation of flow that brings many 
audiences who are curious to bridge cultural styles in music.

There are some great names here though, including Pavarotti and
more (Celtic woman's Hayley Westenra etc)

Overall there is a good deal here to make the download worthwhile
and what you don't appreciate for your own sort of style you
could always leave out of what goes into your own collection
without feeling any regret of having tried the offering en masse.

enjoy
