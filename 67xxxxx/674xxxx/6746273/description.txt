Costume Quest is a Halloween adventure from Tim Schafer's Double Fine Productions. In this charming role-playing game, choose your hero and trick-or-treat through three beautiful environments full of Double Fine humor and story. Complete quests, build your party, and collect costumes along the way that allow you to transform into powerful champions and take down the evil Repugians. This heroic holiday tale will capture the imaginations of kids and kids-at-heart.
Continue the Costume Quest adventure with the Grubbins on Ice DLC pack, included free in the PC version! Face new enemies while collecting additional quests, costumes, battle stamps and creepy treat cards. Help the monsters overthrow Araxia to bring peace back to Repugia!


In the small town of Auburn Pines (Auburn Pines) is nearing Halloween. Neighborhood kids dress up in costumes vengeance, ready to go to the great campaign for candy. Nobody suspects that the city faces an invasion of hundreds of hungry Halloween monsters.
Lovely twins Rennes (Wren) and Reynolds (Reynold) are going to go in search of Halloween Adventure, like this one of the twins kidnapped by the evil Slut (Grubbin).
Your task - to save the lost twin of monsters and their hostess Nestupy (Dorsilla) with the help of loyal friends, fabulous costumes and Halloween magic drops ...

- Use all your skill and wit, and an extensive wardrobe to rid the countryside of the invasion of horrible monsters.
- Wear the necessary combination of materials to sew new costumes and turn into a knight, unicorn, ninjas, vampires, and many other characters.
- Redeem the collected items to combat the signs and pay for their learning of new skills. Also, carefully check the private notebook of completed assignments and received items to be able to track their progress.


Play as Wren or Reynold, twin siblings who absolutely adore each other, in this adventure role-playing game. Explore the suburban neighborhoods, a monster-infested shopping mall, and a mysterious town carnival.
Find hidden items, complete quests, and meet colorful characters along the way. Use the special abilities of your costumes to puzzle your way through blocked areas. Stuff your candy pail with tasty treats while using your wit, bravery and magical costumes to rid each neighborhood of creepy monsters!
