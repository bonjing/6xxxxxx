Game information :
=================

Warlords Battlecry II is a sequel to the popular & award-winning Warlords 
Battlecry by SSG. It is a Fantasy Real-Time Strategy game, where you build a 
hero - your main character - from battle to battle, gaining new skills, spells 
and levels, and ultimately becoming the mightiest force in the Realms of 
Etheria! 

Finally, role-playing heroes meet in real-time strategy. Develop your hero 
through scenarios, campaigns and multi-player games in a magical fantasy 
environment.
