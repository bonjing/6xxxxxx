In this feature-length film based on the Flintstones TV show, secret agent
Rock Slag is injured during a chase in Bedrock. Slag's chief decides to replace
the injured Slag with Fred Flintstone, who just happens to look like him. 
The trip takes Fred to Paris and Rome, which is good for Wilma, Barney, and 
Betty-but can Fred foil the mysterious Green Goose's evil plan for a destructive
missile without letting his wife and friends in on his secret?
