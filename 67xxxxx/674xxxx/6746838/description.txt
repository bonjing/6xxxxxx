American Military Aircraft is a comprehensive guide to more than 200 combat aircraft and helicopters which have kept the U.S. at the cutting edge of aviation technology. Each aircraft is covered in detail with feature boxes outlining its development, specifications, performance data and variants. Unique graphics allow the read to compare specific features like firepower, troop-carrying capacity and bombload to those of other aircraft of the same type.488 pages Full Color
