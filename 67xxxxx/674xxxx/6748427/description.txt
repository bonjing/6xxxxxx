Rating System

You will only see the rating system if you import the library into Calibre. The ratings are listed under the "Quality" column.

 
1 Star - Uncorrect, raw scan straight from source (12 Books)
 
2 Stars - First Release, maybe page numbers throughout book or other formating errors making it difficult to read (49 Books)
 
3 Stars - Minor formatting errors, no TOC (581 Books)
 
4 Stars - Close to retail, minus formatting, Includes TOC (1,418 Books)
 
5 Stars - Full Retail or indistinguishable (2,526 Books)
