This book provides recommendations from Master Gardeners from the University of Minnesota Extension Service. They recommend the best plants for thirty tough garden sites, such as dry shade, slopes, lakeshores, etc. Also includes lists of plants with special traits such as self-seeding, long-blooming, minimal litter trees, and more.
Description: 	



40 pages. Includes photographs.

Authors:Meyer, Mary
Brown, Deborah L.
Zins, Mike
Issue Date: 	2007
