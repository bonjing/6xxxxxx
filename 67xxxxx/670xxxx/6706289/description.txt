Help Abby run the NiceCorp Factory by filling orders for wonderful goodies! Use the mysterious Wondermachine9000 to convert ordinary materials like buttons, candy, and Everything Nice, into wonderful toys and tasty delights. Finish up by boxing up your gifts with a bow, and sending them out the door. Use your Time Management skills to purchases upgrades and transform NiceCorp into a top-notch assembly line.

* Fast-paced gameplay
* Purchase upgrades
* Create Everything Nice!
