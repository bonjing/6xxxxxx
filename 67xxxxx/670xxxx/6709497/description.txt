Battle of the Blades S03E02: Rock and Roll - Last 4 couples perform

Battle of the Blades is a Canadian figure skating reality show and competition that airs on CBC. The series resembles Dancing with the Stars, but in this case, professional figure skaters are teamed with well-known hockey players.

If you are a fan of figure skating, ice hockey or Dancing With The Stars, this show is worth a try.

The couples skating in this episode are:

- Kim Navarro and Russ Courtnall
- Elena Berezhnaya and Curtis Leschyshyn
- Marcy Hinzmann-Harris and Todd Simpson
- Tessa Bonhomme and David Pelletier

Two of the judges are the same each week: Sandra Bezik and Jeremy Roenick. The guest judge this week is Darcy Tucker.

The hosts are Kurt Browning and Ron Maclean.

This episode aired on CBC on Sep 26, 2011. 

CBC has a big website for this show at http://www.cbc.ca/battle/
