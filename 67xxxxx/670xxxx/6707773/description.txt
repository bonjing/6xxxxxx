iSucceed: secrets for the average joe and jane written by Adrian Shepherd is a book that cuts through all the fluff and gets right to the heart of what it takes to succeed in life. iSucceed is broken down into 10 chapters - gratitude, people, the mind, language, self-development, the laws of life, human nature, overcoming obstacles, time and preparing to fly. This is Chapter 1 of the book that delves into just how much we have to be thankful for.
