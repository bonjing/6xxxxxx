Pro Pinball Trilogy (Jimbus edition), just install and play.
-------------------


This edition works under XP, Vista and 7 (32/64 bit) and contains the following games:


- Pro Pinball: Timeshock! v1.07 (1997).

- Pro Pinball: Big race USA v1.19 (1998).

- Pro Pinball: Fantastic Journey v1.07 (1999).

- A unique installer for all of them.



The text of the games are in English, Spanish, French and German.


Every game includes a non-official executable (1.20 beta version), just in case there were trouble using the official executable.
