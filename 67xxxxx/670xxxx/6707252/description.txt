About the Author
Irvin Tucker has over twenty years of experience teaching introductory economics at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte and the University of South Carolina. He earned his B.S. in economics at N.C. State University and his M.A. and PH.D. in economics from the University of South Carolina. --This text refers to an out of print or unavailable edition of this title.
