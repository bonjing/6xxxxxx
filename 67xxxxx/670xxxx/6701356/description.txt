OPP 1991 Hannibal CD HNCD 9301, various artists performing Nino Rota music for films by Federico Fellini. Produced by Hal Willner, long-time music director on "Saturday Night Live." Sensitive pop and jazz renditions by Fred Hopkins, Ron Carter, Carla Bley, Muhal Richard Abrams, William G. Fischer, Warren Smith, Henry Threadgill, Bill Frisell, Amina Claudine Myers, Steve Lacy, Claudio Roditi, Wynton Marsalis, Dave Samuels, Jay Hoggard, Jaki Byard, David Amram and others.
