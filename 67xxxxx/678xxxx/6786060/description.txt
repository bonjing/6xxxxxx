Ricky Gervais~Out of England 2 {2010}

Two time Emmy and Golden Globe Award-winning actor/comedian/writer/director Ricky Gervais stars in the follow-up to his acclaimed 2008 stand-up special, Ricky Gervais: Out of England. Taped before a live audience at the Chicago Theater, Ricky Gervais: Out of England 2 features new material and showcases Gervais' signature humor and high-spirited, offbeat obsevations. As in his previous special, the show includes
Gervais' unique takes on a variety of disparate (and often poltically incorrect) issues, including: acts of God, the recession, the problem with heroin, fat people, famine in Africa, animal homosexuality, the story of Noah's Ark, and more.
