DizNfo v4.02 is a file_id.diz and NFO viewer. With nine skin's to choose from, can have any background .jpg or .bmp graphic, or no background graphic and any color background or font color.  

DizNfo has URL clickable links, plus you can make them any color. 

Tired of opening NFO's in notepad or wordpad and not being able to read them?  DizNfo fixes all that. v4.02 adds a help file and minor bug fixes.  

Try it, you won't go back to what you were using.
