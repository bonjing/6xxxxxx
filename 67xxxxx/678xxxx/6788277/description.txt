
Popular vacation destinations are looking to upgrade their local attractions and your skills are needed in Build-a-lot: On Vacation! Use your building skills to spruce up the local attractions, create 4-star lodging and plan the most exciting get-a-ways to charm tourists and locals alike! Can you get the gondola working at the ski resort? Will the fairgrounds get constructed in time for the hot air balloon festival? Find out in this fun Strategy game!
