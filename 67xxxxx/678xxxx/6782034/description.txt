Four the Record is the fourth studio album by American country music artist Miranda Lambert, scheduled for release on November 1, 2011 through the record label RCA Nashville. The album's first single, "Baggage Claim," became Lambert's highest debuting single at number 33 on Billboard's Hot Country Songs chart.
