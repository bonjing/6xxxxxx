               Tropico 4 sees you return  as  El  Presidente,  the
               tyrannical (or benevolent) dictator of  the  island
               paradise of Tropico.  The  world  is  changing  and
               Tropico  is  moving  with  the  times.  Tropico   4
               features all  new  missions  across  new  maps  and
               includes exciting and fun new features to keep  the
               action fresh and challenging. You  can  even  elect
               ministers  into  power  to  help  get   your   more
               controversial   decisions   passed   through    the
               government. But remember to keep your friends close
               and your enemies closer as everyone has an  agenda!
               Tropico  4  also  brings  a  new  level  of  social
               interaction  with  the  addition  of  Facebook  and
               Twitter  integration.  Post  comments  on   Twitter
               direct from the game and have updates go  out  when
               you complete missions or unlock  new  achievements.
               You can even take screenshots of  your  island  and
               post them on  your  Tropico  4  Facebook  page  and
               compare your interactive Dictator  ranking  on  the
               online leaderboards. Every little  bit  helps  when
               you are bringing your island into the 21st Century!
