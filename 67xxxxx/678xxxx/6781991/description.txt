SOUTHERN AFRICAN BIRD SOUNDS - Guy Gibbon

Set of six CDs concisely presents the sounds of over 900 Southern African bird species. The birds are presented in South African number order, and are announced by number and English name. Each recording is approximately 30 seconds in length with 10 species per track and 15 tracks per CD.
