Mirror Mixup is the picture puzzle game with a great new twist. Levels are solved by selecting areas of the picture and twisting them! Mirror Mixup features 4 great game modes. There are 150 levels to solve and 200 unlockable bonus levels. The 'Custom Play' mode also allows you to use your own pictures and music.
