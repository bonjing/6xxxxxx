Season 1, Episode 1 Aired: 1/10/2006
 
Sensing Murder (New Zeeland version 16:9)

The Patient Killer

This episode deals with the murder of Henderson school girl Tracey Ann Patient in 1976. Tracey vanished from the busy Great North Road. The following morning, her body was discovered 16km away in a layby in the Waitakere Ranges. She had been strangled with a pair of pantyhose. Psychics Deb Webber and Sue Nicholson are each produced with a photo of Tracey, and are able to produce some astounding new leads.
