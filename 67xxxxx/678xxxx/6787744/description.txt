Ready your ears and open your mind to the sounds of what was one of the biggest years in dance music history!

Ministry of Sound present The 2012 Annual - 2 Discs of the best dance and club tracks which have torn apart dance floors throughout 2011, dotted between the freshest of future anthems, destined to be the sounds of 2012.

This album features all the latest in dance remixes of superstar artists such as LMFAO, Calvin Harris, Swedish House Mafia, Example, PNAU, Martin Solveig and Havana Brown
