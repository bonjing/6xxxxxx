Gridlines is an absorbing square-forming puzzle game. Take turns drawing one line at a time on the game grid. Complete a square and you get the points, but be careful not to give away too many squares to your opponent.

Gridlines Features :

4 game modes including Classic, Bonuses & Custom.
5 difficulty settings. Play long or short games.
Challenge a human friend in the Two Player mode.
Save your current game and come back to it later.
4 beautiful locations featuring relaxing music.
Play your own music with the built in MP3 player.
