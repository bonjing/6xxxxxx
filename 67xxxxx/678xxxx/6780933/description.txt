Frogs vs. Storks is a fun strategy-puzzle game for all ages! The game opens on a beautiful pond, filled with funny little frogs. All is peaceful until the storks arrive, determined to have frog legs for dinner! Can you outsmart the storks and make the pond a nice place to live again? Leap across lily pads to save your amphibious friends in Frogs vs. Storks!
