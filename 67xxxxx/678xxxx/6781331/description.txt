DCC 1992 Gold CD reissue of classic jazz performances of songs from Lerner and Loewe's "My Fair Lady," played by drummer Shelly Manne, bassist Leroy Vinegar and pianist Andre Previn, who be musical director of the film version of "My Fair Lady" six years later. Remastered by Steve Hoffman from the original two-track Contemporary master tapes recorded August 17, 1956.
