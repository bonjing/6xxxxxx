The Lady Vanishes (Originally The Wheel Spins)

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Ethen Lina White; Full-cast dramatisation starring Renee Asherson, Jenny Funnell, Mark Tandy, Geraldine Fitzgerald and Michael Roberts; Dramatised by Neville Teller.

Iris Carr befriends English governess Miss Froy on a train journey through Europe, but then Miss Froy disappears and no one else remembers her.
