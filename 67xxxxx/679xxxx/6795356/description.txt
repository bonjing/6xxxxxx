The two Kroke concerts at Radio Krakow Studio on October 9th and 10th, 2003, have been something special. They were concerts of a group that after years of intensive touring all over Europe felt obliged to thank their friends in their home town for their long-time support, and at the same time it was an opportunity to introduce percussionist Tomasz Grochot, with whom the Trio worked since their collaboration with Nigel Kennedy for several concerts and studio sessions.
