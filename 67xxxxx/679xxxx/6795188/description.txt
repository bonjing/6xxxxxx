Battleground is the forthcoming second studio album released by British boy band The Wanted, scheduled for release on November 4, 2011, through Island Records. The album's release is preceded by the singles, "Gold Forever", which debuted at #3 on the UK Singles Chart in March 2011, "Glad You Came", which debuted at #1 on the UK Singles Chart in July 2011, and "Lightning", scheduled for release on October 16, 2011. The album will be available in two different editions - a standard thirteen track edition, and a deluxe eighteen-track edition encased in a collector's box with five postcards of the band.[1] The album's tracklisting was confirmed by Digital Spy on October 4, 2011.
