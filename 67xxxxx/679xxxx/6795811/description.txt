Broadcast on KGMB, channel 9 in Honolulu, this special edition of Emme & Friends was associate products by James MacArthur, who also shared hosting duties with Emme Tomimbang.

The 47-minute program\'s subject was a look back at Hawaii Five-O and included interviews with nearly every regular cast member, a number of frequent guest players, and many behind-the-scenes personnel. Various locations familiar to Five-O viewers were also featured, as well as archival footage from both in front of and behind the camera.
