Kalabra started to play together at the Royal Academy of Music in Stockholm 1995.
After their second concert, they were offered a record contract from Caprice Records.
1998 Kalabra won the international competition for traditional music bands at Kaustinen
Folk Music Festival in Finland and fROOTS Magazine wrote about them: Kalabra's impressive debut album seems something of a recapitulation of a whole lot of Swedish tradition-rooted music.
