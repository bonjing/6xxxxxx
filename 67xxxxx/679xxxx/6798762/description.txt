CHATRAPATI SHIVAJI

Music by C. Ramchandra ,

Lighting by G. Shinde, 
Sound Recording: Jadhav and Mole,

Still Photography by Dada Gajbar,

Dress designed by Vishwasrao, 
Dance directed by Hazarilal, Aadamsaheb, Kumar Atre and Kusum, 
Location: Jayprabha Studios (Kolhapur).

The first hindi song written by Shilenra for Marathi movie,

Parshwnath Aaltekar played Mirza raje Jaysingh in Marathi version whereas Pruthvi Raj Kapoor played the same role in Hindi version.
