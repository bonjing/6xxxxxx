  CO2 Pistols & Rifles


 In this guide to CO2-powered pistols and rifles, James E. House ofers a comprehensive history of CO2 guns, dating back to the 1930s, and a discussion of propulsion, pellets and sights. The book also offers sections on target shooting, games and varmints.
