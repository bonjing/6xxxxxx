Super Street Fighter IV Arcade Edition (Taito Type X2 nesys 1.0)
----------------------------------------------------------------



This DVD-5 version has:


- The game with multiplayer mode available through L.A.N., but not two players in the same computer (this will come with nesys 1.1).

- Every fighter unlocked, even Oni and Evil Ryu.

- Guides with the movements of all the characters, their strong and weak points, etc.

- Another guide about how to play in L.A.N.

- A trainer.
