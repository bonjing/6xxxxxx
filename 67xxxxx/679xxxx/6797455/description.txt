North Square is a British drama about a group of young, sexy, but irreverent barristers all hoping to make their mark in the legal profession at a Leeds defense chambers. Under the leadership of their by-the-book Chief Clerk Peter McLeish, who is clever and obsessed by work. Peter doesn't appear to have a social life. He's struggling to make this new enterprise work and will do anything to make it succeed and beat his arch rival Marlowe.
