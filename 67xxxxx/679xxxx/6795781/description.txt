Title: Sid Meier's Civil War Collection
Genre: strategy / historical / real-time
Released: 20th September 2000
Developer: Firaxis Games
Publisher: Electronic Arts
Description:
Command your troops! Attack from the trees to protect your brigade, rally around a General for a quicker recovery, entrench your troops as your cannons blast the hillside, and much, much more! This definitive Civil War Collection includes Sid Meier's Gettysburg! and Sid Meier's Antietam!, along with the all-new South Mountain campaign which features eight new scenarios and two new maps.

Bonus content: manuals, patch.

How to play:
1) Download the game;
2) Mount or burn disc image;
3) Run autorun.exe to install (it will start automatically if autorun is enabled);
4) After installation is complete, run SMG_2000_XP_Compatibility_Update.exe to patch Sid Meier's Gettysburg;
5) Play the games with the disc inserted.

Notice!
These games are old, but I played them without any problems on Windows 7 x64.

If you like the game, BUY it!
