Pink Floyd
Embryo: The Formative Years 1969-1972

This is a compilation meant to introduce new listeners to much of the pre-Dark Side Of the Moon works of Pink Floyd. It is sort of a companion 

piece to "Echoes", hence no tracks found on "Echoes: The Best of Pink Floyd" are repeated here. The tracks are segued together in a similar 

manner.

1. Grantchester Meadows (1969 - Ummagumma)
2. San Tropez (1971 - Meddle)
3. Biding My Time (1969 - Relics - Ummagumma era outtake
4. Green Is The Colour (1969 - More soundtrack)
5. Come In Number 51, Your Time Is Up (1970 - Zabriskie Point soundtrack)
6. Wot's..Uh The Deal (1972 - Obscured By Clouds)
7. Fat Old Sun (1970 - Atom Heart Mother)
8. Cymbaline (1969 - More Soundtrack)
9. If (1970 - Atom Heart Mother)
10. Country Song (Alternate Mix) (1970 - Zabriskie Point Outtake - "A Journey Through Space and Time" cd)
11. Fearless (1971 - Meddle)
12. The Narrow Way Part 3 (1969 - Ummagumma)
13. Embryo (BBC Live at Paris Cinema London) (1970 - The BBC Archives 1970 a/k/a "Mooed Music" cd)
14. Love Scene Version 6: Blues (1970 - Zabriskie Point Outtake from expanded Rhino 1997 reissue)

Enjoy and if you like what you hear please support this band and pick up their excellent official releases!
