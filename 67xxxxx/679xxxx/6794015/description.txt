Baby Got Boobs - Britney Brighton (Brittny Blew) in: Bed and Breakfast part 1 - August 27th, 2008



Britney runs a bed and breakfast with her mom: Rachel. Today Rachel brought 
two hitchhikers (Mikey and Charles) in need of a place to stay. After being 
formally introduced, Britney goes to show Charles his room for the day and 
then she goes back to her own room. Charles decides to follow her and ends 
up catching a glimpse of Britney getting undressed in her room. The peep show 
is over when Britney realizes that Charles is watching her but instead of 
getting mad; Britney shows him how the girls in her small town like to kill time!
