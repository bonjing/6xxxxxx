All of the Above is the eighth praise and worship album by Hillsong United, and their first ever studio recorded project and the first of a three-part global project. The album includes a DVD containing three live worship tracks recorded at the 2006 Encounterfest youth conference, a sermon from Phil Dooley and a bonus features section. It debuted at #6 on the ARIA Albums Chart on 26 March 2007.

It has also reached number 1 on the Billboard\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\'s Top Christian/Gospel Albums, on the iTunes Christian music digital album sales, No. 1 on the Nielsen Christian SoundScan chart, and has moved to No. 60 on the Billboard US Top 200.
