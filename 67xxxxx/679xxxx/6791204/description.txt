Search for Satan

History Documentary hosted by Andy Hamilton, published by BBC in 2011 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Just how did the Devil get inside our heads? And who put him there? For Halloween, award-winning comedy writer and performer Andy Hamilton, the creator and star of Radio 4's acclaimed infernal comedy Old Harry's Game, explores just who the devil Satan is, where he comes from and what he's been up to all this time.
