The Secret Parts

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Eve Brook; Full-cast dramatisation starring Celia Imrie, Frances Barber, Nathaniel Parker, John Rowe, Nicholas Woollaton and Victoria Wicks; Dramatised by David Edgar.

When she finds the body of one of her colleagues lying in a council house corridor, councillor Helena Kerr has immediate suspicions about the identity of the murderer - her main political opponent - and she sets out to prove it.
