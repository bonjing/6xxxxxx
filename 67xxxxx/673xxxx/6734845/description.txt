Today We have lost one of the finest singers of our nation.Jagjit Singh, with his golden voice managed to conquer the industry and became one India's finest ghazal singers.So At Djluv we pay a tribute to this great singer and release this album which highlights various aspects of the life and times of Jagjit Singh, and also so that users can get acquainted with his works .
