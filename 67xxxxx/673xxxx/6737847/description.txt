V Rally 3 includes all of the most coveted rally cars on todays circuit, and each one is completely customizable. Scurrying spectators and deforming car parts help make your track environments come alive, and the new collision engine and physics models let you feel what its like to be a real pro rally driver. You can race the worlds top rally cars, including the Subaru Impreza, Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution 7, Citroen Xsara, Ford Focus, and more. You will fight your way through the most extreme driving conditions in six different locations: Finland, Sweden, France, Germany, Great Britain, and Corsica. The cars are created with more than 15,000 polygons. The game includes both single and multiplayer versions of Time Attack and Challenge modes.
