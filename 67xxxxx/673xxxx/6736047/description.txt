Pacific Northwest (DK Eyewitness Travel Guides) by Stephen Brewer DK Publishing | English | 2010 | ISBN: 0756661900 | 352 pages | PDF

From soaring snow-covered mountains to arid deserts, deep river gorges to the beautiful island-dotted coastline, this guide showcases one of the world's most beautiful regions. In-depth coverage is given to the region's top cities: Portland, Seattle and Vancouver - known for their thriving arts scene, as well as the throngs of impressive natural wonders which are never too far way. Aided by excellent maps, floor plans and illustrations, visitors will be finding their way around the sights - from fish markets to world-class museums, just like the locals. This guidebook is all you will need to get the best that Oregon, Washington and British Columbia have to offer.
