One album. 60 tracks. Thousands of memories. The tracks found on '60 Best Trance Hits Ever' can best be described as one big trip down memory lane. These are the anthems you've danced to in the past two decades, the themes that kept you going when you were in need of energy and the jewels that still rock you today.

60 timeless trance tracks, including classics like 'Energy 52 - Cafe Del Mar', 'Veracocha - Carte Blanche', 'Rui Da Silva - Touch Me', as well as the more recent 'Wippenberg - Chakalaka', 'Dash Berlin - Till The Sky Falls Down' en 'Armin van Buuren ft. Sharon den Adel - In & Out of Love'. But '60 Best Trance Hits Ever' also indulges you with the sounds of Motorcycle, Southside Spinners, Tiesto, ATB, Andain, Markus Schulz, Mauro Picotto, Oceanlab and Sander Kleinenberg. Legendary productions, resounding the past, rocking today and still moving you tomorrow. This is '60 Best Trance Hits Ever'!
