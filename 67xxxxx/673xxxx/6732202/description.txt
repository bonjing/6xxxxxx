Nightwish - Finnish metal band, performing songs mainly in English. It was formed in 1996 in Kitee. Early Works Nightwish characterized by a combination of female vocals of the former academic vocalist Tarja Turunen and unique atmosphere of power-metal. This style is often defined as power metal and symphonic metal. Voice of vocalist Anette Olzon current differs significantly from the previous vote of the group.
