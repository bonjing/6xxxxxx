Murder West One

From BBC Radio 4 Extra. Written by Nick Fisher; Full-cast dramatisations starring Imelda Staunton as Julie Enfield.

Episode 1: A Cure for Death

A mysterious death leads to an investigation of Harley Street doctors and cutting-edge medicine of a very non-Hippocratic variety.

Episode 2: The Art of the Matter

A strange theft and a clever scam in one of London's art galleries around New Bond Street ultimately leads to murder.

Episode 3: The Five Star Killing

At a glitzy London hotel, the body of an unknown woman is found in the service area where champagne bottles are the only litter.

Episode 4: Soho Espresso

A dead man is found above a coffee bar, but when robbery appears not to be the motive - then what is?
