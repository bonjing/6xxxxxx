Be a Virtual Druggist..!!!

name: NoseCandy .apk
version : 2.0.1
REQUIRES ANDROID:1.5 and up
CATEGORY:Casual

* Vial, razor, $100 bill all included
* Shake phone to splash the blow
* Phone vibrates when you're high
* Five difficulty levels
* Global leaderboard for scoring
* Free Play and Play For Score modes
* installable to SD card (Froyo and later)

Installation Notes:

1) Go to settings - applications - enable the 'unknown sources' option.          
2) Put the .apk files somewhere on your SD card.                                 
3) Open your file manager on the device (like Astro Manager from market). 
                    
4) Select a .apk file.                                                           
5) If you get the 'complete action using' pop-up, choose 'package installer'.

Tested on:

HTC Desire HD
HTC Wildfire
HTC Wildfire S
Samsung Galaxy S
