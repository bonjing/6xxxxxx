


Bruce Wayne returns to Gotham City after extensive training in martial arts. James Gordon also arrives in Gotham with his wife Barbara, but begins a flirtatious relationship with co-worker Detective Sarah Essen. Bruce first decides to don the Batman costume and become a vigilante; becoming partners with Lieutenant James Gordon in the process. James Gordon notices the corruption in Gotham City and wants to fix it. The film mainly focuses on both of these characters.
