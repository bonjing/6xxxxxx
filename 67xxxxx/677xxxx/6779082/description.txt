About 4MLinux.
4MLinux is a mini Linux distro focusing on the following "4M":
 - Maintenance (system rescue Live CD),
 - Multimedia (e.g., playing video DVDs),
 - Miniserver (using the inetd daemon),
 - Mystery (console games). 
http://4mlinux.com/about.html

2011-10-28

4MLinux-3.0-game-edition released.
All 4MLinux applications have been compiled under Slackware 13.37 (x86/32-bit/SMP):
 - kernel 2.6.39.2,
 - glibc 2.13,
 - gcc 4.5.2.
