Hi,

Time for Porcupine Tree. One of the best Indie Bands around.

While looking through the material i found that there was a lot, but hardly complete and much of poor quality. So I collected all i could find and selected the best quality, resulting in an almost complete Discography.

Its quite a lot so i divided it into two parts: 1 an essential survival kit (10 cdees) and the rest that maybe nice additions or just for people who love Porcupine Tree (very much).

If you download the lot, please seed and forget all other collections. If you have any additions, please refer to this collection to make it complete.
