RELEASE NOTES

 Stronghold 3 is the long-awaited third installment in the award winning
 castle-building series. Set 10 years after the original, the Wolf it
 seems, has cheated death and during his painful recovery he s become
 even more bitter and twisted. Now he seeks revenge. Raiding villages
 and towns during the dead of night, the Wolf has created a tide of
 panic and you and your allies are the only force that stand against
 him. In this stunning new update to the world's favorite Castle Sim
 will you triumph by day or die by night?
