Apple's iOS firmware v5.0 for iPod Touch 4G. Original Firmware from Apple.com.

**To restore using this firmware in iTunes, go tohow you would normally restore, but instead of clicking restore, hold Shift (Command on Macs) then click it, and select this IPSW. Same method for updating. Make a backup manually before Updating/Restoring (Right click on iPod/iPhone on right dock --&gt; Back Up)**
