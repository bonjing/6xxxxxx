Pure Hip Hop delivers a quality retrospective selection of the genres biggest hitters across 3CDs.
A joint venture combining the hit-heavy catalogues of both Warner Music and Universal guarantees the appearance of the biggest possible names with confirmed tracks from Jay Z, Kanye West, 50 Cent, Busta Rhymes, Diddy Dirty Money, Wiz Khalifa, Flo Rida, The Beastie Boys, Notorious B.I.G., Dr Dre ft Snoop, Pete Rock & CL Smooth, The Jungle Brothers, Coolio, Gangstarr, Arrested Development, Missy Elliot, Grandmaster Flash, The Sugarhill Gang, Stetsasonicthe list goes on.
In short it's a 3CD retrospective with 60 huge tracks marketed under the successful Pure branding that has great crossover and gifting potential as we near the crucial Xmas trading period
