Hurtigruten: Time-lapse (DVD NTSC)

The download contain an ISO-file to be burned as an 4.7GB DVD-disc. The DVD will only play in a NTSC-compatible DVD-player. NTSC is used in North-America, part of South-America and Japan.

The download also comes with cover- and disc-art.
