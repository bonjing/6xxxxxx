Taken from the website of the Australian Recording Industry Association.

The distribution of tracks over the decade isn't necessarily even due to the shift to the recording of digital sales in 2006. This change doesn't really show itself until 2008; the distribution shows 43 tracks from 2008 and 2009, and 57 from the proceeding eight years. So if you're looking for more tracks from earlier in the decade, hang out for the end-of-year charts for those years, which I'm hoping to have ready by early 2012.
