Plot: A serial murderer of teenage girls is loose, and working the case are a possibly corrupt detective Lou Miles a probably corrupt chief inspector Brodsky and his "favorite snitch." Dark personal histories and current politics mix to get the ambitious DA and her investigator involved. 

Director: Randy Bradshaw
  Writer: Ian Adams (based on his novel Bad Faith)
   Stars: Tony Nardi, John Kapelos and Kenneth Welsh 

About Ian Adams. Born in 1937, he is a Canadian author of fiction and non-fiction novels. Originally a journalist, he is now best known for his writing: his most successful novel to date is Agent of Influence which has also been made into a film. He currently lives in Toronto, Ontario.
