At Your Inconvenience is the second studio album by English rapper Professor Green, it will be released on 28 October 2011. "At Your Inconvenience" was released as a promotional single on 26 July. The first official single "Read All About It" was released on 21 Septmember 2011.[1] Some guests from his debut album appear on the album, including Ed Drewett, Fink and Emile Sande, New guests include Slaughterhouse and Bad Meet Evils, Royce da 5'9, Kobe, Luciana, Ruth Anne, Sierra Kusterbeck and Haydon.
