Nederlands:

 
Tijdens de Tweede Wereldoorlog waren er meer dan een miljoen Amerikaanse soldaten gelegerd in Engeland. 
Hun aanwezigheid raakte de levens van velen, tot in de verste uithoeken van het land. 
Zo ook in een klein Engels plaatsje waar de drie Amerikaanse soldaten Matt, John en Danny geluk vinden bij de Britse vrouwen Jean, Helen en Mollie. 
Tegen de achterkant van de grimmige oorlog is een klein stukje geluk veel waard, al duurt het maar even en zijn de culturele verschillen groot.


Engels:


During WWII, the United States set up army bases in Great Britain as part of the war effort. 
Against their proper sensibilities, many of the Brits don't much like the brash Yanks, 
One Yank/Brit relationship that develops is between married John, an Army Captain, and the aristocratic Helen, whose naval husband is away at war. 
Helen does whatever she needs to support the war effort. Helen loves her husband, but Helen and John are looking for some comfort during the difficult times.
Another relationship develops between one of John's charges, Matt, a talented mess hall cook, and Jean. 
Jean is apprehensive at first about even seeing Matt, who is persistent in his pursuit of her. 
Jean is in a committed relationship with the kind Ken, her childhood sweetheart who is also away at war...
