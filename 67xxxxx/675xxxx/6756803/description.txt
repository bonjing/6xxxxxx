When Charles Lee Ray needs to get quick escape from cop, Mike Norris, he takes his soul and buries it into playful, seemingly good guy doll Chucky. 
Little does he know a little boy by the name of Andy Barclay will be the new owner of him soon-to-come. 
Charles confides in Andy while he commits numerous murders. Once the adults catch up to Andy\'s story as truth, it\'s too late.
