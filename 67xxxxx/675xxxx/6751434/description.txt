Sogno ribelle is a 1992 compilation album from Italian rock band Litfiba that contains new versions of old songs with a more rock sounding, live tracks and an unreleased song "Linea d'ombra".

Litfiba is an Italian rock band formed in Florence in early 1980. Originated from the meeting of 5 punk, New Wave and simply rock music fans with then no musical background. The band evolved from British influenced new-wave rock to a more personal rock sound influenced by Mediterranean vibes; the songs are mostly sung in Italian.
