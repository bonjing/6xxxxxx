Romania (DC No Reservations)

Culture, Travel Documentary hosted by Anthony Bourdain and published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC No Reservations series in 2008 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Tony and Zamir have had many adventures. This time they're in Romania to celebrate Zamir's birthday. Along the way, they learn about vampires, communists, and the strong cultural history of Romania.
